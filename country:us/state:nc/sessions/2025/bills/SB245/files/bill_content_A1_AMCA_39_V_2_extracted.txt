Representative Lambeth
1 moves to amend the bill on page 1, lines 2-4, by rewriting those lines to read:
2 "AN ACT TO AUTHORIZE ADDITIONAL REMOTE RENEWALS OF DRIVERS
3 LICENSES, TO ELIMINATE THE DRIVING LOG REQUIREMENT AND AUTHORIZE
4 REMOTE ISSUANCE FOR FULL PROVISIONAL LICENSES, TO MAKE A TECHNICAL
5 CORRECTION RELATED TO POSSESSION OF EMBALMING FLUID, AND TO MAKE
6 VARIOUS APPROPRIATIONS.";
7
8 and on page 2, lines 28-29, by rewriting those lines to read:
9 "SECTION 3.5.(a) G.S. 90-96.2(c3)(3a), as enacted by S.L. 2025-71, reads as
10 rewritten:
11 "(3a) A violation of G.S. 90-113.154 G.S. 90-113.154(b)(1) punishable as a Class I
12 felony."
13 SECTION 3.5.(b) This section becomes effective December 1, 2025, and applies to
14 offenses committed on or after that date.
15 SECTION 4.(a) There is appropriated from the IT Reserve established in Section
16 2.2(d) of S.L. 2023-134 to the Administrative Office of the Courts, Budget Fund 100051, the
17 sum of five million dollars ($5,000,000) in nonrecurring funds for the 2025-2026 fiscal year to
18 be used for time-limited technology and business process positions related to eCourts
19 implementation.
20 SECTION 4.(b) There is appropriated from the IT Reserve established in Section
21 2.2(d) of S.L. 2023-134 to the Department of Information Technology the sum of fifteen million
22 dollars ($15,000,000) in nonrecurring funds for the 2025-2026 fiscal year to be used for
23 cybersecurity.
24 SECTION 4.(c) This section is effective July 1, 2025.
25 SECTION 4.5.(a) There is appropriated from the General Fund to the State Bureau
26 of Investigation (Bureau) the sum of one million four hundred thousand dollars ($1,400,000) in
27 recurring funds beginning in the 2025-2026 fiscal year to be used to create up to 10 nonsworn
28 administrative positions to support the Bureau.
29 SECTION 4.5.(b) There is appropriated from the General Fund to the State Highway
30 Patrol the sum of three million dollars ($3,000,000) in recurring funds beginning in the
31 2025-2026 fiscal year to be used to hire 26 full-time nonsworn administrative positions.
32 SECTION 4.5.(c) This section is effective July 1, 2025.
