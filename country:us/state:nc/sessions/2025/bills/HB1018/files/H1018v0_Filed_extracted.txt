GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF NORTH CAROLINA
Apr 21, 2026
SESSION 2025
HOUSE PRINCIPAL CLERK
H D
Short Title: East Spencer/Remove Cap:Satellite Annexations. (Local)
Sponsors: Representative Warren.
Referred to:
1 A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
2 AN ACT REMOVING THE CAP ON SATELLITE ANNEXATIONS FOR THE TOWN OF
3 EAST SPENCER.
4 The General Assembly of North Carolina enacts:
5 SECTION 1. G.S. 160A-58.1 reads as rewritten:
6 "§ 160A-58.1. Petition for annexation; standards.
7 …
8 (b) A noncontiguous area proposed for annexation must meet all of the following
9 standards:
10 …
11 (5) The area within the proposed satellite corporate limits, when added to the area
12 within all other satellite corporate limits, may not exceed ten percent (10%)
13 of the area within the primary corporate limits of the annexing city.
14 This subdivision does not apply to the Cities of Archdale, Asheboro,
15 Belmont, Claremont, Concord, Conover, Durham, Elizabeth City, Gastonia,
16 Greenville, Hickory, Kannapolis, Kings Mountain, Locust, Lowell, Marion,
17 Mount Airy, Mount Holly, New Bern, Newton, Oxford, Randleman, Roanoke
18 Rapids, Rockingham, Saluda, Sanford, Salisbury, Shelby, Southport,
19 Statesville, and Washington and the Towns of Ahoskie, Angier, Apex, Ayden,
20 Belville, Benson, Bladenboro, Bridgeton, Bunn, Burgaw, Calabash, Carthage,
21 Catawba, China Grove, Clayton, Columbia, Columbus, Cramerton, Creswell,
22 Dallas, Dobson, East Spencer, Four Oaks, Franklin, Franklinton,
23 Franklinville, Fuquay-Varina, Garner, Godwin, Goldston, Granite Quarry,
24 Green Level, Grimesland, Harrisburg, Holly Ridge, Holly Springs,
25 Hookerton, Hope Mills, Huntersville, Jamestown, Kenansville, Kenly,
26 Knightdale, Landis, Laurel Park, Liberty, Lillington, Louisburg, Maggie
27 Valley, Maiden, Mayodan, Maysville, Middlesex, Midland, Mocksville,
28 Morrisville, Mount Pleasant, Nashville, North Wilkesboro, Norwood, Oak
29 Island, Oakboro, Ocean Isle Beach, Pembroke, Pine Level, Pollocksville,
30 Princeton, Ramseur, Ranlo, Richlands, Rockwell, Rolesville, Rutherfordton,
31 Shallotte, Siler City, Smithfield, Spencer, Spring Lake, Stanley, Stem, Stovall,
32 Surf City, Swansboro, Taylorsville, Troutman, Troy, Vass, Wallace, Warsaw,
33 Watha, Waynesville, Weldon, Wendell, West Jefferson, Wilson's Mills,
34 Windsor, Wingate, Yadkinville, Youngsville, and Zebulon.
35 …."
36 SECTION 2. This act is effective when it becomes law.
