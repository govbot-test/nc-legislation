GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF NORTH CAROLINA
SESSION 2025
H 2
Committee Substitute Favorable 5/6/26
Short Title: Remove Cap: Satellite Annexations. (Local)
Sponsors:
Referred to:
April 22, 2026
1 A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
2 AN ACT REMOVING THE CAP ON SATELLITE ANNEXATIONS FOR THE TOWNS OF
3 CLEVELAND AND SPRUCE PINE.
4 The General Assembly of North Carolina enacts:
5 SECTION 1. G.S. 160A-58.1, as amended by Section 1 of S.L. 2025-14, reads as
6 rewritten:
7 "§ 160A-58.1. Petition for annexation; standards.
8 …
9 (b) A noncontiguous area proposed for annexation must meet all of the following
10 standards:
11 …
12 (5) The area within the proposed satellite corporate limits, when added to the area
13 within all other satellite corporate limits, may not exceed ten percent (10%)
14 of the area within the primary corporate limits of the annexing city.
15 This subdivision does not apply to the Cities of Archdale, Asheboro,
16 Belmont, Claremont, Concord, Conover, Durham, Elizabeth City, Gastonia,
17 Greenville, Hickory, Kannapolis, Kings Mountain, Locust, Lowell, Marion,
18 Mount Airy, Mount Holly, New Bern, Newton, Oxford, Randleman, Roanoke
19 Rapids, Rockingham, Saluda, Sanford, Salisbury, Shelby, Southport,
20 Statesville, and Washington and the Towns of Ahoskie, Angier, Apex, Ayden,
21 Belville, Benson, Bladenboro, Bridgeton, Bunn, Burgaw, Calabash, Carthage,
22 Catawba, China Grove, Clayton, Cleveland, Columbia, Columbus,
23 Cramerton, Creswell, Dallas, Dobson, Four Oaks, Franklin, Franklinton,
24 Franklinville, Fuquay-Varina, Garner, Godwin, Goldston, Granite Quarry,
25 Green Level, Grimesland, Harrisburg, Holly Ridge, Holly Springs,
26 Hookerton, Hope Mills, Huntersville, Jamestown, Kenansville, Kenly,
27 Knightdale, Landis, Laurel Park, Liberty, Lillington, Louisburg, Madison,
28 Maggie Valley, Maiden, Mayodan, Maysville, Middlesex, Midland,
29 Mocksville, Morrisville, Mount Pleasant, Nashville, North Wilkesboro,
30 Norwood, Oak Island, Oakboro, Ocean Isle Beach, Pembroke, Pine Level,
31 Pollocksville, Princeton, Ramseur, Ranlo, Richlands, Rockwell, Rolesville,
32 Rutherfordton, Shallotte, Siler City, Smithfield, Spencer, Spring Lake, Spruce
33 Pine, Stanley, Stem, Stovall, Surf City, Swansboro, Taylorsville, Troutman,
34 Troy, Vass, Wallace, Warsaw, Watha, Waynesville, Weldon, Wendell, West
35 Jefferson, Wilson's Mills, Windsor, Wingate, Yadkinville, Youngsville, and
36 Zebulon.

General Assembly Of North Carolina Session 2025
1 …."
2 SECTION 2. This act is effective when it becomes law.
