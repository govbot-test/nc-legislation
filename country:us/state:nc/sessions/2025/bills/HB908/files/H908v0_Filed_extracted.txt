GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF NORTH CAROLINA
Apr 10, 2025
SESSION 2025
HOUSE PRINCIPAL CLERK
H D
Short Title: Discretionary Dismissal of Charges/Capacity. (Public)
Sponsors: Representative Greene.
Referred to:
1 A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
2 AN ACT TO GIVE JUDGES DISCRETION REGARDING THE DISMISSAL OF CRIMINAL
3 CHARGES WHEN A DEFENDANT LACKS CAPACITY TO PROCEED.
4 The General Assembly of North Carolina enacts:
5 SECTION 1. G.S. 15A-1008(a) reads as rewritten:
6 "(a) When a defendant lacks capacity to proceed, the court shall may dismiss the charges
7 upon the earliest of the following occurrences:
8 …."
9 SECTION 2. This act is effective when it becomes law and applies to offenses
10 committed on or after that date.
