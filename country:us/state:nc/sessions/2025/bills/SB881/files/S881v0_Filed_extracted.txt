SENATE BILL DRS45490-MCa-291
Short Title: Funds for Pitt Co. Boys & Girls Clubs. (Public)
Sponsors: Senator Smith (Primary Sponsor).
Referred to:
1 A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
2 AN ACT TO APPROPRIATE FUNDS FOR THE BOYS & GIRLS CLUBS OF THE
3 COASTAL PLAIN.
4 The General Assembly of North Carolina enacts:
5 SECTION 1. There is appropriated from the General Fund to the Office of State
6 Budget and Management the sum of five hundred thousand dollars ($500,000) in nonrecurring
7 funds for the 2026-2027 fiscal year to provide a directed grant to the Boys & Girls Clubs of the
8 Coastal Plain Foundation for operational expenses and expansion of programs. These funds shall
9 be used for the Clubs in Pitt County only.
10 SECTION 2. This act becomes effective July 1, 2026.
