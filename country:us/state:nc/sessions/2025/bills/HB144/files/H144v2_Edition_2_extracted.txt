Section 1:
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF NORTH CAROLINA
SESSION 2025
H 2
Committee Substitute Favorable 5/19/26
Short Title: Elect SBE/Superintendent as SBE Chair. (Public)
Sponsors:
Referred to:
February 18, 2025
1 A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
2 AN ACT TO AMEND THE CONSTITUTION OF NORTH CAROLINA TO ELECT
3 MEMBERS OF THE STATE BOARD OF EDUCATION, TO MAKE THE
4 SUPERINTENDENT OF PUBLIC INSTRUCTION THE CHAIR OF THE STATE BOARD
5 OF EDUCATION AS AN EX OFFICIO MEMBER, AND TO REQUIRE THAT
6 VACANCY APPOINTMENTS BE FILLED AS PROVIDED BY LAW.
7 The General Assembly of North Carolina enacts:
8 SECTION 1.(a) Section 4 of Article IX of the Constitution of North Carolina reads
9 as rewritten:
10 "Sec. 4. State Board of Education.
11 (1) Board. The State Board of Education shall consist of the Lieutenant Governor, the
12 Treasurer, and eleven members appointed by the Governor, subject to confirmation by the
13 General Assembly in joint session. The General Assembly shall divide the State into eight
14 educational districts. Of the appointive members of the Board, one shall be appointed from each
15 of the eight educational districts and three shall be appointed from the State at large.
16 Appointments shall be for overlapping terms of eight years. Appointments to fill vacancies shall
17 be made by the Governor for the unexpired terms and shall not be subject to confirmation.the
18 Superintendent of Public Instruction, and a number of elected members equal to the membership
19 of the House of Representatives of the Congress of the United States apportioned to the State by
20 federal law.
21 (2) Superintendent of Public Instruction. The Superintendent of Public Instruction shall
22 be the secretary chair and chief administrative officer of the State Board of Education.
23 (3) Elected members. Elected members of the State Board of Education shall be elected
24 to overlapping four-year terms of office. Elected members shall be elected from districts
25 established by the General Assembly by the voters of each district in the manner prescribed by
26 law. Vacancies for elected members shall be filled for the remainder of the term of office in the
27 manner prescribed by law by the General Assembly."
28 SECTION 1.(b) Subsection (5) of Section 22 of Article II of the Constitution of
29 North Carolina reads as rewritten:
30 "(5) Other exceptions. Every bill:
31 (a) In which the General Assembly makes an appointment or appointments to
32 public office and which contains no other matter;
33 (b) Revising the senate districts and the apportionment of Senators among those
34 districts and containing no other matter;
35 (c) Revising the representative districts and the apportionment of Representatives
36 among those districts and containing no other matter; or
*H144-v-2*
General Assembly Of North Carolina Session 2025
1 (d) Revising the districts for the election of members of the House of
2 Representatives of the Congress of the United States and the apportionment
3 of Representatives among those districts and containing no other
4 matter,matter; or
5 (e) Revising the districts for the election of members of the State Board of
6 Education and the apportionment of Board members among those districts and
7 containing no other matter,
8 shall be read three times in each house before it becomes law and shall be signed by the presiding
9 officers of both houses."
10 SECTION 2. The amendment set out in Section 1 of this act shall be submitted to
11 the qualified voters of the State at a statewide general election to be held in November 2026,
12 which election shall be conducted under the laws then governing elections in the State. Ballots,
13 voting systems, or both may be used in accordance with Chapter 163 of the General Statutes. The
14 question to be used in the voting systems and ballots shall be:
15 "[ ] FOR [ ] AGAINST
16 A constitutional amendment providing for the following changes to the State Board
17 of Education, beginning January 1, 2028:
18 (1) The Superintendent of Public Instruction will serve as a member and chair of
19 the State Board of Education.
20 (2) Except for the Council of State members serving on the State Board of
21 Education, the voters of the district will elect all members of the State Board
22 of Education to four-year terms.
23 (3) State Board of Education members will be elected from districts established
24 by the General Assembly. Bills revising those districts will become law upon
25 ratification.
26 (4) All vacancies for elected positions on the State Board of Education will be
27 filled in a manner provided by law."
28 SECTION 3. If a majority of the votes cast on the question are in favor of the
29 amendment set out in Section 1 of this act, the State Board of Elections shall certify the
30 amendment to the Secretary of State. The Secretary of State shall enroll the amendment so
31 certified among the permanent records of that office.
32 SECTION 4. If the amendment is approved by the qualified voters as provided in
33 this act, Section 1 of this act becomes effective January 1, 2028, and applies to terms of office
34 beginning January 1, 2029. Except as otherwise provided, this act is effective when it becomes
35 law.
