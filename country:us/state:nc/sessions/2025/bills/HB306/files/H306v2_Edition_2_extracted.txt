GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF NORTH CAROLINA
SESSION 2025
H 2
HOUSE BILL 306
Committee Substitute Favorable 4/17/25
Short Title: Affordable Housing for Local Employees. (Local)
Sponsors:
Referred to:
March 6, 2025
1 A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
2 AN ACT AUTHORIZING THE TOWNS OF BLOWING ROCK AND BOONE AND THE
3 COUNTIES OF DARE, DURHAM, AND WATAUGA TO CONSTRUCT AND PROVIDE
4 AFFORDABLE HOUSING FOR TOWN AND COUNTY EMPLOYEES.
5 The General Assembly of North Carolina enacts:
6 SECTION 1. Notwithstanding G.S. 66-58, G.S. 115C-518, Article 12 of Chapter
7 160A of the General Statutes, or any other provision of law, the Towns of Blowing Rock and
8 Boone and the Counties of Dare, Durham, and Watauga (hereinafter "local government unit")
9 are authorized to do all of the following:
10 (1) Either separately or through a partnership, joint venture, land trust, or other
11 similar entity, construct and provide affordable housing for local government
12 unit employees on property owned by the unit.
13 (2) Convey property owned by the local government unit to a partnership, joint
14 venture, land trust, or similar entity for the purpose of constructing, providing,
15 or maintaining affordable housing for unit employees. A unit shall not convey
16 to a partnership, joint venture, land trust, or any other entity any property that
17 was acquired through the exercise of eminent domain on or after the date this
18 act becomes law.
19 (3) Contract with any person, partnership, corporation, or other business entity to
20 finance, construct, or maintain affordable housing for local government unit
21 employees.
22 (4) Rent or sell affordable housing units for residential use exclusively to local
23 government unit employees. A unit may establish reasonable rents or sales
24 prices, charge below-market rates, offer below-market financing, and place
25 reasonable restrictions and buyback provisions on the resale of the affordable
26 housing units.
27 SECTION 2. Any affordable housing units constructed pursuant to the provisions of
28 this act shall comply with all applicable building codes, zoning ordinances, and any other State
29 and local regulations related to the construction of residential homes.
30 SECTION 3. This act is effective when it becomes law.
