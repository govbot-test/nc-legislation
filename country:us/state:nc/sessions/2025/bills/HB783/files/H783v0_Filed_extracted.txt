H.B. 783
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF NORTH CAROLINA
Apr 3, 2025
SESSION 2025
HOUSE PRINCIPAL CLERK
H D
HOUSE BILL DRH40123-LGa-49
Short Title: Funds for Sidekicks Academy. (Public)
Sponsors: Representative Alston.
Referred to:
1 A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
2 AN ACT TO APPROPRIATE FUNDS FOR SIDEKICKS ACADEMY, INC., FOR
3 OPERATING EXPENSES.
4 The General Assembly of North Carolina enacts:
5 SECTION 1. There is appropriated from the General Fund to the Office of State
6 Budget and Management the sum of one hundred thousand dollars ($100,000) in nonrecurring
7 funds for the 2025-2026 fiscal year to provide a directed grant to Sidekicks Academy, Inc., to
8 provide its program that helps empower marginalized students in the Durham Public Schools
9 through the combination of the martial arts, character development, and community support by
10 creating a safe and inclusive environment where every student can thrive and realize their full
11 potential. These funds shall be used as follows:
12 (1) Ninety thousand dollars ($90,000) for students' training attire, safety
13 equipment, an intra-school Taekwondo tournament, digital advertising
14 upkeep, and staff salary.
15 (2) Ten thousand dollars ($10,000) for experiential education, which includes
16 field trips to local Taekwondo tournaments, local museum visits, and
17 educational and sporting events on local college campuses.
18 SECTION 2. This act becomes effective July 1, 2025.
