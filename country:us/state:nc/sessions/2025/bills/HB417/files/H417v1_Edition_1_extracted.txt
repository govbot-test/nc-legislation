GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF NORTH CAROLINA
SESSION 2025
H 1
HOUSE BILL 417
Short Title: Funds for Hoke County Park. (Public)
Sponsors: Representative G. Pierce.
For a complete list of sponsors, refer to the North Carolina General Assembly web site.
Referred to: Appropriations, if favorable, Rules, Calendar, and Operations of the House
March 18, 2025
1 A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
2 AN ACT TO APPROPRIATE FUNDS TO HOKE COUNTY FOR UPGRADES AT THE
3 ROCKFISH PARK IN THE TOWN OF RAEFORD.
4 The General Assembly of North Carolina enacts:
5 SECTION 1. There is appropriated from the General Fund to the Office of State
6 Budget and Management the sum of fifty thousand dollars ($50,000) in nonrecurring funds for
7 the 2025-2026 fiscal year to provide a directed grant to Hoke County for upgrades at Rockfish
8 Park in the Town of Raeford.
9 SECTION 2. This act becomes effective July 1, 2025.
