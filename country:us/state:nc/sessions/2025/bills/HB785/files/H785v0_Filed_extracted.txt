GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF NORTH CAROLINA
Apr 3, 2025
SESSION 2025
HOUSE PRINCIPAL CLERK
H D
HOUSE BILL DRH10369-NGa-105
Short Title: School Funding Flexibility Study. (Public)
Sponsors: Representative Lofton.
Referred to:
1 A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
2 AN ACT TO STUDY BUDGET FLEXIBILITY FOR LOCAL BOARDS OF EDUCATION.
3 The General Assembly of North Carolina enacts:
4 SECTION 1. The Department of Public Instruction shall study methods of
5 implementing budget flexibility guidelines for local school administrative units. Flexibilities
6 include methods of combining allotments, allowing transfers between allotments, or other
7 methods the Department deems viable. The Department shall submit a report to the Joint
8 Legislative Education Oversight Committee and the Fiscal Research Division on or before April
9 1, 2026. The report shall identify areas where a greater amount of flexibility would enable local
10 boards of education to meet the needs of students, teachers, administrators, and other school
11 employees, along with any legislative recommendations.
12 SECTION 2. There is appropriated from the General Fund to the Department of
13 Public Instruction the sum of fifty thousand dollars ($50,000) in nonrecurring funds for the
14 2025-2026 fiscal year to be used to conduct the study required by this act.
15 SECTION 3. This act becomes effective July 1, 2025.
