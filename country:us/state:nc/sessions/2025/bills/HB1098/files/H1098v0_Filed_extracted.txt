GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF NORTH CAROLINA

SESSION 2025
HOUSE PRINCIPAL CLERK

HOUSE BILL DRH10600-MWa-90
Short Title: Rural Commuter Microtransit Pilot Grant. (Public)
Sponsors: Representative Reives.
Referred to:
1 A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
2 AN ACT TO CREATE A RURAL COMMUTER MICROTRANSIT PILOT GRANT
3 PROGRAM.
4 The General Assembly of North Carolina enacts:
5 SECTION 1. The Department of Transportation, Division of Integrated Mobility,
6 shall establish the Rural Commuter Microtransit Pilot Grant Program for the purpose of providing
7 public transportation in rural counties.
8 SECTION 2. The following definitions apply in this act:
9 (1) Rural county. – A county with 250 people or fewer per square mile.
10 (2) Rural microtransit. – A public car or shuttle service operating in a rural county.
11 (3) Service operation. – The loading and unloading of passengers in a rural
12 county.
13 SECTION 3. The following entities shall be eligible to receive a program grant:
14 (1) Counties.
15 (2) Regional councils of government.
16 (3) Municipalities with agreements with rural county governments or regional
17 councils of government.
18 (4) Regional transit authorities with agreements with rural county governments
19 or regional councils of government.
20 SECTION 4. The following are the eligible uses for the program grant:
21 (1) Rural microtransit system that addresses workforce mobility and commuter
22 needs in rural areas during peak hours and must include a minimum twenty
23 percent (20%) employer contribution.
24 (2) General use microtransit during off-peak hours including on-demand shuttle
25 use for the elderly, families with children, and individuals with disabilities
26 that prioritizes medical appointments and grocery shopping.
27 SECTION 5. A program grant must include a minimum fifty percent (50%) local
28 match which can take the form of reimbursements to local citizens.
29 SECTION 6. The Division shall establish procedures and adopt rules for allocating
30 grant funds that evaluate need, scalability, feasibility, and community partnerships.
31 SECTION 7. There is appropriated from the Highway Fund to the Department of
32 Transportation, Division of Integrated Mobility, for the 2026-2027 fiscal year the sum of thirty
33 million dollars ($30,000,000) in nonrecurring funds for the purposes of this act.
34 SECTION 8. This act becomes effective July 1, 2026.
