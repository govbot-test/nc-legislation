GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF NORTH CAROLINA
SESSION 2025
S Simple
Resolution
Adopted
SENATE RESOLUTION 1085
Adopted 6/4/26
Sponsors: Senator Rabon (Primary Sponsor).
Referred to: Select Committee on Nominations
June 2, 2026
2 GOVERNORS OF THE UNIVERSITY OF NORTH CAROLINA.
3 Whereas, O. Temple Sloan, III, of Wake County was elected by the Senate in 2023 to
4 serve as a member of the Board of Governors of The University of North Carolina for a term of
5 four years; and
6 Whereas, O. Temple Sloan, III, resigned from the Board of Governors of The
7 University of North Carolina in April 2025; and
8 Whereas, G.S. 116-7(c) directs the chamber that originally elected a vacating member
9 of the Board of Governors of The University of North Carolina to elect a person to fill that
10 vacancy for the remainder of the unexpired term; and
11 Whereas, the Senate may determine its own procedure, and the Chair of the Senate
12 Select Committee on Nominations has confirmed that Leigh Jeffreys Fanning has no
13 disqualifying statutory disabilities; Now, therefore,
14 Be it resolved by the Senate:
15 SECTION 1. Based upon the resignation of O. Temple Sloan, III, Leigh Jeffreys
16 Fanning of Pitt County is elected to the Board of Governors of The University of North Carolina
17 for a term commencing upon the effective date of this resolution and ending June 30, 2027.
18 SECTION 2. This resolution is effective upon adoption.
