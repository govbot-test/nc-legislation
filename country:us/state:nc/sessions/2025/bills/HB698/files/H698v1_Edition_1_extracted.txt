1 A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
2 AN ACT TO TRANSFER THE NORTH CAROLINA CENTER FOR MISSING PERSONS TO
3 THE STATE HIGHWAY PATROL.
4 The General Assembly of North Carolina enacts:
5 SECTION 1. All functions, powers, duties, and obligations vested in the North
6 Carolina Center for Missing Persons in the Department of Public Safety are transferred to, vested
7 in, and consolidated within the State Highway Patrol by a Type I transfer, as defined in
8 G.S. 143A-6.
9 SECTION 2. Article 17 of Chapter 143B of the General Statutes, as enacted by S.L.
10 2024-57, is amended by adding a new Part 4 to be entitled "North Carolina Center for Missing
11 Persons."
12 SECTION 3. Subpart B of Part 5 of Article 13 of Chapter 143B of the General
13 Statutes is recodified as Part 4 of Article 17 of Chapter 143B of the General Statutes, as enacted
14 by S.L. 2024-57, as follows:
