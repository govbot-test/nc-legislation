Committee Substitute Favorable 3/18/25
Short Title: City of Southport/Deannexations. (Local)
Sponsors:
Referred to:
March 10, 2025
1 A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
2 AN ACT REMOVING CERTAIN DESCRIBED PROPERTY FROM THE CORPORATE
3 LIMITS OF THE CITY OF SOUTHPORT.
4 The General Assembly of North Carolina enacts:
5 SECTION 1. The following described property, identified by Brunswick County
6 Tax Office Parcel Number, is removed from the corporate limits of the City of Southport:
7 221MB015, 221MB020, and 221MB02001.
8 SECTION 2. This act has no effect upon the validity of any liens of the City of
9 Southport for ad valorem taxes or special assessments outstanding before the effective date of
10 this act. Such liens may be collected or foreclosed upon after the effective date of this act as
11 though the property were still within the corporate limits of the City of Southport.
12 SECTION 3. This act becomes effective June 30, 2025. Property in the territory
13 described in Section 1 of this act as of January 1, 2025, is no longer subject to municipal taxes
14 for taxes imposed for taxable years beginning on or after July 1, 2025.
