GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF NORTH CAROLINA
SESSION 2025
S 1
SENATE BILL 360
Short Title: Healthy Students - A Nurse in Every School. (Public)
Sponsors: Senators Adcock, Hise, and Hanig (Primary Sponsors).
Referred to: Rules and Operations of the Senate
March 24, 2025
1 A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
2 AN ACT TO REQUIRE AT LEAST ONE SCHOOL NURSE IN EVERY SCHOOL IN A
3 PUBLIC SCHOOL UNIT BEGINNING IN THE 2025-2026 SCHOOL YEAR AND TO
4 APPROPRIATE ADDITIONAL FUNDS TO MEET THAT REQUIREMENT.
5 Whereas, the health, well-being, and educational success of public school students in
6 North Carolina are top priorities of the General Assembly; and
7 Whereas, employing school nurses has been shown to have a critical and positive
8 impact on the health, well-being, and educational success of public school students in
9 kindergarten through grade 12 due to services directed toward keeping students healthy, in class,
10 and ready to learn; Now, therefore,
11 The General Assembly of North Carolina enacts:
12 SECTION 1.(a) G.S. 115C-47 is amended by adding a new subdivision to read:
13 "(70) To Provide at Least One School Nurse in Every School. – Local boards of
14 education shall ensure that each school within the local school administrative
15 unit is staffed by at least one full-time, permanent school nurse."
16 SECTION 1.(b) G.S. 115C-150.12C is amended by adding a new subdivision to
17 read:
18 "(37) To provide at least one school nurse in every school. – The board of trustees
19 shall ensure that each school is staffed by at least one full-time, permanent
20 school nurse."
21 SECTION 1.(c) G.S. 115C-218.75 is amended by adding a new subsection to read:
22 "(p) At Least One School Nurse. – A charter school shall ensure that the school is staffed
23 by at least one full-time, permanent school nurse."
24 SECTION 1.(d) G.S. 115C-238.66 is amended by adding a new subdivision to read:
25 "(23) At least one school nurse required. – A regional school shall ensure that the
26 school is staffed by at least one full-time, permanent school nurse."
27 SECTION 1.(e) G.S. 116-239.8(b) is amended by adding a new subdivision to read:
28 "(26) At least one school nurse required. – A laboratory school shall ensure that the
29 school is staffed by at least one full-time, permanent school nurse."
30 SECTION 2. There is appropriated from the General Fund to the Department of
31 Public Instruction for the 2025-2026 fiscal year the sum of ninety-five million dollars
32 ($95,000,000) in recurring funds to increase the School Health Personnel Allotment. These funds
33 shall be used to increase positions for school nurses in order to provide at least one full-time,
34 permanent school nurse in every school in a public school unit.
35 SECTION 3. Funds provided pursuant to this act shall supplement and not supplant
36 any funds from any source already provided for school nurses.
