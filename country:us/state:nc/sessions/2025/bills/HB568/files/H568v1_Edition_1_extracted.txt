5 SECTION 1.1. Article 16 of Chapter 95 of the General Statutes is amended by
6 adding a new section to read:
7 "§ 95-136.2. Commissioner and employees not subject to subpoena for testimony except in
8 certain circumstances.
9 (a) Neither the Commissioner nor any employee or former employee of the Department
10 is subject to a subpoena for appearance for purposes of inquiry into any occupational safety and
11 health inspection, except in one of the following circumstances:
12 (1) An enforcement proceeding is brought under this Article.
13 (2) An action is filed in which the Department is a party.
14 (3) The Commissioner consents in writing to waive the exemption provided by
15 this section.
16 (4) A court finds all of the following:
17 a. The information sought is essential to the underlying case.
18 b. There are no reasonable alternative means for acquiring the
19 information.
20 c. A significant injustice would occur if the requested testimony was not
21 available.
22 (b) The party that issued the subpoena shall pay to the Department a witness fee in the
23 amount of five hundred dollars ($500.00) per day.
24 (c) This section does not apply to a subpoena requesting only documents or other
25 records."
26 SECTION 1.2. G.S. 150B-21.5 is amended by adding a new subsection to read:
27 "(c1) OSHA Standard. – The Occupational Safety and Health Division of the Department

29 hearing when it proposes to adopt a rule that concerns an occupational safety and health standard
30 that is identical to a federal regulation promulgated by the Secretary of the United States
31 Department of Labor. The Division shall file the rule with the Commission for the purpose of
32 receiving written objections to the rule in accordance with G.S. 150B-21.3(b2)."
