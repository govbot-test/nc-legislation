Section 1:
FILED SENATE
Feb 10, 2025
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF NORTH CAROLINA
S.B. 70
SESSION 2025
PRINCIPAL CLERK
S D
Short Title: Funds for Town of Wendell/Carver School. (Public)
Sponsors: Senator Blue (Primary Sponsor).
Referred to:
1 A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
2 AN ACT TO APPROPRIATE FUNDS TO THE TOWN OF WENDELL TO STABILIZE THE
3 OLD CARVER SCHOOL.
4 The General Assembly of North Carolina enacts:
5 SECTION 1. There is appropriated from the General Fund to the Office of State
6 Budget and Management the sum of two million dollars ($2,000,000) in nonrecurring funds for
7 the 2025-2026 fiscal year to provide a directed grant to the Town of Wendell to provide
8 community engagement, development of construction drawings, and stabilization of the old
9 Carver School owned by Pleasant Grove Baptist Church in partnership with Pleasant Grove CDC
10 and Wake County. The project includes the rehabilitation of the site into a comprehensive
11 community center, including a business incubator, technology center, gymnasium, performance
12 space, and classrooms.
13 SECTION 2. This act becomes effective July 1, 2025.
