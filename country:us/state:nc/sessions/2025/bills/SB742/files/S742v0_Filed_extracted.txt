Section 1:
FILED SENATE
Mar 25, 2025
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF NORTH CAROLINA
S.B. 742
SESSION 2025
PRINCIPAL CLERK
S D
Short Title: Funds for CW Williams Comm. Health Center. (Public)
Sponsors: Senator Mohammed (Primary Sponsor).
Referred to:
1 A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
2 AN ACT TO APPROPRIATE FUNDS FOR THE C.W. WILLIAMS COMMUNITY HEALTH
3 CENTER.
4 The General Assembly of North Carolina enacts:
5 SECTION 1. There is appropriated from the General Fund to the Office of State
6 Budget and Management the sum of five hundred thousand dollars ($500,000) in non-recurring
7 funds for the 2025-2026 fiscal year to provide a directed grant to The C.W. Williams Community
8 Health Center, Inc., a nonprofit organization, to assist in the completion of its full-service
9 community health center located in Mecklenburg County.
10 SECTION 2. This act becomes effective July 1, 2025.
*DRS15307-LGa-73*
