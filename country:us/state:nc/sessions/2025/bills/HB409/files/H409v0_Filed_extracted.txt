Section 1:
H.B. 409
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF NORTH CAROLINA
Mar 17, 2025
SESSION 2025
HOUSE PRINCIPAL CLERK
H D
Short Title: Funds for Caswell County Vol. Fire Department. (Public)
Sponsors: Representative Price.
Referred to:
1 A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
2 AN ACT TO APPROPRIATE FUNDS TO CASWELL COUNTY FOR THE REPLACEMENT
3 OF THE CASVILLE VOLUNTEER FIRE DEPARTMENT'S FACILITY.
4 The General Assembly of North Carolina enacts:
5 SECTION 1. There is appropriated from the General Fund to the Office of State
6 Budget and Management the sum of one million seven hundred thousand dollars ($1,700,000) in
7 nonrecurring funds for the 2025-2026 fiscal year to provide a directed grant to Caswell County
8 for the replacement of the Casville Volunteer Fire Department's facility.
9 SECTION 2. This act becomes effective July 1, 2025.
*DRH40250-LGa-153*
