4 The General Assembly of North Carolina enacts:
5 SECTION 1.(a) G.S. 55-16-22(a) reads as rewritten:
6 "§ 55-16-22. Annual report.
7 (a) Requirement. – Except as provided in G.S. 55-16-22.3 and in subsections (a1) and
8 (a2) of this section, each domestic corporation and each foreign corporation authorized to transact
9 business in this State shall deliver an annual report directly to the Secretary of State in electronic
10 form or in paper form as prescribed by the Secretary of State under this section."
11 SECTION 1.(b) Article 16 of Chapter 55 of the General Statutes is amended by
12 adding a new section to read:
13 "§ 55-16-22.3. Exemptions for corporations owned by deployed members of the Armed
14 Forces.
15 (a) Definitions. As used in this section, the following terms have the following meanings:
16 (1) Armed Forces. – The United States Air Force, Army, Coast Guard, Marine
17 Corps, Navy, or Space Force, or any reserve component of the foregoing.
18 (2) Deployed member. – A member of the Armed Forces who is removed from
19 his or her county of residence pursuant to an official order for a deployment
20 period that ends on or after the ninetieth day preceding the due date of the
21 annual report required by G.S. 55-16-22.
22 (b) Notwithstanding G.S. 55-16-22, an annual report is deemed timely filed if it is filed
23 by a domestic or foreign corporation (i) in which more than fifty percent (50%) of the ownership
24 interest is owned by one or more deployed members and (ii) within 90 days of the end of the
25 deployment period. The following provisions apply:
26 (1) Prior to the start of the deployment, the corporation shall file electronically
27 with the Secretary of State a sworn affidavit of deployment executed by the
28 deployed member that includes the following information:
29 a. The full name of the deployed member.
30 b. The name of the corporation and the state under whose law it is
31 incorporated.
32 c. The percentage ownership interest in the corporation currently held by
33 the deployed member.
34 d. The expected start and end dates of the deployment.

11 SECTION 2.(b) Article 2 of Chapter 57D of the General Statutes is amended by
12 adding a new section to read:
13 "§ 57D-2-26. Exemptions for LLCs owned by deployed members of the Armed Forces.
14 (a) Definitions. As used in this section, the following terms have the following meanings:
15 (1) Armed Forces. – The United States Air Force, Army, Coast Guard, Marine

18 his or her county of residence pursuant to an official order for a deployment
19 period that ends on or after the ninetieth day preceding the due date of the
20 annual report required by G.S. 57D-2-24.

22 by an LLC or foreign LLC (i) in which more than fifty percent (50%) of the ownership interest
23 is owned by one or more deployed members and (ii) by April 15 of the year immediately
24 following the end of the deployment period. The following provisions apply:
25 (1) Prior to the start of the deployment, the LLC or foreign LLC shall file
26 electronically with the Secretary of State a sworn affidavit of deployment
27 executed by the deployed member that includes the following information:
28 a. The full name of the deployed member.

30 different name under which the foreign LLC is authorized to transact
31 business in this State, and the name of the jurisdiction under whose
32 law the foreign LLC is organized.
33 c. The percentage ownership interest in the LLC or foreign LLC
34 currently held by the deployed member.
35 d. The expected start and end dates of the deployment.
36 e. A statement either certifying that the information contained in the most
37 recently filed annual report has not changed or setting forth the
38 updated information required by G.S. 57D-2-24.
39 (2) In the event the deployment is extended beyond the date stated in the affidavit
40 of deployment, the LLC or foreign LLC shall file electronically with the
41 Secretary of State, within 180 days of the end date stated in the affidavit of
42 deployment filed with the Secretary of State pursuant to subdivision (1) of this
43 subsection, a sworn affidavit of extended deployment by an authorized
44 representative of the corporation that includes the following information:
45 a. The title or position in the LLC or foreign LLC held by the affiant.
46 b. The full name of the deployed member.
47 c. The name of the LLC or foreign LLC and, for a foreign LLC, any
48 different name under which the foreign LLC is authorized to transact
49 business in this State, and the name of the jurisdiction under whose
50 law the foreign LLC is organized.
