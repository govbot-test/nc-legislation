Section 1:
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF NORTH CAROLINA
SESSION 2025
H 2
Committee Substitute Favorable 3/25/25
Short Title: Waive Annual Report/Fee For Deployed Military. (Public)
Sponsors:
Referred to:
March 3, 2025
1 A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
2 AN ACT TO WAIVE THE ANNUAL REPORT REQUIREMENT AND FEE FOR BUSINESS
3 ENTITIES OWNED BY DEPLOYED MEMBERS OF THE ARMED FORCES.
4 The General Assembly of North Carolina enacts:
5 SECTION 1.(a) G.S. 55-16-22(a) reads as rewritten:
6 "§ 55-16-22. Annual report.
7 (a) Requirement. – Except as provided in G.S. 55-16-22.3 and in subsections (a1) and
8 (a2) of this section, each domestic corporation and each foreign corporation authorized to transact
9 business in this State shall deliver an annual report directly to the Secretary of State in electronic
10 form or in paper form as prescribed by the Secretary of State under this section."
11 SECTION 1.(b) Article 16 of Chapter 55 of the General Statutes is amended by
12 adding a new section to read:
13 "§ 55-16-22.3. Exemptions for corporations owned by deployed members of the Armed
14 Forces.
15 (a) For the purposes of this section, a deployed member of the Armed Forces is a member
16 of the United States Air Force, Army, Coast Guard, Marine Corps, or Navy, or a member of any
17 reserve component, who is removed from his or her county of residence pursuant to an official
18 order for a deployment period that ends on or after the ninetieth day preceding the due date of
19 the annual report required by G.S. 55-16-22.
20 (b) Notwithstanding G.S. 55-16-22, an annual report is deemed timely filed if it is filed
21 by a domestic or foreign corporation (i) in which more than fifty percent (50%) of the ownership
22 interest is owned by one or more deployed members of the Armed Forces and (ii) within 90 days
23 of the end of the deployment period. The following provisions apply:
24 (1) Prior to the start of the deployment, the corporation shall file notice of
25 deployment with the Secretary of State in electronic or paper form as
26 prescribed by the Secretary of State. The notice shall include the following:
27 a. The name of the corporation and the state under whose law it is
28 incorporated.
29 b. A copy of the orders for deployment or written verification from the
30 commanding officer.
31 c. A statement either certifying that the information contained in the most
32 recently filed annual report has not changed or setting forth the
33 updated information required by G.S. 55-16-22(a3)(2) through (5).
34 (2) The due date of the corporation's next annual report is the ninetieth business
35 day following the end of the deployment. If the deployment is extended, the
36 corporation's annual report is due the ninetieth business day following the end
*H250-v-2*
General Assembly Of North Carolina Session 2025
1 of the extension period. At the time of filing, the corporation may submit a
2 copy of orders or written verification from the commanding officer of the
3 extension period, and the report is retroactively deemed timely filed.
4 (3) The grounds for dissolution under G.S. 55-14-20 apply to corporations that
5 are subject to this section only if the period of delinquency for the applicable
6 ground is 180 days or more past the end of the deployment period indicated
7 in the documentation provided to the Secretary of State under this section.
8 (c) Any fees required by G.S. 55-1-22 for documents filed under subdivision (b)(1) of
9 this section are waived. Additionally, the fee required by G.S. 55-1-22 for the annual report due
10 on the ninetieth business day following the end of deployment under subdivision (b)(2) of this
11 section is waived."
12 SECTION 2.(a) G.S. 57D-2-24 reads as rewritten:
13 "§ 57D-2-24. Annual report for Secretary of State.
14 (a) Excluding professional limited liability companies governed by G.S. 57D-2-02,
15 G.S. 57D-2-02 and except as provided in G.S. 57D-2-26, each LLC and each foreign LLC
16 authorized to transact business in this State must deliver to the Secretary of State for filing annual
17 reports on a form prescribed by, and in the manner required by, the Secretary of State and as
18 otherwise provided in subsection (b) of this section. Each annual report must specify the year for
19 which the report applies and provide the information required by this subsection. The information
20 must be current as of the date the limited liability company completes the report. If the
21 information in the limited liability company's most recent annual report has not changed, the
22 limited liability company may certify in its annual report that the information has not changed in
23 lieu of restating the information.
24 The following information must be included in each annual report:
25 (1) The name of the limited liability company and, in the case of a foreign LLC,
26 any different name that the foreign LLC is authorized under Article 3 of
27 Chapter 55D of the General Statutes to use to transact business in this State,
28 as provided in the foreign LLC's certificate of authority.
29 (2) In the case of a foreign LLC, the name of the jurisdiction under whose law the
30 foreign LLC is organized.
31 (3) The street address, and the mailing address if different from the street address,
32 of the limited liability company's registered office in the State, the county in
33 which the registered office is located, the name of its registered agent at that
34 office, and a statement of any change of the registered office or registered
35 agent.
36 (4) The address and telephone number of its principal office.
37 (5) The names, titles, and business addresses of the limited liability company's
38 principal company officials.
39 (6) A brief description of the nature of its business.
40 …."
41 SECTION 2.(b) Article 2 of Chapter 57D of the General Statutes is amended by
42 adding a new section to read:
43 "§ 57D-2-26. Exemptions for LLCs owned by deployed members of the Armed Forces.
44 (a) For the purposes of this section, a deployed member of the Armed Forces is a member
45 of the United States Air Force, Army, Coast Guard, Marine Corps, or Navy, or a member of any
46 reserve component, who is removed from his or her county of residence pursuant to an official
47 order for a deployment period that ends on or after the ninetieth day preceding the due date of
48 the annual report required by G.S. 57D-2-24.
49 (b) Notwithstanding G.S. 57D-2-24, an annual report is deemed timely filed if it is filed
50 by an LLC or foreign LLC (i) in which more than fifty percent (50%) of the ownership interest

1 is owned by one or more deployed members of the Armed Forces and (ii) by April 15 of the year
2 immediately following the end of the deployment period. The following provisions apply:
3 (1) Prior to the start of the deployment, the LLC or foreign LLC shall file notice
4 of deployment with the Secretary of State in electronic or paper form as
5 prescribed by the Secretary of State. The notice shall include the following:
6 a. The name of the LLC or foreign LLC and, for a foreign LLC, any
7 different name under which the foreign LLC is authorized to transact
8 business in this State, and the name of the jurisdiction under whose
9 law the foreign LLC is organized.
10 b. A copy of the orders for deployment or written verification from the
11 commanding officer.
12 c. A statement either certifying that the information contained in the most
13 recently filed annual report has not changed or setting forth the
14 updated information required by G.S. 57D-2-24.
15 (2) The due date of the LLC's or foreign LLC's next annual report is April 15 of
16 the year immediately following the end of the deployment.
17 (3) If the extension of a deployment results in the annual report being deemed
18 delinquent, the LLC or foreign LLC may submit a copy of orders or written
19 verification from the commanding officer of the original deployment and
20 extension period at the time the annual report is submitted. The report is
21 retroactively deemed timely filed, and any adverse action taken by the
22 Secretary of State shall be reversed.
23 (4) The grounds for dissolution under G.S. 57D-6-06 apply to LLCs and foreign
24 LLCs that are subject to this section only if the period of delinquency for the
25 applicable ground is 180 days or more past the end of the deployment period
26 indicated in the documentation provided to the Secretary of State under this
27 section.
28 (c) Any fees required by G.S. 57D-1-22 for documents filed under subdivision (b)(1) of
29 this section are waived. Additionally, the fee required by G.S. 57D-1-22 for the annual report
30 due April 15 of the year immediately following the end of deployment under subdivision (b)(2)
31 of this section is waived."
32 SECTION 3.(a) G.S. 59-84.4 reads as rewritten:
33 "§ 59-84.4. Annual report for Secretary of State.
34 (a) Each Except as provided in G.S. 59-84.6, each registered limited liability partnership
35 and each foreign limited liability partnership authorized to transact business in this State shall
36 deliver to the Secretary of State for filing an annual report, in a form prescribed by the Secretary
37 of State, that sets forth all of the following:
38 (1) The name of the registered limited liability partnership or foreign limited
39 liability partnership and the state or country under whose law it is formed.
40 (2) The street address, and the mailing address if different from the street address,
41 of the registered office, the county in which the registered office is located,
42 and the name of its registered agent at that office in this State, and a statement
43 of any change of the registered office or registered agent, or both.
44 (3) The street address and telephone number of its principal office.
45 (4) A brief description of the nature of its business.
46 (5) The fiscal year end of the partnership.
47 If the information contained in the most recently filed annual report has not changed, a
48 certification to that effect may be made instead of setting forth the information required by
49 subdivisions (2) through (4) of this subsection. The Secretary of State shall make available the
50 form required to file an annual report.
51 …."

41 SECTION 4. The Secretary of State shall make available the form or forms needed
42 for the notice of deployment required by this act and shall take any other action necessary to
43 allow business entities to begin filing pursuant to this act on October 1, 2025.
44 SECTION 5. Sections 1 through 3 of this act become effective October 1, 2025. The
45 remainder of this act is effective when it becomes law.
