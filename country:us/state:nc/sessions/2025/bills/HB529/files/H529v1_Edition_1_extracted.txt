GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF NORTH CAROLINA
SESSION 2025
H 1
Short Title: Clean Repeal of HB2. (Public)
Sponsors: Representative Dahle.
For a complete list of sponsors, refer to the North Carolina General Assembly web site.
Referred to: Rules, Calendar, and Operations of the House
March 27, 2025
1 A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
2 AN ACT TO MAKE A CLEAN REPEAL OF HOUSE BILL 2.
3 The General Assembly of North Carolina enacts:
4 SECTION 1. Article 81A of Chapter 143 of the General Statutes is repealed.
5 SECTION 2. G.S. 95-25.1(d) is repealed.
6 SECTION 3. This act is effective when it becomes law.
