SENATE BILL DRS45269-NE-77
Short Title: Allow Consecutive Remote License Renewals. (Public)
Sponsors: Senators Grafstein, Murdock, and Everitt (Primary Sponsors).
Referred to:
1 A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
2 AN ACT TO ALLOW CONSECUTIVE REMOTE LICENSE RENEWALS FOR NON-REAL
3 ID COMPLIANT DRIVERS LICENSES.
4 The General Assembly of North Carolina enacts:
5 SECTION 1. G.S. 20-7(f) reads as rewritten:
6 "(f) Duration and Renewal of Licenses. – Drivers licenses shall be issued and renewed
7 pursuant to the provisions of this subsection:
8 …
9 (6) Remote renewal or conversion. – Subject to the following requirements and
10 limitations, the Division may offer remote renewal of a drivers license or
11 remote conversion of a full provisional license issued by the Division:
12 a. Requirements. – To be eligible for remote renewal or conversion under
13 this subdivision, a person must meet all of the following requirements:
14 …
15 4. For a remote renewal, renewal:
16 I. Of a license that complies with regulations adopted
17 pursuant to the REAL ID Act of 2005, 49 U.S.C. §
18 30301, the most recent renewal was an in-person
19 renewal and not a remote renewal under this
20 subdivision.
21 II. Of a drivers license that is not REAL ID compliant, a
22 person may remotely renew a license on two
23 consecutive occasions. After two consecutive remote
24 renewals, a non-REAL ID compliant license shall be
25 renewed in person.
26 …."
27 SECTION 2. This act becomes effective January 1, 2026, and applies to licenses
28 renewed on or after that date.
