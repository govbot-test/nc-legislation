Section 1:
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF NORTH CAROLINA
SESSION 2025
S 1
Short Title: Funds for Westmoreland Road Interchange. (Public)
Sponsors: Senator Mohammed (Primary Sponsor).
Referred to: Rules and Operations of the Senate
March 26, 2025
1 A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
2 AN ACT TO APPROPRIATE FUNDS FOR THE WESTMORELAND ROAD
3 INTERCHANGE.
4 The General Assembly of North Carolina enacts:
5 SECTION 1. Of the funds appropriated from the Highway Fund to the Department
6 of Transportation for the 2025-2026 fiscal year, the sum of nine million five hundred thousand
7 dollars ($9,500,000) in nonrecurring funds shall be used for the planning and design of the
8 Westmoreland Road Interchange (Interstate 77 Exit 27) in the Town of Cornelius.
9 SECTION 2. This act becomes effective July 1, 2025.
*S726-v-1*
