GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF NORTH CAROLINA
SESSION 2025
S 1
SENATE BILL 340
Short Title: Funds for Pittsboro Fire Services. (Public)
Sponsors: Senator Murdock (Primary Sponsor).
Referred to: Rules and Operations of the Senate
March 20, 2025
1 A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
2 AN ACT TO APPROPRIATE FUNDS FOR EMERGENCY FIRE SERVICES IN THE TOWN
3 OF PITTSBORO.
4 The General Assembly of North Carolina enacts:
5 SECTION 1. There is appropriated from the General Fund to the Office of State
6 Budget and Management the sum of ten million dollars ($10,000,000) in nonrecurring funds for
7 the 2025-2026 fiscal year to provide a directed grant to the Town of Pittsboro to enhance its
8 emergency fire services. These funds shall be used as follows:
9 (1) Seven million dollars ($7,000,000) for the construction of a new fire station.
10 (2) One million eight hundred thousand dollars ($1,800,000) for a new ladder
11 truck.
12 (3) One million two hundred thousand dollars ($1,200,0000) for a new fire
13 engine.
14 SECTION 2. This act becomes effective July 1, 2025.
