Section 1:
FILED SENATE
Feb 25, 2025
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF NORTH CAROLINA
S.B. 157
SESSION 2025
PRINCIPAL CLERK
S D
Short Title: Funds For Johnson C. Smith Tech Project. (Public)
Sponsors: Senator Waddell (Primary Sponsor).
Referred to:
1 A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
2 AN ACT TO APPROPRIATE FUNDS TO JOHNSON C. SMITH UNIVERSITY TO
3 SUPPORT ITS INCLUSIVE TECH-INNOVATION PILOT PROJECT.
4 The General Assembly of North Carolina enacts:
5 SECTION 1. There is appropriated from the General Fund to the Office of State
6 Budget and Management the sum of one hundred thousand dollars ($100,000) in nonrecurring
7 funds for the 2025-2026 fiscal year to provide a directed grant to Johnson C. Smith University to
8 support the University's Inclusive Tech-Innovation Pilot Project to create a vibrant and connected
9 community that leverages broadband to support economic growth and prosperity within the
10 anchor community.
11 SECTION 2. This act becomes effective July 1, 2025.
*DRS15063-LGa-61*
