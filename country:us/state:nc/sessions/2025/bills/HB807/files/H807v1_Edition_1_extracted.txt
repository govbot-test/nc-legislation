8 PART IV. PROVIDE FOR A ONE-TO-ONE DEVICE REFRESH
9 SECTION 4.(a) Part 3A of Article 8 of Chapter 115C is amended by adding a new
10 section to read:
11 "§ 115C-102.10. Device refresh and replacement program.
12 (a) The State Board of Education shall adopt by rule standards for a one-to-one electronic
13 device refresh and replacement program. The purpose of this program shall be to provide schools
14 funds to update or replace electronic devices used directly by students and teachers for
15 instruction. At a minimum, the standards shall include:
16 (1) A statewide schedule for routine replacement of electronic devices, based on
17 the average operating lifecycle of the devices most commonly used.
18 (2) A description of the lack or impairment of functionality a device must have in
19 order to be eligible for replacement, including how functionality will be
20 verified.
21 (3) Instructions on how to dispose of devices no longer in use, including whether
22 those devices should be sold or recycled.
23 (b) Local school administrative units shall administer the one-to-one electronic device
24 refresh and replacement program in accordance with the standards set by the State Board."

27 ($152,620,000) in recurring funds for the 2025-2026 fiscal year for the Department to establish
28 the one-to-one device refresh program, as enacted by this act.
