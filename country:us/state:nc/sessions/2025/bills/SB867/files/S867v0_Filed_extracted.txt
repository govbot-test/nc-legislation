SENATE BILL DRS45493-NLa-137
Short Title: Increased Admin Fund for Medicaid. (Public)
Sponsors: Senator Lowe (Primary Sponsor).
Referred to:
1 A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
2 AN ACT TO PROVIDE FUNDING FOR INCREASED ADMINISTRATIVE COSTS IN THE
3 MEDICAID PROGRAM DUE TO FEDERAL CHANGES.
4 The General Assembly of North Carolina enacts:
5 SECTION 1. There is appropriated from the General Fund to the Department of
6 Health and Human Services (DHHS) the sum of six million five hundred thousand dollars
7 ($6,500,000) in nonrecurring funds for the 2025-2026 fiscal year. These funds shall be deposited
8 in the Health Advancement Receipts Special Fund under G.S. 143C-9-10 and shall be used for
9 administrative costs as allowed under G.S. 108A-147.13(a)(2). DHHS shall use these funds to
10 provide funding to county departments of social services to support the counties with
11 administrative costs. Notwithstanding the limitations on funding sources contained in
12 G.S. 108A-54.3B, the use of these funds shall not require the Secretary of the Department of
13 Health and Human Services to complete the requirements of G.S. 108A-54.3B(d).
14 SECTION 2. This act is retroactively effective July 1, 2025.
