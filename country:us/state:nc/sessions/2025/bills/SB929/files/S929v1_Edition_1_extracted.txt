GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF NORTH CAROLINA
SESSION 2025
S 1
SENATE BILL 929
Short Title: Forsyth County Affordable Housing Funding. (Public)
Sponsors: Senator Lowe (Primary Sponsor).
Referred to: Rules and Operations of the Senate
April 30, 2026
1 A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
2 AN ACT TO APPROPRIATE FUNDS FOR AFFORDABLE HOUSING IN FORSYTH
3 COUNTY.
4 The General Assembly of North Carolina enacts:
5 SECTION 1. There is appropriated from the General Fund to the Office of State
6 Budget and Management the sum of one million dollars ($1,000,000) in nonrecurring funds for
7 the 2026-2027 fiscal year to provide a directed grant to Forsyth County to be allocated to Habitat
8 for Humanity of Forsyth County, Inc., for use in constructing affordable housing in Forsyth
9 County and the City of Winston-Salem.
10 SECTION 2. This act becomes effective July 1, 2026.
