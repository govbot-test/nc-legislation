10 SECTION 13.(a) The Revisor of Statutes shall cause to be printed, as annotations to
11 Chapter 55 of the published General Statutes, all relevant portions of the Official Comments to
12 the Model Business Corporation Act and all explanatory comments of the drafters of this act as
13 the Revisor may deem appropriate.
14 SECTION 13.(b) Parts I, II, III, IV, V, and VII of this act become effective October
15 1, 2025. Part VIII of this act becomes effective January 1, 2026, and applies to attested written
16 wills stored as electronic records on or after that date, regardless of the date of execution of the
17 attested written will. Except as otherwise provided, the remainder of this act is effective when it
18 becomes law.
