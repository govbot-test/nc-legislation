Section 1:
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF NORTH CAROLINA
SESSION 2025
H 1
Short Title: Counties/Franchise Ambulance Service. (Public)
Sponsors: Representative Pless.
For a complete list of sponsors, refer to the North Carolina General Assembly web site.
Referred to: Emergency Management and Disaster Recovery, if favorable, State and Local
Government, if favorable, Rules, Calendar, and Operations of the House
February 27, 2025
1 A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
2 AN ACT REPEALING THE LAW THAT AUTHORIZES COUNTIES TO FRANCHISE
3 AMBULANCE SERVICES AND PROVIDING THAT COUNTY OFFICIALS SHALL
4 NOT BE REQUIRED TO APPROVE MODIFICATIONS IN THE LEVEL OF CARE
5 PROVIDED BY EMS PROVIDERS THAT ARE NOT OWNED OR OPERATED BY A
6 CITY IN THE COUNTY OR THE COUNTY RESPONSIBLE FOR THE EMERGENCY
7 MANAGEMENT SYSTEM.
8 The General Assembly of North Carolina enacts:
9 SECTION 1. G.S. 153A-250 is repealed.
10 SECTION 2. Notwithstanding any provision of or any rule or regulation adopted
11 pursuant to the authority granted in G.S. 143-508(b), Article 7 of Chapter 131E of the General
12 Statutes, or any other provision of law, a county manager or EMS administrator delegated the
13 authority to act on behalf of the county shall not be required to sign a document or otherwise
14 approve of a modification in the level of care being provided by a provider within the county's
15 emergency management system. For purposes of this section, a "modification" means either an
16 increase or decrease in level of care. The provisions of this section do not apply if the provider
17 is a city located within the county or the county responsible for the emergency management
18 system.
19 SECTION 3. This act is effective when it becomes law. Section 2 of this act applies
20 to any modification in the level of care occurring on or after the date this act becomes law.
*H219-v-1*
