Section 1:
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF NORTH CAROLINA
SESSION 2025
S 1
SENATE RESOLUTION 150
Sponsors: Senator Rabon (Primary Sponsor).
Referred to: Rules and Operations of the Senate
February 25, 2025
2 CONFIRMATION OF JOEY R. HOPKINS AS SECRETARY OF THE DEPARTMENT
3 OF TRANSPORTATION.
4 Whereas, pursuant to G.S. 143B-9(a), the Governor shall notify the President of the
5 Senate of the name of each person to be nominated as head of a principal State department; and
6 Whereas, each nomination is subject to senatorial advice and consent in conformance
7 with Section 5(8) of Article III of the North Carolina Constitution; and
8 Whereas, the Governor has appointed Joey R. Hopkins to serve as Secretary of the
9 Department of Transportation pending further action by the Senate; Now, therefore,
10 Be it resolved by the Senate:
11 SECTION 1. That the Senate shall hereby consider whether to confirm Joey R.
12 Hopkins as Secretary of the Department of Transportation.
13 SECTION 2. This resolution is effective upon adoption.
