2 AN ACT TO SCHEDULE RECITATION OF THE PLEDGE OF ALLEGIANCE AT THE

7 PART I. REQUIRE PLEDGE OF ALLEGIANCE TO BE SCHEDULED AT THE
8 BEGINNING OF THE SCHOOL DAY
9 SECTION 1.(a) G.S. 115C-47(29a) reads as rewritten:
10 "(29a) To Require the Display of the United States and North Carolina Flags, and to
11 Require the Recitation of the Pledge of Allegiance. – Local boards of
12 education shall adopt policies to (i) require the display of the United States
13 and North Carolina flags in each classroom, when available, (ii) require that
14 recitation of the Pledge of Allegiance be scheduled on a daily basis, basis
15 within one hour of the beginning of the instructional day, and (iii) provide
16 age-appropriate instruction on the meaning and historical origins of the flag
17 and the Pledge of Allegiance. These policies shall not compel any person to
18 stand, salute the flag, or recite the Pledge of Allegiance. If flags are donated
19 or are otherwise available, flags shall be displayed in each classroom."
