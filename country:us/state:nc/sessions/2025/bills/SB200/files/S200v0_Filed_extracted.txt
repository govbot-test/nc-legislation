Section 1:
FILED SENATE
Feb 27, 2025
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF NORTH CAROLINA
S.B. 200
SESSION 2025
PRINCIPAL CLERK
S D
Short Title: Housing Support for Hurricane Impacted Areas. (Public)
Sponsors: Senators Daniel, Moffitt, and Mayfield (Primary Sponsors).
Referred to:
1 A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
2 AN ACT TO APPROPRIATE ADDITIONAL FUNDS TO THE NORTH CAROLINA
3 HOUSING TRUST FUND FOR HURRICANE HELENE RELIEF.
4 The General Assembly of North Carolina enacts:
5 SECTION 1. There is appropriated from the General Fund to the North Carolina
6 Housing Trust Fund the sum of one hundred fifty million dollars ($150,000,000) in nonrecurring
7 funds for the 2025-2026 fiscal year to be administered in accordance with the provisions of
8 Chapter 122E of the General Statutes in those North Carolina counties designated before, on, or
9 after the effective date of this act under a major disaster declaration by the President of the United
10 States under the Stafford Act (P.L. 93-288) as a result of Hurricane Helene.
11 SECTION 2. This act becomes effective July 1, 2025.
*DRS35094-NOa-23B*
