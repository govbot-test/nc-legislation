GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF NORTH CAROLINA
SESSION 2025
S 1
Short Title: An Act for Better Education. (Public)
Sponsors: Senator Lowe (Primary Sponsor).
Referred to: Rules and Operations of the Senate
March 26, 2025
1 A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
2 AN ACT TO REENACT THE SALES AND USE TAX HOLIDAY FOR SCHOOL SUPPLIES.
3 The General Assembly of North Carolina enacts:
4 SECTION 1. G.S. 105-164.13C is reenacted as it existed immediately before its
5 repeal.
6 SECTION 2. This act becomes effective July 1, 2025, and applies to sales made on
7 or after that date.
