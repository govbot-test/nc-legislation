GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF NORTH CAROLINA
SESSION 2025
H 1
HOUSE BILL 526
Short Title: Funds for Onslow Co. Sheriff's Office. (Public)
Sponsors: Representative Gable.
For a complete list of sponsors, refer to the North Carolina General Assembly web site.
Referred to: Appropriations, if favorable, Rules, Calendar, and Operations of the House
March 27, 2025
1 A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
2 AN ACT TO APPROPRIATE FUNDS TO ESTABLISH A DRUG TESTING LAB AT THE
3 ONSLOW COUNTY SHERIFF'S OFFICE.
4 The General Assembly of North Carolina enacts:
5 SECTION 1. There is appropriated from the General Fund to the Office of State
6 Budget and Management the sum of two hundred nine thousand dollars ($209,000) in
7 nonrecurring funds for the 2025-2026 fiscal year to provide a directed grant to Onslow County
8 to establish a drug testing laboratory at the Sheriff's Office to ensure timely and accurate drug
9 testing results for law enforcement purposes.
10 SECTION 2. This act becomes effective July 1, 2025.
