Section 1:
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF NORTH CAROLINA
SESSION 2025
H 1
Short Title: Eliminate PET Scanners from CON Review. (Public)
Sponsors: Representative Campbell.
For a complete list of sponsors, refer to the North Carolina General Assembly web site.
Referred to: Health, if favorable, Regulatory Reform, if favorable, Rules, Calendar, and
Operations of the House
April 3, 2025
1 A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
2 AN ACT ELIMINATING THE PURCHASE OF POSITRON EMISSION TOMOGRAPHY
3 SCANNERS FROM CERTIFICATE OF NEED REVIEW.
4 The General Assembly of North Carolina enacts:
5 SECTION 1. G.S. 131E-176(16)f1.8. is repealed.
6 SECTION 2. This act is effective when it becomes law.
