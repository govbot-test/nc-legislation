6 "§ 105-275. Property classified and excluded from the tax base.

8 of the North Carolina Constitution and are excluded from tax:
9 …
10 (5b) Motor vehicles owned by a person who has a one hundred percent (100%)
11 disability rating certified by the United States Department of Veterans Affairs.
12 …."

14 vehicles registered on or after that date.
