GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF NORTH CAROLINA
SESSION 2025
S 1
SENATE BILL 922
Short Title: Fund International Black Theatre Festival. (Public)
Sponsors: Senator Lowe (Primary Sponsor).
Referred to: Rules and Operations of the Senate
April 30, 2026
1 A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
2 AN ACT TO APPROPRIATE FUNDS FOR THE INTERNATIONAL BLACK THEATRE
3 FESTIVAL.
4 The General Assembly of North Carolina enacts:
5 SECTION 1. There is appropriated from the General Fund to the Office of State
6 Budget and Management the sum of two hundred thousand dollars ($200,000) in nonrecurring
7 funds for the 2026-2027 fiscal year to provide a directed grant to the City of Winston-Salem for
8 planning and costs to support operations and cultural programming associated with the
9 International Black Theatre Festival scheduled to be held in the City in July and August of 2026.
10 SECTION 2. This act becomes effective July 1, 2026.
