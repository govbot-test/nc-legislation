1 A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
2 AN ACT TO REQUIRE AND FUND THE DISPLAY OF THE UNITED STATES AND
3 NORTH CAROLINA FLAGS ON PUBLIC SCHOOL GROUNDS AND IN EVERY
4 PUBLIC SCHOOL CLASSROOM.
5 The General Assembly of North Carolina enacts:
6 SECTION 1.(a) G.S. 115C-47(29a) reads as rewritten:
7 "(29a) To Require the Display of the United States and North Carolina Flags, and to
8 Require the Recitation of the Pledge of Allegiance. – Local boards of
9 education shall adopt policies to (i) require the display of the United States
10 and North Carolina flags on school grounds near the main entrance to each
11 school and in each classroom, when available, (ii) require that recitation of the
12 Pledge of Allegiance be scheduled on a daily basis, and (iii) provide
13 age-appropriate instruction on the meaning and historical origins of the flag
14 and the Pledge of Allegiance. These policies shall not compel any person to
15 stand, salute the flag, or recite the Pledge of Allegiance. If flags are donated
16 or are otherwise available, flags shall be displayed in each classroom."
