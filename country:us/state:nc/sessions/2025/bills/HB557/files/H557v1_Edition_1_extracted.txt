GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF NORTH CAROLINA
SESSION 2025
H 1
HOUSE BILL 557
Short Title: The Andy Griffith Show as State TV Show. (Public)
Sponsors: Representatives N. Jackson, Biggs, Stevens, and K. Hall (Primary Sponsors).
For a complete list of sponsors, refer to the North Carolina General Assembly web site.
Referred to: State and Local Government, if favorable, Rules, Calendar, and Operations of the
House
March 31, 2025
1 A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
2 AN ACT ADOPTING "THE ANDY GRIFFITH SHOW" AS THE OFFICIAL TELEVISION
3 SHOW OF THE STATE OF NORTH CAROLINA.
4 Whereas, Andy Samuel Griffith was born on June 1, 1926, in the City of Mount Airy
5 in Surry County, North Carolina; and
6 Whereas, Mr. Griffith graduated from Mount Airy High School in 1944 and attended
7 the University of North Carolina at Chapel Hill where he earned a bachelor's degree in music;
8 and
9 Whereas, Mr. Griffith was a cast member in "The Lost Colony" where he played
10 various roles, including Sir Walter Raleigh; and
11 Whereas, Mr. Griffith had a seven-decade career in music and television as a singer,
12 actor, comedian, producer, and writer; and
13 Whereas, Mr. Griffith loved North Carolina from the mountains to the coast and spent
14 his retirement on the Outer Banks in Dare County where his final resting place is Roanoke Island;
15 and
16 Whereas, Mr. Griffith celebrated his native State of North Carolina with iconic
17 comedy tracks, including "North Carolina, My Home State" and "What It Was, Was Football";
18 and
19 Whereas, in 1960, Mr. Griffith took his celebration of North Carolina to Hollywood
20 where he starred in "The Andy Griffith Show" for a run of 249 episodes from 1960 to 1968; and
21 Whereas, performing as Andy Taylor, Mr. Griffith highlighted people, places, and
22 attitudes from North Carolina that brought an awareness and fondness for the Old North State on
23 a television show that dominated the national television ratings and ended its final season as the
24 number one show in America; and
25 Whereas, "The Andy Griffith Show" inspired spinoff shows and an annual festival in
26 Mount Airy, "Mayberry Days"; and
27 Whereas, the positive character values and awareness of North Carolina make "The
28 Andy Griffith Show" must-see-TV for anyone who loves North Carolina; and
29 Whereas, all North Carolinians should know the location of "Old Man Kelsey's
30 Woods," how to avoid a "loaded goat," and the marvelous existence of Mr. McBeevee; and
31 Whereas, school children and parents should know how to handle a "spoiled kid" and
32 to delicately navigate a situation involving "Aunt Bea's Pickles"; and
33 Whereas, society should continue to be aware of the talents of "The Darlings," the
34 mercurial nature of Ernest T. Bass, and the danger of Otis with a "leg of lamb"; and

General Assembly Of North Carolina Session 2025
1 Whereas, people of all ages can appreciate a good "citizen's arrest," dealing with a
2 well-intentioned friend like Barney Fife, a tough teacher like Helen Crump, and a nosy neighbor
3 like Clara Edwards; and
4 Whereas, most importantly, everyone should know better than to accept a peanut
5 butter and jelly sandwich from Leon; and
6 Whereas, it is fitting to recognize the positive impact of "The Andy Griffith Show"
7 on our State and nation by adopting the show as the official television show of the State of North
8 Carolina; Now, therefore,
9 The General Assembly of North Carolina enacts:
10 SECTION 1. Chapter 145 of the General Statutes is amended by adding a new
11 section to read:
12 "§ 145-52. State television show.
13 "The Andy Griffith Show" is adopted as the official television show of the State of North
14 Carolina."
15 SECTION 2. This act is effective when it becomes law.
