Section 1:
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF NORTH CAROLINA
SESSION 2025
H 1
Short Title: Support for the Senior Tar Heel Legislature. (Public)
Sponsors: Representatives G. Pierce, Reives, and White (Primary Sponsors).
For a complete list of sponsors, refer to the North Carolina General Assembly web site.
Referred to: Appropriations, if favorable, Rules, Calendar, and Operations of the House
March 5, 2025
1 A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
2 AN ACT APPROPRIATING FUNDS TO THE DEPARTMENT OF HEALTH AND HUMAN
3 SERVICES, DIVISION OF AGING AND ADULT SERVICES, TO SUPPORT THE
4 NORTH CAROLINA SENIOR TAR HEEL LEGISLATURE.
5 The General Assembly of North Carolina enacts:
6 SECTION 1. There is appropriated from the General Fund to the Department of
7 Health and Human Services, Division of Aging and Adult Services, the sum of thirty thousand
8 dollars ($30,000) in nonrecurring funds for the 2025-2026 fiscal year to be used to cover costs
9 associated with the operations of the North Carolina Senior Tar Heel Legislature created by
10 G.S. 143B-181.55.
11 SECTION 2. This act becomes effective July 1, 2025.
*H280-v-1*
