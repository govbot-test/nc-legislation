Section 1:
FILED SENATE
Feb 11, 2025
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF NORTH CAROLINA
S.B. 78
SESSION 2025
PRINCIPAL CLERK
S D
Short Title: Official Fried Apple Pie Festival. (Public)
Sponsors: Senator Hise (Primary Sponsor).
Referred to:
1 A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
2 AN ACT DESIGNATING SPARTA'S FRIED APPLE PIE FESTIVAL AS THE OFFICIAL
3 FRIED APPLE PIE FESTIVAL OF THE STATE OF NORTH CAROLINA.
4 Whereas, the Town of Sparta is home to the annual Fried Apple Pie Festival, which
5 is held at the Alleghany County Fairgrounds; and
6 Whereas, during the Festival, visitors are able to sample a variety of foods from food
7 trucks, listen to music performers, buy crafts from vendors, and enjoy a wide range of fun
8 activities suitable for the entire family; and
9 Whereas, the Festival holds a fried apple pie contest with awards given to first,
10 second, and third place winners as well as an award for the best fried apple pie chosen by those
11 in attendance; and
12 Whereas, the Fried Apple Pie Festival is a unique event held in the State and should
13 be adopted as the official Fried Apple Pie Festival of the State of North Carolina; Now, therefore,
14 The General Assembly of North Carolina enacts:
15 SECTION 1. Chapter 145 of the General Statutes is amended by adding a new
16 section to read:
17 "§ 145-52. Official Fried Apple Pie Festival.
18 The Fried Apple Pie Festival, held the first Saturday in the month of May of every year in the
19 Town of Sparta, is adopted as the official Fried Apple Pie Festival of the State of North Carolina."
20 SECTION 2. This act is effective when it becomes law.
*DRS35040-LG-15*
