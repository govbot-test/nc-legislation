Section 1:
FILED SENATE
Apr 29, 2026
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF NORTH CAROLINA
S.B. 919
SESSION 2025
PRINCIPAL CLERK
S D
Short Title: Funds for Smart Start of Forsyth County. (Public)
Sponsors: Senator Lowe (Primary Sponsor).
Referred to:
1 A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
2 AN ACT TO APPROPRIATE FUNDS TO SMART START OF FORSYTH COUNTY.
3 The General Assembly of North Carolina enacts:
4 SECTION 1. There is appropriated from the General Fund to the Department of
5 Health and Human Services, Division of Child Development and Early Education, the sum of
6 two hundred fifty thousand dollars ($250,000) in nonrecurring funds for the 2026-2027 fiscal
7 year to provide a directed grant to Smart Start of Forsyth County to expand early childhood
8 education and family support services.
9 SECTION 2. This act becomes effective July 1, 2026.
*DRS45498-MCa-301*
