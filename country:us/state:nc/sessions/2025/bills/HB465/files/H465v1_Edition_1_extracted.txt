GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF NORTH CAROLINA
SESSION 2025
H 1
HOUSE BILL 465
Short Title: Waive Renewal Fee/Military Drivers Licenses. (Public)
Sponsors: Representatives Winslow, Schietzelt, Paré, and Lowery (Primary Sponsors).
For a complete list of sponsors, refer to the North Carolina General Assembly web site.
Referred to: Finance, if favorable, Rules, Calendar, and Operations of the House
March 20, 2025
1 A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
2 AN ACT WAIVING THE FEE TO RENEW A DRIVERS LICENSE WITH AN ACTIVE
3 DUTY MILITARY DESIGNATION OR A VETERAN MILITARY DESIGNATION.
4 The General Assembly of North Carolina enacts:
5 SECTION 1. G.S. 20-7 reads as rewritten:
6 "§ 20-7. Issuance and renewal of drivers licenses.
7 …
8 (i) Fees. – The fee for a regular drivers license is the amount set in the following table
9 multiplied by the number of years in the period for which the license is issued:
10 Class of Regular License Fee for Each Year
11 Class A $5.50
12 Class B $5.50
13 Class C $5.50
14 The fee for a motorcycle endorsement is two dollars and fifty-five cents ($2.55) for each year
15 of the period for which the endorsement is issued. The appropriate fee shall be paid before a
16 person receives a regular drivers license or an endorsement.
17 The fee to renew a drivers license shall be waived for any license with: (i) an active duty
18 military designation pursuant to subsection (q) of this section or (ii) a veteran military designation
19 pursuant to subsection (q1) of this section.
20 …."
21 SECTION 2. This act becomes effective July 1, 2025, and applies to licenses
22 renewed on or after that date.
