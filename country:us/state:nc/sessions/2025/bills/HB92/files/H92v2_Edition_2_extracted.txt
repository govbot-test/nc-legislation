GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF NORTH CAROLINA
SESSION 2025
H 2
Committee Substitute Favorable 3/5/25
Short Title: NC Digital Assets Investments Act. (Public)
Sponsors:
Referred to:
February 12, 2025
1 A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
2 AN ACT TO AUTHORIZE THE STATE TREASURER TO INVEST IN QUALIFYING
3 DIGITAL ASSETS.
4 The General Assembly of North Carolina enacts:
5 SECTION 1. Article 6 of Chapter 147 of the General Statutes is amended by adding
6 a new section to read:
7 "§ 147-69.2E. Investments in digital assets.
8 (a) The following definitions apply in this section:
9 (1) Digital asset. – A virtual currency, cryptocurrency, native electronic asset,
10 stablecoin, nonfungible token, or any other asset that is only digital and that
11 confers economic, proprietary, or access rights or powers.
12 (2) Exchange-traded product. – A security or a security covered under federal law,
13 as defined in G.S. 78A-2, that meets any of the following requirements:
14 a. The security is listed, or authorized for listing, on the New York Stock
15 Exchange, the American Stock Exchange, or the National Association
16 of Securities Dealers Automated Quotations national market system
17 (NASDAQ), or any successor to these entities.
18 b. The security is listed, or authorized for listing, on a national securities
19 exchange or system, or on a tier or segment of the exchange or system,
20 designated by the United States Securities and Exchange Commission
21 in 17 C.F.R. § 230.146(b).
22 c. The security is a security of the same issuer that is equal in seniority
23 to, or that is senior to, a security described in sub-subdivisions a. and
24 b. of this subdivision.
25 (b) The State Treasurer may invest the cash of the funds described in G.S. 147-69.1(b)
26 and G.S. 147-69.2(a) in digital assets that are in an exchange-traded product.
27 (c) An investment in digital assets from any of these funds shall not exceed, in the
28 aggregate, ten percent (10%) of the balance of the fund at the time of the investment."
29 SECTION 2. G.S. 147-69.1 reads as rewritten:
30 "§ 147-69.1. Investments authorized for General Fund and Highway Funds assets.
31 (a) The Governor and Council of State, with the advice and assistance of the State
32 Treasurer, shall adopt such rules and regulations as shall be necessary and appropriate to
33 implement the provisions of this section.
34 (b) This section applies to funds held by the State Treasurer to the credit of:of each of the
35 following:
36 (1) The General Fund;Fund.

30 Treasurer to subsequently dispose of the investment or fail to honor any contractual commitments
31 as a result of changes in market values, ratings, or other investment qualifications. For purposes
32 of computing market values on which percentage limitations on investments in this subsection
33 are based, all investments shall be valued as of the last date of the most recent fiscal quarter.

35 investment management arrangements according to the primary investment type or primary
36 strategy utilized under the arrangement authorized under subsection (e) of this section. No

38 of this section. The State Treasurer shall select from among the following investments subject to
39 the following limitations and requirements:
