Section 1:
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF NORTH CAROLINA
SESSION 2025
S 1
Short Title: Dealer License Renewal Modifications. (Public)
Sponsors: Senators Jackson, Lazzara, and Sawyer (Primary Sponsors).
Referred to: Rules and Operations of the Senate
March 25, 2025
1 A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
2 AN ACT EXTENDING THE DEALER LICENSE RENEWAL GRACE PERIOD AND
3 ALIGNING DEALER REGISTRATION PLATE RENEWALS WITH DEALER LICENSE
4 RENEWALS.
5 The General Assembly of North Carolina enacts:
6
7 PART I. EXTEND DEALER LICENSE GRACE PERIOD
8 SECTION 1.(a) G.S. 20-295(b) reads as rewritten:
9 "§ 20-295. Action on application; grace period while application for license renewal is
10 pending.
11 …
12 (b) Pending License Renewal Grace Period. – When an application for license renewal
13 has been timely submitted prior to expiration of the license, the license shall remain valid for up
14 to 30 60 days after the expiration date until the Division grants or denies the application. The
15 Division shall (i) ensure that any database maintained by the Division that indicates the status of
16 a license issued under this Article reflects that the license continues to be valid during this period
17 and (ii) send a temporary license to the renewal applicant for display to evidence extension of
18 the validity of the license to engage in business in this State while the Division reviews the
19 renewal application. The temporary license issued by the Division pursuant to this subsection
20 shall contain on its face the following notation: "This temporary license is issued pursuant to
21 G.S. 20-295 during a license renewal application review by the North Carolina Division of Motor
22 Vehicles and is valid to engage in business in this State with all rights and privileges of a license."
23 SECTION 1.(b) This section becomes effective October 1, 2025, and applies to
24 licenses issued or renewed on or after that date.
25
26 PART II. ALIGN DEALER REGISTRATION PLATE RENEWAL WITH DEALER
27 LICENSE RENEWALS
28 SECTION 2.(a) G.S. 20-79 reads as rewritten:
29 "§ 20-79. Dealer license plates.
30 …
31 (c) Form and Duration. – A dealer license plate is subject to G.S. 20-63, except for the
32 requirement that the plate display the registration number of a motor vehicle and the requirement
33 that the plate be a "First in Flight" plate, a "First in Freedom" plate, or a "National/State Mottos"
34 plate. A dealer license plate must have a distinguishing symbol identifying the plate as a dealer
35 license plate. The symbol may vary depending upon the classification of dealer license plate
*S401-v-1*
General Assembly Of North Carolina Session 2025
1 issued. The Division must provide suitably reduced sized license plates for motorcycle dealers
2 and manufacturers.
3 A dealer license plate is issued for a period of one year. two years. The Division shall vary
4 the expiration dates of dealer registration renewals so that an approximately equal number expires
5 at the end of each month, quarter, or other period consisting of one or more months. months to
6 coincide with G.S. 20-288(c). A dealer license plate may be transferred from one vehicle to
7 another. When the Division issues a dealer plate, it may issue a registration that expires at the
8 end of any monthly interval. When one of the following occurs, a dealer must surrender to the
9 Division all dealer license plates issued to the dealer:
10 …
11 (c1) Dealer Plate Mandatory Replacement. – Notwithstanding G.S. 20-63.1, registration
12 plates issued under this section shall be replaced every three four years.
13 …."
14 SECTION 2.(b) This section becomes effective October 1, 2025, and applies to
15 registration plates issued on or after that date.
16
17 PART III. EFFECTIVE DATE
18 SECTION 3. Except as otherwise provided, this act is effective when it becomes
19 law.
