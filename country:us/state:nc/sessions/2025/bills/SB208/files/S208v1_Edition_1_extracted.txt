GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF NORTH CAROLINA
SESSION 2025
S 1
Short Title: SchCalFlex/Durham/Aug 10 & Assmnts. (Local)
Sponsors: Senators Chitlik and Murdock (Primary Sponsors).
Referred to: Rules and Operations of the Senate
March 3, 2025
1 A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
2 AN ACT TO PROVIDE FLEXIBILITY TO DURHAM COUNTY SCHOOLS IN ADOPTING
3 THE SCHOOL CALENDAR.
4 The General Assembly of North Carolina enacts:
5 SECTION 1. G.S. 115C-84.2(d) reads as rewritten:
6 "(d) Opening and Closing Dates. – Local boards of education shall determine the dates of
7 opening and closing the public schools under subdivision (a)(1) of this section. Except for
8 year-round schools, the opening date for students shall be no earlier than the Monday closest to
9 August 26, August 10, and the closing date for students shall be no later than the Friday closest
10 to June 11. On a showing of good cause, the State Board of Education may waive the requirement
11 that the opening date for students be no earlier than the Monday closest to August 26 and may
12 allow the local board of education to set an opening date no earlier than the Monday closest to
13 August 19, to the extent that school calendars are able to provide sufficient days to accommodate
14 anticipated makeup days due to school closings. A local board may revise the scheduled closing
15 date if necessary in order to comply with the minimum requirements for instructional days or
16 instructional time. For purposes of this subsection, the term "good cause" means that schools in
17 any local school administrative unit in a county have been closed eight days per year during any
18 four of the last 10 years because of severe weather conditions, energy shortages, power failures,
19 or other emergency situations.
20 The required opening and closing dates under this subsection shall not apply to any school
21 that a local board designated as having a modified calendar for the 2003-2004 school year or to
22 any school that was part of a planned program in the 2003-2004 school year for a system of
23 modified calendar schools, so long as the school operates under a modified calendar."
24 SECTION 2. G.S. 115C-174.12(a)(4) reads as rewritten:
25 "(4) All annual assessments of student achievement adopted by the State Board of
26 Education pursuant to G.S. 115C-174.11(c)(1) and (3) and all final exams for
27 courses shall be administered within the final 10 instructional days of the
28 school year for year-long courses and within the final five instructional days
29 of the semester for semester courses. Exceptions shall be permitted to
30 accommodate a student's individualized education program and section 504
31 (29 U.S.C. § 794) plans and for the administration of final exams for courses
32 with national or international curricula required to be held at designated times.
33 A local board of education that has implemented a school calendar that
34 concludes the fall semester prior to December 31 may administer assessments
35 prior to the conclusion of that semester."
36 SECTION 3. This act applies only to Durham County Schools.
*S208-v-1*
General Assembly Of North Carolina Session 2025
1 SECTION 4. This act is effective when it becomes law and applies beginning with
2 the 2025-2026 school year.
