1 A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
2 AN ACT TO APPROPRIATE FUNDS TO WAYNE COUNTY FOR VARIOUS PUBLIC
3 PROJECTS.
4 The General Assembly of North Carolina enacts:
5 SECTION 1. There is appropriated from the General Fund to the Office of State
6 Budget and Management the sum of fifteen million seven hundred sixty thousand dollars
7 ($15,760,000) in nonrecurring funds for the 2025-2026 fiscal year to provide a directed grant to
8 Wayne County to be used as follows:
9 (1) Five million dollars ($5,000,000) for a capital project at Rosewood Middle
10 School.
11 (2) Two million dollars ($2,000,000) to update the athletic track at Dillard Middle
12 School.
13 (3) One million dollars ($1,000,000) for water and sewer upgrades for the Village
14 of Walnut Creek.
15 (4) Seven hundred fifty thousand dollars ($750,000) for "Saving Union Station"
16 revitalization project.
17 (5) Five hundred thousand dollars ($500,000) for a training facility for the Wayne
18 County Sheriff's Office.
19 (6) One million dollars ($1,000,000) for Wayne County United Way.
20 (7) Two hundred fifty thousand dollars ($250,000) for Wayne County Alcohol
21 Anonymous.
22 (8) Two hundred fifty thousand dollars ($250,000) for the Tuscarora Council
23 Scouting Program.
24 (11) Five million dollars ($5,000,000) for the Mattamuskeet Lodge Restoration
25 Initiative.
26 (12) Ten thousand dollars ($10,000) for Pricetown Volunteer Fire Department.
27 SECTION 2. There is appropriated from the General Fund to the Administrative
28 Office of the Courts the sum of one hundred sixty-two thousand three hundred fifty-one dollars
29 ($162,351) in recurring funds for each year of the 2025-2027 fiscal biennium and three thousand
30 seven hundred fifty-two dollars ($3,752) in nonrecurring funds in the 2025-2026 fiscal year to
31 be used to hire one assistant district attorney for Prosecutorial District 9.
32 SECTION 3. There is appropriated from the General Fund to the Administrative
33 Office of the Courts, Office of Indigent Defense Services, the sum of two million three hundred
34 forty-seven thousand seventy-one dollars ($2,347,071) in recurring funds for each year of the
35 2025-2027 fiscal biennium and ninety-six thousand seven hundred thirty dollars ($96,730) in
