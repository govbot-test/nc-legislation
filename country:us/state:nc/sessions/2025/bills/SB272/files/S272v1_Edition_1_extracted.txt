GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF NORTH CAROLINA
SESSION 2025
S 1
SENATE BILL 272
Short Title: Insurance Producers/Exchange of Business. (Public)
Sponsors: Senators Corbin and Johnson (Primary Sponsors).
Referred to: Rules and Operations of the Senate
March 13, 2025
1 A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
2 AN ACT CLARIFYING THE LAWS RELATING TO THE EXCHANGE OF BUSINESS
3 BETWEEN INSURANCE PRODUCERS.
4 The General Assembly of North Carolina enacts:
5 SECTION 1. G.S. 58-33-10 reads as rewritten:
6 "§ 58-33-10. Definitions.
7 As used in this Article, the following definitions apply:
8 …
9 (4a) "Exchange business," "exchange of business," or "proper exchange of
10 business" means the forwarding of insurance business from one producer duly
11 licensed for the line of insurance being forwarded to another producer duly
12 licensed for that line of insurance where both producers are appointed with an
13 insurer that can accommodate the risk under conditions favorable to the
14 insured.
15 (4a)(4b) "FINRA" means the Financial Industry Regulatory Authority or any
16 successor entity.
17 …."
18 SECTION 2. G.S. 58-33-82 reads as rewritten:
19 "§ 58-33-82. Commissions.
20 …
21 (e) Commissions, fees, or other valuable consideration for the sale, solicitation, or
22 negotiation of insurance may be assigned or directed to be paid in the following circumstances:
23 …
24 (4) To an agency principal who is an owner, shareholder, member, partner,
25 director, employee, or agent of that agency for business placed by a producer
26 on behalf of that agency who is duly licensed and appointed as required by
27 this section.
28 (5) In connection with the exchange of business where both producers are duly
29 licensed with appropriate company appointments and have complied with all
30 of the requirements of G.S. 58-33-82.1.
31 …."
32 SECTION 3. Article 33 of Chapter 58 of the General Statutes is amended by adding
33 a new section to read:
34 "§ 58-33-82.1. Exchange of business.
35 (a) Producers may exchange business, and split the commission involved with that
36 business, if the producer forwarding the business and the producer receiving the business both:

1 (1) Are licensed in all lines of insurance involved in the exchange.
2 (2) Sign the insurer's insurance application or are otherwise disclosed to the
3 insurer and the consumer.
4 (3) Have a good-faith belief that the exchange of business complies with the
5 requirements of this Article.
6 (b) This section does not limit:
7 (1) The exchange of business among specialty lines, nonstandard and professional
8 liability business placed through a surplus lines producer or written on an
9 excess rate or other individually rated risk basis.
10 (2) The exchange of business in connection with risk sharing plans."
11 SECTION 4. This act is effective when it becomes law and applies to contracts
12 entered into or renewed on or after that date.
