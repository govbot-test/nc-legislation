17 PART II. ESTABLISH WORKGROUP TO EXAMINE POTENTIAL FOR
18 DEVELOPMENT OF GROUP LIABILITY INSURANCE PLAN OPPORTUNITIES
19 FOR CERTAIN ENTITIES PROVIDING SERVICES FOR MINORS
20 SECTION 2.1. The Commissioner of the Department of Insurance shall establish a
21 workgroup to examine the potential for developing group liability insurance plan opportunities
22 for all child care providers and for all nongovernmental contractors that contract with the
23 Department of Health and Human Services and any county or local agency administering
24 programs of public assistance pursuant to Chapter 108A of the General Statutes for the provision
25 of services to minors which are licensed under Article 2 of Chapter 122C of the General Statutes
26 or Article 1A of Chapter 131D of the General Statutes. The workgroup shall consist of
27 representatives from all of the following:
28 (1) The Department of Insurance.
29 (2) The insurance industry.
30 (3) NC Licensed Child Care Association.
31 (4) The Department of Health and Human Services, Division of Child
32 Development and Early Education.
33 (5) The American Tort Reform Association.
34 (6) Benchmarks NC.
35 SECTION 2.2. The workgroup shall develop findings and recommendations related
36 to at least the following:
37 (1) Potential methods for creating group liability insurance plan opportunities for
38 all child care providers.
39 (2) Reforms that could reduce group liability insurance plan premiums.
40 (3) Tort reforms that could reduce the liability damages of child care providers.
41 SECTION 2.3. By January 1, 2026, the Department of Insurance shall report the
42 findings and recommendations of the workgroup to the Joint Legislative Oversight Committee
43 on Health and Human Services, the Joint Legislative Oversight Committee on General
44 Government, and the Fiscal Research Division.

46 PART III. EFFECTIVE DATE
