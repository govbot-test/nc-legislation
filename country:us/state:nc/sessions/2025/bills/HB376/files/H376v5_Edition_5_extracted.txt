23 COMMERCIAL WASTEWATER DESIGN FLOW RATE STUDY
24 SECTION 5.(a) The Environmental Management Commission shall study whether
25 the wastewater design flow rates established pursuant to 15A NCAC 02T .0114(c) and (d)
26 (Wastewater Design Flow Rates) as those rates apply to commercial and industrial water users
27 accurately measure the actual flow rates used by those water users. In conducting the study, the
28 Commission shall consider all of the following:
29 (1) Reduced water consumption associated with new plumbing fixtures, fittings,
30 and appliances, including any reductions attributable to standards adopted
31 under the North Carolina Building Code or federal efficiency standards since
32 the flow rates in the rule were last established.
33 (2) Actual metered water use and measured wastewater flows from commercial
34 and industrial water users in the State.
35 (3) Wastewater design flow rates used by other states for comparable commercial
36 and industrial water users.
37 SECTION 5.(b) The Environmental Management Commission shall report its
38 findings to the Environmental Review Commission no later than January 1, 2027.
