Section 1:
FILED SENATE
Mar 17, 2025
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF NORTH CAROLINA
S.B. 319
SESSION 2025
PRINCIPAL CLERK
S D
Short Title: LSAUs to Maintain AIG Services in Alt. Progs. (Public)
Sponsors: Senator Everitt (Primary Sponsor).
Referred to:
1 A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
2 AN ACT TO REQUIRE SCHOOLS TO CONTINUE TO PROVIDE ALL APPROPRIATE
3 EDUCATIONAL SERVICES TO ACADEMICALLY OR INTELLECTUALLY GIFTED
4 STUDENTS IF THE STUDENT IS PLACED INTO AN ALTERNATIVE LEARNING
5 PROGRAM OR SCHOOL.
6 The General Assembly of North Carolina enacts:
7 SECTION 1. G.S. 115C-150.7(b) is amended by adding a new subdivision to read:
8 "(7a) A procedure for continuing to provide services to a child who is placed into
9 an alternative learning program or alternative school pursuant to Article 8C of
10 this Chapter."
11 SECTION 2. This act is effective when it becomes law.
*DRS45180-NG-58*
