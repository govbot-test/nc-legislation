Section 1:
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF NORTH CAROLINA
SESSION 2025
S 1
Short Title: Repeal the NC Drug Tax. (Public)
Sponsors: Senators Mayfield and Meyer (Primary Sponsors).
Referred to: Rules and Operations of the Senate
March 20, 2025
1 A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
2 AN ACT TO REPEAL THE NORTH CAROLINA DRUG TAX.
3 The General Assembly of North Carolina enacts:
4 SECTION 1. Article 2D of Subchapter I of Chapter 105 of the General Statutes is
5 repealed.
6 SECTION 2. This act does not affect the rights or liabilities of the State, a taxpayer,
7 or another person arising under a statute amended or repealed by this act before the effective date
8 of its amendment or repeal; nor does it affect the right to any refund or credit of a tax that accrued
9 under the amended or repealed statute before the effective date of its amendment or repeal.
10 SECTION 3. This act is effective when it becomes law.
*S334-v-1*
