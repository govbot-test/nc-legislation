H.B. 128
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF NORTH CAROLINA
Feb 13, 2025
SESSION 2025
HOUSE PRINCIPAL CLERK
H D
HOUSE BILL DRH40095-MGa-54
Short Title: Establish Prostate Cancer Control Program. (Public)
Sponsors: Representative R. Pierce.
Referred to:
1 A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
2 AN ACT APPROPRIATING FUNDS TO THE DEPARTMENT OF HEALTH AND HUMAN
3 SERVICES, DIVISION OF PUBLIC HEALTH, TO ESTABLISH AND ADMINISTER A
4 PROSTATE CANCER CONTROL PROGRAM.
5 The General Assembly of North Carolina enacts:
6 SECTION 1. There is appropriated from the General Fund to the Department of
7 Health and Human Services, Division of Public Health, the sum of two million dollars
8 ($2,000,000) in recurring funds for the 2025-2026 fiscal year and the sum of two million dollars
9 ($2,000,000) in recurring funds for the 2026-2027 fiscal year to be used to establish and
10 administer a statewide Prostate Cancer Control Program that provides free or low-cost prostate
11 cancer screenings and follow-up to any man residing in North Carolina who meets all of the
12 following criteria:
13 (1) Is uninsured or underinsured.
14 (2) Is not a beneficiary of Medicare Part B or Medicaid.
15 (3) Is between 50 and 70 years of age and without a family history of prostate
16 cancer or is between 40 and 70 years of age with a family history of prostate
17 cancer. For the purpose of this subdivision, a man with a family history of
18 prostate cancer means any man with at least one first-degree relative who (i)
19 was diagnosed with prostate cancer, (ii) developed prostate cancer, (iii) died
20 as a result of prostate cancer, (iv) was diagnosed with a cancer known to be
21 associated with increased risk of prostate cancer, or (v) has a genetic alteration
22 known to be associated with increased risk of prostate cancer.
23 (4) Has a household income below two hundred fifty percent (250%) of the
24 federal poverty level.
25 SECTION 2. This act becomes effective July 1, 2025.
