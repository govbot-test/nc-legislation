Section 1:
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF NORTH CAROLINA
SESSION 2025
S 1
Short Title: Funds for Transitional Housing in Pitt County. (Public)
Sponsors: Senator Smith (Primary Sponsor).
Referred to: Rules and Operations of the Senate
March 6, 2025
1 A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
2 AN ACT TO APPROPRIATE FUNDS TO CAREE TO SUPPORT ITS TRANSITIONAL
3 HOUSING EFFORTS IN PITT COUNTY.
4 The General Assembly of North Carolina enacts:
5 SECTION 1. There is appropriated from the General Fund to the Office of State
6 Budget and Management the sum of fifty-five thousand dollars ($55,000) in nonrecurring funds
7 for the 2025-2026 fiscal year to provide a directed grant to Citizens Advocating for Racial Equity
8 and Equality (CAREE), a nonprofit organization, to support the organization's transitional
9 housing efforts in Pitt County.
10 SECTION 2. This act becomes effective July 1, 2025.
*S237-v-1*
