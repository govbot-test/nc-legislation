5 TOWN OF VASS DEANNEXATION
6 SECTION 2.(a) The following described property, identified by Moore County Tax
7 Property Identification Numbers, is removed from the corporate limits of the Town of Vass:
8 950300365918, 950300363850, 950300362794, and 950300361693.
9 SECTION 2.(b) This section has no effect upon the validity of any liens of the Town
10 of Vass for ad valorem taxes or special assessments outstanding before the effective date of this
11 section. Such liens may be collected or foreclosed upon after the effective date of this section as
12 though the property were still within the corporate limits of the Town of Vass.
13 SECTION 2.(c) This section becomes effective June 30, 2026. Property in the
14 territory described in this section as of January 1, 2026, is no longer subject to municipal taxes
15 for taxes imposed for taxable years beginning on or after July 1, 2026.
