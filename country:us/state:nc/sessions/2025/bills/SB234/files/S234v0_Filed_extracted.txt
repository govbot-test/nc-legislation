Section 1:
FILED SENATE
Mar 5, 2025
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF NORTH CAROLINA
S.B. 234
SESSION 2025
PRINCIPAL CLERK
S D
Short Title: Funds for Greenville Fire Truck. (Public)
Sponsors: Senator Smith (Primary Sponsor).
Referred to:
1 A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
2 AN ACT TO APPROPRIATE FUNDS TO THE GREENVILLE FIRE DEPARTMENT TO
3 ACQUIRE A NEW FIRE TRUCK.
4 The General Assembly of North Carolina enacts:
5 SECTION 1. There is appropriated from the General Fund to the Office of State
6 Budget and Management the sum of six hundred ninety thousand dollars ($690,000) in
7 nonrecurring funds for the 2025-2026 fiscal year to provide a directed grant to the City of
8 Greenville to acquire a new fire truck.
9 SECTION 2. This act becomes effective July 1, 2025.
*DRS45125-LGa-63*
