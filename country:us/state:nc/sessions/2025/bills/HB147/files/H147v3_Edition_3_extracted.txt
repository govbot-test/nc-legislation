1 A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
2 AN ACT TO REMOVE CERTAIN DESCRIBED PROPERTY FROM THE CORPORATE
3 LIMITS OF THE CITIES OF ELIZABETH CITY AND KING.
4 The General Assembly of North Carolina enacts:
5 SECTION 1.(a) The following described property is removed from the corporate
6 limits of the City of Elizabeth City:
7 All that certain lot or parcel of land situated in Mount Hermon Township, Pasquotank County,
8 North Carolina, and more particularly described as follows: Being Lot No. 1, containing 6.89
9 acres, as shown and delineated on that map or plat entitled in part, "Minor Subdivision for Storage
10 to Go, LLC", dated 04/16/2021, prepared by Timmons Group, and recorded in Map Book 73,
11 Page 23, of the Pasquotank County Registry.
12 SECTION 1.(b) This section has no effect upon the validity of any liens of the City
13 of Elizabeth City for ad valorem taxes or special assessments outstanding before the effective
14 date of this section. Such liens may be collected or foreclosed upon after the effective date of this
15 section as though the property were still within the corporate limits of the City of Elizabeth City.
16 SECTION 1.(c) This section becomes effective June 30, 2025. Property in the
17 territory described in Section 1(a) of this act as of January 1, 2025, is no longer subject to
18 municipal taxes for taxes imposed for taxable years beginning on or after July 1, 2025.
19 SECTION 2.(a) The following described property is removed from the corporate
20 limits of the City of King:
21 TRACTS I AND II
22 Parcels identified by Stokes County Tax Property Identification Numbers 6901279265 and
23 6901371165.
24 TRACT III
25 All of the portion of Century Oak Drive Right of Way lying South of a new line crossing
26 from the most Southeasterly corner of Stokes County Tax Property Identification Number
27 6901370748 to the most Southwesterly corner of Stokes County Tax Property Identification
28 Number 6901372761 as Century Oak Drive Right of Way appears in the Stokes County Tax
29 Property GIS System.
30 SECTION 2.(b) This section has no effect upon the validity of any liens of the City
31 of King for ad valorem taxes or special assessments outstanding before the effective date of this
32 section. Such liens may be collected or foreclosed upon after the effective date of this section as
33 though the property were still within the corporate limits of the City of King.
