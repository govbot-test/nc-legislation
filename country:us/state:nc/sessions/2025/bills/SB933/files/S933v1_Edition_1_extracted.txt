GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF NORTH CAROLINA
SESSION 2025
S 1
SENATE BILL 933
Short Title: Funds for Forsyth Tech Boost. (Public)
Sponsors: Senator Lowe (Primary Sponsor).
Referred to: Rules and Operations of the Senate
April 30, 2026
1 A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
2 AN ACT TO APPROPRIATE FUNDS TO THE COMMUNITY COLLEGES SYSTEM
3 OFFICE TO ALLOCATE TO FORSYTH TECHNICAL COMMUNITY COLLEGE TO
4 SUPPORT THE BOOST PROGRAM.
5 The General Assembly of North Carolina enacts:
6 SECTION 1. There is appropriated from the General Fund to the Community
7 Colleges System Office the sum of one million dollars ($1,000,000) in recurring funds beginning
8 with the 2026-2027 fiscal year to be allocated to Forsyth Technical Community College to
9 support the Boost college-to-career program.
10 SECTION 2. This act becomes effective July 1, 2026.
