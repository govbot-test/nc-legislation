H.B. 758
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF NORTH CAROLINA
Apr 2, 2025
SESSION 2025
HOUSE PRINCIPAL CLERK
H D
HOUSE BILL DRH40399-LGa-150
Short Title: Funds for Mind and Heart Haven Project. (Public)
Sponsors: Representative K. Brown.
Referred to:
1 A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
2 AN ACT TO APPROPRIATE FUNDS FOR THE MIND AND HEART HAVEN PROJECT.
3 The General Assembly of North Carolina enacts:
4 SECTION 1. There is appropriated from the General Fund to the Office of State
5 Budget and Management the sum of one hundred thousand dollars ($100,000) in nonrecurring
6 funds for the 2025-2026 fiscal year to provide a directed grant to Agency for Families in Distress,
7 Inc., a nonprofit organization, to help fund "The Mind and Heart Haven Project" which seeks to
8 address the growing mental health crisis in Winston-Salem, Forsyth County, and North Carolina
9 as a whole.
10 SECTION 2. This act becomes effective July 1, 2025.
