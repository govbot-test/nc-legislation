Section 1:
FILED SENATE
Mar 5, 2025
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF NORTH CAROLINA
S.B. 238
SESSION 2025
PRINCIPAL CLERK
S D
Short Title: Edgecombe and Pitt County School Nurses. (Public)
Sponsors: Senator Smith (Primary Sponsor).
Referred to:
1 A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
2 AN ACT TO FUND A SCHOOL NURSE FOR EVERY PUBLIC SCHOOL IN EDGECOMBE
3 AND PITT COUNTIES.
4 The General Assembly of North Carolina enacts:
5 SECTION 1. There is appropriated from the General Fund to the Department of
6 Public Instruction the sum of five hundred thirty thousand dollars ($530,000) in recurring funds
7 for the 2025-2026 fiscal year to be allocated to Edgecombe County Public Schools to be used to
8 provide at least one full-time, permanent school nurse in each public school in Edgecombe
9 County. Funds provided pursuant to this act shall supplement and not supplant any funds from
10 any source already provided for the same purposes.
11 SECTION 2. There is appropriated from the General Fund to the Department of
12 Public Instruction the sum of one million six hundred seventy-five thousand dollars ($1,675,000)
13 in recurring funds for the 2025-2026 fiscal year to be allocated to Pitt County Schools to be used
14 to provide at least one full-time, permanent school nurse in each public school in Pitt County.
15 Funds provided pursuant to this act shall supplement and not supplant any funds from any source
16 already provided for the same purposes.
17 SECTION 3. This act is effective July 1, 2025.
*DRS45121-NHa-81*
