Section 1:
FILED SENATE
Feb 5, 2025
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF NORTH CAROLINA
S.B. 56
SESSION 2025
PRINCIPAL CLERK
S D
Short Title: Disaster Recovery Act of 2025 - Part I. (Public)
Sponsors: Senators Hise, Daniel, and Moffitt (Primary Sponsors).
Referred to:
1 A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
2 AN ACT TO ENACT THE DISASTER RECOVERY ACT OF 2025 – PART I.
3 The General Assembly of North Carolina enacts:
4
5 PART I. TITLE AND SCOPE OF ACT
6 SECTION 1.1. Title. – This act shall be known as "The Disaster Recovery Act of
7 2025 – Part I."
8 SECTION 1.2. Maximum Amounts; Effectuate Savings. – The appropriations and
9 allocations made in this act are for maximum amounts necessary to implement this act. Savings
10 shall be effected where the total amounts appropriated or allocated are not required to implement
11 this act.
12 SECTION 1.3. Scope. – Unless otherwise provided, Section 2 of this act applies to
13 the North Carolina counties in the affected area, as defined in Section 1.4 of this act.
14 SECTION 1.4. Definitions. – Unless otherwise provided, the following definitions
15 apply in this act:
16 (1) Affected area. – The counties designated before, on, or after the effective date
17 of this act under a major disaster declaration by the President of the United
18 States under the Stafford Act (P.L. 93-288) as a result of Hurricane Helene.
19 (2) Helene Fund. – The Hurricane Helene Disaster Recovery Fund established in
20 Section 4.1 of S.L. 2024-51.
21 (3) SERDRF. – The State Emergency Response and Disaster Relief Fund
22 established in G.S. 166A-19.42.
23
24 PART II. SERDRF FUNDING
25 SECTION 2. Intent to Transfer Disaster Relief Funds. – It is the intent of the General
26 Assembly to transfer funds from the SERDRF to the Helene Fund for further disaster relief and
27 recovery assistance.
28
29 PART III. EFFECTIVE DATE
30 SECTION 3. This act is effective when it becomes law.
*DRS25000-NBa-44S*
