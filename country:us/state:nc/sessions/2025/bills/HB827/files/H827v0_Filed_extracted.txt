Short Title: Create Annual Sex Off Registry Fee. (Public)
Sponsors: Representative Scott.
Referred to:
1 A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
2 AN ACT TO CREATE AN ANNUAL SEX OFFENSE REGISTRY FEE TO SUPPORT THE
3 WORK OF MAINTAINING THAT REGISTRY.
4 The General Assembly of North Carolina enacts:
5 SECTION 1. G.S. 14-208.7 is amended by adding the following new subsections to
6 read:
7 "(e) Except as provided in subsection (f) of this section, each person required to register
8 under this section shall pay an annual fee equal to one and one-quarter percent (1.25%) of the
9 current federal poverty level guidelines for a one-person household published by the United
10 States Department of Health and Human Services. This annual fee shall support local
11 responsibilities required by this Article, including the regular verification of registrants and the
12 retention, maintenance, and dissemination of registrant records. This annual fee shall be collected
13 each year by the sheriff to whom the registrant returns the registrant's verification following the
14 anniversary of the registrant's initial registration date. Failure to pay this annual fee shall not
15 affect in any way the registrant's ability to register, verify information, or otherwise comply with
16 the requirements of this Part, though the county may seek collection of unpaid annual fees under
17 this subsection as a claimant agency under Chapter 105A of the General Statutes. The proceeds
18 of this annual fee shall be retained by the sheriff's office collecting the annual fee and shall
19 supplement local funds provided to the sheriff's office. No registrant shall be required to pay the
20 annual fee required by this subsection more than once in a calendar year.
21 (f) Each sheriff shall determine on January 1 of each year whether to collect the annual
22 fee required by subsection (e) of this section within that sheriff's county. Once this decision is
23 made, it shall be the decision for that county for the remainder of that calendar year, regardless
24 of whether the person holding the office of sheriff changes during that time. If a sheriff does not
25 make any specific determination under this subsection, the sheriff shall collect the annual fee
26 imposed by subsection (e) of this section pursuant to the provisions of this section.
27 (g) Upon request of the registrant, the sheriff required to collect the annual fee under
28 subsection (e) of this section, or the sheriff's designee, shall determine if the registrant is indigent
29 and may, upon a determination of indigency, waive all or part of the annual fee. This
30 determination shall be made within 10 business days of the date the registrant's request is
31 received. A request under this subsection, as well as the subsequent determination and action of
32 the sheriff or the sheriff's designee in response to that request, shall be recorded in written
33 findings by the sheriff's office and retained by that office with the registrant's records. A
34 registrant's request under this subsection shall be made in each calendar year in which the
35 registrant seeks review under this subsection, and the sheriff or sheriff's designee shall make a
36 new determination and record unique written findings upon each request.

1 (h) Notwithstanding subsection (g) of this section, a registrant (i) incarcerated or (ii)
2 under the supervision of the Department of Adult Correction, Division of Community
