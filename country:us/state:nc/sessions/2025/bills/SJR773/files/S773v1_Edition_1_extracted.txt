GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF NORTH CAROLINA
SESSION 2025
S 1
SENATE JOINT RESOLUTION 773
Sponsors: Senator Rabon (Primary Sponsor).
Referred to: Calendar 9/22/2025
September 22, 2025
1 A JOINT RESOLUTION PROVIDING FOR THE CONFIRMATION OF SALLIE
2 SHUPING-RUSSELL TO THE NORTH CAROLINA INVESTMENT AUTHORITY
3 BOARD OF DIRECTORS.
4 Whereas, under the provisions of G.S. 147-71.1, appointments by the Governor to the
5 North Carolina Investment Authority Board of Directors are subject to confirmation by the
6 General Assembly by joint resolution; and
7 Whereas, the Governor wishes to appoint Sallie Shuping-Russell to the North
8 Carolina Investment Authority Board of Directors; Now, therefore,
9 Be it resolved by the Senate, the House of Representatives concurring:
10 SECTION 1. The appointment of Sallie Shuping-Russell of Orange County to a term
11 on the North Carolina Investment Authority Board of Directors that began July 1, 2025, and
12 expires June 30, 2029, is confirmed.
13 SECTION 2. This resolution is effective upon ratification.
