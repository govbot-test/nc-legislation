Section 1:
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF NORTH CAROLINA
SESSION 2025
RESOLUTION 2025-9
SENATE JOINT RESOLUTION 773
A JOINT RESOLUTION PROVIDING FOR THE CONFIRMATION OF SALLIE
SHUPING-RUSSELL TO THE NORTH CAROLINA INVESTMENT AUTHORITY
BOARD OF DIRECTORS.
Whereas, under the provisions of G.S. 147-71.1, appointments by the Governor to the
North Carolina Investment Authority Board of Directors are subject to confirmation by the
General Assembly by joint resolution; and
Whereas, the Governor wishes to appoint Sallie Shuping-Russell to the North
Carolina Investment Authority Board of Directors; Now, therefore,
Be it resolved by the Senate, the House of Representatives concurring:

Section 2:
SECTION 1. The appointment of Sallie Shuping-Russell of Orange County to a term
on the North Carolina Investment Authority Board of Directors that began July 1, 2025, and
expires June 30, 2029, is confirmed.

Section 3:
SECTION 2. This resolution is effective upon ratification.
In the General Assembly read three times and ratified this the 24th day of September,

Section 4:
2025.
s/ Ralph Hise
Presiding Officer of the Senate
s/ Howard Penny, Jr.
Presiding Officer of the House of Representatives

GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF NORTH CAROLINA
SESSION 2025
RATIFIED BILL
RESOLUTION 2025-9
SENATE JOINT RESOLUTION 773
A JOINT RESOLUTION PROVIDING FOR THE CONFIRMATION OF SALLIE
SHUPING-RUSSELL TO THE NORTH CAROLINA INVESTMENT AUTHORITY
BOARD OF DIRECTORS.
Whereas, under the provisions of G.S. 147-71.1, appointments by the Governor to the
North Carolina Investment Authority Board of Directors are subject to confirmation by the
General Assembly by joint resolution; and
Whereas, the Governor wishes to appoint Sallie Shuping-Russell to the North
Carolina Investment Authority Board of Directors; Now, therefore,
Be it resolved by the Senate, the House of Representatives concurring:
SECTION 1. The appointment of Sallie Shuping-Russell of Orange County to a term
on the North Carolina Investment Authority Board of Directors that began July 1, 2025, and
expires June 30, 2029, is confirmed.
SECTION 2. This resolution is effective upon ratification.
In the General Assembly read three times and ratified this the 24th day of September,
2025.
s/ Ralph Hise
Presiding Officer of the Senate
s/ Howard Penny, Jr.
Presiding Officer of the House of Representatives
