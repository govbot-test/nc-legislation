Section 1:
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF NORTH CAROLINA
SESSION 2025
H 1
Short Title: Health Care Security Act. (Public)
Sponsors: Representatives Crawford, Pittman, Cohn, and Jeffers (Primary Sponsors).
For a complete list of sponsors, refer to the North Carolina General Assembly web site.
Referred to: Rules, Calendar, and Operations of the House
April 1, 2025
1 A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
2 AN ACT TO ELIMINATE THE STATUTORY TRIGGER FOR DISCONTINUATION OF
3 COVERAGE FOR THE MEDICAID EXPANSION POPULATION IF THE FEDERAL
4 MEDICAL ASSISTANCE PERCENTAGE FALLS BELOW NINETY PERCENT.
5 The General Assembly of North Carolina enacts:
6 SECTION 1. G.S. 108A-54.3C is repealed.
7 SECTION 2. This act is effective when it becomes law.
