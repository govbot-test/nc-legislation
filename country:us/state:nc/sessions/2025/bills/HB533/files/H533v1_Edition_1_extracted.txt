GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF NORTH CAROLINA
SESSION 2025
H 1
HOUSE BILL 533
Short Title: Funds/Various Public Projects in Chatham Co. (Public)
Sponsors: Representative Reives.
For a complete list of sponsors, refer to the North Carolina General Assembly web site.
Referred to: Appropriations, if favorable, Rules, Calendar, and Operations of the House
March 27, 2025
1 A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
2 AN ACT TO APPROPRIATE FUNDS FOR VARIOUS PUBLIC PROJECTS IN THE TOWNS
3 OF SILER CITY AND PITTSBORO.
4 The General Assembly of North Carolina enacts:
5 SECTION 1.(a) There is appropriated from the General Fund to the Office of State
6 Budget and Management the sum of two million four hundred fifty thousand dollars ($2,450,000)
7 in nonrecurring funds for the 2025-2026 fiscal year to provide a directed grant to the Town of
8 Siler City for its Downtown Streetscape Project. These funds shall be used as follows:
9 (1) Two million dollars ($2,000,000) to run underground power lines and widen
10 sidewalks for outside dining along Chatham Avenue.
11 (2) Four hundred fifty thousand dollars ($450,000) to implement design plans and
12 hire a main street coordinator.
13 SECTION 1.(b) There is appropriated from the General Fund to the Office of State
14 Budget and Management the sum of ten million four hundred thousand dollars ($10,400,000) in
15 nonrecurring funds for the 2025-2026 fiscal year to provide a directed grant to the Town of Siler
16 City for replacing the Town's condemned recreational building and improving its recreational
17 field. These funds shall be used as follows:
18 (1) Ten million dollars ($10,000,000) for designing and building a new
19 recreational facility.
20 (2) Four hundred thousand dollars ($400,000) for improvements at Bray Soccer
21 Stadium to support increased usage of the facilities and local AAU
22 tournaments.
23 SECTION 1.(c) There is appropriated from the General Fund to the Office of State
24 Budget and Management the sum of five million dollars ($5,000,000) in nonrecurring funds for
25 the 2025-2026 fiscal year to provide a directed grant to the Town of Siler City to design and build
26 a new fire station.
27 SECTION 2. There is appropriated from the General Fund to the Office of State
28 Budget and Management the sum of ten million dollars ($10,000,000) in nonrecurring funds for
29 the 2025-2026 fiscal year to provide a directed grant to the Town of Pittsboro to enhance its
30 emergency fire services. These funds shall be used as follows:
31 (1) Seven million dollars ($7,000,000) to design and build a new fire station.
32 (2) One million eight hundred thousand dollars ($1,800,000) to purchase a new
33 ladder truck.
34 (3) One million two hundred thousand dollars ($1,200,000) to purchase a new fire
35 engine.
