5 SECTION 1. G.S. 14-7.41(a) reads as rewritten:
6 "(a) A person who is convicted of a firearm-related felony and is also convicted of the
7 status offense must, upon conviction or plea of guilty under indictment as provided in this Article,
8 be sentenced as a Class C felon (except where the felon has been sentenced as a Class A, B1, or
9 B2 felon). However, in no case shall the person receive a minimum term of imprisonment of less
10 than 120 months. The court may not suspend the sentence and may not place the person sentenced
11 on probation."
