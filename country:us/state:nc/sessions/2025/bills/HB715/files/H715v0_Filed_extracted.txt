Section 1:
H.B. 715
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF NORTH CAROLINA
Apr 2, 2025
SESSION 2025
HOUSE PRINCIPAL CLERK
H D
Short Title: Universal Pre-K. (Public)
Sponsors: Representative Brockman.
Referred to:
1 A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
2 AN ACT TO APPROPRIATE FUNDS TO EXPAND ACCESS TO CHILDREN IN THIS
3 STATE SEEKING TO PARTICIPATE IN THE NORTH CAROLINA
4 PREKINDERGARTEN (NC PRE-K) PROGRAM.
5 The General Assembly of North Carolina enacts:
6 SECTION 1. There is appropriated from the General Fund to the Department of
7 Health and Human Services, Division of Child Development and Early Education, the sum of
8 thirteen million three hundred thousand dollars ($13,300,000) in recurring funds for each year of
9 the 2025-2027 fiscal biennium to provide funds for slots to expand access for a child in this State
10 seeking to participate in the North Carolina Prekindergarten (NC Pre-K) program.
11 SECTION 2. This act becomes effective July 1, 2025.
*DRH40381-LUa-78*
