GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF NORTH CAROLINA
SESSION 2025
SESSION LAW 2025-90
SENATE BILL 775
AN ACT TO APPOINT PERSONS TO VARIOUS PUBLIC OFFICES BY THE GENERAL
ASSEMBLY UPON THE RECOMMENDATIONS OF THE PRESIDENT PRO TEMPORE
OF THE SENATE AND THE SPEAKER OF THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES AND
TO MAKE CORRECTIONS TO PREVIOUS APPOINTMENTS.
The General Assembly of North Carolina enacts:
PART I. PRESIDENT PRO TEMPORE'S RECOMMENDATIONS
SECTION 1.1. Effective immediately, Richard "Rick" Parrotte of New Hanover
County is appointed to the Disciplinary Hearing Commission of the North Carolina State Bar for
a term expiring June 30, 2027, to fill the unexpired term of W. Ellis Boyle.
SECTION 1.2. Effective immediately, Joe Knott of Wake County is appointed to
the State Property Tax Commission for a term expiring June 30, 2027, to fill the unexpired term
of W. Ellis Boyle.
SECTION 1.3. Effective immediately, Dr. Marc Sosne of New Hanover County is
appointed to the Cape Fear Community College Board of Trustees for a term expiring June 30,
2026, to fill the unexpired term of Bruce Moskowitz.
SECTION 1.4. Effective immediately, Dennis Mabe of Randolph County is
appointed to the Randolph Community College Board of Trustees for a term expiring June 30,
2026, to fill the unexpired term of J. Brooke Schmidly.
SECTION 1.5. Effective immediately, William A. "Billy" Yow of Guilford County
is appointed to the North Carolina Well Contractors Certification Commission for a term expiring
June 30, 2026, to fill the unexpired term of Chris Deal.
SECTION 1.6. Effective immediately, Edward "Ned" Curran of Mecklenburg
County is appointed to the Metropolitan Public Transportation Authority for a term expiring June
30, 2027.
SECTION 1.7. Effective immediately, Sonya Barber of Bladen County is appointed
to the Bladen Community College Board of Trustees for a term expiring June 30, 2029, to fill the
unexpired term of Dixon Nance.
PART II. SPEAKER'S RECOMMENDATIONS
SECTION 2.1. Joaquin S. "Steve" Fuster of Wake County is appointed to the Home
Inspector's Licensure Board for a term expiring July 1, 2029, to fill the unexpired term of the
Honorable W. H. "Bill" Morris.
SECTION 2.2. Effective December 1, 2025, Matthew L. Bork of New Hanover
County, John Hayes of Caldwell County, and the Honorable Charles McGrady of Henderson
County are appointed to the Continuing Care Advisory Committee for terms expiring November
30, 2028.
SECTION 2.3. David Longo of Mecklenburg County is appointed to the
Metropolitan Public Transportation Authority for a term of two years to commence upon
approval by the Secretary of State of the articles of incorporation of the Metropolitan Public
Transportation Authority.

SECTION 2.5. Justin W. Whiteside of Brunswick County is appointed to the
