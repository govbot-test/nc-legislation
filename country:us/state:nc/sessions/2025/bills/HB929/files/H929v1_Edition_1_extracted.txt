GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF NORTH CAROLINA
SESSION 2025
H 1
HOUSE BILL 929
Short Title: Water Safety Bill. (Public)
Sponsors: Representative Almond.
For a complete list of sponsors, refer to the North Carolina General Assembly web site.
Referred to: Rules, Calendar, and Operations of the House
April 14, 2025
1 A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
2 AN ACT TO PROMOTE PUBLIC HEALTH BY PROHIBITING THE ADDITION OF
3 FLUORIDE FROM WATER SYSTEMS.
4 The General Assembly of North Carolina enacts:
5 SECTION 1. G.S. 130A-325 is amended by adding a new subdivision to read:
6 "(7) The introducing or depositing of fluoride or any chemical containing fluoride
7 to a public water system by any person. This subdivision does not apply to (i)
8 any water treatment process where fluoride is naturally occurring in a water
9 source or (ii) the use of fluoride for an industrial or manufacturing process not
10 related to public water treatment."
11 SECTION 2. This act becomes effective January 1, 2026.
