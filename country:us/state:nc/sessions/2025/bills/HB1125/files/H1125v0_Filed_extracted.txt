HOUSE BILL DRH40668-NBa-192
Short Title: The Wakaboomee Program. (Public)
Sponsors: Representative K. Brown.
Referred to:
1 A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
2 AN ACT TO APPROPRIATE FUNDS TO WAKABOOMEE FOR S.T.E.A.M.
3 OPPORTUNITIES FOR RURAL AND UNDERSERVED COMMUNITIES.
4 The General Assembly of North Carolina enacts:
5 SECTION 1. There is appropriated from the General Fund to the Office of State
6 Budget and Management the sum of one hundred thousand dollars ($100,000) in nonrecurring
7 funds for the 2026-2027 fiscal year to be allocated as a grant to Wakaboomee, a nonprofit
8 corporation, to expand the Wakaboomee's statewide reach into Johnston and Wayne Counties,
9 bringing programming to rural and underserved communities, delivering science, technology,
10 engineering, arts, and mathematics to community members.
11 SECTION 2. This act becomes effective July 1, 2026.
