Section 1:
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF NORTH CAROLINA
SESSION 2025
S 1
Short Title: Funds for Goodwill Industries. (Public)
Sponsors: Senator Lowe (Primary Sponsor).
Referred to: Rules and Operations of the Senate
April 30, 2026
1 A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
2 AN ACT TO APPROPRIATE FUNDS TO GOODWILL INDUSTRIES.
3 The General Assembly of North Carolina enacts:
4 SECTION 1. There is appropriated from the General Fund to the Office of State
5 Budget and Management the sum of two hundred thousand dollars ($200,000) in nonrecurring
6 funds for the 2026-2027 fiscal year to provide a directed grant to Goodwill Industries, Inc., to
7 support workforce development and job training programs in Forsyth County.
8 SECTION 2. This act becomes effective July 1, 2026.
*S928-v-1*
