Section 1:
H.B. 386
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF NORTH CAROLINA
Mar 12, 2025
SESSION 2025
HOUSE PRINCIPAL CLERK
H D
Short Title: Increase Vehicle Registration Fee/SHP Pay. (Public)
Sponsors: Representative Clampitt.
Referred to:
1 A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
2 AN ACT INCREASING VEHICLE REGISTRATION FEES FOR PRIVATE PASSENGER
3 VEHICLES TO FUND STATE HIGHWAY PATROL SALARIES AND
4 APPROPRIATING FUNDS TO PROVIDE COMPENSATION INCREASES TO
5 MEMBERS OF THE STATE HIGHWAY PATROL.
6 The General Assembly of North Carolina enacts:
7
8 PART I. VEHICLE REGISTRATION FEE INCREASE
9 SECTION 1.(a) G.S. 20-4.02 reads as rewritten:
10 "§ 20-4.02. Quadrennial adjustment of certain fees and rates.
11 (a) Adjustment for Inflation. – Beginning July 1, 2020, and every four years thereafter,
12 the Division shall adjust the fees and rates imposed pursuant to the statutes listed in this
13 subsection for inflation in accordance with the Consumer Price Index computed by the Bureau
14 of Labor Statistics. The adjustment for per transaction rates in subdivision (8b) of this subsection
15 shall be rounded to the nearest cent and all other adjustments under this subsection shall be
16 rounded to the nearest twenty-five cents (25¢):
17 …
18 (11) G.S. 20-87, except for including the additional fee for private passenger
19 vehicles set forth in G.S. 20-87(5) but excluding the additional fee set forth in
20 G.S. 20-87(6) for private motorcycles.
21 …."
22 SECTION 1.(b) G.S. 20-87 reads as rewritten:
23 "§ 20-87. Passenger vehicle registration fees.
24 These fees shall be paid to the Division annually for the registration and licensing of
25 passenger vehicles, according to the following classifications and schedules:
26 …
27 (5) Private Passenger Vehicles. –
28 There shall be paid to the Division annually, as of the first day of January, for
29 the registration and licensing of private passenger vehicles, fees
30 according to the following classifications and schedules:
31 Private passenger vehicles of not more than fifteen passengers.........$38.75
32 Private passenger vehicles over fifteen passengers............................. 43.25
33 An additional fee of six dollars and twenty-five cents ($6.25) is imposed on
34 each private passenger vehicle registered under this subdivision in addition to
35 the base fee. The Commissioner shall, on a monthly basis, transfer the revenue
36 from the additional fee to the Department of Public Safety. The funds
*DRH40238-NEfa-55A*
General Assembly Of North Carolina Session 2025
1 transferred pursuant to this subdivision shall, in addition to any other funds
2 appropriated for this purpose, be used to fund State Highway Patrol salaries.
3 (5a) Provided, that Notwithstanding subdivision (5) of this section, a fee of only
4 one dollar and thirty cents ($1.30) shall be charged for any vehicle given by
5 the federal government to any veteran on account of any disability suffered
6 during war so long as such vehicle is owned by the original donee or other
7 veteran entitled to receive such gift under Title 38, section 252, United States
8 Code Annotated.
9 …."
10 SECTION 1.(c) This section becomes effective July 1, 2025, and applies to vehicle
11 registrations issued or renewed on or after that date.
12
13 PART II. APPROPRIATION TO PROVIDE COMPENSATION INCREASES TO
14 MEMBERS OF THE STATE HIGHWAY PATROL
15 SECTION 2.(a) Effective July 1, 2025, there is appropriated from the General Fund
16 to the Reserve for Compensation Increases to provide pay raises for the State Highway Patrol the
17 sum of forty million four hundred thousand dollars ($40,400,000) in recurring funds for each
18 year of the 2025-2027 fiscal biennium to establish the pay scales set forth in this section.
19 SECTION 2.(b) Effective July 1, 2025, the law enforcement officers of the State
20 Highway Patrol shall be paid based upon the following experience-based annual salary schedule:
21 Years of Experience 2025-2027 Biennium
22 0 $56,000
23 1 62,125
24 2 68,250
25 3 75,375
26 4 80,500
27 5 86,625
28 6 92,750
29 7 98,875
30 8+ 105,000
31 SECTION 2.(c) Effective July 1, 2025, State Highway Patrol employees who are
32 not subject to an experience-based annual salary schedule shall be paid at the following rates by
33 rank:
34 Rank 2025-2027 Biennium
35 Sergeant $120,750
36 First Sergeant 132,825
37 Lieutenant 146,108
38 Captain 160,718
39 Major 176,790
40 Lieutenant Colonel 194,469
41 Deputy Commander 204,193
42 Colonel 224,612
43
44 PART III. EFFECTIVE DATE
45 SECTION 3. Except as otherwise provided, this act is effective when it becomes
46 law.
