H.B. 679
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF NORTH CAROLINA
Apr 1, 2025
SESSION 2025
HOUSE PRINCIPAL CLERK
H D
HOUSE BILL DRH10320-LGa-134
Short Title: Funds for Spring Lake Projects. (Public)
Sponsors: Representative Colvin.
Referred to:
1 A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
2 AN ACT TO APPROPRIATE FUNDS FOR VARIOUS PUBLIC PROJECTS IN THE TOWN
3 OF SPRING LAKE.
4 The General Assembly of North Carolina enacts:
5 SECTION 1. There is appropriated from the General Fund to the Office of State
6 Budget and Management the sum of five million four hundred thousand dollars ($5,400,000) in
7 nonrecurring funds for the 2025-2026 fiscal year to provide a directed grant to the Town of Spring
8 Lake as follows:
9 (1) Two hundred thousand dollars ($200,000) to complete the restoration of the
10 Historic Spring Lake Civic Center.
11 (2) One hundred thousand dollars ($100,000) for the Spring Lake Fire
12 Department.
13 (3) Five million dollars ($5,000,000) for infrastructure needs.
14 (4) One hundred thousand dollars ($100,000) to assist the Manchester Fire
15 Department or any other rural fire department that serves the Spring Lake area.
16 SECTION 2. This act becomes effective July 1, 2025.
