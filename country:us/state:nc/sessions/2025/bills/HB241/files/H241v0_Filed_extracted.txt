Section 1:
H.B. 241
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF NORTH CAROLINA
Feb 26, 2025
SESSION 2025
HOUSE PRINCIPAL CLERK
H D
Short Title: Funds for Person County Projects. (Public)
Sponsors: Representative Jeffers.
Referred to:
1 A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
2 AN ACT TO APPROPRIATE FUNDS FOR VARIOUS PUBLIC PROJECTS IN PERSON
3 COUNTY.
4 The General Assembly of North Carolina enacts:
5 SECTION 1. There is appropriated from the General Fund to the Office of State
6 Budget and Management the sum of ten million dollars ($10,000,000) in nonrecurring funds for
7 the 2025-2026 fiscal year to provide a directed grant to the City of Roxboro for repairs and
8 upgrades to the City's wastewater and treatment plant.
9 SECTION 2. There is appropriated from the General Fund to the Office of State
10 Budget and Management the sum of eight hundred thousand dollars ($800,000) in nonrecurring
11 funds for the 2025-2026 fiscal year to provide a directed grant to Person County to enable the
12 Recreation, Arts, and Parks Department to construct a splash pad.
13 SECTION 3. There is appropriated from the General Fund to the Office of State
14 Budget and Management the sum of five million dollars ($5,000,000) in nonrecurring funds for
15 the 2025-2026 fiscal year to provide a directed grant to Piedmont Community College to upfit
16 and purchase equipment for its Center for Health, Advanced Technology, and Trades.
17 SECTION 4. This act becomes effective July 1, 2025.
*DRH30118-LGa-88*
