Section 1:
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF NORTH CAROLINA
SESSION 2025
H 1
Short Title: Shotgun House Funding Act. (Public)
Sponsors: Representatives Baker and K. Brown (Primary Sponsors).
For a complete list of sponsors, refer to the North Carolina General Assembly web site.
Referred to: Appropriations, if favorable, Rules, Calendar, and Operations of the House
April 14, 2025
1 A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
2 AN ACT TO PROVIDE AN APPROPRIATION FOR A MULTIUSE CENTER.
3 The General Assembly of North Carolina enacts:
4 SECTION 1. There is appropriated from the General Fund to the Office of State
5 Budget and Management the nonrecurring sum of seven hundred fifty thousand dollars
6 ($750,000) for the 2025-2026 fiscal year to be allocated as a directed grant to Triad Cultural Arts,
7 Inc., a nonprofit corporation, to be used for the development of the Shotgun House Legacy Site
8 project in the City of Winston-Salem.
9 SECTION 2. This act becomes effective July 1, 2025.
*H995-v-1*
