3 "ALLOW OFF-SITE FOOD SERVICE FOR WORKPLACE EVENTS
4 SECTION 17.3. G.S. 130A-248 is amended by adding a new subsection to read:
5 "(c4) Notwithstanding any provision of this Part, a permitted food establishment may serve
6 food or drink in a workplace setting at an offsite location for the employees of that designated
7 workplace and their invited guests. Food may be sold individually to employees or guests of the
8 designated workplace. The food establishment shall notify the local health department before
9 initiating offsite service at a designated workplace. The food establishment shall provide an
10 offsite location schedule to the local health department upon the request of the local health
11 department. The food establishment shall comply with all of the following requirements, and if
12 the local health department inspects the offsite location, only these requirements shall be
13 assessed:
14 (1) All food served at the offsite location shall be prepared and cooked at the
15 permitted food establishment. Food may be assembled during service at the
16 offsite location with no further cooking.
17 (2) Assembling and serving food or drink shall only take place indoors.
18 (3) Food or drink shall be protected from contamination during transportation,
19 display, assembling, and service.
20 (4) Utensils used during food service shall be returned to the permitted food
21 establishment to be washed, rinsed, and sanitized. The permitted food
22 establishment shall provide extra serving utensils from the food establishment
23 to the offsite location.
24 (5) The food establishment shall utilize Time as a Public Health Control as
25 required in Section 3-501.19 of the NC Food Code.
26 (6) No permitted food establishment shall operate in the same location for more
27 than three days in a seven-day period.
28 (7) Food employees shall be employed by the holder of the food establishment
29 permit and at least one food employee shall always be present from the time
30 food or drink leaves the permitted establishment until the end of the serving
31 period at the offsite location.
