5 The General Assembly of North Carolina enacts:
6 SECTION 1. G.S. 115C-310.13 reads as rewritten:
7 "§ 115C-310.13. Advanced teaching roles salary supplements.
8 (a) ATR units may designate up to fifteen percent (15%) of the teachers in each ATR
9 school as adult leadership teachers and five percent (5%) of the teachers in each ATR school as
10 classroom excellence teachers. To the extent funds are made available for the purpose, teachers
11 serving in an advanced teaching role are eligible to receive salary supplements as follows:
12 (1) Ten thousand dollars ($10,000) for adult leadership teachers. An ATR unit
13 may divide the salary supplement for an adult leadership teacher between the
14 adult leadership teacher and collaborating teachers who team with the adult
15 leadership teacher in a community of practice participating in the R3 Program
16 Community of Practice model.
17 (2) Three thousand dollars ($3,000) for classroom excellence teachers.
18 (b) Notwithstanding G.S. 115C-310.5, to the extent funds are made available for this
19 purpose, the State Board of Education shall award funds to ATR units for annual salary
20 supplements for teachers, and the receiving ATR units shall provide the salary supplement to
21 participating teachers in accordance with this section. If State funds are insufficient to cover the
22 full amount of supplement identified in subsection (a) of this section, the State Board of
23 Education and the ATR unit shall disburse any supplement funds pro rata. If funds have been
24 disbursed pro rata, ATR units are encouraged but not required to fund the remainder of the
25 supplement from alternate funding sources.
26 (c) The following additional requirements apply to salary supplements received pursuant
27 to this section:
28 (1) Loss of a salary supplement received pursuant to this section for any reason
29 shall not be considered a demotion under Part 3 of Article 22 of Chapter 115C
30 of the General Statutes.
31 (2) A teacher is eligible to continue receiving a salary supplement pursuant to this
32 section as long as he or she remains an adult leadership teacher or a classroom
33 excellence teacher.adult leadership teacher, a collaborating teacher teamed
34 with an adult leadership teacher, or a classroom excellence teacher.
35 (3) A teacher is eligible to receive no more than one annual salary supplement
36 pursuant to this section at any time.
