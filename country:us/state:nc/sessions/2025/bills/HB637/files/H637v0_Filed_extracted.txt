6 SECTION 1. G.S. 115C-310.13 reads as rewritten:
7 "§ 115C-310.13. Advanced teaching roles salary supplements.
8 (a) ATR units may designate up to fifteen percent (15%) of the teachers in each ATR
9 school as adult leadership teachers and five percent (5%) of the teachers in each ATR school as
10 classroom excellence teachers. To the extent funds are made available for the purpose, teachers
11 serving in an advanced teaching role are eligible to receive salary supplements as follows:ATR
12 units shall be allocated funds for salary supplements in the following amounts for each teacher
13 identified as serving in the following advanced teaching roles:
14 (1) Ten thousand dollars ($10,000) for adult leadership teachers.
15 (2) Three thousand dollars ($3,000) for classroom excellence teachers.
16 (a1) The salary supplement for an adult leadership teacher, as identified in subdivision
17 (a)(1) of this section, may be shared with teachers who team with the adult leadership teacher if
18 the teachers are in a community of practice addressing an instructional problem of practice in a
19 school participating in the R3 Program Community of Practice model.
20 (b) Notwithstanding G.S. 115C-310.5, to the extent funds are made available for this
21 purpose, the State Board of Education shall award funds to ATR units for annual salary
22 supplements for teachers, and the receiving ATR units shall provide the salary supplement to
23 participating teachers in accordance with this section. If State funds are insufficient to cover the
24 full amount of supplement identified in subsection (a) of this section, the State Board of
25 Education and the ATR unit shall disburse any supplement funds pro rata. If funds have been
26 disbursed pro rata, ATR units are encouraged but not required to fund the remainder of the
27 supplement from alternate funding sources.
28 (c) The following additional requirements apply to salary supplements received pursuant
29 to this section:
30 (1) Loss of a salary supplement received pursuant to this section for any reason
31 shall not be considered a demotion under Part 3 of Article 22 of Chapter 115C
32 of the General Statutes.
33 (2) A teacher is eligible to continue receiving a salary supplement pursuant to this
34 section as long as he or she remains an adult leadership teacher or teacher, a
35 classroom excellence teacher.teacher, or a qualifying teacher teamed with an
36 adult leadership teacher.
