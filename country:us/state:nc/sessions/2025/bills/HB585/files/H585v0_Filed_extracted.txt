18 "§ 7A-354.2. Human trafficking reporting and response system.
19 The North Carolina Human Trafficking Commission shall coordinate and support the
20 development or use of a secure, technology-driven, and survivor-centered human trafficking
21 reporting and response system, with a focus on areas of the State where access to anti-human
22 trafficking resources is most limited."
23 SECTION 2.(a) There is appropriated from the General Fund to the Administrative
24 Office of the Courts, North Carolina Human Trafficking Commission, the sum of three hundred
25 thousand dollars ($300,000) in recurring funds for the 2025-2026 fiscal year to be used to
26 coordinate and support the development or use of a secure, technology-driven, and
27 survivor-centered human trafficking reporting and response system, with a targeted focus on
28 western North Carolina, where access to anti-human trafficking resources remains extremely
29 limited.
30 SECTION 2.(b) The uses for the funds appropriated in this section may include, but
31 are not limited to, each of the following:
32 (1) Expanding the reach of a reporting and response system, as referenced in
33 subsection (a) of this section, in western North Carolina.
34 (2) Funding one full-time regional operations coordinator to perform at a
35 minimum each of the following job functions in relation to the reporting and
36 response system required to be developed or used pursuant to G.S. 7A-354.2:
