Section 1:
FILED SENATE
Mar 13, 2025
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF NORTH CAROLINA
S.B. 292
SESSION 2025
PRINCIPAL CLERK
S D
Short Title: Funds/Town of Grifton Resiliency. (Public)
Sponsors: Senator Smith (Primary Sponsor).
Referred to:
1 A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
2 AN ACT TO APPROPRIATE FUNDS TO THE TOWN OF GRIFTON FOR DESIGN OF
3 FLOOD RESILIENCY PROJECTS.
4 The General Assembly of North Carolina enacts:
5 SECTION 1. The sum of three million dollars ($3,000,000) in nonrecurring funds
6 for the 2025-2026 fiscal year is appropriated from the General Fund to the Town of Grifton for
7 design and construction of flood mitigation and resiliency projects.
8 SECTION 2. This act becomes effective July 1, 2025.
*DRS15128-MHa-86*
