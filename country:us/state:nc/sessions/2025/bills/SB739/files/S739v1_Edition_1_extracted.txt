Section 1:
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF NORTH CAROLINA
SESSION 2025
S 1
Short Title: Funds for Oyster Farming. (Public)
Sponsors: Senator Sanderson (Primary Sponsor).
Referred to: Rules and Operations of the Senate
March 26, 2025
1 A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
2 AN ACT TO APPROPRIATE FUNDS FOR OYSTER FARMING IN HYDE COUNTY.
3 The General Assembly of North Carolina enacts:
4 SECTION 1. There is appropriated from the General Fund to the Office of State
5 Budget and Management the sum of six million dollars ($6,000,000) in nonrecurring funds for
6 the 2025-2026 fiscal year to provide a directed grant to Hyde County to be used to assist with the
7 purchase of property for oyster farming. The County shall collaborate with North Carolina
8 Marine Industrial Park Authority on this project.
9 SECTION 2. This act becomes effective July 1, 2025.
*S739-v-1*
