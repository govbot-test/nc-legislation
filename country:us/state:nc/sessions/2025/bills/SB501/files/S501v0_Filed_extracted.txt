Section 1:
FILED SENATE
Mar 25, 2025
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF NORTH CAROLINA
S.B. 501
SESSION 2025
PRINCIPAL CLERK
S D
Short Title: Funds for TEAPOT Project. (Public)
Sponsors: Senator Sanderson (Primary Sponsor).
Referred to:
1 A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
2 AN ACT TO APPROPRIATE FUNDS TO THE TOWN OF EDENTON FOR DOWNTOWN
3 DEVELOPMENT.
4 The General Assembly of North Carolina enacts:
5 SECTION 1. There is appropriated from the General Fund to the Office of State
6 Budget and Management the sum of eleven million dollars ($11,000,000) in nonrecurring funds
7 for the 2025-2026 fiscal year for the downtown development in the Town of Edenton, known as
8 the TEAPOT Project. These funds may be used for building renovation and additional downtown
9 development.
10 SECTION 2. This act becomes effective July 1, 2025.
*DRS35200-LGa-113*
