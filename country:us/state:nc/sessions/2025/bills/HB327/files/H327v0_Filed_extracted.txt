H.B. 327
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF NORTH CAROLINA
Mar 6, 2025
SESSION 2025
HOUSE PRINCIPAL CLERK
H D
HOUSE BILL DRH30113-LGa-89
Short Title: Funds for Hope Mills/Capital Projects. (Public)
Sponsors: Representative F. Jackson.
Referred to:
1 A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
2 AN ACT TO APPROPRIATE FUNDS TO THE TOWN OF HOPE MILLS FOR VARIOUS
3 CAPITAL PROJECTS.
4 The General Assembly of North Carolina enacts:
5 SECTION 1. There is appropriated from the General Fund to the Office of State
6 Budget and Management the sum of sixteen million five hundred sixty-four thousand dollars
7 ($16,564,000) in nonrecurring funds for the 2025-2026 fiscal year to provide a directed grant to
8 the Town of Hope Mills for the following capital projects:
9 (1) Thirteen million one hundred thirty-four thousand dollars ($13,134,000) for
10 the construction of a new community center.
11 (2) Three million four hundred thirty thousand dollars ($3,430,000) to design and
12 renovate the Hope Mills Senior Center to make it ADA compliant.
13 SECTION 2. This act becomes effective July 1, 2025.
