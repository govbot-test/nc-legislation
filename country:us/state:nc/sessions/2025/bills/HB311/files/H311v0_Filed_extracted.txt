Section 1:
H.B. 311
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF NORTH CAROLINA
Mar 5, 2025
SESSION 2025
HOUSE PRINCIPAL CLERK
H D
Short Title: Funds for the Town of Gibson. (Public)
Sponsors: Representative G. Pierce.
Referred to:
1 A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
2 AN ACT TO APPROPRIATE FUNDS TO THE TOWN OF GIBSON FOR VARIOUS PUBLIC
3 PURPOSES.
4 The General Assembly of North Carolina enacts:
5 SECTION 1. There is appropriated from the General Fund to the Office of State
6 Budget and Management the sum of two hundred thirty-one thousand dollars ($231,000) in
7 nonrecurring funds for the 2025-2026 fiscal year to provide a directed grant to the Town of
8 Gibson to be used as follows:
9 (1) Fifty-five thousand dollars ($55,000) for a generator at main lift station.
10 (2) Eighteen thousand dollars ($18,000) for a portable generator.
11 (3) Thirty thousand dollars ($30,000) for lift station rehabilitation.
12 (4) Eighteen thousand dollars ($18,000) for new water meters.
13 (5) Forty-five thousand dollars ($45,000) for ditch work.
14 (6) Sixty-five thousand dollars ($65,000) for Town vehicles.
15 SECTION 2. This act becomes effective July 1, 2025.
*DRH30154-LGa-138*
