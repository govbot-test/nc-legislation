1 A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
2 AN ACT TO PROVIDE FUNDS TO ALLEVIATE FLASH FLOODING IN THE TOWN OF
3 AYDEN.
4 The General Assembly of North Carolina enacts:
5 SECTION 1. The sum of three million dollars ($3,000,000) in nonrecurring funds
6 for the 2026-2027 fiscal year is appropriated from the General Fund to the Town of Ayden for
7 stormwater management projects intended to mitigate flooding hazard in the Kennedy Estates
8 subdivision of the Town.
9 SECTION 2. This act becomes effective July 1, 2026.
