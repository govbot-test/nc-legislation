Section 1:
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF NORTH CAROLINA
SESSION 2025
S 1
Short Title: Smith Reynolds Airport Hangar Improvements. (Public)
Sponsors: Senator Lowe (Primary Sponsor).
Referred to: Rules and Operations of the Senate
April 30, 2026
1 A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
2 AN ACT TO FUND RENOVATION, STRUCTURAL REPAIRS, MODERNIZATION, AND
3 NECESSARY INFRASTRUCTURE ENHANCEMENTS OF HANGAR FACILITIES
4 LOCATED AT SMITH REYNOLDS AIRPORT.
5 The General Assembly of North Carolina enacts:
6 SECTION 1. There is appropriated from the General Fund to the Department of
7 Transportation, Division of Aviation, the following amounts in nonrecurring funds for the
8 2026-2027 fiscal year to be allocated to Forsyth County for renovation, structural repairs,
9 modernization, and necessary infrastructure enhancements of hangar facilities at the Smith
10 Reynolds Airport, according to the following schedule:
11
12 Item FY 2026-27
13 Hangar Facility at 4001 North Liberty Street $11,500,000
14 Hangar Facility at 3820 North Liberty Street $4,000,000
15
16 SECTION 2. This act becomes effective July 1, 2026.
*S931-v-1*
