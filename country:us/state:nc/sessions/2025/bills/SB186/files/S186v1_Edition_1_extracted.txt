Section 1:
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF NORTH CAROLINA
SESSION 2025
S 1
Short Title: Funds for Ladder Truck for WS Fire Station 19. (Public)
Sponsors: Senator Lowe (Primary Sponsor).
Referred to: Rules and Operations of the Senate
February 27, 2025
1 A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
2 AN ACT TO APPROPRIATE FUNDS TO PURCHASE A LADDER TRUCK FOR FIRE
3 STATION 19 IN WINSTON-SALEM.
4 The General Assembly of North Carolina enacts:
5 SECTION 1. There is appropriated from the General Fund to the Office of State
6 Budget and Management the sum of two million dollars ($2,000,000) in nonrecurring funds for
7 the 2025-2026 fiscal year to provide a directed grant to the City of Winston-Salem for the
8 purchase of a ladder truck for the fire department's Station 19.
9 SECTION 2. This act becomes effective July 1, 2025.
*S186-v-1*
