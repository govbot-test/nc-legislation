Section 1:
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF NORTH CAROLINA
SESSION 2025
S 1
Short Title: Funds for Pauli Murray Center. (Public)
Sponsors: Senators Murdock and Chitlik (Primary Sponsors).
Referred to: Rules and Operations of the Senate
March 17, 2025
1 A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
2 AN ACT TO APPROPRIATE FUNDS FOR THE PAULI MURRAY CENTER IN DURHAM.
3 The General Assembly of North Carolina enacts:
4 SECTION 1. There is appropriated from the General Fund to the Office of State
5 Budget and Management the sum of one hundred fifty thousand dollars ($150,000) in
6 nonrecurring funds for the 2025-2026 fiscal year to provide a directed grant to the Pauli Murray
7 Center for History and Social Justice for the development of the Pauli Murray Center Green. The
8 Green will provide functional access to the Pauli Murray Center and provide critical drainage
9 and stormwater management systems to the Center.
10 SECTION 2. This act becomes effective July 1, 2025.
*S288-v-1*
