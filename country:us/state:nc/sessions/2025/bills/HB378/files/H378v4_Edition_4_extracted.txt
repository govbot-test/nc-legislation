41 uniformed service or that the applicant intends to serve in the uniformed service.
42 "§ 116-44.9C. Deferment.
43 (a) Deferment Requests. – The Board of Governors of The University of North Carolina
44 shall adopt a policy requiring constituent institutions to provide for enrollment deferment for
45 members of the uniformed services and spouses of members of the uniformed services if the
46 deferment is requested at least 30 days prior to enrollment in a constituent institution.

48 shall be granted deferments of at least two years after entry into the reserve Armed Forces. All
49 other members and spouses of members of the uniformed services shall be granted deferments
50 of at least five years after entry into the uniformed services."
