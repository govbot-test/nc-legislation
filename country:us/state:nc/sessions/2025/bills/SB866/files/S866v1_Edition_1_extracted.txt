3 SECTION 1.(e) G.S. 115C-562.6 reads as rewritten:
4 "§ 115C-562.6. Scholarship endorsement.
5 The Authority shall remit, at least two times each school year, scholarship grant funds
6 awarded to eligible students to the nonpublic school school. Scholarship grant funds awarded to
7 eligible students to a private school shall be remitted for endorsement by at least one of the
8 student's parents or guardians. The parent or guardian shall restrictively endorse the scholarship
9 grant funds awarded to the eligible student for deposit into the account of the nonpublic private
10 school to the credit of the eligible student. The parent or guardian shall not designate any entity
11 or individual associated with the nonpublic private school as the parent's attorney-in-fact to
12 endorse the scholarship grant funds. A parent's or guardian's failure to comply with this section
13 shall result in forfeit of the scholarship grant. A scholarship grant forfeited for failure to comply
14 with this section shall be returned to the Authority to be awarded to another student."
