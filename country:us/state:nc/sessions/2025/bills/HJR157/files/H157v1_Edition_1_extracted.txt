GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF NORTH CAROLINA
SESSION 2025
H 1
HOUSE JOINT RESOLUTION 157
Sponsors: Representative Bell.
Referred to: Rules, Calendar, and Operations of the House
February 21, 2025
1 A JOINT RESOLUTION INVITING HIS EXCELLENCY, GOVERNOR JOSH STEIN, TO
2 ADDRESS A JOINT SESSION OF THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES AND
3 SENATE.
4 Be it resolved by the House of Representatives, the Senate concurring:
5 SECTION 1. A committee of five Representatives and five Senators shall be
6 appointed by the presiding officers of the respective houses to invite His Excellency, Governor
7 Josh Stein, to address a joint session of the House of Representatives and the Senate in the Hall
8 of the House of Representatives at 7:00 P.M. on March 12, 2025.
9 SECTION 2. The members of The Honorable Josh Stein's cabinet, the members of
10 the Council of State, the Justices of the Supreme Court, and the judges of the Court of Appeals
11 are invited to attend the joint session.
12 SECTION 3. This resolution is effective upon ratification.
