GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF NORTH CAROLINA
SESSION 2025
H 1
Short Title: North Carolina Student Lifeline Act. (Public)
Sponsors: Representatives Cunningham, Blackwell, Lambeth, and G. Brown (Primary
Sponsors).
For a complete list of sponsors, refer to the North Carolina General Assembly web site.
Referred to: Education - K-12, if favorable, Health, if favorable, Rules, Calendar, and
Operations of the House
April 7, 2025
1 A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
2 AN ACT TO REQUIRE SCHOOLS TO SHARE THE SUICIDE AND CRISIS LIFELINE
3 PHONE NUMBER IN VARIOUS WAYS.
4 The General Assembly of North Carolina enacts:
5 SECTION 1.(a) G.S. 115C-47 is amended by adding the following new subdivision
6 to read:
7 "(70) To Provide Students the Suicide and Crisis Lifeline Phone Number. – A local
8 board of education shall adopt a policy to ensure all schools in the local school
9 administrative unit provide students the phone number for the Suicide and
10 Crisis Lifeline. The board shall verify that the phone number for the Lifeline
11 is current and accurate annually. If the phone number has changed, schools
12 shall use the updated phone number. Unless an updated phone number exists,
13 the schools shall have the phrase "To reach the Suicide and Crisis Lifeline,
14 call 988 or text HOME to 741741." in the following places:
15 a. On any new student identification (student ID) issued to a student in
16 grades six through 12. The text shall be in a conspicuous location on
17 the student ID. The text may be printed on the ID or affixed by sticker.
18 Nothing in this subdivision requires a school to issue a student ID.
19 b. On the school website.
20 c. On the home screen of any electronic device issued to students.
21 d. On any school agenda or calendar, whether digital or printed.
22 e. On a document during any suicide awareness activity.
23 f. On a document when the student registers to attend the school."
24 SECTION 1.(b) G.S. 115C-150.12C is amended by adding a new subdivision to
25 read:
26 "(37) To provide students the Suicide and Crisis Lifeline phone number. – The
27 board of trustees shall provide students the phone number for the Suicide and
28 Crisis Lifeline. The board shall verify that the phone number for the Lifeline
29 is current and accurate annually. If the phone number has changed, the board
30 shall use the updated phone number. Unless an updated phone number exists,
31 the board shall have the phrase "To reach the Suicide and Crisis Lifeline, call
32 988 or text HOME to 741741." in the following places:
