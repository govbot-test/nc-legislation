Section 1:
H.B. 1160
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF NORTH CAROLINA
Apr 30, 2026
SESSION 2025
HOUSE PRINCIPAL CLERK
H D
Short Title: Reduce Parent Copays/Child Care Subsidy/Funds. (Public)
Sponsors: Representative Lofton.
Referred to:
1 A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
2 AN ACT TO REDUCE PARENT COPAYMENTS FOR SUBSIDIZED CHILD CARE AND
3 TO APPROPRIATE FUNDS FOR THAT PURPOSE.
4 The General Assembly of North Carolina enacts:
5 SECTION 1. Section 9D.3(b) of S.L. 2023-134 reads as rewritten:
6 "SECTION 9D.3.(b) Fees Beginning October 1, 2026, fees for families who are required to
7 share in the cost of care are established based on ten percent (10%) seven percent (7%) of gross
8 family income. When care is received at the blended rate, the copayment shall be eighty-three
9 percent (83%) of the full-time copayment. Copayments for part-time care shall be seventy-five
10 percent (75%) of the full-time copayment."
11 SECTION 2. There is appropriated from the General Fund to the Department of
12 Health and Human Services, Division of Child Development and Early Education, the sum of
13 twenty-five million dollars ($25,000,000) in recurring funds beginning in the 2026-2027 fiscal
14 year to reduce parent copayments for subsidized child care in accordance with Section 1 of this
15 act.
16 SECTION 3. This act becomes effective July 1, 2026.
*DRH30576-LUa-130A*
