Section 1:
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF NORTH CAROLINA
SESSION 2025
S 1
Short Title: Funds for Siler City Streetscape Project. (Public)
Sponsors: Senator Murdock (Primary Sponsor).
Referred to: Rules and Operations of the Senate
March 20, 2025
1 A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
2 AN ACT TO APPROPRIATE FUNDS FOR SILER CITY'S DOWNTOWN STREETSCAPE
3 PROJECT.
4 The General Assembly of North Carolina enacts:
5 SECTION 1. There is appropriated from the General Fund to the Office of State
6 Budget and Management the sum of two million four hundred fifty thousand dollars ($2,450,000)
7 in nonrecurring funds for the 2025-2026 fiscal year to provide a directed grant to Siler City for
8 its downtown streetscape project. These funds shall be used as follows:
9 (1) Two million dollars ($2,000,000) for running underground power lines and
10 widening sidewalks for outside dining along Chatham Avenue during Phase
11 1.
12 (2) Four hundred fifty thousand dollars ($450,000) for implementing design plans
13 and hiring a main street coordinator during Phase 2.
14 SECTION 2. This act becomes effective July 1, 2025.
*S341-v-1*
