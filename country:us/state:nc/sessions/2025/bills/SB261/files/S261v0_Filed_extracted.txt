8 "§ 62-110.9. Requirements concerning reductions in emissions of carbon dioxide from
9 electric public utilities.
10 The Utilities Commission shall take all reasonable steps to achieve a seventy percent (70%)
11 reduction in emissions of carbon dioxide (CO2) emitted in the State from electric generating
12 facilities owned or operated by electric public utilities from 2005 levels by the year 2030 and that
13 result in carbon neutrality by the year 2050. For purposes of this section, (i) "electric public
14 utility" means any electric public utility as defined in G.S. 62-3(23) serving at least 150,000
15 North Carolina retail jurisdictional customers as of January 1, 2021, and (ii) "carbon neutrality"
16 means for every ton of CO2 emitted in the State from electric generating facilities owned or
17 operated by or on behalf of electric public utilities, an equivalent amount of CO2 is reduced,
18 removed, prevented, or offset, provided that the offsets are verifiable and do not exceed five
19 percent (5%) of the authorized reduction goal. In achieving the authorized carbon reduction
20 goals, goal, the Utilities Commission shall:

22 public utilities, including stakeholder input, for the utilities to achieve the

25 grid modernization, storage, energy efficiency measures, demand-side
26 management, and the latest technological breakthroughs to achieve the least
27 cost path consistent with this section to achieve compliance with the
28 authorized carbon reduction goals goal (the "Carbon Plan"). The Carbon Plan
29 shall be reviewed every two years and may be adjusted as necessary in the
30 determination of the Commission and the electric public utilities.
31 (2) Comply with current law and practice with respect to the least cost planning
