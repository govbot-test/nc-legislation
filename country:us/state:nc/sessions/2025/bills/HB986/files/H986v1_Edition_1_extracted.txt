GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF NORTH CAROLINA
SESSION 2025
H 1
HOUSE BILL 986
Short Title: Support Our Teachers. (Public)
Sponsors: Representatives Cotham and Biggs (Primary Sponsors).
For a complete list of sponsors, refer to the North Carolina General Assembly web site.
Referred to: Education - K-12, if favorable, Rules, Calendar, and Operations of the House
April 14, 2025
1 A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
2 AN ACT TO PROHIBIT LOCAL SCHOOL ADMINISTRATIVE UNITS FROM REQUIRING
3 LESSON PLANS OR LESSON OBJECTIVES BE POSTED, TO PROVIDE FOR
4 DUTY-FREE LUNCHES FOR TEACHERS, AND TO REQUIRE THAT TEACHERS
5 WHO LEAD PROFESSIONAL DEVELOPMENT BE COMPENSATED FOR THEIR
6 TIME.
7 The General Assembly of North Carolina enacts:
8 SECTION 1. G.S. 115C-12 is amended by adding the following new subdivisions
9 to read:
10 "(50) Duty to prohibit required posting of lesson plans or objectives. – The State
11 Board shall adopt rules prohibiting local school administrative units from
12 adopting policies that require teachers to post lesson plans or lesson
13 objectives. The State Board shall also not adopt rules requiring posting of
14 lesson plans or lesson objectives.
15 (51) Duty to prohibit schools from requiring teachers to serve additional duties
16 during lunch. – The State Board shall adopt rules prohibiting local school
17 administrative units or schools within local school administrative units from
18 imposing any duties on teachers during the teacher's allotted lunch time."
19 SECTION 2. G.S. 115C-47 is amended by adding a new subdivision to read:
20 "(70) To Provide Compensation for Teachers who Lead Professional Development.
21 – If a teacher employed by a local board of education provides professional
22 development to other teachers in the local school administrative unit, then the
23 local board of education shall compensate the teacher for the time spent
24 leading the professional development."
25 SECTION 3. This act is effective when it becomes law and applies beginning with
26 the 2025-2026 school year.
