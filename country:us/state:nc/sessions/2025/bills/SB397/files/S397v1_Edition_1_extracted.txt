Section 1:
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF NORTH CAROLINA
SESSION 2025
S 1
Short Title: Invest in Durham's Ed/Athletic Infrastructure. (Public)
Sponsors: Senators Chitlik and Murdock (Primary Sponsors).
Referred to: Rules and Operations of the Senate
March 25, 2025
1 A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
2 AN ACT TO ENACT THE INVESTING IN DURHAM'S EDUCATION AND ATHLETIC
3 (IDEA) INFRASTRUCTURE ACT.
4 The General Assembly of North Carolina enacts:
5 SECTION 1. There is appropriated from the General Fund to Durham County the
6 nonrecurring sum of four million five hundred thousand dollars ($4,500,000) for the 2025-2026
7 fiscal year to be used for capital improvements to, and repairs and renovations needs due to
8 deferred maintenance on, (i) public school buildings and athletic fields and (ii) other public
9 school facilities.
10 SECTION 2. This act becomes effective July 1, 2025.
*S397-v-1*
