GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF NORTH CAROLINA
SESSION 2025
S 1
SENATE BILL 486
Short Title: Funds for NC Senior Tar Heel Legislature. (Public)
Sponsors: Senator Murdock (Primary Sponsor).
Referred to: Rules and Operations of the Senate
March 26, 2025
1 A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
2 AN ACT TO APPROPRIATE FUNDING TO SUPPORT THE NORTH CAROLINA SENIOR
3 TAR HEEL LEGISLATURE.
4 The General Assembly of North Carolina enacts:
5 SECTION 1. There is appropriated from the General Fund to the Department of
6 Health and Human Services, Division of Aging, the sum of thirty thousand dollars ($30,000) in
7 recurring funds for each year of the 2025-2027 fiscal biennium to provide support for the North
8 Carolina Senior Tar Heel Legislature.
9 SECTION 2. This act becomes effective July 1, 2025.
