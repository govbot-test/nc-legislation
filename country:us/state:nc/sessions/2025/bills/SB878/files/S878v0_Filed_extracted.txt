Section 1:
FILED SENATE
Apr 28, 2026
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF NORTH CAROLINA
S.B. 878
SESSION 2025
PRINCIPAL CLERK
S D
Short Title: Investment in Pinetops' Police Department. (Public)
Sponsors: Senator Smith (Primary Sponsor).
Referred to:
1 A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
2 AN ACT TO APPROPRIATE FUNDS TO THE TOWN OF PINETOPS TO SUPPORT THE
3 EQUIPMENT AND STAFFING NEEDS OF THE TOWN'S POLICE DEPARTMENT.
4 The General Assembly of North Carolina enacts:
5 SECTION 1. There is appropriated from the General Fund to the Office of State
6 Budget and Management the sum of one hundred fifty thousand dollars ($150,000) in recurring
7 funds beginning in the 2026-2027 fiscal year to provide a directed grant to the Town of Pinetops
8 to support the staffing needs of the Town's police department.
9 SECTION 2. There is appropriated from the General Fund to the Office of State
10 Budget and Management the sum of one hundred thousand dollars ($100,000) in nonrecurring
11 funds for the 2026-2027 fiscal year to provide a directed grant to the Town of Pinetops for
12 equipment needs of the Town's police department.
13 SECTION 3. This act becomes effective July 1, 2026.
*DRS35384-MCa-297*
