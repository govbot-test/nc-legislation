Section 1:
H.B. 232
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF NORTH CAROLINA
Feb 26, 2025
SESSION 2025
HOUSE PRINCIPAL CLERK
H D
Short Title: Funds/Short-Line Railroad Disaster Assistance. (Public)
Sponsors: Representative Pless.
Referred to:
1 A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
2 AN ACT TO APPROPRIATE FUNDS FOR THE REPAIR AND RECONSTRUCTION OF
3 SHORT-LINE RAILROADS IMPACTED BY HURRICANE HELENE.
4 The General Assembly of North Carolina enacts:
5 SECTION 1.(a) The State Controller shall transfer the sum of sixteen million three
6 hundred ninety-two thousand one hundred seventy-six dollars ($16,392,176) in nonrecurring
7 funds for the 2025-2026 fiscal year from funds available in the State Emergency Response and
8 Disaster Relief Reserve to the Department of Transportation's Rail Division (Division) for the
9 Freight Rail & Rail Safety Improvement Fund (Rail Fund) established by G.S. 124-5.1, and those
10 funds are appropriated from the Rail Fund for the purposes set forth in subsection (b) of this
11 section.
12 SECTION 1.(b) The Division shall use the funds transferred and appropriated
13 pursuant to this section to provide grants for recovery and repair of the following short-line
14 railroads damaged by Hurricane Helene:
15 (1) Fourteen million dollars ($14,000,000) to the Blue Ridge Southern Railroad,
16 LLC, for line restoration.
17 (2) Two million three hundred ninety-two thousand one hundred seventy-six
18 dollars ($2,392,176) for track rehabilitation, facility repairs, and revenue loss
19 to the Great Smoky Mountains Railroad, LLC.
20 SECTION 2. This act becomes effective July 1, 2025.
