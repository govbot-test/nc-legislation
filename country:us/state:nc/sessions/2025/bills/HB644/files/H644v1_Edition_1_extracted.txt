Section 1:
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF NORTH CAROLINA
SESSION 2025
H 1
Short Title: DOT Study on Fort Bragg Traffic Congestion. (Public)
Sponsors: Representatives F. Jackson, Pike, Colvin, and Wheatley (Primary Sponsors).
For a complete list of sponsors, refer to the North Carolina General Assembly web site.
Referred to: Appropriations, if favorable, Rules, Calendar, and Operations of the House
April 2, 2025
1 A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
2 AN ACT TO REQUIRE THE DEPARTMENT OF TRANSPORTATION TO PERFORM A
3 STUDY ON REDUCING TRAFFIC CONGESTION NEAR FORT BRAGG.
4 The General Assembly of North Carolina enacts:
5 SECTION 1. There is appropriated from the Highway Fund to the Department of
6 Transportation for the 2025-2026 fiscal year the sum of five hundred thousand dollars ($500,000)
7 in nonrecurring funds to be used by the Department to study and identify the economic benefits
8 from reducing the traffic congestion on the portion of road between the intersection of Vass Road
9 and N.C. Highway 87 and the entrance to Fort Bragg. The study shall include examining
10 expanded rail routes and other surface transportation.
11 SECTION 2. This act becomes effective July 1, 2025.
*H644-v-1*
