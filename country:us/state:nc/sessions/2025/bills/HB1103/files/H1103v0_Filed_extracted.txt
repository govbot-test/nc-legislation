Section 1:
H.B. 1103
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF NORTH CAROLINA
Apr 29, 2026
SESSION 2025
HOUSE PRINCIPAL CLERK
H D
Short Title: Allow Implementation of Chatham Co. UDO. (Local)
Sponsors: Representative Reives.
Referred to:
1 A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
2 AN ACT TO ALLOW CHATHAM COUNTY TO IMPLEMENT AND ENFORCE THE
3 UNIFIED DEVELOPMENT ORDINANCE ENACTED ON NOVEMBER 18, 2024, AND
4 AN ACCOMPANYING ZONING MAP.
5 The General Assembly of North Carolina enacts:
6 SECTION 1.(a) Notwithstanding G.S. 160D-601 and Section 3K.1(c) of S.L.
7 2024-57, the Chatham County Board of Commissioners may implement and enforce all of the
8 following:
9 (1) The unified development ordinance adopted by the Chatham County Board of
10 Commissioners on November 18, 2024.
11 (2) A zoning map developed to accompany the unified development ordinance to
12 be adopted by the Chatham County Board of Commissioners before June 1,
13 2027.
14 SECTION 1.(b) The unified development ordinance and accompanying zoning map
15 shall be effective when approved by the Chatham County Board of Commissioners. The Board
16 of Commissioners shall give such approval on or before June 1, 2027.
17 SECTION 2. This act is effective when it becomes law.
*DRH40674-NR-29*
