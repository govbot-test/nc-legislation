1 A SENATE RESOLUTION RELATING TO THE APPOINTMENT, NOMINATION, AND
2 CONFIRMATION OF LESLIE COOLEY DISMUKES AS SECRETARY OF THE
3 DEPARTMENT OF ADULT CORRECTION.
4 Whereas, pursuant to G.S. 143B-9(a), the Governor shall notify the President of the
5 Senate of the name of each person to be nominated as head of a principal State department; and
6 Whereas, each nomination is subject to senatorial advice and consent in conformance
7 with Section 5(8) of Article III of the North Carolina Constitution; and
8 Whereas, the Governor has appointed Leslie Cooley Dismukes as Secretary of the
9 Department of Adult Correction pending further action by the Senate; Now, therefore,
10 Be it resolved by the Senate:
11 SECTION 1. That the Senate shall hereby consider whether to confirm Leslie
12 Cooley Dismukes as Secretary of the Department of Adult Correction.
13 SECTION 2. This resolution is effective upon adoption.
