HOUSE BILL DRH40055-LUe-34
Short Title: Reduce Early Voting Period. (Public)
Sponsors: Representative Gable.
Referred to:
1 A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
2 AN ACT TO REDUCE THE EARLY VOTING PERIOD TO THE SECOND MONDAY
3 BEFORE THE ELECTION.
4 The General Assembly of North Carolina enacts:
5 SECTION 1. G.S. 164-166.40(b) reads as rewritten:
6 "(b) Not earlier than the third Thursday second Monday before an election in which a voter
7 seeks to vote and not later than 3:00 P.M. on the last Saturday before that election, the voter may
8 appear in person only at the office of the county board of elections, except as provided in
9 G.S. 163-166.35. A county board of elections shall conduct early voting on the last Saturday
10 before the election from 8:00 A.M. until 3:00 P.M."
11 SECTION 2. This act is effective when it becomes law and applies to elections held
12 on or after that date.
