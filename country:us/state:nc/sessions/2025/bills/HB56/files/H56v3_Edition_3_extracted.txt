Section 1:
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF NORTH CAROLINA
SESSION 2025
H 3
Committee Substitute Favorable 3/18/25
Senate Appropriations/Base Budget Committee Substitute Adopted 9/22/25
Short Title: UNC Enrollment. (Public)
Sponsors:
Referred to:
February 5, 2025
1 A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
2 AN ACT TO APPROPRIATE FUNDS TO CONSTITUENT INSTITUTIONS OF THE
3 UNIVERSITY OF NORTH CAROLINA TO ACCOUNT FOR INCREASED
4 ENROLLMENT.
5 The General Assembly of North Carolina enacts:
6
7 UNC ENROLLMENT ADJUSTMENT
8 SECTION 1. There is appropriated from the General Fund to the Board of Governors
9 of The University of North Carolina the sum of forty-six million three hundred seventy-five
10 thousand five hundred eight dollars ($46,375,508) in recurring funds for each year of the
11 2025-2027 fiscal biennium to be allocated to the constituent institutions of The University of
12 North Carolina to account for increased enrollment at those institutions.
13
14 STATE BUDGET ACT APPLICABILITY
15 SECTION 2. If any provision of this act and G.S. 143C-5-4 are in conflict, the
16 provisions of this act shall prevail. The appropriations and the authorizations to allocate and
17 spend funds which are set out in this act shall remain in effect until the Current Operations
18 Appropriations Act for the applicable fiscal year becomes law, at which time that act shall
19 become effective and shall govern appropriations and expenditures. When the Current
20 Operations Appropriations Act for that fiscal year becomes law, the Director of the Budget shall
21 adjust allotments to give effect to that act from July 1 of the fiscal year.
22
23 EFFECTIVE DATE
24 SECTION 3. Except as otherwise provided, this act is effective retroactively to July
25 1, 2025.
