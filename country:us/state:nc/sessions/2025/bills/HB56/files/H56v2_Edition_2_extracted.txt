7 "§ 115C-320. Certain records open to inspection.Publication and inspection of certain

11 (1) Name.
12 (2) Age.
13 (3) Date of original employment or appointment.
14 (4) The terms of any contract by which the employee is employed whether written
15 or oral, past and current, to the extent that the board has the written contract
16 or a record of the oral contract in its possession.
17 (5) Current position.
18 (6) Title.
19 (7) Current salary.total compensation, as defined in sub-subdivision a. of
20 subdivision (1) of subsection (b1) of this section.
21 (8) Date and amount of each increase or decrease in salary total compensation, as
22 defined in sub-subdivision a. of subdivision (1) of subsection (b1) of this
23 section, with that local board of education.
24 (9) Date and type of each promotion, demotion, transfer, suspension, separation,
25 or other change in position classification with that local board of education.
26 (10) Date and general description of the reasons for each promotion with that local
27 board of education.
28 (11) Date and type of each dismissal, suspension, or demotion for disciplinary
29 reasons taken by the local board of education. If the disciplinary action was a
30 dismissal, a copy of the written notice of the final decision of the local board
31 education setting forth the specific acts or omissions that are the basis of the
32 dismissal.
33 (12) The office or station to which the employee is currently assigned.

35 superintendents, assistant superintendents, associate superintendents, directors/coordinators,
36 supervisors, finance officers, all personnel categorized as central office employees by the
