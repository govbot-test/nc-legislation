Section 1:
H.B. 12
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF NORTH CAROLINA
Jan 29, 2025
SESSION 2025
HOUSE PRINCIPAL CLERK
H D
Short Title: Observe Standard Time All Year. (Public)
Sponsors: Representative Warren.
Referred to:
1 A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
2 AN ACT FOR THE STATE TO OBSERVE STANDARD TIME THE WHOLE YEAR.
3 The General Assembly of North Carolina enacts:
4 SECTION 1. Chapter 81A of the General Statutes is amended by adding a new
5 Article to read:
6 "Article 8.
7 "Standard Time.
8 "§ 81A-100. Designation of time.
9 In accordance with subsection (a) of Section 260a of the Uniform Time Act of 1966 (15
10 U.S.C. §§ 260-267) the State and its political subdivisions shall observe standard time at all times
11 throughout the year."
12 SECTION 2. This act becomes effective March 8, 2026.
