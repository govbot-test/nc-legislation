Section 1:
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF NORTH CAROLINA
SESSION 2025
S 1
Short Title: Funds for YMCA REACH and Robinhood. (Public)
Sponsors: Senator Lowe (Primary Sponsor).
Referred to: Rules and Operations of the Senate
April 30, 2026
1 A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
2 AN ACT TO APPROPRIATE FUNDS TO CERTAIN YMCA ENTITIES.
3 The General Assembly of North Carolina enacts:
4 SECTION 1. There is appropriated from the General Fund to the Office of State
5 Budget and Management the sum of five hundred thousand dollars ($500,000) in nonrecurring
6 funds for the 2026-2027 fiscal year to provide two equal directed grants for operations and
7 community programming support to the following entities:
8 (1) The YMCA location commonly referred to as YMCA REACH (Resources for
9 Economic Development, Academics and Community Health) Center at
10 Winston Lake.
11 (2) The YMCA location commonly referred to as the Robinhood Road Family
12 YMCA located in Winston-Salem.
13 SECTION 2. This act becomes effective July 1, 2026.
*S927-v-1*
