6 PART I. CLASSIFYING FOODS CONTAINING VACCINES AS DRUGS
7 SECTION 1. G.S. 106-121 reads as rewritten:
8 "§ 106-121. Definitions and general consideration.
9 For the purpose of this Article:
10 …
11 (6) The term "drug" means
12 a. Articles recognized in the official United States Pharmacopoeia,
13 official Homeopathic Pharmacopoeia of the United States, or official
14 National Formulary, or any supplement to any of them; and
15 b. Articles intended for use in the diagnosis, cure, mitigation, treatment
16 or prevention of disease in man or other animals; and
17 c. Articles (other than food) intended to affect the structure or any
18 function of the body of man or other animals; and
19 d. Articles intended for use as a component of any article specified in
20 paragraphs a, b or c; but does not include devices or their components,
21 parts, or accessories.
22 e. Food that contains a vaccine or vaccine material.
23 …
24 (14g) The term "vaccine or vaccine material" means a substance intended to
25 stimulate the production of antibodies and provide immunity against disease
26 that is (i) prepared from the causative agent of a disease, its products, or a
27 synthetic substitute to act as an antigen without inducing the disease and (ii)
28 authorized or approved by the United States Food and Drug Administration
29 for use in humans.
30 …."
31
32 PART II. INCREASING REGULATION AGAINST HARMFUL ADDITIVES TO FOOD
33 SECTION 2.(a) Article 12 of Chapter 106 of the General Statutes is amended by
34 adding a new section to read:
35 "§ 106-129.1. Prohibited food additives.
*DRH10210-NM-14*

General Assembly Of North Carolina Session 2025
1 A person shall not manufacture, sell, distribute, hold, or offer for sale in commerce a food
2 product for human consumption that contains any of the following substances:
3 (1) Brominated vegetable oil (Chemical Abstract Services number 8016-94-2).
4 (2) Potassium bromate (Chemical Abstract Services number 7758-01-2).
5 (3) Propylparaben (Chemical Abstract Services number 94-13-3).
6 (4) Red 40 (Chemical Abstract Services number 25956-17-6).
7 (5) Yellow 5 (Chemical Abstract Services number 1934-21-0).
8 (6) Yellow 6 (Chemical Abstract Services number 2783-94-0).
9 (7) Blue 1 (Chemical Abstract Services number 68921-42-6).
10 (8) Blue 2 (Chemical Abstract Services number 860-22-0).
11 (9) Green 3 (Chemical Abstract Services number 1342-22-9)."
12 SECTION 2.(b) G.S. 106-124.1 is amended by adding a new subsection to read:
13 "(a1) A person who violates G.S. 106-129.1 shall be liable for a civil penalty not to exceed
14 five thousand dollars ($5,000) for a first violation and not to exceed ten thousand dollars
15 ($10,000) for each subsequent violation. The remedies under this subsection are in addition to,
16 and not in lieu of, any other remedies available under any other provision of law."
17 SECTION 2.(c) This section becomes effective January 1, 2027.
