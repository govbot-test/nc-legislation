GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF NORTH CAROLINA
SESSION 2025
RATIFIED BILL
RESOLUTION 2025-1
SENATE JOINT RESOLUTION 2
A JOINT RESOLUTION ADJOURNING THE 2025 REGULAR SESSION OF THE
GENERAL ASSEMBLY TO A DATE CERTAIN, AS PROVIDED BY LAW.
Whereas, G.S. 120-11.1 provides for the 2025 Regular Session of the General
Assembly to convene at 12:00 noon on Wednesday, January 8, 2025, and on that day meet solely
to elect officers, adopt rules, and otherwise organize the session and provides that, when they
adjourn that day, they stand adjourned until 12:00 noon on Wednesday, January 29, 2025; Now,
therefore,
Be it resolved by the Senate, the House of Representatives concurring:
SECTION 1. When the Senate and the House of Representatives adjourn on
Wednesday, January 8, 2025, they stand adjourned to reconvene on Wednesday, January 29,
2025, at 12:00 noon.
SECTION 2. This resolution is effective upon ratification.
In the General Assembly read three times and ratified this the 8th day of January, 2025.
s/ Ralph Hise
Presiding Officer of the Senate
s/ Destin Hall
Speaker of the House of Representatives
