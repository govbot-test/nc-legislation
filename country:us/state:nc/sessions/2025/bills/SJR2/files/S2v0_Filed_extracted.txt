1 A JOINT RESOLUTION ADJOURNING THE 2025 REGULAR SESSION OF THE
2 GENERAL ASSEMBLY TO A DATE CERTAIN, AS PROVIDED BY LAW.
3 Whereas, G.S. 120-11.1 provides for the 2025 Regular Session of the General
4 Assembly to convene at 12:00 noon on Wednesday, January 8, 2025, and on that day meet solely
5 to elect officers, adopt rules, and otherwise organize the session and provides that, when they
6 adjourn that day, they stand adjourned until 12:00 noon on Wednesday, January 29, 2025; Now,
7 therefore,
8 Be it resolved by the Senate, the House of Representatives concurring:
9 SECTION 1. When the Senate and the House of Representatives adjourn on
10 Wednesday, January 8, 2025, they stand adjourned to reconvene on Wednesday, January 29,
11 2025, at 12:00 noon.
12 SECTION 2. This resolution is effective upon ratification.
