SENATE BILL DRS45107-LGa-29
Short Title: Funds to Repair Orphan Roads in Durham County. (Public)
Sponsors: Senators Chitlik and Murdock (Primary Sponsors).
Referred to:
1 A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
2 AN ACT APPROPRIATING FUNDS FOR THE REPAIR AND IMPROVEMENT OF
3 VARIOUS ORPHAN ROADS IN DURHAM COUNTY.
4 The General Assembly of North Carolina enacts:
5 SECTION 1. There is appropriated from the General Fund to the Office of State
6 Budget and Management the sum of five hundred thousand dollars ($500,000) in nonrecurring
7 funds for the 2025-2026 fiscal year to provide a directed grant to Durham County for the repair
8 and improvement of various orphan roads in non-municipal areas of the County, which would
9 allow the North Carolina Department of Transportation to accept these roads for State
10 maintenance.
11 SECTION 2. This act becomes effective July 1, 2025.
