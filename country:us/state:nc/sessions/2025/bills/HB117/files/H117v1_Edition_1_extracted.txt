Section 1:
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF NORTH CAROLINA
SESSION 2025
H 1
Short Title: Ocean Isle Beach/Sunset Beach/Parking Fees. (Local)
Sponsors: Representative Iler.
For a complete list of sponsors, refer to the North Carolina General Assembly web site.
Referred to: Housing and Development, if favorable, Finance, if favorable, Rules, Calendar,
and Operations of the House
February 17, 2025
1 A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
2 AN ACT ALLOWING THE TOWNS OF OCEAN ISLE BEACH AND SUNSET BEACH TO
3 USE PROCEEDS FROM ON-STREET PARKING IN THE SAME MANNER IN WHICH
4 PROCEEDS FROM OFF-STREET PARKING FACILITIES ARE USED.
5 The General Assembly of North Carolina enacts:
6 SECTION 1. Section 2(b) of S.L. 2021-46, as amended by S.L. 2022-38, reads as
7 rewritten:
8 "SECTION 2.(b) This section applies to the Towns of Holden Beach Beach, Ocean Isle
9 Beach, Sunset Beach, and Surf City only."
10 SECTION 2. This act is effective when it becomes law.
