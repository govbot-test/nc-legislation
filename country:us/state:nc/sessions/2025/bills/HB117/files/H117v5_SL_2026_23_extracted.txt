Section 1:
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF NORTH CAROLINA
SESSION 2025
SESSION LAW 2026-23
AN ACT ALLOWING THE TOWNS OF OCEAN ISLE BEACH AND TOPSAIL BEACH TO
USE PROCEEDS FROM ON-STREET PARKING IN THE SAME MANNER IN WHICH
PROCEEDS FROM OFF-STREET PARKING FACILITIES ARE USED.
The General Assembly of North Carolina enacts:

Section 2:
SECTION 1. Section 2(b) of S.L. 2021-46, as amended by S.L. 2022-38, reads as
rewritten:
"SECTION 2.(b) This section applies to the Towns of Holden Beach Beach, Ocean Isle
Beach, Topsail Beach, and Surf City only."

Section 3:
SECTION 2. This act is effective when it becomes law.
In the General Assembly read three times and ratified this the 25th day of June, 2026.
s/ Rachel Hunt
President of the Senate
s/ John R. Bell, IV
Presiding Officer of the House of Representatives
