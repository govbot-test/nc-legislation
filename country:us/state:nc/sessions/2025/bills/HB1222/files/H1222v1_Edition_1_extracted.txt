Section 1:
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF NORTH CAROLINA
SESSION 2025
H 1
Short Title: Record Development Approval Votes/Brunswick. (Local)
Sponsors: Representative Miller.
For a complete list of sponsors, refer to the North Carolina General Assembly web site.
Referred to: State and Local Government, if favorable, Rules, Calendar, and Operations of the
House
May 6, 2026
1 A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
2 AN ACT TO REQUIRE ALL DEVELOPMENT APPROVALS IN BRUNSWICK COUNTY
3 TO BE DECIDED BY ROLL CALL VOTE OF THE COUNTY BOARD OF
4 COMMISSIONERS.
5 The General Assembly of North Carolina enacts:
6 SECTION 1. S.L. 2019-99 is repealed.
7 SECTION 2. For the purposes of this act, the term "development approval" shall
8 have the same meaning as in G.S. 160D-102(13).
9 SECTION 3. Notwithstanding G.S. 160D-403 and G.S. 160D-705, the Brunswick
10 County Board of Commissioners shall make the final decision on all development approvals in
11 Brunswick County. The official record of all meetings where a development approval is decided
12 shall reflect how each member voted on each development approval before the Board.
13 SECTION 4. This act is effective when it becomes law.
*H1222-v-1*
