GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF NORTH CAROLINA
SESSION 2025
H 1
HOUSE BILL 201
Short Title: Route 25/70 Vehicle Trailer Restrictions. (Local)
Sponsors: Representative Pless.
For a complete list of sponsors, refer to the North Carolina General Assembly web site.
Referred to: Transportation, if favorable, Rules, Calendar, and Operations of the House
February 26, 2025
1 A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
2 AN ACT TO RESTRICT MOTOR VEHICLE TRAILER COMBINATIONS ON U.S. ROUTE
3 25/70 IN MADISON COUNTY.
4 The General Assembly of North Carolina enacts:
5 SECTION 1. A motor vehicle combination consisting of a trailer in excess of 30 feet
6 or a motor vehicle and trailer combination in excess of 48 total feet may not be operated on the
7 following designated route:
8 (1) The portion of U.S. Route 25/70 beginning in Madison County at the
9 intersection of N.C. Highway 213 and continuing northerly until the
10 intersection of U.S. Route 25/70 and N.C. Highway 208.
11 (2) The portion of U.S. Route 25/70 beginning at the intersection of N.C.
12 Highway 208 and continuing westerly to the Tennessee state line.
13 SECTION 2. The penalties for a violation of Section 1 of this act shall be the same
14 as those set forth in subsections (h) and (i) of G.S. 20-115.1.
15 SECTION 3. This act applies only to Madison County.
16 SECTION 4. This act becomes effective December 1, 2025, and applies to offenses
17 committed on or after that date.
