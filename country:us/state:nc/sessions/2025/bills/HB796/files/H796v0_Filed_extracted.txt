H.B. 796
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF NORTH CAROLINA
Apr 7, 2025
SESSION 2025
HOUSE PRINCIPAL CLERK
H D
Short Title: The Student Mental Health Line Awareness Act. (Public)
Sponsors: Representative Buansi.
Referred to:
1 A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
2 AN ACT TO REQUIRE SUICIDE SAFETY NUMBERS ON STUDENT IDENTIFICATION
3 CARDS IN PUBLIC SCHOOLS SERVING STUDENTS IN GRADES SIX THROUGH
4 TWELVE.
5 The General Assembly of North Carolina enacts:
6 SECTION 1.(a) G.S. 115C-47 is amended by adding a new subdivision to read:
7 "(70) To Print Safety Numbers on Student Identification Cards (Student IDs). – A
8 local board of education shall ensure that any new student ID issued to a
9 student in grade six through 12 shall have printed in a conspicuous location
10 on the ID the following text and phone numbers:
11 a. Call or text the Lifeline at 988, available 24 hours a day.
12 b. The Crisis Text line can be accessed by texting HOME to 741741.
13 c. The telephone number for the NC Statewide Warmline is
14 1-855-733-7762.
15 The local board of education shall verify that the numbers being printed on
16 student IDs are accurate and current on an annual basis and shall use updated
17 contact information for the resources listed in this section if found. Nothing in
18 this subdivision shall be construed to require the issuance of student IDs."
19 SECTION 1.(b) G.S. 115C-150.12C is amended by adding a new subdivision to
20 read:
21 "(37) To print safety numbers on student identification cards (student IDs). – The
22 board of trustees shall ensure that any new student ID issued to a student in
23 grade six through 12 shall have printed in a conspicuous location on the ID
24 the following text and phone numbers:
25 a. Call or text the Lifeline at 988, available 24 hours a day.
26 b. The Crisis Text line can be accessed by texting HOME to 741741.
27 c. The telephone number for the NC Statewide Warmline is
28 1-855-733-7762.
29 The board shall verify that the numbers being printed on student IDs are
30 accurate and current on an annual basis and shall use updated contact
31 information for the resources listed in this section if found. Nothing in this
32 subdivision shall be construed to require the issuance of student IDs."
33 SECTION 1.(c) G.S. 115C-218.75 is amended by adding a new subsection to read:
34 "(p) To Print Safety Numbers on Student Identification Cards (Student IDs). – A charter
35 school shall ensure that any new student ID issued to a student in grade six through 12 shall have
36 printed in a conspicuous location on the ID the following text and phone numbers:

33 subdivision shall be construed to require the issuance of student IDs."
34 SECTION 1.(f) Part 4 of Article 39 of Chapter 115C of the General Statutes is
35 amended by adding a new section to read:
36 "§ 115C-566.5. To provide safety numbers on student identification cards.
37 To Print Safety Numbers on Student Identification Cards (Student IDs). – Nonpublic schools
38 operating pursuant to Part 1 or Part 2 of this Article are encouraged to include on any student ID
39 issued to a student in grade six through 12 in a conspicuous location on the ID the following text
40 and phone numbers:
41 (1) Call or text the Lifeline at 988, available 24 hours a day.
42 (2) The Crisis Text line can be accessed by texting HOME to 741741.
43 (3) The telephone number for the NC Statewide Warmline is 1-855-733-7762.
44 The school is encouraged to verify that the numbers being printed on student IDs are accurate
45 and current on an annual basis and use updated contact information for the resources listed in this
46 section if found. Nothing in this section shall be construed to require the issuance of student IDs."
47 SECTION 2. This act is effective when it becomes law and applies beginning with
48 the 2025-2026 school year.
