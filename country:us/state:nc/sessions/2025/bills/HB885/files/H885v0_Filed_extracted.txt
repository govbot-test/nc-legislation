24 SECTION 3. Part 10B of Article 3 of Chapter 143B of the General Statutes is
25 amended by adding a new section to read:
26 "§ 143B-168.10L. Disciplinary actions in early child care settings.
27 All early child care providers, including public school units, as defined in G.S. 115C-5, and
28 private schools offering prekindergarten services in this State, shall annually report to the
29 Department of Public Instruction detailed data on suspensions and expulsions. The data shall
30 include (i) the number of suspension or expulsion actions taken, (ii) the reasons for the suspension
31 or expulsion, (iii) the duration of the suspension or expulsion, and (iv) the demographic
32 information of the students suspended or expelled, appropriately redacted to adhere to applicable
33 privacy laws. The Department of Public Instruction shall analyze the data collected pursuant to
34 this section to identify patterns and inform strategies for minimizing suspensions and expulsions,
35 while emphasizing non-exclusionary disciplinary practices. The Department of Public
36 Instruction shall issue any specific reporting guidelines and formats on data collected under this
37 section, consistent with any federal law and data the Department of Public Instruction may
38 currently collect on disciplinary actions in early child care settings."
