1 A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
2 AN ACT REMOVING CERTAIN DESCRIBED PROPERTY FROM THE CORPORATE
3 LIMITS OF THE CITY OF SOUTHPORT, REMOVING CERTAIN DESCRIBED
4 PROPERTY FROM THE CORPORATE LIMITS AND THE EXTRATERRITORIAL
5 JURISDICTION OF THE TOWN OF YADKINVILLE, AND REMOVING CERTAIN
6 DESCRIBED PROPERTY FROM THE CORPORATE LIMITS OF THE CITY OF
7 KANNAPOLIS.
8 The General Assembly of North Carolina enacts:
9 SECTION 1.(a) The following described property, identified by Brunswick County
10 Tax Office Parcel Number, is removed from the corporate limits of the City of Southport:
11 221MB015, 221MB020, and 221MB02001.
12 SECTION 1.(b) This section has no effect upon the validity of any liens of the City
13 of Southport for ad valorem taxes or special assessments outstanding before the effective date of
14 this section. Such liens may be collected or foreclosed upon after the effective date of this section
15 as though the property were still within the corporate limits of the City of Southport.
16 SECTION 2.(a) The following described property, identified by Yadkin County Tax
17 Property Identification Number, is removed from the corporate limits and the extraterritorial
18 jurisdiction of the Town of Yadkinville: 5816103271.
19 SECTION 2.(b) This section has no effect upon the validity of any liens of the Town
20 of Yadkinville for ad valorem taxes or special assessments outstanding before the effective date
21 of this section. Such liens may be collected or foreclosed upon after the effective date of this
22 section as though the property were still within the corporate limits of the Town of Yadkinville.
23 SECTION 3.(a) The following described properties, identified by Cabarrus County
24 Tax Property Identification Numbers, are removed from the corporate limits of the City of
25 Kannapolis: 46928542120000 and 46927593330000.
26 SECTION 3.(b) This section has no effect upon the validity of any liens of the City
27 of Kannapolis for ad valorem taxes or special assessments outstanding before the effective date
28 of this section. Such liens may be collected or foreclosed upon after the effective date of this
29 section as though the property were still within the corporate limits of the City of Kannapolis.
30 SECTION 4. Except as otherwise provided, this act is effective when it becomes
31 law.
