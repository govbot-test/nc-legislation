1 A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
2 AN ACT REMOVING CERTAIN DESCRIBED PROPERTY FROM THE CORPORATE
3 LIMITS OF THE TOWN OF FOUR OAKS, THE CITY OF CREEDMOOR, AND THE
4 CITY OF KANNAPOLIS.
5 The General Assembly of North Carolina enacts:
6 SECTION 1.(a) The following described property is removed from the corporate
7 limits of the Town of Four Oaks:
8 TRACT I:
9 Being all of Area 1, containing 11.609 acres, more or less, as shown on that map entitled
10 "Estate Division by Will Plat for Bill Ray Price Estate" recorded in Map Book 95, Page 204 of
11 the Johnston County Registry.
12 TRACT II:
13 Being all of that certain tract or parcel of land described as "Remaining Combined Area,"
14 containing 14.432 acres, more or less, as shown on that map entitled "Minor Subdivision Plat for
15 Pamela Rae Price" recorded in Map Book 96, Page 278 of the Johnston County Registry.
16 SECTION 1.(b) This section has no effect upon the validity of any liens of the Town
17 of Four Oaks for ad valorem taxes or special assessments outstanding before the effective date
18 of this section. Such liens may be collected or foreclosed upon after the effective date of this
19 section as though the property were still within the corporate limits of the Town of Four Oaks.
20 SECTION 1.(c) This section becomes effective June 30, 2025. Property in the
21 territory described in Section 1(a) of this act as of January 1, 2025, is no longer subject to
22 municipal taxes for taxes imposed for taxable years beginning on or after July 1, 2025.
23 SECTION 2.(a) The following described property, identified by Granville County
24 Tax Property Identification Number, is removed from the corporate limits of the City of
25 Creedmoor: 180603417847.
26 SECTION 2.(b) This section has no effect upon the validity of any liens of the City
27 of Creedmoor for ad valorem taxes or special assessments outstanding before the effective date
28 of this section. Such liens may be collected or foreclosed upon after the effective date of this
29 section as though the property were still within the corporate limits of the City of Creedmoor.
30 SECTION 2.(c) This section becomes effective June 30, 2025. Property in the
31 territory described in Section 2(a) of this act as of January 1, 2025, is no longer subject to
32 municipal taxes for taxes imposed for taxable years beginning on or after July 1, 2025.
33 SECTION 3.(a) The following described properties, identified by Cabarrus County
34 Tax Property Identification Numbers, are removed from the corporate limits of the City of
35 Kannapolis: 46928542120000 and 46927593330000.
