GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF NORTH CAROLINA
SESSION 2025
SESSION LAW 2026-2
AN ACT REMOVING CERTAIN DESCRIBED PROPERTY FROM THE CORPORATE
LIMITS OF THE CITY OF SOUTHPORT, REMOVING CERTAIN DESCRIBED
PROPERTY FROM THE CORPORATE LIMITS AND THE EXTRATERRITORIAL
JURISDICTION OF THE TOWN OF YADKINVILLE, REMOVING CERTAIN
DESCRIBED PROPERTY FROM THE CORPORATE LIMITS OF THE CITY OF
KANNAPOLIS, REMOVING CERTAIN DESCRIBED PROPERTY FROM THE
EXTRATERRITORIAL JURISDICTION OF THE TOWN OF SUNSET BEACH, AND
REMOVING THE CAP ON SATELLITE ANNEXATIONS FOR THE TOWN OF EAST
SPENCER.
The General Assembly of North Carolina enacts:
PART I. CITY OF SOUTHPORT DEANNEXATION

Section 2:
SECTION 1.(a) The following described property, identified by Brunswick County
Tax Office Parcel Number, is removed from the corporate limits of the City of Southport:
221MB015, 221MB020, and 221MB02001.

Section 3:
SECTION 1.(b) This section has no effect upon the validity of any liens of the City
of Southport for ad valorem taxes or special assessments outstanding before the effective date of
this section. Such liens may be collected or foreclosed upon after the effective date of this section
as though the property were still within the corporate limits of the City of Southport.

Section 4:
SECTION 1.(c) This section becomes effective June 30, 2026. Property in the
territory described in subsection (a) of this section as of January 1, 2026, is no longer subject to
municipal taxes for taxes imposed for taxable years beginning on or after July 1, 2026.
PART II. TOWN OF YADKINVILLE DEANNEXATION/ETJ

Section 5:
SECTION 2.(a) The following described property, identified by Yadkin County Tax
Property Identification Number, is removed from the corporate limits and the extraterritorial
jurisdiction of the Town of Yadkinville: 5816103271.

Section 6:
SECTION 2.(b) This section has no effect upon the validity of any liens of the Town
of Yadkinville for ad valorem taxes or special assessments outstanding before the effective date
of this section. Such liens may be collected or foreclosed upon after the effective date of this

G.S. 160D-202(h).
