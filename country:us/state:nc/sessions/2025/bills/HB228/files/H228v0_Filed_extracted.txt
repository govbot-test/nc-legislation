Section 1:
H.B. 228
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF NORTH CAROLINA
Feb 26, 2025
SESSION 2025
HOUSE PRINCIPAL CLERK
H D
Short Title: City of Asheville/Funds for Study. (Public)
Sponsors: Representative Prather.
Referred to:
1 A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
2 AN ACT APPROPRIATING FUNDS TO THE CITY OF ASHEVILLE TO CONDUCT OR
3 FACILITATE A STUDY ON THE FEASIBILITY OF EXTENDING THE CITY'S WATER
4 SYSTEM TO THE NEW PISGAH VIEW STATE PARK.
5 The General Assembly of North Carolina enacts:
6 SECTION 1. There is appropriated from the General Fund to the City of Asheville
7 the sum of twenty-five thousand dollars ($25,000) in nonrecurring funds for the 2025-2026 fiscal
8 year for the purpose of conducting or facilitating a study on the feasibility and cost of extending
9 the City's water system several miles to serve the new Pisgah View State Park.
10 SECTION 2. This act becomes effective July 1, 2025.
*DRH40150-LMa-84*
