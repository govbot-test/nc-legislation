GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF NORTH CAROLINA
SESSION 2025
H 1
HOUSE BILL 447
Short Title: Funds for Kernersville Event Center. (Public)
Sponsors: Representative Lambeth.
For a complete list of sponsors, refer to the North Carolina General Assembly web site.
Referred to: Appropriations, if favorable, Rules, Calendar, and Operations of the House
March 20, 2025
1 A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
2 AN ACT TO APPROPRIATE FUNDS FOR THE KERNERSVILLE EVENT CENTER.
3 The General Assembly of North Carolina enacts:
4 SECTION 1. There is appropriated from the General Fund to the Office of State
5 Budget and Management the sum of thirty-four million dollars ($34,000,000) in nonrecurring
6 funds for the 2025-2026 fiscal year to provide a directed grant to the Town of Kernersville for
7 the Kernersville Event Center.
8 SECTION 2. This act becomes effective July 1, 2025.
