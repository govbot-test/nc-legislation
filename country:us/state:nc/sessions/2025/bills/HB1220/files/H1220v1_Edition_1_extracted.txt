GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF NORTH CAROLINA
SESSION 2025
H 1
Short Title: Amend Stedman Charter. (Local)
Sponsors: Representative Wheatley.
For a complete list of sponsors, refer to the North Carolina General Assembly web site.
Referred to: State and Local Government, if favorable, Rules, Calendar, and Operations of the
House
May 5, 2026
1 A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
2 AN ACT TO REPEAL A PROVISION OF THE CHARTER OF THE TOWN OF STEDMAN
3 CONCERNING SALE OF ALCOHOLIC BEVERAGES.
4 The General Assembly of North Carolina enacts:
5 SECTION 1. Section 8 of Chapter 67 of the Private Laws of 1913 is repealed.
6 SECTION 2. This act is effective when it becomes law.
