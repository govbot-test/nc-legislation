Section 1:
FILED SENATE
Mar 25, 2025
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF NORTH CAROLINA
S.B. 565
SESSION 2025
PRINCIPAL CLERK
S D
Short Title: Investment in Pinetops' Police Department. (Public)
Sponsors: Senator Smith (Primary Sponsor).
Referred to:
1 A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
2 AN ACT TO APPROPRIATE FUNDS TO THE TOWN OF PINETOPS TO SUPPORT THE
3 EQUIPMENT AND STAFFING NEEDS OF THE TOWN'S POLICE DEPARTMENT.
4 The General Assembly of North Carolina enacts:
5 SECTION 1. There is appropriated from the General Fund to the Office of State
6 Budget and Management the sum of one hundred fifty thousand dollars ($150,000) in recurring
7 funds for each year of the 2025-2027 fiscal biennium to provide a directed grant to the Town of
8 Pinetops to support the staffing needs of the Town's police department.
9 SECTION 2. There is appropriated from the General Fund to the Office of State
10 Budget and Management sum of one hundred thousand dollars ($100,000) in nonrecurring funds
11 for the 2025-2026 fiscal year to provide a directed grant to the Town of Pinetops for equipment
12 needs of the Town's Police Department.
13 SECTION 3. This act becomes effective July 1, 2025.
*DRS15261-LGa-65A*
