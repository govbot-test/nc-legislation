12 SECTION 3.24.(a) G.S. 135-5 is amended by adding the following new subsection
13 to read:
14 "(aaaa) After October 1, 2025, but on or before November 30, 2025, a one-time, cost-of-living
15 supplement payment shall be made to, or on account of, beneficiaries who are living as of October
16 1, 2025, and whose retirement commenced on or before October 1, 2025. The payment shall be
17 one percent (1%) of the beneficiary's annual retirement allowance payable as of October1, 2025,
18 and shall not be prorated for date of retirement commencement. If the beneficiary dies before the
19 payment is made, then the payment shall be payable to the member's legal representative. No
20 beneficiary shall have a vested right to any future supplemental payments under this Article.
21 (bbbb) After September 1, 2026, but on or before October 31, 2026, a one-time,
22 cost-of-living supplement payment shall be made to, or on account of, beneficiaries who are
23 living as of September 1, 2026, and whose retirement commenced on or before September 1,
24 2026. The payment shall be two percent (2%) of the beneficiary's annual retirement allowance
25 payable as of September 1, 2026, and shall not be prorated for date of retirement commencement.
26 If the beneficiary dies before the payment is made, then the payment shall be payable to the
27 member's legal representative. No beneficiary shall have a vested right to any future
28 supplemental payments under this Article."
29 SECTION 3.24.(b) G.S. 135-65 is amended by adding the following new subsection
30 to read:
31 "(ll) After October 1, 2025, but on or before November 30, 2025, a one-time, cost-of-living
32 supplement payment shall be made to, or on account of, beneficiaries who are living as of
