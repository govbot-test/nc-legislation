Section 1:
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF NORTH CAROLINA
SESSION 2025
S 3
Judiciary Committee Substitute Adopted 5/6/25
House Committee Substitute Favorable 6/10/25
Short Title: Allow Counties to Sign MOU. (Local)
Sponsors:
Referred to:
February 27, 2025
1 A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
2 AN ACT TO ALLOW THE MITCHELL COUNTY SHERIFF'S OFFICE AND THE YANCEY
3 COUNTY SHERIFF'S OFFICE TO ENTER INTO MEMORANDUMS OF
4 UNDERSTANDING WITH THE UNICOI COUNTY, TENNESSEE, SHERIFF'S OFFICE
5 TO PERFORM LAW ENFORCEMENT SPECIAL OPERATIONS MISSIONS AND
6 COOPERATIVE LAW ENFORCEMENT ACTIONS ACROSS STATE LINES.
7 The General Assembly of North Carolina enacts:
8 SECTION 1.(a) Notwithstanding any provision of law to the contrary, the Mitchell
9 County Sheriff's Office and the Yancey County Sheriff's Office may enter into memorandums of
10 understanding with the Unicoi County, Tennessee, Sheriff's Office to engage across state lines in
11 law enforcement special operations missions and cooperative law enforcement actions.
12 SECTION 1.(b) Any memorandum of understanding entered into pursuant to this
13 section shall specify the manner in which liability claims for damage to persons or property as a
14 result of law enforcement special operations missions and cooperative law enforcement actions
15 will be shared or assigned. While ensuring public safety, each memorandum of understanding
16 shall limit to the greatest extent possible the liability of Mitchell County, Yancey County, and
17 the State.
18 SECTION 2. No later than November 1 of each year, any memorandum of
19 understanding entered into that year pursuant to this act shall be reported to the Department of
20 Justice.
21 SECTION 3. This act is effective when it becomes law.
*S176-v-3*
