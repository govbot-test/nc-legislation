32 "§ 143-64.18B. Guaranteed energy savings contracts.
33 (a) A governmental unit may enter into a guaranteed energy savings contract with a
34 qualified provider if all of the following apply:
35 (1) The term of the contract does not exceed 20 years from the date of the
36 installation and acceptance by the governmental unit of the energy
37 conservation measures provided for under the contract.
38 (2) The governmental unit finds that the energy savings resulting from the
39 performance of the contract will equal or exceed the total cost of the contract.
40 (3) The energy conservation measures to be installed under the contract are for an
41 existing building or utility system, or utility consuming device or equipment
42 when the utility cost is paid by the governmental unit.
43 (b) Before entering into a guaranteed energy savings contract, the governmental unit shall
44 provide published notice of the time and place or of the meeting at which it proposes to award
45 the contract, the names of the parties to the proposed contract, and the contract's purpose. The
46 notice must be published at least 15 days before the date of the proposed award or meeting.

51 performance. Any bonds required by this subsection shall be subject to the provisions of Article
