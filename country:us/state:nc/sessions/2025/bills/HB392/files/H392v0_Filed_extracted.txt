Section 1:
H.B. 392
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF NORTH CAROLINA
Mar 13, 2025
SESSION 2025
HOUSE PRINCIPAL CLERK
H D
Short Title: Funds/Forsyth United Way/Summer Learning. (Public)
Sponsors: Representative Baker.
Referred to:
1 A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
2 AN ACT TO APPROPRIATE FUNDS FOR THE UNITED WAY OF FORSYTH COUNTY
3 TO FUND SUMMER LEARNING PROGRAMS.
4 The General Assembly of North Carolina enacts:
5 SECTION 1. There is appropriated to the Office of State Budget and Management
6 the sum of two hundred fifty thousand dollars ($250,000) in nonrecurring funds for the
7 2025-2026 fiscal year to provide a directed grant to The United Way of Forsyth County, Inc., a
8 nonprofit organization, to fund summer learning programs through various community agencies.
9 The United Way shall develop and issue a Request for Proposal (RFP) to contract with
10 community agencies that will be supporting summer learning programs, either as an extension of
11 services already offered or through vetted organizations that desire to offer summer learning
12 opportunities for students that will not be eligible for summer learning programs through the
13 local school system.
14 SECTION 2. This act becomes effective July 1, 2025.
*DRH30153-LGa-82*
