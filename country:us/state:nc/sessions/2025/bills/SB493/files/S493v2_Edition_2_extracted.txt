8 PART I. SCHOOLS A PERMITTED USE IN COMMERCIAL ZONES
9 SECTION 1.1. Part 1 of Article 9 of Chapter 160D of the General Statutes is
10 amended by adding a new section to read:
11 "§ 160D-917. Public school sites in commercial zones.
12 In areas zoned for commercial use, zoning regulations shall permit, by right or by special use,
13 the siting of a school building that is primarily used for the instruction of students and is under
14 the control of a public school unit as defined in G.S. 115C-5."
