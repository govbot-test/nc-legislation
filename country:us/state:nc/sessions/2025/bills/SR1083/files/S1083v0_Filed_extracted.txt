FILED SENATE
May 18, 2026
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF NORTH CAROLINA
S.R. 1083
SESSION 2025
PRINCIPAL CLERK
S D
SENATE SIMPLE RESOLUTION DRSR15369-BX-3
Sponsors: Senator Rabon (Primary Sponsor).
Referred
to:
2 CONFIRMATION OF DANIEL JOHNSON AS SECRETARY OF THE DEPARTMENT
3 OF TRANSPORTATION.
4 Whereas, pursuant to G.S. 143B-9(a), the Governor shall notify the President of the
5 Senate of the name of each person to be nominated as the head of a principal State department;
6 and
7 Whereas, each nomination is subject to senatorial advice and consent in conformance
8 with Section 5(8) of Article III of the North Carolina Constitution; and
9 Whereas, the Governor has appointed Daniel Johnson as Secretary of the Department
10 of Transportation pending further action by the Senate; Now, therefore,
11 Be it resolved by the Senate:
12 SECTION 1. That the Senate shall hereby consider whether to confirm Daniel
13 Johnson as Secretary of the Department of Transportation.
14 SECTION 2. This resolution is effective upon adoption.
*DRSR15369-BX-3*
