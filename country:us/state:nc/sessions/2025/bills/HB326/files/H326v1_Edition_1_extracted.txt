Section 1:
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF NORTH CAROLINA
SESSION 2025
H 1
Short Title: DPI to Study Increased Teacher Planning. (Public)
Sponsors: Representative F. Jackson.
For a complete list of sponsors, refer to the North Carolina General Assembly web site.
Referred to: Appropriations, if favorable, Rules, Calendar, and Operations of the House
March 10, 2025
1 A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
2 AN ACT TO REQUIRE THE DEPARTMENT OF PUBLIC INSTRUCTION TO STUDY
3 METHODS OF INCREASING TEACHER PLANNING TIME.
4 The General Assembly of North Carolina enacts:
5 SECTION 1.(a) The Department of Public Instruction shall study methods of
6 increasing teacher planning time or decreasing teacher obligations that decrease existing planning
7 time.
8 SECTION 1.(b) The Department shall report to the Joint Legislative Education
9 Oversight Committee by February 15, 2026, on the results of the study. The report shall include
10 at least the following:
11 (1) Recommendations to increase teacher planning time.
12 (2) Recommendations to decrease teacher obligations that decrease existing
13 planning time.
14 (3) Costs associated with any recommendations given in the report.
15 (4) Any other information the Department deems relevant.
16 SECTION 2. There is appropriated from the General Fund to the Department of
17 Public Instruction the sum of five thousand dollars ($5,000) in nonrecurring funds for the
18 2025-2026 fiscal year to conduct the study required by Section 1 of this act. The Department may
19 use these funds to partner with a third party to conduct the study.
20 SECTION 3. This act becomes effective July 1, 2025.
*H326-v-1*
