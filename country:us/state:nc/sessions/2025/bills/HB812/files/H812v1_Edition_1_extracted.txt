GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF NORTH CAROLINA
SESSION 2025
H 1
HOUSE BILL 812
Short Title: UNC-Pembroke Presidential Library Funds/DJT. (Public)
Sponsors: Representatives Lowery, B. Jones, and Moss (Primary Sponsors).
For a complete list of sponsors, refer to the North Carolina General Assembly web site.
Referred to: Appropriations, if favorable, Rules, Calendar, and Operations of the House
April 8, 2025
1 A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
2 AN ACT TO APPROPRIATE FUNDS FOR A PRESIDENTIAL LIBRARY IN HONOR OF
3 PRESIDENT DONALD JOHN TRUMP AT THE UNIVERSITY OF NORTH CAROLINA
4 AT PEMBROKE.
5 The General Assembly of North Carolina enacts:
6 SECTION 1. There is appropriated from the General Fund to the Board of Governors
7 of The University of North Carolina the sum of ten million dollars ($10,000,000) in nonrecurring
8 funds for the 2025-2026 fiscal year to be allocated to the University of North Carolina at
9 Pembroke (UNCP) to construct a presidential library in honor of President Donald John Trump.
10 UNCP may partner with the federal National Archives and Records Administration to the extent
11 necessary to complete the project.
12 SECTION 2. This act becomes effective July 1, 2025.
