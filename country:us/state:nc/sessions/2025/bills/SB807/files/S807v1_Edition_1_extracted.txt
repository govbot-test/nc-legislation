Section 1:
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF NORTH CAROLINA
SESSION 2025
S 1
Short Title: Restore Down-Zoning Auth./Woodfin. (Local)
Sponsors: Senator Mayfield (Primary Sponsor).
Referred to: Rules and Operations of the Senate
April 22, 2026
1 A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
2 AN ACT TO RESTORE THE AUTHORITY TO INITIATE DOWN-ZONING IN THE TOWN
3 OF WOODFIN.
4 The General Assembly of North Carolina enacts:
5 SECTION 1. G.S. 160D-601(d) reads as rewritten:
6 "(d) Down-Zoning. – No amendment to zoning regulations or a zoning map that
7 down-zones property shall be initiated, enacted, or enforced initiated nor is it enforceable without
8 the written consent of all property owners whose property is the subject of the down-zoning
9 amendment. amendment unless the down-zoning amendment is initiated by the local
10 government. For purposes of this section, "down-zoning" means a zoning ordinance that affects
11 an area of land in one of the following ways:
12 (1) By decreasing the development density of the land to be less dense than was
13 allowed under its previous usage.
14 (2) By reducing the permitted uses of the land that are specified in a zoning
15 ordinance or land development regulation to fewer uses than were allowed
16 under its previous usage.
17 (3) By creating any type of nonconformity on land not in a residential zoning
18 district, including a nonconforming use, nonconforming lot, nonconforming
19 structure, nonconforming improvement, or nonconforming site element."
20 SECTION 2.(a) This act applies only to the Town of Woodfin.
21 SECTION 2.(b) This act is effective when it becomes law and applies retroactively
22 to December 11, 2024. Any adopted ordinance affected by Section 3K.1 of S.L. 2024-57 shall
23 be in effect as it was on or before December 11, 2024.
