Section 1:
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF NORTH CAROLINA
SESSION 2025
S 1
Short Title: Funds for VSO in Jones County. (Public)
Sponsors: Senators Jackson and Lazzara (Primary Sponsors).
Referred to: Rules and Operations of the Senate
February 27, 2025
1 A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
2 AN ACT TO APPROPRIATE FUNDS FOR A PART-TIME VETERAN SERVICES OFFICER
3 IN JONES COUNTY.
4 The General Assembly of North Carolina enacts:
5 SECTION 1. There is appropriated from the General Fund to the Department of
6 Military and Veterans Affairs the sum of thirty thousand dollars ($30,000) in recurring funds for
7 each year of the 2025-2027 fiscal biennium to fund a part-time Veteran Services Officer position
8 in Jones County.
9 SECTION 2. This act becomes effective July 1, 2025.
*S183-v-1*
