GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF NORTH CAROLINA
SESSION 2025
H 1
HOUSE BILL 85
Short Title: Certain Prohibitions/Working/Voting Process. (Public)
Sponsors: Representatives Kidwell, Moss, Ward, and Wheatley (Primary Sponsors).
For a complete list of sponsors, refer to the North Carolina General Assembly web site.
Referred to: Election Law, if favorable, Judiciary 1, if favorable, Rules, Calendar, and
Operations of the House
February 11, 2025
1 A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
2 AN ACT TO PROHIBIT INDIVIDUALS FROM WORKING IN THE ELECTIONS PROCESS
3 FOR FAILURE TO EXERCISE DUE CARE AND DILIGENCE WHILE WORKING IN
4 ANY JOB RELATED TO THE ELECTION PROCESS.
5 The General Assembly of North Carolina enacts:
6 SECTION 1. Article 4 of Chapter 163 of the General Statutes is amended by adding
7 a new section to read:
8 "§ 163-33.4. Require exercise of due care and diligence during election process.
9 Notwithstanding any other provision of law to the contrary, an individual, whether paid or a
10 volunteer, who is found to have not exercised due care and diligence while performing
11 elections-related work during an elections process is prohibited from further work in any
12 elections process."
13 SECTION 2. This act is effective when it becomes law.
