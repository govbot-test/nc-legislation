Section 1:
H.B. 371
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF NORTH CAROLINA
Mar 11, 2025
SESSION 2025
HOUSE PRINCIPAL CLERK
H D
Short Title: DMV Materials in Additional Languages. (Public)
Sponsors: Representative Branson.
Referred to:
1 A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
2 AN ACT TO REQUIRE THE DIVISION OF MOTOR VEHICLES OF THE DEPARTMENT
3 OF TRANSPORTATION TO PROVIDE MATERIALS REQUIRED FOR LICENSURE IN
4 ADDITIONAL LANGUAGES.
5 The General Assembly of North Carolina enacts:
6 SECTION 1. G.S. 20-7 is amended by adding a new subsection to read:
7 "(c2) Provision of Materials in Additional Languages. – The Division shall provide an
8 option for applicants to (i) receive the driver license handbook prepared pursuant to G.S. 20-88.1
9 and (ii) take the written test required for licensure under this section in all of the following
10 languages:
11 (1) Khmer.
12 (2) Each language that is the primary language of at least two percent (2%) of the
13 State's population."
14 SECTION 2. This act becomes effective January 1, 2026.
*DRH30166-NE-91*
