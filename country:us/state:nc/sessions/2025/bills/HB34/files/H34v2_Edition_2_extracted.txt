GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF NORTH CAROLINA
SESSION 2025
H 2
HOUSE BILL 34
Committee Substitute Favorable 2/25/25
Short Title: Establish Larceny of Mail Offense. (Public)
Sponsors:
Referred to:
February 4, 2025
1 A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
2 AN ACT TO INCREASE THE PUNISHMENT FOR LARCENY OF MAIL.
3 The General Assembly of North Carolina enacts:
4 SECTION 1. G.S. 14-72 is amended by adding a new subsection to read:
5 "(c1) Notwithstanding the provision of subsection (a) of this section, where the larceny or
6 receiving or possession of stolen goods is of mail, the person shall be sentenced at one class level
7 higher than the principal offense for which the person was convicted. For the purposes of this
8 section, the term "mail" means a letter, package, bag, or other item of value sent or delivered to
9 another by any method of delivery, including through a common carrier, commercial delivery
10 service, or private delivery."
11 SECTION 2. This act becomes effective December 1, 2025, and applies to offenses
12 committed on or after that date.
