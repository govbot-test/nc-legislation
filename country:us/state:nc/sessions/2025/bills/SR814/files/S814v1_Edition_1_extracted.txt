GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF NORTH CAROLINA
SESSION 2025
S 1
SENATE RESOLUTION 814
Sponsors: Senator Rabon (Primary Sponsor).
Referred to: Rules and Operations of the Senate
April 22, 2026
1 A SENATE RESOLUTION RELATING TO THE APPOINTMENT, NOMINATION, AND
2 CONFIRMATION OF NATHANIEL DENNY AS THE SECRETARY OF THE
3 DEPARTMENT OF INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY.
4 Whereas, pursuant to G.S. 143B-9(a), the Governor shall notify the President of the
5 Senate of the name of each person to be nominated as head of a principal State department; and
6 Whereas, each nomination is subject to senatorial advice and consent in conformance
7 with Section 5(8) of Article III of the North Carolina Constitution; and
8 Whereas, the Governor has appointed Nathaniel Denny to serve as Secretary of the
9 Department of Information Technology pending further action by the Senate; Now, therefore,
10 Be it resolved by the Senate:
11 SECTION 1. That the Senate shall hereby consider whether to confirm Nathaniel
12 Denny as Secretary of the Department of Information Technology.
13 SECTION 2. This resolution is effective upon adoption.
