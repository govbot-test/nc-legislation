Section 1:
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF NORTH CAROLINA
SESSION 2025
RESOLUTION 2025-7
SENATE JOINT RESOLUTION 768
A JOINT RESOLUTION RELATING TO THE APPOINTMENT OF KENNETH L.
GOODMAN TO THE NORTH CAROLINA INDUSTRIAL COMMISSION.
Whereas, G.S. 97-77(a1) provides that appointments by the Governor to the North
Carolina Industrial Commission are subject to confirmation by the General Assembly; and
Whereas, the Governor wishes to reappoint Kenneth L. Goodman of Richmond
County to the North Carolina Industrial Commission; Now, therefore,
Be it resolved by the Senate, the House of Representatives concurring:

Section 2:
SECTION 1. The appointment of Kenneth L. Goodman of Richmond County to a
term on the North Carolina Industrial Commission, that began on May 1, 2025, and expires on
April 30, 2031, is confirmed.

Section 3:
SECTION 2. This resolution is effective upon ratification.
In the General Assembly read three times and ratified this the 30th day of June, 2025.
s/ Phil Berger
President Pro Tempore of the Senate
s/ Donna McDowell White
Presiding Officer of the House of Representatives

GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF NORTH CAROLINA
SESSION 2025
RATIFIED BILL
RESOLUTION 2025-7
SENATE JOINT RESOLUTION 768
A JOINT RESOLUTION RELATING TO THE APPOINTMENT OF KENNETH L.
GOODMAN TO THE NORTH CAROLINA INDUSTRIAL COMMISSION.
Whereas, G.S. 97-77(a1) provides that appointments by the Governor to the North
Carolina Industrial Commission are subject to confirmation by the General Assembly; and
Whereas, the Governor wishes to reappoint Kenneth L. Goodman of Richmond
County to the North Carolina Industrial Commission; Now, therefore,
Be it resolved by the Senate, the House of Representatives concurring:
SECTION 1. The appointment of Kenneth L. Goodman of Richmond County to a
term on the North Carolina Industrial Commission, that began on May 1, 2025, and expires on
April 30, 2031, is confirmed.
SECTION 2. This resolution is effective upon ratification.
In the General Assembly read three times and ratified this the 30th day of June, 2025.
s/ Phil Berger
President Pro Tempore of the Senate
s/ Donna McDowell White
Presiding Officer of the House of Representatives
