Section 1:
FILED SENATE
Apr 30, 2026
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF NORTH CAROLINA
S.B. 1058
SESSION 2025
PRINCIPAL CLERK
S D
Short Title: Critical Funds for Mecklenburg County. (Public)
Sponsors: Senators Mohammed and Salvador (Primary Sponsors).
Referred to:
1 A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
2 AN ACT TO PROVIDE CRITICAL FUNDS FOR MECKLENBURG COUNTY.
3 The General Assembly of North Carolina enacts:
4 SECTION 1. There is appropriated from the General Fund to the Office of State
5 Budget and Management the sum of five million dollars ($5,000,000) in nonrecurring funds for
6 the 2026-2027 fiscal year to provide a directed grant to the Charlotte Mecklenburg Library
7 Foundation, a nonprofit organization, for the construction of the new main library.
8 SECTION 2. There is appropriated from the General Fund to the Office of State
9 Budget and Management the sum of five hundred thousand dollars ($500,000) in nonrecurring
10 funds for the 2026-2027 fiscal year to provide a directed grant to The C.W. Williams Community
11 Health Center, Inc., a nonprofit organization, to assist in the completion of its full-service
12 community health center located in Mecklenburg County.
13 SECTION 3. There is appropriated from the Highway Fund to the Department of
14 Transportation the sum of nine million five hundred thousand dollars ($9,500,000) in
15 nonrecurring funds for the 2026-2027 fiscal year, to be used for the planning and design of the
16 Westmoreland Road Interchange (Interstate 77 Exit 27) in the Town of Cornelius.
17 SECTION 4. There is appropriated from the General Fund to the Office of State
18 Budget and Management the sum of seven million five hundred thousand dollars ($7,500,000)
19 in nonrecurring funds for the 2026-2027 fiscal year to be used to provide directed grants as
20 follows:
21 (1) Five million dollars ($5,000,000) to the Habitat for Humanity of the Charlotte
22 Region, Inc., a nonprofit corporation, for costs associated with providing
23 affordable housing in the Charlotte region.
24 (2) Two million five hundred thousand dollars ($2,500,000) to the City of
25 Charlotte for operational expenses and other costs of The Umbrella Center in
26 Charlotte.
27 SECTION 5. There is appropriated from the General Fund to the Office of State
28 Budget and Management the sum of six million five hundred thousand dollars ($6,500,000) in
29 nonrecurring funds for the 2026-2027 fiscal year to provide a directed grant to The Discovery
30 Place-Charlotte Nature Museum, a nonprofit organization, for capital improvements to the
31 museum facilities.
32 SECTION 6. This act becomes effective July 1, 2026.
