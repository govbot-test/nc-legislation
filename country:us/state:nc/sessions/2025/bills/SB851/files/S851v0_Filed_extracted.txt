SENATE BILL DRS45452-NEf-120
Short Title: America's Semiquincentennial License Plate. (Public)
Sponsors: Senators Berger, Rabon, and Alexander (Primary Sponsors).
Referred to:
1 A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
2 AN ACT TO AUTHORIZE THE DIVISION OF MOTOR VEHICLES TO PRODUCE AN
3 AMERICA'S SEMIQUINCENTENNIAL SPECIAL REGISTRATION PLATE.
4 The General Assembly of North Carolina enacts:
5 SECTION 1. G.S. 20-79.4(b) reads as rewritten:
6 "(b) Types. – The Division shall issue the following types of special registration plates:
7 …
8 () America's Semiquincentennial. – Issuable to a registered owner of a motor
9 vehicle in accordance with G.S. 20-81.12. The plate shall bear the phrase
10 "America 250" and other imagery commemorating the semiquincentennial.
11 …."
12 SECTION 2. The Revisor of Statutes is authorized to alphabetize, number, and
13 renumber the special registration plates listed in G.S. 20-79.4(b) to ensure that all of the special
14 registration plates are listed in alphabetical order and numbered accordingly.
15 SECTION 3. This act becomes effective July 1, 2026.
