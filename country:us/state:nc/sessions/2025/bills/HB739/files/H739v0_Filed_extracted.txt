Section 1:
H.B. 739
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF NORTH CAROLINA
Apr 2, 2025
SESSION 2025
HOUSE PRINCIPAL CLERK
H D
Short Title: Funds for Wilson & Nash Counties. (Public)
Sponsors: Representative Pittman.
Referred to:
1 A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
2 AN ACT TO APPROPRIATE FUNDS FOR VARIOUS PUBLIC PURPOSES IN WILSON
3 AND NASH COUNTIES.
4 The General Assembly of North Carolina enacts:
5 SECTION 1. There is appropriated from the General Fund to the Office of State
6 Budget and Management the sum of sixty-one million three hundred thousand dollars
7 ($61,300,000) in nonrecurring funds for the 2025-2026 fiscal year to provide directed grants as
8 follows:
9 (1) Two million five hundred thousand dollars ($2,500,000) to the Town of
10 Lucama for water infrastructure.
11 (2) Four million five hundred thousand dollars ($4,500,000) to the Town of
12 Stantonsburg for water and sewer projects.
13 (3) Five million dollars ($5,000,000) to Wilson County for allocation to the
14 County's volunteer fire departments.
15 (4) Fifteen million dollars ($15,000,000) to Wilson County for school
16 construction.
17 (5) Twenty million dollars ($20,000,000) to Wilson County for the construction
18 of a new courthouse.
19 (6) Eight hundred thousand dollars ($800,000) to Wilson County for the Sheriff's
20 Office Training Center.
21 (7) One million dollars ($1,000,000) to Wilson County to provide Narcan for the
22 Wilson County schools and Wilson County libraries.
23 (8) Eight million dollars ($8,000,000) to the Wilson Housing Authority.
24 (9) Three million dollars ($3,000,000) to Wilson County for the Wilson Ride
25 Program.
26 (10) One million five hundred thousand dollars ($1,500,000) to the Town of
27 Sharpsburg for the construction of a new Police Department.
28 SECTION 2. This act becomes effective July 1, 2025.
