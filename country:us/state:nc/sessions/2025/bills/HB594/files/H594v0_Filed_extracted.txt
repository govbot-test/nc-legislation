Section 1:
H.B. 594
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF NORTH CAROLINA
Mar 31, 2025
SESSION 2025
HOUSE PRINCIPAL CLERK
H D
Short Title: Funds/Kings Grant Connectivity. (Public)
Sponsors: Representative Wheatley.
Referred to:
1 A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
2 AN ACT TO APPROPRIATE FUNDS TO THE CITY OF FAYETTEVILLE FOR THE
3 CONSTRUCTION OF A SECOND ENTRANCE INTO THE KINGS GRANT
4 NEIGHBORHOOD.
5 The General Assembly of North Carolina enacts:
6 SECTION 1. There is appropriated from the General Fund to the Office of State
7 Budget and Management the sum of five million dollars ($5,000,000) in nonrecurring funds for
8 the 2025-2026 fiscal year to provide a directed grant to the City of Fayetteville for the
9 construction of a second entrance into the Kings Grant neighborhood, which would provide an
10 additional point of ingress/egress for the neighborhood.
11 SECTION 2. This act becomes effective July 1, 2025.
*DRH30268-LGa-90*
