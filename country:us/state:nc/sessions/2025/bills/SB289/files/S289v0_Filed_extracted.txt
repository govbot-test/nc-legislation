Section 1:
FILED SENATE
Mar 13, 2025
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF NORTH CAROLINA
S.B. 289
SESSION 2025
PRINCIPAL CLERK
S D
Short Title: Funds for the Stagville Memorial Project. (Public)
Sponsors: Senators Murdock and Chitlik (Primary Sponsors).
Referred to:
1 A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
2 AN ACT TO APPROPRIATE FUNDS FOR THE STAGVILLE MEMORIAL PROJECT.
3 The General Assembly of North Carolina enacts:
4 SECTION 1. There is appropriated from the General Fund to the Office of State
5 Budget and Management the sum of two hundred thirty-seven thousand dollars ($237,000) in
6 nonrecurring funds for the 2025-2026 fiscal year to provide a directed grant to The Stagville
7 Memorial Project to support the design and development of an art installation honoring the
8 history of the Stagville descendants on a plot of land across from the Museum of Durham History
9 in the City of Durham.
10 SECTION 2. This act becomes effective July 1, 2025.
*DRS45155-LGa-53*
