SENATE BILL DRS35019-NMe-18
Short Title: China Grove Even Year Elections. (Local)
Sponsors: Senator Ford (Primary Sponsor).
Referred to:
1 A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
2 AN ACT TO PROVIDE THAT REGULAR MUNICIPAL ELECTIONS IN THE TOWN OF
3 CHINA GROVE SHALL BE HELD IN EVEN-NUMBERED YEARS.
4 The General Assembly of North Carolina enacts:
5 SECTION 1.(a) Article III of the Charter of the Town of China Grove, being Chapter
6 42 of the 2002 Session Laws, reads as rewritten:
7 "ARTICLE III. ELECTIONS.
8 "Section 3.1. Regular Municipal Elections. Regular municipal elections shall be held in
9 each odd-numbered even-numbered year in accordance with the uniform municipal election laws
10 of North Carolina. Elections shall be conducted on a nonpartisan basis and the results determined
11 using the nonpartisan plurality method as provided in G.S. 163-292.
12 "Section 3.2. Election of Aldermen. The Town shall continue to have staggered terms for its
13 Aldermen. Two Three Aldermen shall be elected at the regular municipal election in 2003 2026
14 to four-year terms and quadrennially thereafter. Three Two Aldermen shall be elected at the
15 regular municipal election in 2005 2028 to four-year terms and quadrennially thereafter.
16 "Section 3.3. Election of Mayor. A Mayor shall be elected in the regular municipal election
17 in 2003 2028 to a four-year term and quadrennially thereafter.
18 …."
19 SECTION 1.(b) No municipal elections shall be conducted in the Town of China
20 Grove in 2025. The terms of office for the three Aldermen serving on the effective date of this
21 act whose terms are set to expire in 2025 are extended by one year. The terms of the two
22 Aldermen and the Mayor serving on the effective date of this act whose terms are set to expire
23 in 2027 are extended by one year. Regular municipal elections shall be conducted in the Town
24 of China Grove in even-numbered years beginning in 2026 and biennially thereafter.
25 SECTION 2. This act is effective when it becomes law and applies to elections held
26 on or after that date.
