Section 1:
FILED SENATE
Mar 25, 2025
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF NORTH CAROLINA
S.B. 500
SESSION 2025
PRINCIPAL CLERK
S D
Short Title: New Health and DSS Building. (Public)
Sponsors: Senator Sanderson (Primary Sponsor).
Referred to:
1 A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
2 AN ACT TO APPROPRIATE FUNDS FOR A NEW DEPARTMENT OF HEALTH AND
3 SOCIAL SERVICES BUILDING IN PAMLICO COUNTY.
4 The General Assembly of North Carolina enacts:
5 SECTION 1. There is appropriated from the General Fund to the Office of State
6 Budget and Management the sum of fourteen million dollars ($14,000,000) in nonrecurring funds
7 for the 2025-2026 fiscal year to provide a directed grant to Pamlico County for engineering and
8 constructing a new facility to house the County's Department of Health and Social Services on
9 property owned by the County. The former facility has been abandoned due to moisture and mold
10 problems.
11 SECTION 2. This act becomes effective July 1, 2025.
*DRS35201-LGa-104*
