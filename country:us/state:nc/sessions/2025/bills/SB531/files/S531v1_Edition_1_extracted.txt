Section 1:
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF NORTH CAROLINA
SESSION 2025
S 1
Short Title: Extended Inpatient Treatment Reimbursable. (Public)
Sponsors: Senator Burgin (Primary Sponsor).
Referred to: Rules and Operations of the Senate
March 26, 2025
1 A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
2 AN ACT TO REIMBURSE PRIVATE ENTITIES FOR INPATIENT TREATMENT
3 SERVICES PERFORMED ON BEHALF OF CLIENTS UNDER A COMMITMENT
4 ORDER AND EXTENDING BEYOND THIRTY DAYS.
5 The General Assembly of North Carolina enacts:
6 SECTION 1. Article 4 of Chapter 122C of the General Statutes is amended by adding
7 a new section to read:
8 "§ 122C-146A. Reimbursement for extended inpatient treatment.
9 An LME or LME/MCO, as appropriate, shall reimburse the costs incurred by private entities
10 for inpatient treatment services extending beyond 30 days performed on behalf of clients under
11 a commitment order."
12 SECTION 2. This act is effective when it becomes law and applies to treatment by
13 private entities beginning on or after that date.
*S531-v-1*
