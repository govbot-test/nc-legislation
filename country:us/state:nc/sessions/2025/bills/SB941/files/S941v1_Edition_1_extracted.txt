GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF NORTH CAROLINA
SESSION 2025
S 1
SENATE BILL 941
Short Title: Exempt Treasurer from DHHS Review. (Public)
Sponsors: Senator Burgin (Primary Sponsor).
Referred to: Rules and Operations of the Senate
April 30, 2026
1 A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
2 AN ACT TO EXEMPT THE DEPARTMENT OF STATE TREASURER FROM
3 DEPARTMENT OF HEALTH AND HUMAN SERVICES REVIEW.
4 The General Assembly of North Carolina enacts:
5 SECTION 1.(a) G.S. 131E-184 is amended by adding a new subsection to read:
6 "(i) The Department shall exempt from review any new institutional health service
7 established, developed, or acquired by any entity working in partnership or conjunction with the
8 Department of State Treasurer."
9 SECTION 1.(b) There is appropriated from the General Fund to the Department of
10 State Treasurer the sum of fifty thousand dollars ($50,000) in nonrecurring funds for the
11 2026-2027 fiscal year to take any actions permitted by the changes implemented in this act.
12 SECTION 2. This act is effective July 1, 2026.
