GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF NORTH CAROLINA
SESSION 2025
RATIFIED BILL
RESOLUTION 2025-3
HOUSE JOINT RESOLUTION 1007
A JOINT RESOLUTION ADJOURNING THE 2025 GENERAL ASSEMBLY TO A DATE
CERTAIN.
Be it resolved by the House of Representatives, the Senate concurring:
SECTION 1. When the House of Representatives and the Senate adjourn on
Thursday, April 17, 2025, they stand adjourned to reconvene on Monday, April 28, 2025, at
12:00 noon.
SECTION 2. This resolution is effective upon ratification.
In the General Assembly read three times and ratified this the 17th day of April, 2025.
s/ Phil Berger
President Pro Tempore of the Senate
s/ Timothy Reeder, MD
Presiding Officer of the House of Representatives
