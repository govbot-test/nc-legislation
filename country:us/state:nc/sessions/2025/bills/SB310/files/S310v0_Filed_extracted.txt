SENATE BILL DRS15057-NIf-19
Short Title: Religious Property - Tax Exemption. (Public)
Sponsors: Senators Britt, B. Newton, and Daniel (Primary Sponsors).
Referred to:
1 A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
2 AN ACT TO EXTEND THE LOOKBACK PERIOD FOR CERTAIN LATE APPLICATIONS
3 FOR PROPERTY TAX EXEMPTION AND TO ALLOW THE RELEASE OF TAXES
4 LEVIED AGAINST CERTAIN PROPERTY.
5 The General Assembly of North Carolina enacts:
6 SECTION 1. An owner of property claiming an exemption from taxation under
7 G.S. 105-278.3 may file a late application in accordance with G.S. 105-282.1(a1) which,
8 notwithstanding the calendar year restriction under that subsection, may apply to property taxes
9 levied by the county or municipality during the three calendar years immediately preceding the
10 effective date of this act. Notwithstanding G.S. 105-380 and G.S. 105-381, if a late application
11 submitted in accordance with this section is approved, the taxes levied against the property
12 covered by that application for up to the three calendar years immediately preceding the effective
13 date of this act shall be released, provided, however, that no taxpayer may receive a refund of
14 taxes previously paid under this section.
15 SECTION 2. This act is effective when it becomes law and applies to applications
16 submitted on or after that date.
