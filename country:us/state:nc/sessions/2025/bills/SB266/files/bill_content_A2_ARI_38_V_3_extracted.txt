Senator Moffitt
1 moves to amend the bill on page 2, lines 39 and 40,
2 by inserting between those lines:
3
4 "SECTION 2.1(a) All units of local government in the counties designated before,
5 on, or after the effective date of this act under a major disaster declaration by the President of the
6 United States under the Stafford Act (P.L. 93-288) as a result of Hurricane Helene managing
7 vegetative debris removal within their jurisdictions shall transport the material to a composting
8 site for reuse as mulch or soil amendment when the transportation and disposal costs for
9 processing tree stumps and other vegetative debris at a composting site are equal to or less than
10 the costs associated with transportation and disposal at a landfill. The Department of
11 Environmental Quality and the Department of Agriculture and Consumer Services, to the extent
12 they assist in removal of vegetative debris in the affected area, shall comply with and assist local
13 governments in complying with the requirements of this section.
14 SECTION 2.1(b) This section is effective when it becomes law and expires July 1,
15 2027.".
