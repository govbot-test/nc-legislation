SESSION 2025
SENATE BILL 266
RATIFIED BILL
AN ACT TO ELIMINATE THE INTERIM DATE FOR CARBON REDUCTION BY
CERTAIN ELECTRIC PUBLIC UTILITIES, TO ALLOW AN ALTERNATIVE COST
RECOVERY MECHANISM FOR THE FINANCING COSTS OF CONSTRUCTION
WORK IN PROGRESS FOR BASELOAD ELECTRIC GENERATING FACILITIES, TO
MODIFY THE STATUTES GOVERNING COST RECOVERY FOR FUEL-RELATED
CHARGES AND PERFORMANCE-BASED RATEMAKING, AND TO CODIFY A
PROVISION AUTHORIZING SECURITIZATION OF COSTS FOR RETIREMENT OF
COAL-FIRED GENERATING UNITS.
The General Assembly of North Carolina enacts:
