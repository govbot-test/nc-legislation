Section 1:
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF NORTH CAROLINA
SESSION 2025
S 1
Corrected Copy 2/3/25
Short Title: Hertford County Rural Development Authority. (Local)
Sponsors: Senator Hanig (Primary Sponsor).
Referred to: Rules and Operations of the Senate
February 3, 2025
1 A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
2 AN ACT TO AUTHORIZE HERTFORD COUNTY TO ESTABLISH A RURAL
3 DEVELOPMENT AUTHORITY.
4 The General Assembly of North Carolina enacts:
5 SECTION 1. Section 23 of Chapter 988 of the 1965 Session Laws, as amended by
6 Chapter 931 of the 1969 Session Laws, and by Section 14 of S.L. 2023-143, reads as rewritten:
7 "Sec. 23. This Act shall only apply to the counties of Cherokee, Clay, Graham, Hertford,
8 Jackson, Macon, Swain, Transylvania, and Yancey."
9 SECTION 2. This act is effective when it becomes law.
*S37-v-1*
