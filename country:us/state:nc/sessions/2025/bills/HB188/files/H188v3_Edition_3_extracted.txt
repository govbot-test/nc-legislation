1 A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
2 AN ACT TO AMEND THE REQUIREMENTS FOR CERTAIN AUTOMATICALLY
3 RENEWING CONSUMER CONTRACTS.
4 The General Assembly of North Carolina enacts:
5 SECTION 1. G.S. 75-41 reads as rewritten:
6 "§ 75-41. Contracts with automatic renewal clauses.
7 (a) Any person engaged in commerce that sells, leases, or offers to sell or lease, any
8 products or services to a consumer pursuant to a contract where the contract automatically renews
9 unless the consumer cancels the contract, that contains an automatic renewal provision for a term
10 of more than one month shall do all of the following:
11 (1) Disclose the automatic renewal clause clearly and conspicuously in the
12 contract or contract offer.Provide a disclosure statement that provides notice
13 of all of the following clearly and conspicuously:
14 a. That the contract will be automatically renewed unless the consumer
15 gives notice to the person of the consumer's intention to terminate the
16 contract prior to the renewal date.
17 b. The length of the initial term of the contract and the length of each
18 renewal period under the contract.
19 c. The amount to be charged to the consumer for the initial term of the
20 contract and the amount to be charged to the consumer for any renewal
21 periods, if known.
22 d. If any terms of the contract will change upon contract renewal, a list
23 and explanation of those terms.
24 e. An electronic mail address, mailing address, toll-free telephone
25 number, or another cost-effective, timely, and easy-to-use mechanism
26 that the consumer may use to terminate the automatic renewal. A
27 consumer who enters into a contract online shall be permitted to cancel
28 the contract online.
29 (1a) The disclosure statement required by subdivision (1) of this subsection must
30 meet the following requirements:
31 a. If written, the required disclosure language must differ from the
32 surrounding contract terms in one or more of the following ways:
33 1. The font must be larger.
34 2. The font must be a different font.
35 3. The font must be a different color.

34 (f) For purposes of this section, the term "automatic renewal provision" means a
35 provision under which a contract is automatically renewed at the end of a definite term for a
36 subsequent term of more than one month unless the consumer gives notice to the person of the
37 consumer's intention to terminate the contract."
38 SECTION 2. This act becomes effective January 1, 2026, and applies to contracts
39 entered into on or after that date.
