37 "Sec. 9.2. Beginning with the 2003-2004 fiscal year, the base amount of funding for current
38 expense expenditures from local funds shall include the previous year's level of current expense

41 school expenditures for the two most recent available fiscal years for low-wealth counties in
42 North Carolina (all local expenditures shall include local current expense expenditures incurred
43 by charter schools within the appropriate districts), as determined by the Superintendent of Public
44 Instruction or that person's designee. The average percentage change shall be calculated by (i)
45 adding together for each of the two previous fiscal years the total current local expense
46 expenditures for all low-wealth counties, (ii) dividing each of those totals, respectively, by the
47 number of low-wealth counties receiving low-wealth funding in each year to obtain an average
48 low-wealth county local current expense expenditure for each year, and (iii) comparing the two
49 averages. The average percentage change shall equal the percent difference between the averages
50 for the two years. The resulting product shall then be multiplied by a ratio consisting of the
51 Average Daily Membership used to distribute State funding for the succeeding fiscal year as
