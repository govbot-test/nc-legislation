GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF NORTH CAROLINA
SESSION 2025
S 1
SENATE BILL 1021
Short Title: Funds for Pineville Pedestrian Beacon. (Public)
Sponsors: Senator Salvador (Primary Sponsor).
Referred to: Rules and Operations of the Senate
May 4, 2026
1 A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
2 AN ACT TO APPROPRIATE FUNDS TO THE TOWN OF PINEVILLE FOR A
3 PEDESTRIAN HYBRID BEACON.
4 The General Assembly of North Carolina enacts:
5 SECTION 1. There is appropriated from the General Fund to the Office of State
6 Budget and Management the sum of five hundred forty-five thousand five hundred eighty-eight
7 dollars ($545,588) in nonrecurring funds for the 2026-2027 fiscal year to provide a directed grant
8 to the Town of Pineville for the construction of a pedestrian hybrid beacon and a crosswalk across
9 Hwy 51 and Main St. at the Town Hall to increase pedestrian safety and contribute to the
10 walkability of the Town.
11 SECTION 2. This act becomes effective July 1, 2026.
