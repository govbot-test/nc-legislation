H.B. 329
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF NORTH CAROLINA
Mar 6, 2025
SESSION 2025
HOUSE PRINCIPAL CLERK
H D
HOUSE BILL DRH10140-NH-94
Short Title: Tobacco and Hemp on Nonpublic School Grounds. (Public)
Sponsors: Representative Cunningham.
Referred to:
1 A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
2 AN ACT TO ENCOURAGE NONPUBLIC SCHOOLS TO PROHIBIT THE USE OF
3 TOBACCO AND HEMP PRODUCTS ON SCHOOL GROUNDS.
4 The General Assembly of North Carolina enacts:
5 SECTION 1. G.S. 115C-551(b) reads as rewritten:
6 "(b) All private church schools and all schools of religious charter are encouraged to do
7 the following:
8 …
9 (5) Tobacco and hemp products. – Adopt a policy to prohibit the use of tobacco
10 and hemp products on school grounds, using the requirements of Article 29A
11 of this Chapter as guidance for the policy."
12 SECTION 2. G.S. 115C-559(b) reads as rewritten:
13 "(b) All qualified nonpublic schools are encouraged to do the following:
14 …
15 (5) Tobacco and hemp products. – Adopt a policy to prohibit the use of tobacco
16 and hemp products on school grounds, using the requirements of Article 29A
17 of this Chapter as guidance for the policy."
18 SECTION 3. This act is effective when it becomes law.
