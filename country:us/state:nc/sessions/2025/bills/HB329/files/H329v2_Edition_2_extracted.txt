GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF NORTH CAROLINA
SESSION 2025
H 2
HOUSE BILL 329
Committee Substitute Favorable 3/25/25
Short Title: Tobacco and Hemp on Nonpublic School Grounds. (Public)
Sponsors:
Referred to:
March 10, 2025
1 A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
2 AN ACT TO ENCOURAGE NONPUBLIC SCHOOLS TO PROHIBIT THE USE OF
3 TOBACCO PRODUCTS AND HEMP-DERIVED CONSUMABLE PRODUCTS ON
4 SCHOOL GROUNDS.
5 The General Assembly of North Carolina enacts:
6 SECTION 1. G.S. 115C-551(b) reads as rewritten:
7 "(b) All private church schools and all schools of religious charter are encouraged to do
8 the following:
9 …
10 (5) Tobacco products and hemp-derived consumable products. – Adopt a policy
11 to prohibit the use of tobacco products and hemp-derived consumable
12 products on school grounds, using the requirements of Article 29A of this
13 Chapter as guidance for the policy."
14 SECTION 2. G.S. 115C-559(b) reads as rewritten:
15 "(b) All qualified nonpublic schools are encouraged to do the following:
16 …
17 (5) Tobacco products and hemp-derived consumable products. – Adopt a policy
18 to prohibit the use of tobacco products and hemp-derived consumable
19 products on school grounds, using the requirements of Article 29A of this
20 Chapter as guidance for the policy."
21 SECTION 3. This act is effective when it becomes law.
