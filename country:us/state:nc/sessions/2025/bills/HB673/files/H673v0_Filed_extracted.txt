H.B. 673
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF NORTH CAROLINA
Apr 1, 2025
SESSION 2025
HOUSE PRINCIPAL CLERK
H D
HOUSE BILL DRH10305-MCa-173
Short Title: Neonatal Transport Assistance. (Public)
Sponsors: Representative Alston.
Referred to:
1 A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
2 AN ACT TO PROVIDE FUNDING FOR NEONATAL CRITICAL CARE TRANSPORT.
3 The General Assembly of North Carolina enacts:
4 SECTION 1. There is appropriated from the General Fund the recurring sum of six
5 hundred fifty-six thousand dollars ($656,000) for each year of the 2025-2027 fiscal biennium to
6 the Board of Governors of The University of North Carolina to be allocated to the UNC Health
7 Care System solely for covering the aggregate costs of transportation services, including
8 ambulance services, for neonatal patients from a hospital in the UNC Health Care System to
9 another hospital in situations where insurance payors or insurance companies deny coverage for
10 transportation and mileage fees. The Board of Governors of The University of North Carolina
11 shall submit an annual report to the General Assembly detailing the amount of funds expended,
12 the number of neonatal transports covered, and any other data deemed relevant regarding the
13 effectiveness of funding for the purpose in this act in reducing delays in neonatal care.
14 SECTION 2. This act becomes effective July 1, 2025.
