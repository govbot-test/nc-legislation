FILED SENATE
Jun 25, 2026
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF NORTH CAROLINA
S.J.R. 1088
SESSION 2025
PRINCIPAL CLERK
S D
SENATE JOINT RESOLUTION DRSJR35479-BX-14
Sponsors: Senator Rabon (Primary Sponsor).
Referred
to:
2 APPOINTMENT OF FLOYD MCKISSICK TO THE NORTH CAROLINA UTILITIES
3 COMMISSION.
4 Whereas, under the provisions of G.S. 62-10, appointments made by the Governor to
5 membership on the North Carolina Utilities Commission are subject to confirmation by the
6 General Assembly by joint resolution; and
7 Whereas, the Governor has submitted to the presiding officers of the House of
8 Representatives and the Senate the name of his appointee, Floyd McKissick, to serve on the North
9 Carolina Utilities Commission for a term expiring June 30, 2029, to fill the unexpired term of
10 Charlotte Mitchell; Now, therefore,
11 Be it resolved by the Senate, the House of Representatives concurring:
12 SECTION 1. The appointment of Floyd McKissick to serve on the North Carolina
13 Utilities Commission, beginning upon confirmation and expiring June 30, 2029, is confirmed.
14 SECTION 2. This resolution is effective upon ratification.
*DRSJR35479-BX-14*
