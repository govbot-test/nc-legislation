Section 1:
H.B. 359
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF NORTH CAROLINA
Mar 10, 2025
SESSION 2025
HOUSE PRINCIPAL CLERK
H D
Short Title: Jacksonville Infrastructure Fund. (Public)
Sponsors: Representative Gable.
Referred to:
1 A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
2 AN ACT TO APPROPRIATE FUNDS TO THE CITY OF JACKSONVILLE FOR WATER
3 AND WASTEWATER INFRASTRUCTURE PROJECTS.
4 The General Assembly of North Carolina enacts:
5 SECTION 1. There is appropriated from the General Fund to the City of Jacksonville
6 (city) the sum of two million dollars ($2,000,000) in nonrecurring funds for the 2025-2026 fiscal
7 year to be used by the city for water and wastewater infrastructure projects.
8 SECTION 2. This act becomes effective July 1, 2025.
*DRH40227-NIa-108*
