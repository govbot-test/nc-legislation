46 PART V. CATAWBA/LINCOLN COUNTY BOUNDARY
47 SECTION 5.1. The following shall apply with respect to the legal boundary line
48 between Catawba County and Lincoln County:
49 (1) The legal boundary line shall be as depicted by the Census Redistricting Data
50 P.L. 94-171 TIGER/Line Shapefiles associated with the 2020 federal
51 decennial census.

1 (2) Any completed county boundary survey delivered by the North Carolina
2 Geodetic Survey in accordance with Article 3 of Chapter 153A of the General
3 Statutes that is not consistent with subdivision (1) of this section shall not be
4 binding upon Catawba County and Lincoln County.
5 SECTION 5.2. The legal boundary line as described in subdivision (1) of Section
6 5.1 of this Part shall be the county boundary submitted to the U.S. Census Bureau as part of the
7 Boundary and Annexation Survey in advance of the 2030 federal decennial census.
8 SECTION 5.3.(a) All papers, documents, and instruments required or permitted to
9 be filed or registered related to residents or property shall be filed in the county in which the
10 property is located pursuant to the boundary described in this Part despite the fact that papers,
11 documents, and instruments required or permitted to be filed or registered with respect to such
12 residents or property may have been previously recorded in the other county.
13 SECTION 5.3.(b) All public records related to residents and property located in
14 areas affected by the establishment of the boundary line that were filed or recorded prior to the
15 effective date of this Part in the adjoining county shall remain in the respective adjoining county
16 where filed or recorded, and such records shall be valid public records as to the property and
17 persons involved, even though they are recorded in an adjoining county where the property is not
18 located as evidenced by the boundary described in this Part.
19 SECTION 5.3.(c) For parcels of real property affected by the establishment of the
20 boundary described in this Part, the counties shall record a Notice of Affected Parcel in the office
21 of the register of deeds in each county. The register of deeds shall register and index the Notice
22 of Affected Parcel in accordance with the provisions of Article 2 of Chapter 161 of the General
23 Statutes. Notwithstanding G.S. 161-10 or any other provision of law, no fee or tax may be
24 collected for the Notice of Affected Parcel recorded pursuant to this Part. The Notice of Affected
25 Parcel shall contain at least the following information from the tax records of the counties in
26 which the affected parcels are listed:
27 (1) Reference to this act.
28 (2) The names of the record owners of the affected parcel according to the tax
29 records of the counties as of June 30, 2025.
30 (3) The property address of the affected parcel.
31 (4) A tax parcel identification number or other applicable identifier used by a
32 county tax office, if available.
33 (5) A brief description of the affected parcel, if available.
34 (6) A source deed reference for the affected parcel, if available.
35 SECTION 5.4. The boards of education of Catawba County and Lincoln County
36 shall cooperate with each other on behalf of residents who have students affected by the boundary
37 change to ensure that a transition is made that provides students with a choice to remain in their
38 current school system until graduation from high school.
39 SECTION 5.5. The boards of elections of Catawba County and Lincoln County shall
40 immediately after July 1, 2025, transfer the voter registration records pertaining to individuals
41 residing in areas affected by subdivision (1) of Section 5.1 of this Part to the adjoining county's
42 board of elections, and thereafter the registered voters so transferred shall be validly registered
43 to vote in that adjoining county.
44 SECTION 5.6. Catawba County, Lincoln County, the elected and appointed officials
45 of Catawba and Lincoln Counties, and employees of Catawba and Lincoln Counties shall not
46 incur any liability under any local or general law, ordinance, rule, or regulation for any act or
47 failure to act relating to taxation, school attendance, land use controls, elections, or any other
48 governmental function as it relates to the currently used boundary line between Catawba and
49 Lincoln Counties.
50 SECTION 5.7. This Part becomes effective June 30, 2025, for taxable years
51 beginning on or after July 1, 2025.
