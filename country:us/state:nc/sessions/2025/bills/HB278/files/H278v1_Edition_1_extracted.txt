GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF NORTH CAROLINA
SESSION 2025
H 1
HOUSE BILL 278
Short Title: Protect Military Votes. (Public)
Sponsors: Representatives Morey, Majeed, Ager, and Harrison (Primary Sponsors).
For a complete list of sponsors, refer to the North Carolina General Assembly web site.
Referred to: Rules, Calendar, and Operations of the House
March 5, 2025
1 A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
2 AN ACT TO CODIFY THE RULE OF THE STATE BOARD OF ELECTIONS STATING
3 COVERED VOTERS VOTING A NORTH CAROLINA MILITARY-OVERSEAS
4 BALLOT ARE NOT REQUIRED TO INCLUDE PHOTO IDENTIFICATION WHEN
5 CASTING A BALLOT.
6 The General Assembly of North Carolina enacts:
7 SECTION 1. Part 1 of Article 21A of Chapter 163 of the General Statutes is amended
8 by adding a new section to read:
9 "§ 163-258.21. No photo ID required.
10 A covered voter voting under this Article shall not be required to include a photocopy of
11 identification described in G.S. 163-166.16(a) or an affidavit as described in
12 G.S. 163-166.16(d)(1), (d)(2), or (d)(3) when submitting a ballot in accordance with this Article."
13 SECTION 2. This act is effective when it becomes law and applies to ballots cast on
14 or after that date.
