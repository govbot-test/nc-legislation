H.B. 534
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF NORTH CAROLINA
Mar 26, 2025
SESSION 2025
HOUSE PRINCIPAL CLERK
H D
HOUSE BILL DRH40319-NGa-121
Short Title: Funds for Sexual Assault Nurse Programs. (Public)
Sponsors: Representative Campbell.
Referred to:
1 A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
2 AN ACT TO APPROPRIATE FUNDS TO CERTAIN COMMUNITY COLLEGES TO
3 ESTABLISH TRAINING SITES FOR SEXUAL ASSAULT NURSE EXAMINERS.
4 The General Assembly of North Carolina enacts:
5 SECTION 1.(a) There is appropriated from the General Fund to the Community
6 Colleges System Office the sum of seven hundred thousand dollars ($700,000) in nonrecurring
7 funds for the 2025-2026 fiscal year to be allocated to Rowan-Cabarrus Community College and
8 Wake Technical Community College in equal amounts to establish training sites for certification
9 as a Sexual Assault Nurse Examiner.
10 SECTION 1.(b) Funds allocated pursuant to subsection (a) of this section shall be
11 used as follows by each community college:
12 (1) Up to one hundred five thousand dollars ($105,000) shall be used to hire one
13 full-time faculty member.
14 (2) Up to one hundred five thousand dollars ($105,000) shall be used to hire one
15 clinical coordinator.
16 (3) Up to forty-five thousand dollars ($45,000) shall be used for equipment and
17 supplies.
18 (4) Up to ninety-five thousand dollars ($95,000) shall be used for program
19 development and accreditation costs.
20 SECTION 2. This act is effective when it becomes law.
