24 SEXUALLY VIOLENT PREDATOR DETERMINATIONS
25 SECTION 2.1.(a) G.S. 14-208.20(b) reads as rewritten:
26 "(b) Prior to sentencing a person as a sexually violent predator, the court shall order a
27 presentence investigation in accordance with G.S. 15A-1332(c). However, the study of the
28 defendant and whether the defendant is a sexually violent predator shall be conducted by a board
29 of experts selected by the Division of Prisons of the Department of Adult Correction. The board
30 of experts shall be composed of at least four people. Two of the board members shall be experts
31 in the field of the behavior and treatment of sexual offenders, one of whom shall be selected from
32 a panel of experts in those fields provided by the North Carolina Medical Society and not be
33 employed full time with the Division of Prisons of the Department of Adult Correction or
34 employed on a full-time basis with any other State agency. One of the board members shall be a
35 victims' rights advocate, and one of the board members shall be a representative of law
36 enforcement agencies."
37 SECTION 2.1.(b) This section becomes effective December 1, 2025, and applies to
38 boards of experts selected on or after that date.
