18 ESTABLISH PLAN FOR MEDICAID MANAGED CARE COST-SAVINGS AND
19 EFFICIENCY MEASURES
20 SECTION 1.8.(a) The Department of Health and Human Services (DHHS) is
21 directed to develop a plan for improved health outcomes, program integrity, cost-savings, and
22 efficiency measures in the Medicaid program. In developing this plan, DHHS shall consult with
23 relevant stakeholders. The plan shall include all of the following:
24 (1) Reduction of DHHS administrative expenses.
25 (2) Flexibilities for prepaid health plans, as defined in G.S. 108D-1, to manage
26 service utilization and costs and align claims operations with national
27 standards and best practices.
28 (3) Improved alignment of prepaid health plan contract incentives and prepaid
29 health plan cost containment efforts through full risk-sharing, value-based
30 arrangements, and appropriate oversight of delegated care management
31 entities.
32 (4) Implementation of the plan no later than April 1, 2026.
33 SECTION 1.8.(b) No later than November 11, 2025, DHHS shall submit a report on
34 the plan for cost-savings and efficiency measures, as required in subsection (a) of this section, to
35 the Joint Legislative Oversight Committee on Medicaid and the Fiscal Research Division. DHHS
36 shall provide monthly updates on implementation of the plan and further updates upon request
37 of the Chairs of the Joint Legislative Oversight Committee on Medicaid.
38
39 MEDICAID REDETERMINATIONS

44 SECTION 1.9.(b) The Office of the State Auditor shall conduct a performance audit
45 of a sample of county departments of social services modeled after the audit required by Section
46 11.5 of S.L. 2015-7.
47 SECTION 1.9.(c) No later than April 1, 2026, the Office of the State Auditor shall
48 report the findings of the examination required in subsection (a) of this section and the results of
49 the performance audit required in subsection (b) of this section to the Joint Legislative Oversight
50 Committee on Medicaid and the Fiscal Research Division.

41 STATE BUDGET ACT APPLICABILITY
42 SECTION 3.3. If any provision of this act and G.S. 143C-5-4 are in conflict, the
43 provisions of this act shall prevail. The appropriations and the authorizations to allocate and
44 spend funds which are set out in this act shall remain in effect until the Current Operations
45 Appropriations Act for the applicable fiscal year becomes law, at which time that act shall
46 become effective and shall govern appropriations and expenditures. When the Current
47 Operations Appropriations Act for that fiscal year becomes law, the Director of the Budget shall
48 adjust allotments to give effect to that act from July 1 of the fiscal year.

18 ESTABLISH PLAN FOR MEDICAID MANAGED CARE COST-SAVINGS AND
19 EFFICIENCY MEASURES
20 SECTION 1.8.(a) The Department of Health and Human Services (DHHS) is
21 directed to develop a plan for improved health outcomes, program integrity, cost-savings, and
22 efficiency measures in the Medicaid program. In developing this plan, DHHS shall consult with
23 relevant stakeholders. The plan shall include all of the following:
24 (1) Reduction of DHHS administrative expenses.
25 (2) Flexibilities for prepaid health plans, as defined in G.S. 108D-1, to manage
26 service utilization and costs and align claims operations with national
27 standards and best practices.
28 (3) Improved alignment of prepaid health plan contract incentives and prepaid
29 health plan cost containment efforts through full risk-sharing, value-based
30 arrangements, and appropriate oversight of delegated care management
31 entities.
32 (4) Implementation of the plan no later than April 1, 2026.
33 SECTION 1.8.(b) No later than November 11, 2025, DHHS shall submit a report on
34 the plan for cost-savings and efficiency measures, as required in subsection (a) of this section, to
35 the Joint Legislative Oversight Committee on Medicaid and the Fiscal Research Division. DHHS
36 shall provide monthly updates on implementation of the plan and further updates upon request
37 of the Chairs of the Joint Legislative Oversight Committee on Medicaid.
