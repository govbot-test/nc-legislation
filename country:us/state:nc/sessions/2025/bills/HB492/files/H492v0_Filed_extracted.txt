Section 1:
H.B. 492
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF NORTH CAROLINA
Mar 24, 2025
SESSION 2025
HOUSE PRINCIPAL CLERK
H D
Short Title: Repeal Parents' Bill of Rights. (Public)
Sponsors: Representative Dahle.
Referred to:
1 A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
2 AN ACT TO REPEAL SESSION LAW 2023-106 AND TO MAKE CONFORMING
3 CHANGES.
4 The General Assembly of North Carolina enacts:
5
6 PART I. REPEAL SESSION LAW 2023-106
7 SECTION 1. S.L. 2023-106 is repealed.
8
9 PART II. CONFORMING CHANGES
10 SECTION 2. Section 7.81 of S.L. 2023-134 is repealed.
11 SECTION 3. Section 2.8 of S.L. 2024-1 is repealed.
12 SECTION 4. G.S. 115C-150.16 reads as rewritten:
13 "§ 115C-150.16. Applicability of Chapter.
14 Except as otherwise provided in this Article and Article 7B of this Chapter, Article, the
15 requirements of this Chapter shall not apply to the schools for the deaf and blind. Schools for the
16 deaf and blind shall be considered a State agency, as defined in G.S. 143C-1-1, and shall comply
17 with all requirements for State agencies unless otherwise specified in this Article. Schools for the
18 deaf and blind shall not be considered local school administrative units."
19 SECTION 5. G.S. 115C-218.10 reads as rewritten:
20 "§ 115C-218.10. Charter school exemptions.
21 Except as provided in this Article and Article 7B of this Chapter, Article, and pursuant to the
22 provisions of its charter, a charter school is exempt from statutes and rules applicable to a local
23 board of education or local school administrative unit."
24 SECTION 6. G.S. 115C-238.60(b) reads as rewritten:
25 "(b) Except as otherwise provided in this Part and Article 7B of this Chapter, Part, a
26 regional school is exempt from statutes and rules applicable to a local board of education or local
27 school administrative unit."
28 SECTION 7. G.S. 116-239.5(d) reads as rewritten:
29 "(d) Except as otherwise provided in this Article and Article 7B of Chapter 115C of the
30 General Statutes, Article, the Subcommittee, the chancellor of each constituent institution that
31 operates a laboratory school, and the laboratory school are exempt from statutes and rules
32 applicable to a local board of education or local school administrative unit."
33
34 PART III. EFFECTIVE DATE
35 SECTION 8. This act is effective when it becomes law.
*DRH10213-NG-57*
