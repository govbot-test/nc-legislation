19 acceleration or speed in accordance with the applicable speed limit.

32 driving privilege to, a person who is subject to this section, in addition to any other restriction or
33 condition, it shall require the person to agree to and shall indicate on the person's drivers license
34 that the person may only operate a vehicle equipped with a functioning ISA system approved by
35 the Commissioner.
