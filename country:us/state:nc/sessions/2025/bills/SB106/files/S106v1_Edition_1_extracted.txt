Section 1:
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF NORTH CAROLINA
SESSION 2025
S 1
Short Title: Funds/Onslow Water and Sewer Authority. (Public)
Sponsors: Senator Lazzara (Primary Sponsor).
Referred to: Rules and Operations of the Senate
February 18, 2025
1 A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
2 AN ACT TO APPROPRIATE FUNDS TO THE ONSLOW WATER AND SEWER
3 AUTHORITY FOR CONSTRUCTION OF A WATER TOWER.
4 The General Assembly of North Carolina enacts:
5 SECTION 1. The sum of three million dollars ($3,000,000) in nonrecurring funds
6 for the 2025-2026 fiscal year is appropriated from the General Fund to the Onslow Water and
7 Sewer Authority for the construction of a water tower serving North Topsail Beach.
8 SECTION 2. This act becomes effective July 1, 2025.
*S106-v-1*
