GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF NORTH CAROLINA
SESSION 2025
H 1
HOUSE BILL 654
Short Title: Adopt Girl Scouts Week. (Public)
Sponsors: Representatives Cervania, Howard, and Johnson-Hostler (Primary Sponsors).
For a complete list of sponsors, refer to the North Carolina General Assembly web site.
Referred to: State and Local Government, if favorable, Rules, Calendar, and Operations of the
House
April 2, 2025
1 A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
2 AN ACT DESIGNATING A STATEWIDE GIRL SCOUTS WEEK TO RECOGNIZE THE
3 IMPORTANCE AND CONTRIBUTIONS OF THE GIRL SCOUTS AND CELEBRATE
4 THE GIRL SCOUT GOLD AWARD RECIPIENTS.
5 Whereas, since its founding on March 12, 1912, Girl Scouts of the USA has
6 empowered millions of girls and young women by fostering leadership, confidence, and character
7 through community service and personal development; and
8 Whereas, the Girl Scout Gold Award, established in 1916, represents the highest
9 achievement in Girl Scouting, recognizing Senior and Ambassador Girl Scouts who demonstrate
10 extraordinary leadership through sustainable and measurable Take Action projects that address
11 pressing community issues; and
12 Whereas, Gold Award Girl Scouts in North Carolina have tackled critical challenges
13 such as environmental sustainability, educational disparities, and public health awareness,
14 creating lasting impacts within their communities; and
15 Whereas, the dedication and accomplishments of these young leaders inspire others
16 to contribute positively to society and exemplify the core values of courage, confidence, and
17 character; and
18 Whereas, Girl Scouts Week provides an opportunity to celebrate the organization's
19 rich history and the remarkable achievements of its members, especially those who have earned
20 the prestigious Gold Award; Now, therefore,
21 The General Assembly of North Carolina enacts:
22 SECTION 1. Chapter 103 of the General Statutes is amended by adding a new
23 section to read:
24 "§ 103-19. Girl Scouts Week.
25 The Sunday through Saturday week of each year containing the twelfth day of March is
26 designated as Girl Scouts Week in North Carolina."
27 SECTION 2. This act is effective when it becomes law.
