Section 1:
H.B. 35
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF NORTH CAROLINA
Feb 3, 2025
SESSION 2025
HOUSE PRINCIPAL CLERK
H D
Short Title: Establish Military Appreciation Month. (Public)
Sponsors: Representative B. Jones.
Referred to:
1 A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
2 AN ACT TO DESIGNATE THE MONTH OF NOVEMBER OF EACH YEAR AS MILITARY
3 APPRECIATION MONTH.
4 The General Assembly of North Carolina enacts:
5 SECTION 1. Chapter 103 of the General Statutes is amended by adding a new
6 section to read:
7 "§ 103-19. Military Appreciation Month.
8 The month of November of each year is designated as Military Appreciation Month in the
9 State of North Carolina."
10 SECTION 2. This act is effective when it becomes law.
*DRH40039-LG-30*
