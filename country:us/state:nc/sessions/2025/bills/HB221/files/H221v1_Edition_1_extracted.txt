GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF NORTH CAROLINA
SESSION 2025
H 1
HOUSE BILL 221
Short Title: Tabor City/Railroad Revitalization Project. (Local)
Sponsors: Representative B. Jones.
For a complete list of sponsors, refer to the North Carolina General Assembly web site.
Referred to: State and Local Government, if favorable, Finance, if favorable, Rules, Calendar,
and Operations of the House
February 27, 2025
1 A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
2 AN ACT AUTHORIZING THE TOWN OF TABOR CITY TO PARTICIPATE IN RAILROAD
3 REVITALIZATION PROGRAMS TO ENSURE CONTINUED OR IMPROVED RAIL
4 SERVICE TO THE TOWN.
5 The General Assembly of North Carolina enacts:
6 SECTION 1. The Town of Tabor City is authorized to participate in State and federal
7 railroad revitalization programs necessary to ensure continued or improved rail service to the
8 Town as authorized in Article 2D of Chapter 136 of the General Statutes. The Town is authorized
9 to enter into contracts with the North Carolina Department of Transportation to provide for the
10 nonfederal matching funds for railroad revitalization programs. Such funds may be comprised of
11 State funds distributed under the provisions of G.S. 136-44.38 and of Town funds. Subject to the
12 provisions of G.S. 160A-209(d), the Town is authorized to levy local property taxes for railroad
13 revitalization programs. Town funds for any project authorized by this act may not exceed ten
14 percent (10%) of total project costs.
15 SECTION 2. This act applies only to the Town of Tabor City.
16 SECTION 3. This act is effective when it becomes law.
