FILED SENATE
Feb 27, 2025
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF NORTH CAROLINA
S.B. 216
SESSION 2025
PRINCIPAL CLERK
S D
Short Title: Sharpsburg Satellite Annexation. (Local)
Sponsors: Senator Smith (Primary Sponsor).
Referred to:
1 A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
2 AN ACT TO REMOVE THE CAP ON SATELLITE ANNEXATIONS FOR THE TOWN OF
3 SHARPSBURG.
4 The General Assembly of North Carolina enacts:
5 SECTION 1. G.S. 160A-58.1 reads as rewritten:
6 "§ 160A-58.1. Petition for annexation; standards.
7 …
8 (b) A noncontiguous area proposed for annexation must meet all of the following
9 standards:
10 …
11 (5) The area within the proposed satellite corporate limits, when added to the area
12 within all other satellite corporate limits, may not exceed ten percent (10%)
13 of the area within the primary corporate limits of the annexing city.
14 This subdivision does not apply to the Cities of Archdale, Asheboro,
15 Belmont, Claremont, Concord, Conover, Durham, Elizabeth City, Gastonia,
16 Greenville, Hickory, Kannapolis, Kings Mountain, Locust, Lowell, Marion,
17 Mount Airy, Mount Holly, New Bern, Newton, Oxford, Randleman, Roanoke
18 Rapids, Rockingham, Saluda, Sanford, Salisbury, Shelby, Southport,
19 Statesville, and Washington and the Towns of Ahoskie, Angier, Apex, Ayden,
20 Belville, Benson, Bladenboro, Bridgeton, Bunn, Burgaw, Calabash, Carthage,
21 Catawba, China Grove, Clayton, Columbia, Columbus, Cramerton, Creswell,
22 Dallas, Dobson, Four Oaks, Franklin, Franklinton, Franklinville,
23 Fuquay-Varina, Garner, Godwin, Goldston, Granite Quarry, Green Level,
24 Grimesland, Harrisburg, Holly Ridge, Holly Springs, Hookerton, Hope Mills,
25 Huntersville, Jamestown, Kenansville, Kenly, Knightdale, Landis, Laurel
26 Park, Liberty, Lillington, Louisburg, Maggie Valley, Maiden, Mayodan,
27 Maysville, Middlesex, Midland, Mocksville, Morrisville, Mount Pleasant,
28 Nashville, North Wilkesboro, Norwood, Oak Island, Oakboro, Ocean Isle
29 Beach, Pembroke, Pine Level, Pollocksville, Princeton, Ramseur, Ranlo,
30 Richlands, Rockwell, Rolesville, Rutherfordton, Shallotte, Sharpsburg, Siler
31 City, Smithfield, Spencer, Spring Lake, Stanley, Stem, Stovall, Surf City,
32 Swansboro, Taylorsville, Troutman, Troy, Vass, Wallace, Warsaw, Watha,
33 Waynesville, Weldon, Wendell, West Jefferson, Wilson's Mills, Windsor,
34 Wingate, Yadkinville, Youngsville, and Zebulon.
35 …."
36 SECTION 2. This act is effective when it becomes law.
