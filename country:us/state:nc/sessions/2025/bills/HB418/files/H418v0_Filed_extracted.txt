Section 1:
H.B. 418
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF NORTH CAROLINA
Mar 17, 2025
SESSION 2025
HOUSE PRINCIPAL CLERK
H D
Short Title: K-5 Performing and Visual Arts Requirement. (Public)
Sponsors: Representative Carney.
Referred to:
1 A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
2 AN ACT TO SPECIFY INSTRUCTION ON MUSIC AND VISUAL ARTS IN ELEMENTARY
3 SCHOOLS.
4 The General Assembly of North Carolina enacts:
5 SECTION 1. Part 1 of Article 8 of Chapter 115C of the General Statutes is amended
6 by adding the following new sections to read:
7 "§ 115C-81.95. Elementary performing arts education.
8 Each local school administrative unit shall provide all students in kindergarten through grade
9 five instruction in music, dance, or theatre arts that aligns with the Standard Course of Study.
10 The instruction shall meet at least the following criteria:
11 (1) Occur during at least one instructional day out of every five instructional days.
12 (2) Be taught by at least one licensed music, dance, or theatre arts education
13 instructor.
14 (3) Instructional sessions shall last at least 30 consecutive minutes.
15 (4) Instructional sessions shall be given to classes no larger than the maximum
16 classroom size per instructional session for each grade, as provided in
17 G.S. 115C-301.
18 "§ 115C-81.96. Elementary visual arts education.
19 Each local school administrative unit shall provide all students in kindergarten through grade
20 five instruction in the visual arts that aligns with the Standard Course of Study. The instruction
21 shall meet at least the following criteria:
22 (1) Occur during at least one instructional day out of every five instructional days.
23 (2) Be taught by at least one licensed visual arts education instructor.
24 (3) Instructional sessions shall last at least 30 consecutive minutes.
25 (4) Instructional sessions shall be given to classes no larger than the maximum
26 classroom size per instructional session for each grade, as provided in
27 G.S. 115C-301."
28 SECTION 2. This act is effective when it becomes law and applies beginning with
29 the 2026-2027 school year.
