GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF NORTH CAROLINA
SESSION 2025
H 1
HOUSE BILL 782
Short Title: Civic Club Registration Plate Clarification. (Public)
Sponsors: Representatives Biggs, Tyson, Balkcom, and Eddins (Primary Sponsors).
For a complete list of sponsors, refer to the North Carolina General Assembly web site.
Referred to: Transportation, if favorable, Rules, Calendar, and Operations of the House
April 7, 2025
1 A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
2 AN ACT CLARIFYING THE ISSUANCE OF CIVIC CLUB SPECIAL REGISTRATION
3 PLATES.
4 The General Assembly of North Carolina enacts:
5 SECTION 1. G.S. 20-79.4(b)(44) reads as rewritten:
6 "§ 20-79.4. Special registration plates.
7 …
8 (b) Types. – The Division shall issue the following types of special registration plates:
9 …
10 (44) Civic Club. – Issuable to a member of a nationally recognized civic
11 organization whose member clubs in the State are exempt from State corporate
12 income tax under G.S. 105-130.11(a)(5). For purposes of this subdivision, "a
13 member" includes the organization itself if the organization is the registered
14 owner of the vehicle. Examples of these clubs include Jaycees, Kiwanis,
15 Optimist, Lions Club, Rotary, Ruritan, and Shrine. The plate shall bear a word
16 or phrase identifying the civic club and the emblem of the civic club. A person
17 may obtain from the Division a special registration plate under this
18 subdivision for the registered owner of a motor vehicle or a motorcycle. The
19 registration fees and the restrictions on the issuance of a specialized
20 registration plate for a motorcycle are the same as for any motor vehicle. The
21 Division may not issue a civic club plate authorized by this subdivision unless
22 it receives at least 300 applications for that civic club plate."
23 SECTION 2. This act is effective when it becomes law.
