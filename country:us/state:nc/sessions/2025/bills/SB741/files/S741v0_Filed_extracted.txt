Section 1:
FILED SENATE
Mar 25, 2025
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF NORTH CAROLINA
S.B. 741
SESSION 2025
PRINCIPAL CLERK
S D
Short Title: Funds for Charlotte-Mecklenburg Library. (Public)
Sponsors: Senator Mohammed (Primary Sponsor).
Referred to:
1 A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
2 AN ACT TO APPROPRIATE FUNDS TO THE CHARLOTTE MECKLENBURG LIBRARY
3 FOUNDATION FOR THE CONSTRUCTION OF THE NEW MAIN LIBRARY.
4 The General Assembly of North Carolina enacts:
5 SECTION 1. There is appropriated from the General Fund to the Office of State
6 Budget and Management the sum of five million dollars ($5,000,000) in nonrecurring funds for
7 the 2025-2026 fiscal year to provide a directed grant to the Charlotte Mecklenburg Library
8 Foundation, a nonprofit organization, for the construction of the new main library.
9 SECTION 2. This act becomes effective July 1, 2025.
*DRS45364-LGa-72*
