Section 1:
FILED SENATE
Mar 25, 2025
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF NORTH CAROLINA
S.B. 658
SESSION 2025
PRINCIPAL CLERK
S D
Short Title: Funds/Greensboro Blandwood Mansion. (Public)
Sponsors: Senator Robinson (Primary Sponsor).
Referred to:
1 A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
2 AN ACT TO APPROPRIATE FUNS TO PRESERVATION GREENSBORO
3 INCORPORATED FOR OPERATING EXPENSES AND RENOVATION OF THE
4 HISTORIC BLANDWOOD MANSION.
5 The General Assembly of North Carolina enacts:
6 SECTION 1. There is appropriated from the General Fund to the Office of State
7 Budget and Management the sum of one million dollars ($1,000,000) in nonrecurring funds for
8 the 2025-2026 fiscal year to provide a directed grant to Preservation Greensboro Incorporated
9 for operating expenses and restoration of the Blandwood Mansion, the 19th century residence of
10 Governor John Motley Morehead.
11 SECTION 2. This act becomes effective July 1, 2025.
*DRS45341-LGa-109*
