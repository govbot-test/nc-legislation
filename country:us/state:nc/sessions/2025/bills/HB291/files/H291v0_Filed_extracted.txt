Section 1:
H.B. 291
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF NORTH CAROLINA
Mar 4, 2025
SESSION 2025
HOUSE PRINCIPAL CLERK
H D
Short Title: Franklinton Annexations. (Local)
Sponsors: Representative Winslow.
Referred to:
1 A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
2 AN ACT ADDING CERTAIN DESCRIBED NORTH CAROLINA DEPARTMENT OF
3 TRANSPORTATION RIGHTS-OF-WAY TO THE CORPORATE LIMITS OF THE
4 TOWN OF FRANKLINTON.
5 The General Assembly of North Carolina enacts:
6 SECTION 1. The following described North Carolina Department of Transportation
7 rights-of-way are added to the corporate limits of the Town of Franklinton:
8 Starting point: Creek under US Highway 1, US Highway 1, Ending point: US Highway 1
9 Intersection with Eric Medlin Road;
10 NC 56 Intersection with Fred Wilder Road, NC 56 to US 1 Interchange;
11 NC 56 & Lane Store Road Intersection, NC 56, NC 56 & Mays Crossroads Road Intersection;
12 Mays Crossroads Road & NC 56 Intersection, Mays Crossroads Road;
13 Lane Store Road & NC 56 Intersection, Lane Store Road, Lane Store Road & Cedar Creek Road
14 Intersection;
15 Cedar Creek Road and Lane Store Road Intersection, Cedar Creek Road, Cedar Creek Road at
16 crossing point of stream;
17 South Cheatham Street & Janice Avenue Intersection, South Cheatham Street, South Cheatham
18 Street & US Highway 1;
19 US 1A & US 1 (South Main Street & US 1 Intersection), US 1A (Main Street), US 1A & US 1
20 (North Main Street & US 1 Intersection);
21 South Chavis Street & East College Street (east intersection), South Chavis Street, South Chavis
22 Street & East College Street (west intersection);
23 South Chavis & Carver Street Intersection, Carver Street Eastern Terminus of right-of-way;
24 South Chavis Street & Dogwood Lane Intersection, Dogwood Lane Eastern Terminus of
25 right-of-way; and
26 South Chavis Street & Peach Street Intersection, Peach Street Eastern Terminus of right-of-way.
27 SECTION 2. This act becomes effective July 1, 2025.
*DRH30149-LMx-45*
