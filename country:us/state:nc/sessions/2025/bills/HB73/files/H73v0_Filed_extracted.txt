Section 1:
H.B. 73
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF NORTH CAROLINA
Feb 10, 2025
SESSION 2025
HOUSE PRINCIPAL CLERK
H D
Short Title: Energy Security Act of 2025. (Public)
Sponsors: Representative Moss.
Referred to:
1 A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
2 AN ACT TO REQUIRE PUBLIC UTILITIES TO PROVIDE SECURITY SYSTEMS FOR
3 SUBSTATIONS TO PROTECT AGAINST VANDALISM AND OTHER THREATS.
4 The General Assembly of North Carolina enacts:
5 SECTION 1. Article 16 of Chapter 62 of the General Statutes is amended by adding
6 a new section to read:
7 "§ 62-334. Protection of public utility substations.
8 Public utilities shall (i) provide security systems at substations to protect against vandalism
9 and other security threats and (ii) continuously operate the security systems 24 hours a day."
10 SECTION 2. This act becomes effective July 1, 2025.
*DRH40058-NJ-10*
