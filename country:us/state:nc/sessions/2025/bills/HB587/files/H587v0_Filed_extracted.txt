H.B. 587
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF NORTH CAROLINA
Mar 31, 2025
SESSION 2025
HOUSE PRINCIPAL CLERK
H D
HOUSE BILL DRH30270-MQa-79A
Short Title: Corps Community Center Funding. (Public)
Sponsors: Representative Davis.
Referred to:
1 A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
2 AN ACT TO PROVIDE FUNDING TO THE SALVATION ARMY OF CAPE FEAR FOR A
3 COMMUNITY CENTER FACILITY IN WILMINGTON.
4 Whereas, the Salvation Army of Cape Fear has served the Wilmington area for over
5 135 years and continues to provide assistance to residents of New Hanover, Columbus,
6 Brunswick, Bladen, and Pender Counties; and
7 Whereas, the Salvation Army of Cape Fear recognizes and strives to address the
8 critical needs of at-risk communities through its seventeen million five hundred thousand dollar
9 ($17,500,000) Build Hope Capital Campaign, including the planned construction of the Corps
10 Community Center, at a cost of seven million five hundred thousand dollars ($7,500,000),
11 benefitting eastern North Carolina; and
12 Whereas, the Corps Community Center will offer after-school tutoring, business
13 training, and recreational opportunities, while providing the surrounding underserved community
14 with much needed meeting space to the surrounding community; and
15 Whereas, the Corps Community Center will also coordinate with New Hanover
16 County Emergency Management to serve as an emergency shelter during hurricanes for the area's
17 at-risk and homeless community, as well as the community in general; and
18 Whereas, the Corps Community Center and the Build Hope Campaign is part of a
19 public-private partnership with nearly three-quarters of funding secured; and
20 Whereas, the Salvation Army of Cape Fear would be grateful for the support of the
21 General Assembly in securing a portion of funding for the Corps Community Center project;
22 Now, therefore,
23 The General Assembly of North Carolina enacts:
24 SECTION 1. Notwithstanding G.S. 143C-5-2, there is appropriated from the
25 General Fund to the Office of State Budget and Management the sum of two million dollars
26 ($2,000,000) in nonrecurring funds for the 2025-2026 fiscal year to be provided in the form of a
27 grant to The Salvation Army to be used for the construction of the Corps Community Center in
28 Wilmington by the Salvation Army of Cape Fear.
29 SECTION 2. This act becomes effective July 1, 2025.
