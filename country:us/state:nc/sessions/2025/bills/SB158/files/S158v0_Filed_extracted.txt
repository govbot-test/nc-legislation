Short Title: Funds for McCrorey YMCA/Flying Classroom. (Public)

Referred to:
1 A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
2 AN ACT TO APPROPRIATE FUNDS TO THE MCCROREY YMCA OF CHARLOTTE FOR
3 ITS FLYING CLASSROOM PROGRAM.
4 The General Assembly of North Carolina enacts:
5 SECTION 1. There is appropriated from the General Fund to the Office of State
6 Budget and Management the sum of one hundred thousand dollars ($100,000) in nonrecurring
7 funds for the 2025-2026 fiscal year to provide a directed grant to the Young Men's Christian
8 Association of Greater Charlotte to enable the McCrorey YMCA to provide its Flying Classroom
9 program, a unique educational experience that facilitates student engagement through
10 expeditionary and immersive learning experiences in science, technology, mathematics, and
11 literacy.
12 SECTION 2. This act becomes effective July 1, 2025.
