Section 1:
FILED SENATE
Mar 10, 2025
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF NORTH CAROLINA
S.B. 253
SESSION 2025
PRINCIPAL CLERK
S D
Short Title: Funds for Pitt-Greenville Airport. (Public)
Sponsors: Senator Smith (Primary Sponsor).
Referred to:
1 A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
2 AN ACT TO APPROPRIATE FUNDS TO THE PITT-GREENVILLE AIRPORT FOR
3 BUILDING A NEW GENERAL AVIATION TERMINAL.
4 The General Assembly of North Carolina enacts:
5 SECTION 1. Of the funds appropriated from the Highway Fund to the Department
6 of Transportation for the 2025-2026 fiscal year, the sum of ten million dollars ($10,000,000) in
7 nonrecurring funds shall be allocated to the Pitt-Greenville Airport to be used for the purpose of
8 building a new General Aviation Terminal for the purpose of flood mitigation.
9 SECTION 2. This act becomes effective July 1, 2025.
*DRS15108-MWa-35A*
