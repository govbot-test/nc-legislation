Section 1:
H.B. 1202
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF NORTH CAROLINA
Apr 30, 2026
SESSION 2025
HOUSE PRINCIPAL CLERK
H D
Short Title: First Responder Training Track. (Public)
Sponsors: Representative K. Brown.
Referred to:
1 A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
2 AN ACT TO APPROPRIATE FUNDS TO FORSYTH TECHNICAL COMMUNITY
3 COLLEGE TO DEVELOP A LAW ENFORCEMENT DRIVING TRAINING CENTER.
4 The General Assembly of North Carolina enacts:
5 SECTION 1. There is appropriated from the General Fund to the Community
6 Colleges System Office the sum of six million one hundred thousand dollars ($6,100,000) in
7 nonrecurring funds for the 2026-2027 fiscal year to be allocated to Forsyth Technical Community
8 College (Forsyth Tech) to be used to establish a law enforcement driving training center in King,
9 North Carolina, in partnership with the City of Winston-Salem. The center shall include a driving
10 pad, roadway, and instructional building to be used as part of Forsyth Tech's Basic Law
11 Enforcement Training and continuing education programs.
12 SECTION 2. This act becomes effective July 1, 2026.
*DRH10613-NGa-160*
