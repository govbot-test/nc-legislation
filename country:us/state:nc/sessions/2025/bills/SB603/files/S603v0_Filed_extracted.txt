Section 1:
FILED SENATE
Mar 25, 2025
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF NORTH CAROLINA
S.B. 603
SESSION 2025
PRINCIPAL CLERK
S D
Short Title: Funds for Trails and Greenways. (Public)
Sponsors: Senator Mohammed (Primary Sponsor).
Referred to:
1 A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
2 AN ACT TO APPROPRIATE FUNDS FOR TRAILS AND GREENWAYS IN
3 MECKLENBURG, IREDELL, AND DAVIDSON COUNTIES.
4 The General Assembly of North Carolina enacts:
5 SECTION 1. Appropriation. – The sum of one million five hundred thousand dollars
6 ($1,500,000) in nonrecurring funds for the 2025-2026 fiscal year is appropriated from the
7 General Fund to the Department of Natural and Cultural Resources to provide a grant to the Town
8 of Davidson (Town) for the following purposes:
9 (1) One million dollars ($1,000,000) for the construction of portions of the
10 Carolina Thread Trail connecting the Town to trail segments in Iredell and
11 Cabarrus Counties.
12 (2) Five hundred thousand dollars ($500,000) for construction of segments of the
13 West Branch Nature Preserve Greenway in and connecting to the Town.
14 SECTION 2. G.S. 143C-5-2 does not apply to the appropriations in this act.
15 SECTION 3. This act becomes effective July 1, 2025.
*DRS35228-MHa-46*
