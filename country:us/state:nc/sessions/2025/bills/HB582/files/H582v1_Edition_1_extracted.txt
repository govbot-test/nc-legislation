GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF NORTH CAROLINA
SESSION 2025
H 1
HOUSE BILL 582
Short Title: Providing Relief to Impacted Families Act. (Public)
Sponsors: Representatives Johnson and Scott (Primary Sponsors).
For a complete list of sponsors, refer to the North Carolina General Assembly web site.
Referred to: Finance, if favorable, Rules, Calendar, and Operations of the House
April 1, 2025
1 A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
2 AN ACT TO ENACT THE PROVIDING RELIEF TO IMPACTED FAMILIES ACT.
3 The General Assembly of North Carolina enacts:
4 SECTION 1. This act shall be known as the "Providing Relief to Impacted Families
5 Act."
6 SECTION 2. G.S. 105‑449.106(a) reads as rewritten:
7 "(a) Nonprofits. – A nonprofit organization listed below that purchases and uses motor
8 fuel may receive a quarterly refund, for the excise tax paid during the preceding quarter, at a rate
9 equal to the tax rate in effect under G.S. 105-449.80 for the time period for which the refund is
10 claimed, less one cent (1¢) per gallon.
11 Any of the following entities may receive a refund under this subsection:
12 (1) Repealed by Session Laws 2002-108, s. 13, effective January 1, 2003.
13 (2) A private, nonprofit organization that transports passengers under contract
14 with or at the express designation of a unit of local government.
15 (3) A volunteer fire department.
16 (4) A volunteer rescue squad.
17 (5) A sheltered workshop recognized by the Department of Health and Human
18 Services.
19 (6) A nonprofit organization transporting food for free distribution to residents of
20 this State."
21 SECTION 3. This act is effective when it becomes law.
