Short Title: Medicaid Prepaid Health Plan Practices. (Public)
Sponsors:
Referred to:
March 13, 2025
1 A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
2 AN ACT TO ALLOW CERTAIN MEDICAID PREPAID HEALTH PLAN PRACTICES.
3 The General Assembly of North Carolina enacts:
4 SECTION 1. G.S. 108D-65(b), as amended by Section 3C.15 of S.L. 2026-1, reads
5 as rewritten:
6 "(b) Except as required by federal law or regulation, the Department shall not prohibit
7 PHPs from requiring doing any of the following:
8 (1) Requiring itemized bills for inpatient hospital outlier claims that are greater
9 than two hundred fifty thousand dollars ($250,000) or more than two standard
10 deviations from the median mean claim amount of the applicable billing code.
11 (2) Aligning claims operations with national standards for coding, edits, and
12 claims adjudication, except that the Department may set parameters to ensure
13 consistent claims coding among PHPs.
14 (3) Directing inpatient hospital and inpatient hospital laboratory services to
15 outpatient settings where appropriate, except that the Department may
16 establish standards for when direction to outpatient settings is appropriate. The
17 Department is authorized to adopt rules establishing these standards."
18 SECTION 2. This act is effective when it becomes law and applies to contracts
19 entered into or amended on or after that date.
