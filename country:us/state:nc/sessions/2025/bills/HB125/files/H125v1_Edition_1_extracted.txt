Section 1:
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF NORTH CAROLINA
SESSION 2025
H 1
Short Title: Adopt Official State Star. (Public)
Sponsors: Representatives Lambeth, Paré, K. Hall, and White (Primary Sponsors).
For a complete list of sponsors, refer to the North Carolina General Assembly web site.
Referred to: Rules, Calendar, and Operations of the House
February 17, 2025
1 A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
2 AN ACT ADOPTING THE MORAVIAN STAR AS THE OFFICIAL STATE STAR.
3 Whereas, many believe the Moravian star originated in Germany in the 1830s during
4 a geometry lesson at the Moravian Boys School; and
5 Whereas, the star was adopted by the Moravian Church as an advent symbol and later
6 became a popular symbol all around the world; and
7 Whereas, the Moravian star is usually hung the first Sunday of Advent and remains
8 up until Epiphany, January 6; and
9 Whereas, it is fitting to recognize the positive impact the Moravians have had on
10 North Carolina's culture by adopting the Moravian star as the official State star; Now, therefore,
11 The General Assembly of North Carolina enacts:
12 SECTION 1. Chapter 145 of the General Statutes is amended by adding the
13 following new section to read:
14 "§ 145-52. State star.
15 The Moravian star is adopted as the official star of the State of North Carolina."
16 SECTION 2. This act is effective when it becomes law.
