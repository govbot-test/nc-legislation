Section 1:
H.B. 996
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF NORTH CAROLINA
Apr 10, 2025
SESSION 2025
HOUSE PRINCIPAL CLERK
H D
Short Title: Further Limit General Fund Operating Budget. (Public)
Sponsors: Representative Riddell.
Referred to:
1 A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
2 AN ACT TO REDUCE THE INCREASE IN THE STATE GENERAL FUND OPERATING
3 BUDGET BY REDUCING THE STATUTORY CAP ON THE OPERATING BUDGET AS
4 A PERCENTAGE OF TOTAL STATE PERSONAL INCOME TO SIX PERCENT.
5 The General Assembly of North Carolina enacts:
6 SECTION 1. G.S. 143C-4-6 reads as rewritten:
7 "§ 143C-4-6. General Fund operating budget size limited.
8 (a) Size Limitation. – Except as otherwise provided in this section, the General Fund
9 operating budget each fiscal year shall not be greater than seven percent (7%) six percent (6%)
10 of the projected total State personal income for that fiscal year.
11 (a1) Extraordinary Exceedance of Budget Size Limitation. – In each fiscal year, funds in
12 excess of the size limitation set forth in subsection (a) of this section shall be available for
13 expenditure upon a two-thirds vote of the membership of the Senate and the House of
14 Representatives present and voting.
15 …."
16 SECTION 2. This act becomes effective July 1, 2025, and applies to General Fund
17 operating budgets adopted by the General Assembly on or after that date.
*DRH10426-MHa-99A*
