GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF NORTH CAROLINA

SESSION 2025
HOUSE PRINCIPAL CLERK
H D
HOUSE BILL DRH30544-MCa-320
Short Title: Funding for Shotgun House Museum. (Public)
Sponsors: Representative K. Brown.
Referred to:
1 A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
2 AN ACT TO PROVIDE FUNDING FOR THE SHOTGUN HOUSE MUSEUM.
3 The General Assembly of North Carolina enacts:
4 SECTION 1. There is appropriated from the General Fund to the Office of State
5 Budget and Management the sum of two hundred fifty thousand dollars ($250,000) in
6 nonrecurring funds for the 2026-2027 fiscal year to provide a directed grant to Triad Cultural
7 Arts, Inc., a nonprofit organization, to support improvements to the restored Shotgun House in
8 the City of Winston-Salem to further its purpose as serving as an immersive museum for students,
9 visitors, and community gatherings.
10 SECTION 2. This act becomes effective July 1, 2026.
