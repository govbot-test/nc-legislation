Section 1:
FILED SENATE
Feb 27, 2025
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF NORTH CAROLINA
S.B. 203
SESSION 2025
PRINCIPAL CLERK
S D
Short Title: City of Wilmington/Property Conveyances. (Local)
Sponsors: Senators Lee and Rabon (Primary Sponsors).
Referred to:
1 A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
2 AN ACT AUTHORIZING THE CITY OF WILMINGTON TO SELL, EXCHANGE, OR
3 OTHERWISE TRANSFER REAL PROPERTY SUBJECT TO RESTRICTIONS.
4 The General Assembly of North Carolina enacts:
5 SECTION 1. Section 13.8 of the Charter of the City of Wilmington, as amended by
6 Chapter 615 of the Session Laws of 1991, reads as rewritten:
7 "Sec. 13.8. Conditions and Restrictions on the Sale of Property. (a) The City of
8 Wilmington may make any sale, exchange, or transfer of property pursuant to G.S. 160A-268,
9 160A-269, 160A-270, or 160A-271 in any manner authorized by general or local law, subject to
10 such covenants, conditions, and restrictions as the City Council may deem to be in the public
11 interest.
12 (b) Conveyance of real property with restrictions. The City Council may, in addition to
13 other authorized means, approve the sale, exchange, or transfer of the fee or any lesser interest
14 in real property, either by public sale or by negotiated private sale. The transfer shall be in
15 furtherance of adopted City policies or plans for the area. The City may attach to the transfer and
16 to the interest conveyed any covenants, conditions, or restrictions, or a combination of them, the
17 City deems necessary to further the adopted policies or plans. The consideration received by the
18 City for the conveyance may reflect the restricted use of the property resulting from the
19 covenants, conditions, or restrictions. The City may invite bids or written proposals, including
20 detailed development plans and site plans, for the purchase of any such property or property
21 interest, whether by sale, exchange, or other transfer, pursuant to the specifications as may be
22 approved by the City. A sale, exchange, or other transfer of real property, or interest therein,
23 pursuant to this section may be made contingent upon any necessary rezoning of the property.
24 Any conveyance under this section may be made only pursuant to a resolution of the City Council
25 authorizing the conveyance. Notice by publication of the proposed transaction shall be given at
26 least 10 days prior to adoption of the resolution, and the notice shall generally describe (i) the
27 property involved, (ii) the nature of the interest to be conveyed, and (iii) all of the material terms
28 of the proposed transaction, including any covenants, conditions, or restrictions which may be
29 applicable. The notice shall give the time and place of the City Council meeting where the
30 proposed transaction will be considered and shall announce the Council's intention to authorize
31 the proposed transaction. The authority contained in this section is in addition to, and not in
32 limitation of, any other authority granted by this Charter or any other general or local law."
33 SECTION 2. This act is effective when it becomes law.
