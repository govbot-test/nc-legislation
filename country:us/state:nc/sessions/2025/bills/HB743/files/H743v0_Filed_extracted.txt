GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF NORTH CAROLINA

SESSION 2025
HOUSE PRINCIPAL CLERK
H D
HOUSE BILL DRH10293-NL-65
Short Title: Allow Live-In DSPs in Group Homes. (Public)
Sponsors: Representative Crawford.
Referred to:
1 A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
2 AN ACT TO ALLOW LIVE-IN DIRECT SUPPORT PROFESSIONALS IN GROUP HOMES.
3 The General Assembly of North Carolina enacts:
4 SECTION 1. The Department of Health and Human Services, Division of Health
5 Service Regulation (DHSR), shall adopt rules necessary to allow direct support professionals
6 providing services to a client being served in a facility licensed under 10A NCAC 27G
7 .5601(c)(3) to permanently reside in that facility.
8 SECTION 2. DHSR shall report to the Joint Legislative Oversight Committee on
9 Health and Human Services by September 30, 2025, on its progress toward implementing the
10 changes under this act. This report shall include any requested legislative changes.
11 SECTION 3. This act is effective when it becomes law.
