GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF NORTH CAROLINA
SESSION 2025
S 1
SENATE JOINT RESOLUTION 763
Sponsors: Senator Rabon (Primary Sponsor).
Referred to: Rules and Operations of the Senate
May 6, 2025
1 A JOINT RESOLUTION PROVIDING FOR THE CONFIRMATION OF THE
2 APPOINTMENT OF DON VAN DER VAART TO THE UTILITIES COMMISSION.
3 Whereas, under the provisions of G.S. 62-10, appointments made by the State
4 Treasurer to membership on the North Carolina Utilities Commission are subject to confirmation
5 by the General Assembly by joint resolution; and
6 Whereas, the State Treasurer has submitted to the presiding officers of the Senate and
7 the House of Representatives the appointment of Don van der Vaart to serve a term that begins
8 on July 1, 2025, and expires on June 30, 2031, on the North Carolina Utilities Commission; Now,
9 therefore,
10 Be it resolved by the Senate, the House of Representatives concurring:
11 SECTION 1. The appointment of Don van der Vaart of Wake County to the North
12 Carolina Utilities Commission for a term that begins July 1, 2025, and expires June 30, 2031, is
13 confirmed.
14 SECTION 2. This resolution is effective upon ratification.
