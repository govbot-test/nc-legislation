Section 1:
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF NORTH CAROLINA
SESSION 2025
RESOLUTION 2025-4
SENATE JOINT RESOLUTION 763
A JOINT RESOLUTION PROVIDING FOR THE CONFIRMATION OF THE
APPOINTMENT OF DON VAN DER VAART TO THE UTILITIES COMMISSION.
Whereas, under the provisions of G.S. 62-10, appointments made by the State
Treasurer to membership on the North Carolina Utilities Commission are subject to confirmation
by the General Assembly by joint resolution; and
Whereas, the State Treasurer has submitted to the presiding officers of the Senate and
the House of Representatives the appointment of Don van der Vaart to serve a term that begins
on July 1, 2025, and expires on June 30, 2031, on the North Carolina Utilities Commission; Now,
therefore,
Be it resolved by the Senate, the House of Representatives concurring:

Section 2:
SECTION 1. The appointment of Don van der Vaart of Wake County to the North
Carolina Utilities Commission for a term that begins July 1, 2025, and expires June 30, 2031, is
confirmed.

Section 3:
SECTION 2. This resolution is effective upon ratification.
In the General Assembly read three times and ratified this the 22nd day of May, 2025.
s/ Phil Berger
President Pro Tempore of the Senate
s/ Destin Hall
Speaker of the House of Representatives
*S763-v-2*
