Section 1:
FILED SENATE
Mar 25, 2025
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF NORTH CAROLINA
S.B. 596
SESSION 2025
PRINCIPAL CLERK
S D
Short Title: Critical Campus Funding Repairs. (Public)
Sponsors: Senators Blue, Lee, and Robinson (Primary Sponsors).
Referred to:
1 A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
2 AN ACT TO PROVIDE FUNDING TO NORTH CAROLINA CENTRAL UNIVERSITY FOR
3 NEEDED CAMPUS REPAIRS.
4 The General Assembly of North Carolina enacts:
5 SECTION 1. There is appropriated from the State Capital and Infrastructure Fund,
6 established in G.S. 143C-3-4.1(a), to the Board of Governors of The University of North Carolina
7 the sum of one hundred sixty-nine million dollars ($169,000,000) in nonrecurring funds for the
8 2025-2026 fiscal year to be allocated to North Carolina Central University to be used for the
9 following:
10 (1) One hundred million dollars ($100,000,000) for campus-wide use to address
11 critical HVAC, fire safety, and building code deficiencies to repair, upgrade,
12 and replace failing plumbing, HVAC infrastructure, fire alarm, building code,
13 and mechanical systems across the campus.
14 (2) Nine million dollars ($9,000,000) for campus-wide roof replacements and
15 building envelope repairs to prevent further damage and reduce future costs
16 to prevent water damage, mold growth, and further structural deterioration
17 across campus.
18 (3) Sixty million dollars ($60,000,000) to replace failing electrical, storm and
19 domestic water, and steam distribution infrastructure; overhaul electrical and
20 steam distribution systems to prevent energy loss and reduce the associated
21 costs; and replace systems and their components that are at end-of-life and
22 require immediate replacement.
23 SECTION 2. This act becomes effective July 1, 2025.
*DRS45265-MQa-82C*
