1 A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
2 AN ACT TO AUTHORIZE THE DIVISION OF MOTOR VEHICLES TO ISSUE A RED
3 WOLF SPECIAL REGISTRATION PLATE, TO PROVIDE FOR THE DISTRIBUTION
4 OF THE FEES FROM THE PLATE TO SUPPORT RED WOLF CONSERVATION, TO
5 APPROPRIATE FUNDS FOR THE DEVELOPMENT AND INITIAL ISSUANCE OF
6 THE PLATE AND FOR CAMPUS PROMOTION OF THE PLATE, AND TO DIRECT
7 NORTH CAROLINA STATE UNIVERSITY TO PROMOTE THE PLATE ON CAMPUS.
8 Whereas, the red wolf is one of the rarest canids in the world and is native to North
9 Carolina and the southeastern United States; and
10 Whereas, North Carolina holds a unique place in the history and future of red wolf
11 conservation; and
12 Whereas, the preservation of native wildlife is important to the natural heritage,
13 environmental health, educational mission, and cultural identity of the State; and
14 Whereas, the creation of a Red Wolf special registration plate would allow residents
15 of the State to demonstrate support for the preservation of this iconic native species; and
16 Whereas, revenues generated from the issuance of the Red Wolf special registration
17 plate can provide a recurring source of support for red wolf conservation, management, research,
18 monitoring, and habitat preservation and restoration; and
19 Whereas, it is in the interest of the State to provide one-time funds to support the
20 initial design, development, and issuance of the plate so that the plate may be made available
21 without unnecessary delay; and
22 Whereas, North Carolina State University, as a leading public institution with the
23 Wolfpack identity and a broad statewide alumni and supporter network, is well positioned to help
24 promote awareness of the Red Wolf special registration plate and its conservation purpose; Now,
25 therefore,
26 The General Assembly of North Carolina enacts:

3 SECTION 6. North Carolina State University, through the College of Veterinary
4 Medicine, shall actively promote the Red Wolf special registration plate on campus and through
5 appropriate university communications channels by, at a minimum, doing all of the following:
6 (1) Include information about the availability and purpose of the plate on an
7 appropriate University webpage.
8 (2) Promote the plate through student, alumni, faculty, staff, and athletics
9 communications, as appropriate.
10 (3) Coordinate, as appropriate, with the Division of Motor Vehicles and the North
11 Carolina Wildlife Resources Commission on messaging regarding the plate
12 and the conservation purpose for which plate proceeds are used.
13 (4) Make reasonable efforts to market the plate at campus events and through
14 other outreach channels under the University's control.
15 SECTION 7. No later than September 1, 2027, North Carolina State University, in
16 consultation with the Division of Motor Vehicles and the North Carolina Wildlife Resources
17 Commission, shall report to the Joint Legislative Transportation Oversight Committee, the
18 Senate Appropriations Committee on Agriculture, Natural, and Economic Resources, the House
19 Appropriations Committee on Agriculture and Natural and Economic Resources, and the Fiscal
20 Research Division on the University's efforts to promote the plate and the number of plates
21 issued.
22 SECTION 8. There is appropriated from the Highway Fund to the Department of
23 Transportation, Division of Motor Vehicles, the sum of fifteen thousand dollars ($15,000) in
24 nonrecurring funds for the 2026-2027 fiscal year to develop the Red Wolf special registration
25 plate authorized by this act, including plate design, programming, setup, production preparation,
26 and associated administrative costs for initial implementation.
27 SECTION 9. There is appropriated from the General Fund to The University of
28 North Carolina Board of Governors for allocation to North Carolina State University, College of
29 Veterinary Medicine, the sum of fifty thousand dollars ($50,000) in nonrecurring funds for the
30 2026-2027 fiscal year to support the initial promotion and public awareness of the Red Wolf
31 special registration plate established by this act.
32 SECTION 10. Sections 8 and 9 of this act become effective July 1, 2026. The
33 remainder of this act is effective when it becomes law.
