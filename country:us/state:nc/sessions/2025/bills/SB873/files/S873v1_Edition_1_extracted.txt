GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF NORTH CAROLINA
SESSION 2025
S 1
SENATE BILL 873
Short Title: Innovations Waiver Tiers. (Public)
Sponsors: Senator Burgin (Primary Sponsor).
Referred to: Rules and Operations of the Senate
April 29, 2026
1 A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
2 AN ACT TO ESTABLISH A PLAN TO CREATE A TIERED INNOVATIONS WAIVER
3 PROGRAM.
4 The General Assembly of North Carolina enacts:
5 SECTION 1. The Department of Health and Human Services, Division of Health
6 Benefits (DHB), is directed to consult with Local Management Entities/Managed Care
7 Organizations (LME/MCOs) and other stakeholders to develop a plan for addressing the needs
8 of individuals on the NC Innovations waiver waitlist. This plan shall include the creation of a
9 tiered program consisting of three tiers, including a benefit capped at twenty-five thousand
10 dollars ($25,000), a benefit capped at seventy-five thousand dollars ($75,000), and a full waiver
11 benefit.
12 SECTION 2. No later than December 1, 2026, DHB shall submit a report on the plan
13 required by Section 1 of this act to the House of Representatives Committee on Health, the Senate
14 Committee on Health Care, the Joint Legislative Oversight Committee on Medicaid, and the
15 Fiscal Research Division.
16 SECTION 3. Effective July 1, 2025, there is appropriated from the General Fund to
17 the Department of Health and Human Services, Division of Health Benefits (DHB), the sum of
18 one hundred thousand dollars ($100,000) in nonrecurring funds for the 2025-2026 fiscal year to
19 be used to implement this act.
20 SECTION 4. This act is effective when it becomes law.
