1 A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
2 AN ACT TO PROVIDE THAT REGULAR MUNICIPAL ELECTIONS IN ALL OF THE
3 TOWNS IN PAMLICO COUNTY SHALL BE HELD IN EVEN-NUMBERED YEARS.
4 The General Assembly of North Carolina enacts:
5 SECTION 1.(a) Sections 3-3 and 3-4 of the Charter of the Town of Grantsboro,
6 being S.L. 1997-446, as amended by S.L. 2011-83, read as rewritten:
7 "Section 3-3. Term of Office of Council Members. Members of the Council are elected to
8 four-year terms in 2011 and quadrennially thereafter.terms.
9 "Section 3-4. Mayor; Term of Office. The Mayor shall be elected by the qualified voters of
10 the Town in 2011 and quadrennially thereafter for a four-year term."
11 SECTION 1.(b) Section 4-1 of the Charter of the Town of Grantsboro, being S.L.
12 1997-446, reads as rewritten:
13 "Section 4-1. Elections. Regular municipal elections shall be held at the time of the general
14 election in even-numbered years. Council members for the Town of Grantsboro shall be elected
15 on a nonpartisan plurality basis and the results determined in accordance with G.S. 163-292.
16 Except as otherwise provided, the election shall be conducted in accordance with the uniform
17 municipal election laws in Chapter 163 of the General Statutes."
18 SECTION 1.(c) Section 4-2 of the Charter of the Town of Grantsboro, being S.L.
19 1997-446, is repealed.
20 SECTION 1.(d) No municipal elections shall be conducted in the Town of
21 Grantsboro in 2027. The terms of office for the Council members and Mayor in the Town of
22 Grantsboro serving on the effective date of this act shall be extended by one year. Regular
23 municipal elections shall be conducted in the Town of Grantsboro every four years beginning in
24 2028. Beginning in 2028, five Council members and a Mayor shall be elected to serve four-year
25 terms.
26 SECTION 2.(a) Section 3 of the Charter of the Town of Minnesott Beach, being
27 Chapter 890 of the 1971 Session Laws, reads as rewritten:
28 "Sec. 3. The governing body of said Town shall consist of a mayor and four commissioners
29 who shall be elected at the time and in the manner provided in the General
30 Statutes.commissioners. The mayor and four commissioners shall be elected to serve two-year
31 terms. Regular municipal elections shall be held at the time of the general election in each
32 even-numbered year. The mayor and commissioners shall be elected on a nonpartisan plurality
33 basis, and the results determined in accordance with G.S. 163-292. Except as otherwise provided,
34 the election shall be conducted in accordance with the uniform municipal election laws in Chapter
35 163 of the General Statutes."
