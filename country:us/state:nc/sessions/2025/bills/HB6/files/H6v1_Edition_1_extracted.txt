GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF NORTH CAROLINA
SESSION 2025
H 1
HOUSE BILL 6
Short Title: Oriental Election Changes - Even Years. (Local)
Sponsors: Representative Kidwell.
For a complete list of sponsors, refer to the North Carolina General Assembly web site.
Referred to: Rules, Calendar, and Operations of the House
January 30, 2025
1 A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
2 AN ACT TO PROVIDE THAT REGULAR MUNICIPAL ELECTIONS IN THE TOWN OF
3 ORIENTAL SHALL BE HELD IN EVEN-NUMBERED YEARS.
4 The General Assembly of North Carolina enacts:
5 SECTION 1.(a) Section 5 of the Charter of the Town of Oriental, being Chapter 184
6 of the Private Laws of 1899, reads as rewritten:
7 "Section 5. That there shall be held on the First Monday in May, eighteen hundred and ninety-
8 nine, and on the first May every year thereafter, an election for mayor and commissioners for
9 said town at some convenient place therein, to be selected by the commissioners, which election
10 shall be held by a registrar and two poll holders under regulations in other respects prescribed by
11 the state election law, which from time to tome may be enacted – at which election, all duly
12 qualified voters of the State of North Carolina under the state election laws existing at the time
13 of such election, residing in the corporate limits of said town, shall be permitted to
14 vote.Beginning in 2026, regular municipal elections shall be held on the Tuesday after the first
15 Monday in November of each even-numbered year in accordance with the uniform municipal
16 election laws of North Carolina. In 2026 and biennially thereafter, a mayor and five town
17 commissioners shall be selected by the qualified voters of the town to each serve two-year terms."
18 SECTION 1.(b) In the 2025 municipal election, the mayor and five town
19 commissioners shall be elected for a one-year term. Beginning in 2026, municipal elections shall
20 be conducted in the Town of Oriental in even-numbered years and biennially thereafter.
21 SECTION 2. This act is effective when it becomes law and applies to elections held
22 on or after that date.
