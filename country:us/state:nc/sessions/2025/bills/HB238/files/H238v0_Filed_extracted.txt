GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF NORTH CAROLINA

SESSION 2025
HOUSE PRINCIPAL CLERK
H D
HOUSE BILL DRH10093-NGa-69
Short Title: Reimburse Buncombe Co. for Cost of Study. (Public)
Sponsors: Representative Prather.
Referred to:
1 A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
2 AN ACT TO REIMBURSE BUNCOMBE COUNTY FOR THE SCHOOL DISTRICT
3 CONSOLIDATION STUDY REQUIRED BY LAW.
4 The General Assembly of North Carolina enacts:
5 SECTION 1. There is appropriated from the General Fund to the Department of
6 Public Instruction the sum of three hundred thousand dollars ($300,000) in nonrecurring funds
7 for the 2025-2026 fiscal year to be allocated to Buncombe County as a reimbursement for the
8 study of merging the Buncombe County School Administrative Unit and the City of Asheville
9 School Administrative Unit that was required by subsection (e) of Section 4 of S.L. 2023-128.
10 SECTION 2. This act becomes effective July 1, 2025.
