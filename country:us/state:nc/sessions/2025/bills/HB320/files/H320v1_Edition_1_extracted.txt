GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF NORTH CAROLINA
SESSION 2025
H 1
HOUSE BILL 320
Short Title: Town of Pineville/Reserve Police. (Local)
Sponsors: Representative Budd.
For a complete list of sponsors, refer to the North Carolina General Assembly web site.
Referred to: State and Local Government, if favorable, Rules, Calendar, and Operations of the
House
March 10, 2025
1 A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
2 AN ACT TO AMEND THE CHARTER OF THE TOWN OF PINEVILLE TO AUTHORIZE
3 RESERVE POLICE AND THEIR COMPENSATION WHEN CALLED INTO ACTIVE
4 DUTY.
5 The General Assembly of North Carolina enacts:
6 SECTION 1. The Charter of the Town of Pineville, being Chapter 296 of the Session
7 Laws of 1965, as amended, is amended by adding a new section to read:
8 "Sec. 17.A. Reserve Police Division. In addition to any auxiliary police department
9 established pursuant to G.S. 160A-282, the town may establish a reserve police division whose
10 officers may be compensated for any active duty time. The town council shall fix the amount of
11 compensation for officers in the reserve police division and cause the compensation to be paid."
12 SECTION 2. This act is effective when it becomes law.
