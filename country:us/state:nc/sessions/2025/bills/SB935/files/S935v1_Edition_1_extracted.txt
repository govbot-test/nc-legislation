Section 1:
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF NORTH CAROLINA
SESSION 2025
S 1
Short Title: Increase Number of Magistrates/Mecklenburg. (Public)
Sponsors: Senator Mohammed (Primary Sponsor).
Referred to: Rules and Operations of the Senate
April 30, 2026
1 A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
2 AN ACT TO INCREASE THE NUMBER OF MAGISTRATES SERVING IN
3 MECKLENBURG COUNTY.
4 The General Assembly of North Carolina enacts:
5 SECTION 1. G.S. 7A-133(c) reads as rewritten:
6 "(c) Each county shall have the numbers of magistrates and additional seats of district
7 court, as set forth in the following table:
8 Magistrates Additional
9 County Min. Seats of Court
10 …
11 Mecklenburg 38.543.5
12 …."
13 SECTION 2. There is appropriated from the General Fund to the Administrative
14 Office of the Courts the sum of two hundred thirty-six thousand one hundred forty dollars
15 ($236,140) in recurring funds beginning in the 2026-2027 fiscal year to be used to hire five
16 additional magistrates to serve in Mecklenburg County pursuant to Section 1 of this act.
17 SECTION 3. This act becomes effective July 1, 2026.
*S935-v-1*
