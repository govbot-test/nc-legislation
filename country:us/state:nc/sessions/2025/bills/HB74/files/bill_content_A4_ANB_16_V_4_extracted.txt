5 "NORTH CAROLINA STATE BAR GRIEVANCE REVIEW COMMITTEE
6 SECTION 27.11.(a) Establishment; Composition. – There is created the State Bar Review
7 Committee (Committee). The Committee shall be composed of seven members as follows:
8 (1) One member appointed by the President Pro Tempore of the Senate.
9 (2) One member appointed by the Speaker of the House of Representatives.
10 (3) One member appointed by the Governor.
11 (4) Three members appointed by the Chief Justice of the Supreme Court of North
12 Carolina, of which one shall be an Associate Justice of the Supreme Court of
13 North Carolina and one shall be a Judge of the North Carolina Court of
14 Appeals.
15 (5) The President of the State Bar serving in that position on the date this section
16 becomes law, who shall serve until the Committee terminates.Bar or his or her
17 designee.
18 …
19 SECTION 27.11.(c1) Additional Duties. – In addition to the duties described in subsection
20 (c) of this section, the Committee may review any other aspect or area of the North Carolina State
21 Bar the Committee deems concerning, needing improvement, or necessary in fulfilling its duties
22 under this section.
23 …
24 SECTION 27.11.(e) Report. Expiration. – By April 1, 2024, the Committee shall submit a
25 report to the Joint Legislative Commission on Governmental Operations containing any
26 legislative recommendations to address and alleviate the concerns listed in subsection (c) of this
27 section of the grievance review process. The report shall also contain any potential improvements
28 and changes in oversight of the North Carolina State Bar. The Committee shall expire upon
29 submitting the report under this subsection.The Committee shall expire on December 31, 2026.
30 …."".
