H.B. 74
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF NORTH CAROLINA
Feb 10, 2025
SESSION 2025
HOUSE PRINCIPAL CLERK
H D
Short Title: House Budget Technical Corrections. (Public)
Sponsors: Representative Lambeth.
Referred to:
1 A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
2 AN ACT TO MAKE VARIOUS TECHNICAL AND CLARIFYING CHANGES TO THE
3 CURRENT OPERATIONS APPROPRIATIONS ACT OF 2023 AND TO OTHER ACTS.
4 The General Assembly of North Carolina enacts:
5
6 PART I. GENERAL
7
8 EXTEND THE TIME LINE FOR CERTAIN DIRECTED GRANTS TO NON-STATE
9 ENTITIES
10 SECTION 1.1.(a) This section applies to any directed grants appropriated as
11 nonrecurring funds in S.L. 2022-74 for the 2022-2023 fiscal year that (i) remain unexpended as
12 of the effective date of this section and (ii) are subject to reversion on December 31, 2024, as set
13 forth in Section 1 of S.L. 2024-40. Notwithstanding any provision of law to the contrary, the
14 grants described by this section shall not revert on December 31, 2024, and shall remain available
15 for expenditure for the purpose for which the funds were appropriated until the earlier of the date
16 the funds are expended or the date the funds revert pursuant to subsection (b) of this section.
17 SECTION 1.1.(b) Any funds described in subsection (a) of this section that remain
18 unexpended as of December 31, 2024, shall revert to the appropriate fund at the end of the
19 2025-2026 fiscal year.
20 SECTION 1.1.(c) This section is effective retroactively to December 31, 2024.
