3 SECTION 3. Chapter 144 of the General Statutes is amended by adding a new
4 section to read:
5 "§ 144-7.1. Display of official governmental flags on State and local government property.
6 (a) Only the following official governmental flags may be flown or displayed by a State
7 institution or a political subdivision of the State on the premises of a building of a State institution
8 or a political subdivision of the State:
9 (1) The flag of the United States of America.
10 (2) The flag of the State of North Carolina.
11 (3) The flag of a county, city, or other governmental agency, school, or other
12 educational facility.
13 (4) The flag of any military branch of the United States of America.
14 (5) The Prisoner of War/Missing in Action (POW/MIA) flag.
15 (6) The Honor and Remember flag.
16 (7) The flag of nations recognized by the United States of America.
17 (8) The flag of any state or territory of the United States.
18 (9) The flag of a political subdivision of any state or territory of the United States.
19 (10) The flag of any Indian tribe or Indian group recognized by State or federal
20 law.
21 (b) For purposes of this section, the term "displayed" means the governmental flag or an
22 image of the governmental flag is placed anywhere, including the walls of a facility, employee
23 breakroom, and sidewalks.
24 (c) Any employee of the State or political subdivision of the State who violates this
25 section is subject to disciplinary action, including termination from employment."
26 SECTION 4. This act becomes effective October 1, 2025, and applies to violations
27 occurring on or after that date.
