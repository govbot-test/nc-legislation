Section 1:
H.B. 244
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF NORTH CAROLINA
Feb 27, 2025
SESSION 2025
HOUSE PRINCIPAL CLERK
H D
Short Title: Depoliticize Government Property Act. (Public)
Sponsors: Representative Echevarria.
Referred to:
1 A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
2 AN ACT TO PROVIDE THAT ONLY CERTAIN GOVERNMENTAL FLAGS MAY BE
3 FLOWN OR DISPLAYED ON PROPERTY OWNED OR LEASED BY THE STATE OR
4 A POLITICAL SUBDIVISION OF THE STATE.
5 The General Assembly of North Carolina enacts:
6 SECTION 1. Chapter 144 of the General Statutes is amended by adding a new
7 section to read:
8 "§ 144-7.1. Display of official governmental flags on State and local government property.
9 (a) Only the following governmental flags may be flown or displayed on property owned
10 or leased by the State or a political subdivision of the State:
11 (1) The flag of the United States of America.
12 (2) The flag of the State of North Carolina.
13 (3) The flag of a county, city, or other governmental agency, school, or other
14 educational facility.
15 (4) The flag of any military branch of the United States of America.
16 (5) The Prisoner of War/Missing in Action (POW/MIA) flag.
17 (6) The Honor and Remember flag.
18 (7) The flag of nations recognized by the United States of America during a visit
19 by the nation's head of state or national delegation.
20 (b) A governmental flag shall be flown or displayed according to the patriotic customs
21 set forth in 4 U.S.C. §§ 5-10, as amended. For purposes of this section, the term "displayed"
22 means the governmental flag or an image of the governmental flag is placed anywhere on the
23 premises of the property owned or leased by the State or political subdivision of the State,
24 including the walls of a facility, employee breakroom, and sidewalks.
25 (c) Any person violating the provisions of this section, including a second or subsequent
26 violation, shall be guilty of a Class 3 misdemeanor."
27 SECTION 2. G.S. 144-7 reads as rewritten:
28 "§ 144-7. Display of official governmental flags; public restrictions.flags on private
29 property.
30 (a) A county, city, consolidated city-county, or unified government shall not prohibit an
31 official governmental flag from being flown or displayed if the official governmental flag is
32 flown or displayed:
33 (1) In accordance with the patriotic customs set forth in 4 U.S.C. §§ 5-10, as
34 amended; and
35 (2) Upon private or public property with the consent of either the owner of the
36 property or of any person having lawful control of the property.
*DRH10081-LG-16B*
General Assembly Of North Carolina Session 2025
1 …."
2 SECTION 3. This act is effective when it becomes law.
