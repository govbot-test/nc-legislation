Section 1:
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF NORTH CAROLINA
SESSION 2025
H 1
Short Title: Pitt Co. Bd. of Ed. Elect. Partisan. (Local)
Sponsors: Representative Reeder.
For a complete list of sponsors, refer to the North Carolina General Assembly web site.
Referred to: Election Law, if favorable, Rules, Calendar, and Operations of the House
March 6, 2025
1 A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
2 AN ACT TO PROVIDE FOR THE PARTISAN ELECTION OF MEMBERS TO THE PITT
3 COUNTY BOARD OF EDUCATION.
4 The General Assembly of North Carolina enacts:
5 SECTION 1.(a) Section 1 of Chapter 193 of the 1987 Session Laws, as amended by
6 Section 2(a) of S.L. 2013-318 and Section 3(a) of S.L. 2013-318, reads as rewritten:
7 "Section 1. Beginning in December 2014, the Pitt County Board of Education shall consist
8 of nine members. Nine members Members of the Board of Education shall be elected on a
9 partisan basis, as provided in G.S. 163-291, at the time of the general election in each
10 even-numbered year as terms expire. Candidates for election to the Board of Education shall be
11 nominated at the same time and manner as other county officers. Members shall take office on
12 the first Monday in December following the election. Members shall serve until a successor has
13 been elected and qualified. Except as otherwise provided, the election shall be conducted in
14 accordance with Chapters 115C and 163 of the General Statutes. Members shall be elected from
15 single-member districts, as described in Section 5. Only voters residing in the district may vote
16 for the member from that district."
17 SECTION 1.(b) Section 6 of Chapter 193 of the 1987 Session Laws reads as
18 rewritten:
19 "Sec. 6. Vacancies on the Board shall be filled by appointment by the remaining members of
20 the Board. The person appointed shall serve until the next regularly scheduled election for county
21 boards of education, at which time a person shall be elected to fill the remainder of the unexpired
22 term or, if the term is due to expire that year, a new term. in accordance with G.S. 115C-37.1.
23 The person appointed to fill a vacancy must reside in the same district as the departing member."
24 SECTION 1.(c) This section does not affect the term of office of any member elected
25 in 2022 or 2024 to the Pitt County Board of Education. The members of the Pitt County Board
26 of Education elected in 2022 and 2024, or any member appointed to fill a vacancy for the
27 remainder of an unexpired term for a member elected in 2022 or 2024, shall serve until a
28 successor has been elected and qualified. Any vacancy on the Pitt County Board of Education
29 for a member elected in 2022 and 2024 shall be filled by appointment by the remaining members
30 of the Board.
31 SECTION 1.(d) This section is effective when it becomes law and applies beginning
32 with elections held on or after that date.
33 SECTION 2.(a) G.S. 115C-37.1(d) reads as rewritten:
34 "(d) This section shall apply only to the Hickory City Board of Education and the
35 Newton-Conover City Board of Education and in the following counties: Alleghany, Ashe,
*H302-v-1*
General Assembly Of North Carolina Session 2025
1 Beaufort, Brunswick, Burke, Cabarrus, Caldwell, Carteret, Catawba, Cherokee, Clay, Craven,
2 Dare, Davie, Forsyth, Graham, Harnett, Henderson, Hyde, Iredell, Lee, Lincoln, Madison,
3 McDowell, Mitchell, New Hanover, Onslow, Pamlico, Pender, Pitt, Polk, Rutherford, Stanly,
4 Stokes, Surry, Vance, Washington, and Yancey."
5 SECTION 2.(b) This section becomes effective December 1, 2026.
6 SECTION 3. Except as otherwise provided by this act, this act is effective when it
7 becomes law.
