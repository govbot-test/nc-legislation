Section 1:
FILED SENATE
Feb 27, 2025
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF NORTH CAROLINA
S.B. 197
SESSION 2025
PRINCIPAL CLERK
S D
Short Title: Funds for Smith Reynolds Airport MRO Hangar. (Public)
Sponsors: Senator Lowe (Primary Sponsor).
Referred to:
1 A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
2 AN ACT TO PROVIDE FUNDING FOR THE RENOVATION OF THE SMITH REYNOLDS
3 AIRPORT MAINTENANCE REPAIR AND OVERHAUL (MRO) HANGAR.
4 The General Assembly of North Carolina enacts:
5 SECTION 1. There is appropriated from the General Fund to the Department of
6 Transportation, Division of Aviation, the sum of three million five hundred thousand dollars
7 ($3,500,000) in nonrecurring funds for the 2025-2026 fiscal year to be allocated to Forsyth
8 County for the renovation of the Smith Reynolds Airport Maintenance Repair and Overhaul
9 (MRO) hangar.
10 SECTION 2. The funds shall be used for structural repairs, modernization, HVAC
11 upgrades, and necessary infrastructure enhancements.
12 SECTION 3. The Division shall submit a quarterly report on the use of funds to the
13 Joint Legislative Transportation Oversight Committee and the Fiscal Research Division.
14 SECTION 4. This act becomes effective July 1, 2025.
*DRS15080-MWa-30*
