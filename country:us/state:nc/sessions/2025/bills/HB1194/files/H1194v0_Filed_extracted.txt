4 OPERATIONS CENTER.
5 The General Assembly of North Carolina enacts:
6 SECTION 1. There is appropriated from the General Fund to the Office of State
7 Budget and Management the sum of one million dollars ($1,000,000) in nonrecurring funds for
8 the 2026-2027 fiscal year to be allocated in the form of a grant to Forsyth County to assist the
9 County and the City of Winston-Salem in creating a joint city-county emergency operations
10 center to improve coordination and response during emergencies.
11 SECTION 2. This act becomes effective July 1, 2026.
