GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF NORTH CAROLINA
SESSION 2025
S 1
SENATE RESOLUTION 260
Sponsors: Senator Rabon (Primary Sponsor).
Referred to: Health Care
March 11, 2025
1 A SENATE RESOLUTION RELATING TO THE APPOINTMENT, NOMINATION, AND
2 CONFIRMATION OF DR. DEVDUTTA SANGVAI AS SECRETARY OF THE
3 DEPARTMENT OF HEALTH AND HUMAN SERVICES.
4 Whereas, pursuant to G.S. 143B-9(a), the Governor shall notify the President of the
5 Senate of the name of each person to be nominated as head of a principal State department; and
6 Whereas, each nomination is subject to senatorial advice and consent in conformance
7 with Section 5(8) of Article III of the North Carolina Constitution; and
8 Whereas, the Governor has appointed Dr. Devdutta Sangvai to serve as Secretary of
9 the Department of Health and Human Services pending further action by the Senate; Now,
10 therefore,
11 Be it resolved by the Senate:
12 SECTION 1. That the Senate shall hereby consider whether to confirm Dr. Devdutta
13 Sangvai as Secretary of the Department of Health and Human Services.
14 SECTION 2. This resolution is effective upon adoption.
