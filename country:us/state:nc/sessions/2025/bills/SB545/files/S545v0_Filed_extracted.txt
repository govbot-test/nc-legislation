Section 1:
FILED SENATE
Mar 25, 2025
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF NORTH CAROLINA
S.B. 545
SESSION 2025
PRINCIPAL CLERK
S D
Short Title: State Auditor to Audit General Assembly. (Public)
Sponsors: Senator Everitt (Primary Sponsor).
Referred to:
1 A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
2 AN ACT TO DIRECT THE STATE AUDITOR TO PERIODICALLY AUDIT THE GENERAL
3 ASSEMBLY.
4 The General Assembly of North Carolina enacts:
5 SECTION 1. G.S. 147-64.6(c) reads as rewritten:
6 "(c) Responsibilities. – The Auditor is responsible for the following acts and activities:
7 …
8 (24) The Auditor shall periodically audit the General Assembly."
9 SECTION 2. This act is effective when it becomes law.
*DRS45267-MU-35B*
