GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF NORTH CAROLINA
SESSION 2025
H 1
HOUSE BILL 756
Short Title: Funds for Harmony Empowerment Life Center. (Public)
Sponsors: Representative K. Brown.
For a complete list of sponsors, refer to the North Carolina General Assembly web site.
Referred to: Appropriations, if favorable, Rules, Calendar, and Operations of the House
April 7, 2025
1 A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
2 AN ACT TO APPROPRIATE FUNDS TO THE HARMONY EMPOWERMENT LIFE
3 CENTER FOR EXPANSION OF ITS PROGRAM TO ADDITIONAL COUNTIES.
4 The General Assembly of North Carolina enacts:
5 SECTION 1. There is appropriated from the General Fund to the Office of State
6 Budget and Management the sum of one hundred thousand dollars ($100,000) in nonrecurring
7 funds for the 2025-2026 fiscal year to provide a directed grant to the Harmony Empowerment
8 Life Center, a nonprofit organization, to help expand their "Never Limit An Athlete's Mind"
9 program which aids minorities and their families to improve mental health and prepare them for
10 future opportunities into Wake, Durham, Forsyth, Guilford, Mecklenburg, Union, and Anson
11 Counties.
12 SECTION 2. This act becomes effective July 1, 2025.
