SESSION 2025
S 1
SENATE BILL 883
Short Title: Pinetops Utility Department Appropriation. (Public)
Sponsors: Senator Smith (Primary Sponsor).
Referred to: Rules and Operations of the Senate
April 29, 2026
1 A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
2 AN ACT TO APPROPRIATE FUNDS TO THE TOWN OF PINETOPS FOR EQUIPMENT
3 NEEDS OF THE PUBLIC WORKS DEPARTMENT.
4 The General Assembly of North Carolina enacts:
5 SECTION 1. There is appropriated from the General Fund to the Office of State
6 Budget and Management the sum of one hundred thousand dollars ($100,000) in nonrecurring
7 funds for the 2026-2027 fiscal year to provide a directed grant to the Town of Pinetops for
8 allocation to the public works department operated by the town for equipment needs.
9 SECTION 2. This act becomes effective July 1, 2026.
