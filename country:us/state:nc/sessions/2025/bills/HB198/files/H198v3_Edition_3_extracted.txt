8 ALLOW ABC COMMISSION TO OBTAIN A LOAN FOR A NEW STATE
9 WAREHOUSE
10 SECTION 1.(a) Notwithstanding G.S. 18B-208, the Alcoholic Beverage Control
11 Commission may, with the approval of the Governor, obtain a one-time loan of up to three
12 hundred ten million dollars ($310,000,000) from a financial institution to be used for planning,
13 design, construction, and equipping of a new automated warehouse and associated offices for the
14 Commission, provided that the Commission may not pledge real property owned by the State of
15 North Carolina as collateral. The Commission's obtaining of a loan shall not directly or indirectly
16 or contingently obligate the State to levy or to pledge any form of taxation or to make any
17 appropriation for repayment of the loan. Notwithstanding G.S. 18B-902(g), the Commission
18 shall repay the loan, including interest, from the proceeds of permit fees collected by the
19 Commission. The Commission may prepay any obligation under this section, in whole or in part,
20 without penalty unless the loan agreement expressly provides otherwise. To the extent the
21 proceeds from the fees referenced in this subsection are deemed unappropriated, they are hereby
22 appropriated for the purpose of repaying the loan described in this subsection. For purposes of
23 this section, the term "financial institution" means any banking corporation or trust company,
24 savings and loan association, credit union, insurance company, or related corporation,
25 partnership, foundation, or other institution engaged primarily in lending or investing funds.
26 SECTION 1.(b) This section becomes effective July 1, 2026.

11 Commission may, with the approval of the Governor, obtain a one-time loan of up to three
12 hundred ten million dollars ($310,000,000) from a financial institution to be used for planning,
13 design, construction, and equipping of a new automated warehouse and associated offices for the
14 Commission, provided that the Commission may not pledge real property owned by the State of
15 North Carolina as collateral. The Commission's obtaining of a loan shall not directly or indirectly
16 or contingently obligate the State to levy or to pledge any form of taxation or to make any
17 appropriation for repayment of the loan. Notwithstanding G.S. 18B-902(g), the Commission
18 shall repay the loan, including interest, from the proceeds of permit fees collected by the
19 Commission. The Commission may prepay any obligation under this section, in whole or in part,
20 without penalty unless the loan agreement expressly provides otherwise. To the extent the
21 proceeds from the fees referenced in this subsection are deemed unappropriated, they are hereby
22 appropriated for the purpose of repaying the loan described in this subsection. For purposes of
23 this section, the term "financial institution" means any banking corporation or trust company,
24 savings and loan association, credit union, insurance company, or related corporation,
25 partnership, foundation, or other institution engaged primarily in lending or investing funds.
