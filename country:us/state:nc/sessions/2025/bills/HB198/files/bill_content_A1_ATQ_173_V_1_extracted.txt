3 "TOWN OF ANGIER ABC BOARD DISTRIBUTIONS
4 SECTION 31.(a) Section 6 of Chapter 626, Session Laws of 1969, as amended by
5 Chapter 144, Session Laws of 1971, reads as rewritten:
6 "Sec. 6. The net profits, as determined by quarterly audit, shall be distributed and used as
7 follows:
8 1. Ten per cent (10%) of the net profit shall be paid to the Angier Community
9 Library.allocated to recreation programs within the town limits of the Town of Angier.
10 2. Ten per cent (10%) of the net profit may be used by the town Alcoholic Control
11 Board, in its discretion, for educational programs as to the effect of the use of alcoholic beverages
12 and for the rehabilitation of alcoholics. Whenever a person becomes an inebriate from the use of
13 alcoholic beverages and has been committed by the Clerk of Court of Harnett County, as provided
14 in G.S. 35-2, and such person is indigent so that expenses of his care and cure shall constitute a
15 valid charge against the county, as provided in G.S. 35-2, the town Alcoholic Control Board shall
16 pay to the county such charges; provided, that the town Alcoholic Control Board shall not be
17 required to pay any such charges except where it has agreed with the Clerk of Superior Court to
18 pay such charges prior to the person's commitment. The town Alcoholic Control Board is
19 authorized to participate in and to make contributions to public and private organizations which
20 have rehabilitation programs for alcoholics, when the organization and its programs have been
21 approved by the board. Nothing herein shall be construed as limiting the Alcoholic Control
22 Board's discretion in establishing its educational and rehabilitation program and expenditures
23 therefor within the ten per cent (10%) net profits herein allocated.
24 3. The remainder of the net profits shall be allocated to the general fund of the town of
25 Angier. The governing body of the town is hereby authorized to appropriate such funds for any
26 proper governmental purpose. Out of the funds allocated to it, the governing body of the town is
27 authorized, at its discretion, to expend up to twenty-five per cent (25%) of such funds for
28 recreational programs in the town."
29 SECTION 31.(b) Any undistributed funds accrued by the town Alcoholic Control
30 Board prior to the effective date of this act for distribution to the Angier Community Library
31 shall be distributed for the purpose of recreation programs within the town limits of the Town of
32 Angier.".
