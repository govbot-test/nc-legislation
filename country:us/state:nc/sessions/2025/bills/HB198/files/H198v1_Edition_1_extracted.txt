Section 1:
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF NORTH CAROLINA
SESSION 2025
H 1
Short Title: Amend Law on Notice from ALE of Violation. (Public)
Sponsors: Representatives Miller and Pyrtle (Primary Sponsors).
For a complete list of sponsors, refer to the North Carolina General Assembly web site.
Referred to: Alcoholic Beverage Control, if favorable, Judiciary 1, if favorable, Rules,
Calendar, and Operations of the House
February 26, 2025
1 A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
2 AN ACT TO AMEND THE LAW REQUIRING ALCOHOL LAW ENFORCEMENT TO
3 NOTIFY PERMIT HOLDER OF CERTAIN VIOLATIONS COMMITTED ON THE
4 PERMITTED ESTABLISHMENT.
5 The General Assembly of North Carolina enacts:
6 SECTION 1. G.S. 18B-502(c) reads as rewritten:
7 "(c) If any alcohol law enforcement agent or local ABC officer issues a citation to an
8 employee of a permitted establishment, who is not the named holder of an ABC permit for the
9 establishment, for conduct occurring on the premises of the establishment that is a violation of
10 this Chapter or Chapter 14 of the General Statutes, the alcohol law enforcement agent or local
11 ABC officer shall send notice by electronic means or certified mail to the holder of the permit
12 within five business days of the issuance of the citation. If the Commission receives a report from
13 a law enforcement agency other than the Division of Alcohol Law Enforcement of the
14 Department of Public Safety or a local ABC officer documenting violations of this Chapter or
15 Chapter 14 of the General Statutes for conduct occurring on the premises of a permitted
16 establishment, the Commission shall send notice of the alleged violation to the holder of the
17 permit within five business days of receipt of the law enforcement agency report. The written
18 notice shall identify the currently alleged violations and the involved employee. Nothing in this
19 subsection shall prevent or limit the Commission from taking any additional action warranted by
20 the circumstances of the violation."
21 SECTION 2. This act is effective when it becomes law and applies to violations
22 committed on or after that date.
