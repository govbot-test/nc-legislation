GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF NORTH CAROLINA
SESSION 2025
H Simple
Resolution
Adopted
HOUSE RESOLUTION 904
Adopted 4/29/25
For a complete list of sponsors, refer to the North Carolina General Assembly web site.
Referred to: Rules, Calendar, and Operations of the House
April 14, 2025
1 A HOUSE RESOLUTION ELECTING WENDY FLOYD MURPHY, KELLIE HUNT BLUE,
2 CAROLYN COWARD, JOHN A. FRALEY, REGINALD RONALD HOLLEY, AND DR.
3 M. LEE BARNES TO THE BOARD OF GOVERNORS OF THE UNIVERSITY OF
4 NORTH CAROLINA.
5 Whereas, G.S. 116-6(a)(1) directs the House of Representatives to elect by resolution
6 six members to the Board of Governors of The University of North Carolina this year; Now,
7 therefore,
8 Be it resolved by the House of Representatives:
9 SECTION 1. The following persons are elected to the Board of Governors of The
10 University of North Carolina for terms commencing July 1, 2025, and ending June 30, 2029:
11 (1) Wendy Floyd Murphy of Duplin County.
12 (2) Kellie Hunt Blue of Robeson County.
13 (3) John A. Fraley of Iredell County.
14 (4) Carolyn Coward of Buncombe County.
15 (5) Reginald Ronald Holley of Brunswick County.
16 (6) Dr. M. Lee Barnes of Durham County.
17 SECTION 2. This resolution is effective upon adoption.
