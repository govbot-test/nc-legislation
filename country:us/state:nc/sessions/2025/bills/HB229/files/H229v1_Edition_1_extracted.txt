GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF NORTH CAROLINA
SESSION 2025
H 1
HOUSE BILL 229
Short Title: Medicaid Rates/Ambulatory Surgical Centers. (Public)
Sponsors: Representatives Potts, White, Reeder, and Campbell (Primary Sponsors).
For a complete list of sponsors, refer to the North Carolina General Assembly web site.
Referred to: Appropriations, if favorable, Rules, Calendar, and Operations of the House
February 27, 2025
1 A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
2 AN ACT TO ADJUST MEDICAID RATES PAID TO AMBULATORY SURGICAL
3 CENTERS.
4 The General Assembly of North Carolina enacts:
5 SECTION 1. The Department of Health and Human Services, Division of Health
6 Benefits, shall amend the Medicaid State Plan to set and adjust rates for all services provided by
7 licensed ambulatory surgical centers so that these services are reimbursed at a rate of at least
8 ninety-five percent (95%) of the Medicare Ambulatory Surgical Centers fee schedule in effect as
9 of January 1 of each year, as published by the Centers for Medicare and Medicaid Services
10 (CMS). The initial rate adjustments shall be effective on the effective date approved by CMS for
11 the State Plan amendment.
12 SECTION 2. There is appropriated from the General Fund to the Department of
13 Health and Human Services, Division of Health Benefits, the sum of ten million four hundred
14 seventy-six thousand dollars ($10,476,000) in recurring funds for each year of the 2025-2027
15 fiscal biennium to be used to implement the changes required under this act. These funds shall
16 provide a State match for nineteen million one hundred thirty-five thousand dollars
17 ($19,135,000) in recurring federal funds for each year of the 2025-2027 fiscal biennium, and
18 those funds are appropriated to the Division of Health Benefits for this same purpose.
19 SECTION 3. This act is effective July 1, 2025.
