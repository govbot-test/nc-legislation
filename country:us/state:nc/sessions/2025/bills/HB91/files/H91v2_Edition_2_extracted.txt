29 PART II. CONFORMING CHANGES
30 SECTION 2.1. G.S. 1-82 reads as rewritten:
31 "§ 1-82. Venue in all other cases.
32 In all other cases the action must be tried in the county in which the plaintiffs or the
33 defendants, or any of them, reside at its commencement, or if none of the defendants reside in
34 the State, then in the county in which the plaintiffs, or any of them, reside; and if none of the
35 parties reside in the State, then the action may be tried in any county which the plaintiff designates
36 in the plaintiff's summons and complaint, subject to the power of the court to change the place
37 of trial, in the cases provided by statute; provided that any person who has resided on or been
38 stationed in a United States Army, Navy, Marine Corps, Coast Guard, Space Force, or Air Force
39 installation or reservation within this State for a period of one (1) year or more next preceding
40 the institution of an action shall be deemed a resident of the county within which such installation
41 or reservation, or part thereof, is situated and of any county adjacent to such county where such
42 person stationed at such installation or reservation lives in such adjacent county, for the purposes
43 of this section. The term person shall include military personnel and the spouses and dependents
44 of such personnel."
