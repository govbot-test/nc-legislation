15 SECTION 1.(b) Article 49B of Chapter 106 of the General Statutes is amended by
16 adding a new section to read:
17 "§ 106-549.28A. Labeling of manufactured-protein food products.
18 (a) The label of any manufactured-protein food product that contains an identifying meat
19 term shall also contain in at least 20-point font or the size of the surrounding type, whichever is
20 greater, and in close proximity to the name of the product, an appropriate qualifying term.
21 (b) A manufactured-protein food product that does not meet the requirements of this
22 section shall be deemed misbranded for purposes of this Article."
