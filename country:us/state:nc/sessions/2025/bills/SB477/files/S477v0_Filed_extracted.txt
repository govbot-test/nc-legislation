7 NATURAL HERITAGE PROGRAM DATA CONFIDENTIALITY
8 SECTION 1.(a) The Nature Preserves Act, Part 42 of Article 2 of Chapter 143B of
9 the General Statutes, is amended by adding a new section to read:
10 "§ 143B-135.275. Confidentiality of rare species and sensitive natural habitat information.
11 Information concerning the nature and location of any (i) rare species of plants or animals or
12 (ii) sensitive natural habitats may be made available to the public under Chapter 132 of the
13 General Statutes or under any other provision of law, regardless of whether the rare species or
14 sensitive natural habitats are located on public or private property, unless the Department of
15 Natural and Cultural Resources determines that the disclosures would create a risk of harm, theft,
16 or destruction to those species or habitats."
17 SECTION 1.(b) This section is effective when it becomes law.

26 EXPAND AND CODIFY FREE SCHOOL GROUP ADMISSION
27 SECTION 6.(a) Article 2 of Chapter 143B of the General Statutes is amended by
28 adding a new section to read:
29 "§ 143B-53.15 Free admission for school groups.
30 The Department of Natural and Cultural Resources may not impose fees on school groups
31 visiting sites owned or managed by the Department. For purposes of this section, "fees" refers to
32 any regular admission charge and does not include activity fees or a separate admission charge
33 for special or temporary exhibitions."
34 SECTION 6.(b) Section 14.11(g) of S.L. 2015-241 is repealed.
