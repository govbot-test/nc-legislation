24 SECTION 3.1.(c) There is appropriated from the General Fund to the Department
25 of Natural and Cultural Resources the sum of one million five hundred thousand dollars
26 ($1,500,000) for the North Carolina Science Museums Grant Program. These funds shall be used
27 to provide one-time grants to science museums in Western North Carolina that have been
28 engaged in relief efforts and whose normal operations were disrupted, causing a severe loss in
29 revenue due to the extensive damage caused by Hurricane Helene. Grant awards shall not exceed
30 twenty-five percent (25%) of the eligible museums' annual budget with the maximum awards not
31 exceeding one hundred fifty thousand dollars ($150,000). Any remaining funds shall be
32 distributed equally among participating museums after the initial grants are awarded. Eligible
33 museums include the following:
34 (1) Grandfather Mountain Nature Museum.
35 (2) Buncombe County Asheville Museum of Science.
36 (3) North Carolina Arboretum.
37 (4) Western North Carolina Nature Center.
38 (5) Henderson County Hands On! Children's Museum.
39 (6) Team ECCO Aquarium.
40 (7) Macon County Highlands Nature Center.
41 (8) Rutherford County KidSenses Children's Museum.
42 (9) Transylvania County Cradle of Forestry, FIND Outdoors.
43 (10) Pisgah Astronomical Research Institute.
