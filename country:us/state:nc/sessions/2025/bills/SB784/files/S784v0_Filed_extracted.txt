Section 1:
FILED SENATE
Apr 21, 2026
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF NORTH CAROLINA
S.B. 784
SESSION 2025
PRINCIPAL CLERK
S D
Short Title: Ensure Same-Sex Domestic Violence Prot. Order. (Public)
Sponsors: Senator Bradley (Primary Sponsor).
Referred to:
1 A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
2 AN ACT TO ENSURE DOMESTIC VIOLENCE PROTECTIVE ORDERS ARE AVAILABLE
3 FOR SAME-SEX COUPLES.
4 The General Assembly of North Carolina enacts:
5 SECTION 1. G.S. 50B-1(b) reads as rewritten:
6 "(b) For purposes of this section, the term "personal relationship" means a relationship
7 wherein the parties involved:involved either:
8 (1) Are current or former spouses;spouses.
9 (2) Are persons of opposite sex who live together or have lived together;together.
10 (3) Are related as parents and children, including others acting in loco parentis to
11 a minor child, or as grandparents and grandchildren. For purposes of this
12 subdivision, an aggrieved party may not obtain an order of protection against
13 a child or grandchild under the age of 16;16.
14 (4) Have a child in common;common.
15 (5) Are current or former household members;members.
16 (6) Are persons of the opposite sex who are in a dating relationship or have been
17 in a dating relationship. For purposes of this subdivision, a dating relationship
18 is one wherein the parties are romantically involved over time and on a
19 continuous basis during the course of the relationship. A casual acquaintance
20 or ordinary fraternization between persons in a business or social context is
21 not a dating relationship."
22 SECTION 2. There is appropriated from the General Fund to the Administrative
23 Office of the Courts five hundred thousand dollars ($500,000) in nonrecurring funds for the
24 2026-2027 fiscal year to be used to establish a public awareness and prevention campaign
25 regarding domestic violence within same-sex relationships.
26 SECTION 3. This act is effective when it becomes law and applies to domestic
27 violence protection orders issued on or after that date.
