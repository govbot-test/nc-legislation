12 PART IV. FRIDAY INSTITUTE TO STUDY TEACHING FELLOWS PROGRAM
13 PARTICIPATION
14 SECTION 4. There is appropriated from the General Fund to Board of Governors
15 of The University of North Carolina the sum of fifty thousand dollars ($50,000) in nonrecurring
16 funds for the 2026-2027 fiscal year to be allocated to the North Carolina State University for the
17 Friday Institute to study student participation in the North Carolina Teaching Fellows Program,
18 including the reasons some students opt to repay their loans in cash instead of through service.
19 The Friday Institute shall report this information to the Joint Legislative Education Oversight
20 Committee no later than February 15, 2027.
