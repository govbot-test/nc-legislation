GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF NORTH CAROLINA
SESSION 2025
S 1
SENATE BILL 824
Short Title: Edgecombe and Pitt County School Nurses. (Public)
Sponsors: Senator Smith (Primary Sponsor).
Referred to: Rules and Operations of the Senate
April 23, 2026
1 A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
2 AN ACT TO FUND A SCHOOL NURSE FOR EVERY PUBLIC SCHOOL IN EDGECOMBE
3 AND PITT COUNTIES.
4 The General Assembly of North Carolina enacts:
5 SECTION 1. There is appropriated from the General Fund to the Department of
6 Public Instruction the sum of one million two hundred thirty-seven thousand dollars ($1,237,000)
7 in recurring funds beginning in the 2026-2027 fiscal year to be allocated to Edgecombe County
8 Public Schools to be used to provide at least one full-time, permanent school nurse in each public
9 school in Edgecombe County. Funds provided pursuant to this act shall supplement and not
10 supplant any funds from any source already provided for the same purposes.
11 SECTION 2. There is appropriated from the General Fund to the Department of
12 Public Instruction the sum of one million six hundred seventy-eight thousand dollars
13 ($1,678,000) in recurring funds beginning in the 2026-2027 fiscal year to be allocated to Pitt
14 County Schools to be used to provide at least one full-time, permanent school nurse in each public
15 school in Pitt County. Funds provided pursuant to this act shall supplement and not supplant any
16 funds from any source already provided for the same purposes.
17 SECTION 3. This act becomes effective July 1, 2026.
