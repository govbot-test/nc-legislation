GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF NORTH CAROLINA
SESSION 2025
H 1
HOUSE BILL 779
Short Title: Designate Children's Day. (Public)
Sponsors: Representatives Cervania and Schietzelt (Primary Sponsors).
For a complete list of sponsors, refer to the North Carolina General Assembly web site.
Referred to: Rules, Calendar, and Operations of the House
April 7, 2025
1 A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
2 AN ACT DESIGNATING THE FIRST SUNDAY OF JUNE OF EACH YEAR AS
3 CHILDREN'S DAY IN NORTH CAROLINA.
4 Whereas, children represent the future and embody the hopes and aspirations of the
5 people of North Carolina; and
6 Whereas, investing in the health, education, and well-being of our children is
7 paramount to ensuring a prosperous and sustainable society; and
8 Whereas, recognizing the importance of nurturing and protecting our children
9 encourages communities, families, and organizations to collaborate in creating safe and
10 supportive environments; and
11 Whereas, celebrating Children's Day provides an opportunity to acknowledge the
12 achievements and potential of all children, regardless of their background or circumstances; and
13 Whereas, such a day serves to raise awareness about the challenges and issues facing
14 children today, including access to quality education, healthcare, and opportunities for personal
15 growth; and
16 Whereas, the State of North Carolina is committed to promoting policies and
17 initiatives that support the development and well-being of its youngest residents; Now, therefore,
18 The General Assembly of North Carolina enacts:
19 SECTION 1. Chapter 103 of the General Statutes is amended by adding a new
20 section to read:
21 "§ 103-19. Day of the child.
22 The first Sunday of June of each year is designated as Children's Day in North Carolina."
23 SECTION 2. This act is effective when it becomes law.
