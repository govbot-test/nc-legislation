1 A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
2 AN ACT APPROPRIATING FUNDS TO PROVIDE COMPENSATION INCREASES TO
3 MEMBERS OF THE STATE HIGHWAY PATROL.
4 The General Assembly of North Carolina enacts:
5 SECTION 1. Effective July 1, 2025, there is appropriated from the General Fund to
6 the Reserve for Compensation Increases to provide pay raises for the State Highway Patrol the
7 sum of forty million four hundred eighty-two thousand four hundred eighty dollars ($40,482,480)
8 in each year of the 2025-2027 fiscal biennium to establish the pay scales set forth in this act.
9 SECTION 2. Effective July 1, 2025, the law enforcement officers of the State
10 Highway Patrol shall be paid based upon the following experience-based annual salary schedule:
11 Years of Experience 2025-2027 Biennium
12 0 $56,000
13 1 $62,125
14 2 $68,250
15 3 $75,375
16 4 $80,500
17 5 $86,625
18 6 $92,750
19 7 $98,875
20 8+ $105,000
21 SECTION 3. Effective July 1, 2025, Sworn State Highway Patrol employees who
22 are not subject to an experience-based annual salary schedule shall be paid at the following rates
23 by rank:
24 Rank 2025-2027 Biennium
25 Sergeant $120,750
26 First Sergeant $132,825
27 Lieutenant $146,108
28 Captain $160,718
29 Major $176,790
30 Lieutenant Colonel $194,469

33 SECTION 4. Unless otherwise provided, this act is effective when it becomes law.
