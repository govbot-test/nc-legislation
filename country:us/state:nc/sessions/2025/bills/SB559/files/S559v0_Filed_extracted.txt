Section 1:
FILED SENATE
Mar 25, 2025
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF NORTH CAROLINA
S.B. 559
SESSION 2025
PRINCIPAL CLERK
S D
Short Title: Study State Emp. Bereavement Leave . (Public)
Sponsors: Senator Murdock (Primary Sponsor).
Referred to:
1 A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
2 AN ACT AUTHORIZING THE LEGISLATIVE RESEARCH COMMISSION TO STUDY
3 BEREAVEMENT LEAVE FOR STATE EMPLOYEES.
4 The General Assembly of North Carolina enacts:
5 SECTION 1. The Legislative Research Commission (LRC) shall study the need for
6 bereavement leave for State employees. The LRC shall consider relevant data from the Office of
7 State Human Resources as part of its assessment. The LRC shall report its findings and any
8 legislative proposals to the 2026 Regular Session of the 2025 General Assembly.
9 SECTION 2. There is appropriated from the General Fund to the General Assembly
10 the sum of fifty thousand dollars ($50,000) for the 2025-2026 fiscal year to fund the study
11 mandated by this act.
12 SECTION 3. This act becomes effective July 1, 2025.
*DRS35221-LRa-46*
