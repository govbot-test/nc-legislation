Section 1:
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF NORTH CAROLINA
SESSION 2025
H 1
Short Title: Funds for Boys & Girls Clubs. (Public)
Sponsors: Representatives Buansi, Jeffers, Price, and Hawkins (Primary Sponsors).
For a complete list of sponsors, refer to the North Carolina General Assembly web site.
Referred to: Appropriations, if favorable, Rules, Calendar, and Operations of the House
March 18, 2025
1 A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
2 AN ACT TO APPROPRIATE FUNDS TO THE BOYS & GIRLS CLUBS OF DURHAM AND
3 ORANGE COUNTIES.
4 The General Assembly of North Carolina enacts:
5 SECTION 1. There is appropriated from the General Fund to the Office of State
6 Budget and Management the sum of thirty thousand dollars ($30,000) in nonrecurring funds for
7 the 2025-2026 fiscal year to provide a directed grant to the Boys & Girls Clubs of Durham and
8 Orange Counties. These funds shall be used to offer area boys and girls its comprehensive six-part
9 curriculum comprised of the following: (i) Recruiting and Retaining Teens, (ii) Emotional Safety,
10 (iii) Youth as Leaders, (iv) Trauma-Informed Practices, (v) Cultural Responsiveness and
11 Inclusion, and (vi) College and Career Readiness.
12 SECTION 2. This act becomes effective July 1, 2025.
