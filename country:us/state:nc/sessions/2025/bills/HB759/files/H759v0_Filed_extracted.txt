Section 1:
H.B. 759
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF NORTH CAROLINA
Apr 2, 2025
SESSION 2025
HOUSE PRINCIPAL CLERK
H D
Short Title: Family and Community Wellness Act/Funds. (Public)
Sponsors: Representative K. Brown.
Referred to:
1 A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
2 AN ACT TO APPROPRIATE FUNDS TO FORSYTH COUNTY TO SUPPORT THE FAMILY
3 AND COMMUNITY WELLNESS INITIATIVE AND WORK-BASED LEARNING
4 PARENT PROGRAM AND SERVICE-LEARNING PROJECT.
5 The General Assembly of North Carolina enacts:
6 SECTION 1. There is appropriated from the General Fund to the Office of State
7 Budget and Management the sum of one hundred thousand dollars ($100,000) in nonrecurring
8 funds for the 2025-2026 fiscal year to provide a directed grant to Forsyth County to be used as
9 follows:
10 (1) To support the Family and Community Wellness Initiative by funding two
11 mobile units to provide essential health, social services, and nutritional
12 support to residents of Forsyth, Davidson, Davie, Guilford, Randolph, Stokes,
13 and Yadkin Counties.
14 (2) To establish a work-based learning parent program and service-learning
15 project for adult learners to enhance community engagement, economic
16 mobility, and support for families in need.
17 SECTION 2. This act becomes effective July 1, 2025.
*DRH10313-LGa-148*
