FILED SENATE
May 5, 2025
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF NORTH CAROLINA
S.J.R. 765
SESSION 2025
PRINCIPAL CLERK
S D
SENATE JOINT RESOLUTION DRSJR15326-LG-173
Sponsors: Senator Rabon (Primary Sponsor).
Referred
to:
2 ADAMS TO THE BOARD OF REVIEW.
3 Whereas, the provisions of G.S. 96-15.3(c) require appointments of the Governor to
4 the Board of Review to be confirmed by the General Assembly by joint resolution; and
5 Whereas, the Governor has submitted to the General Assembly the name of his
6 appointee to the Board of Review as the member representative of employees; Now, therefore,
7 Be it resolved by the Senate, the House of Representatives concurring:
8 SECTION 1. The appointment of Regina Smith Adams of Durham County to
9 membership on the Board of Review as the member representative of employees for a term
10 beginning July 1, 2025, and expiring June 30, 2029, is confirmed.
11 SECTION 2. This resolution is effective upon ratification.
*DRSJR15326-LG-173*
