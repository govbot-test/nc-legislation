GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF NORTH CAROLINA
SESSION 2025
RATIFIED BILL
RESOLUTION 2025-6
SENATE JOINT RESOLUTION 765
A JOINT RESOLUTION PROVIDING FOR THE CONFIRMATION OF REGINA SMITH
ADAMS TO THE BOARD OF REVIEW.
Whereas, the provisions of G.S. 96-15.3(c) require appointments of the Governor to
the Board of Review to be confirmed by the General Assembly by joint resolution; and
Whereas, the Governor has submitted to the General Assembly the name of his
appointee to the Board of Review as the member representative of employees; Now, therefore,
Be it resolved by the Senate, the House of Representatives concurring:
SECTION 1. The appointment of Regina Smith Adams of Durham County to
membership on the Board of Review as the member representative of employees for a term
beginning July 1, 2025, and expiring June 30, 2029, is confirmed.
SECTION 2. This resolution is effective upon ratification.
In the General Assembly read three times and ratified this the 22nd day of May, 2025.
s/ Phil Berger
President Pro Tempore of the Senate
s/ Destin Hall
Speaker of the House of Representatives
