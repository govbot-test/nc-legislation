Section 1:
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF NORTH CAROLINA
SESSION 2025
H 1
Short Title: Cure Violence Program Grant. (Public)
Sponsors: Representative K. Brown.
For a complete list of sponsors, refer to the North Carolina General Assembly web site.
Referred to: Appropriations, if favorable, Rules, Calendar, and Operations of the House
April 28, 2026
1 A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
2 AN ACT TO APPROPRIATE FUNDS FOR VIOLENCE PREVENTION BENEFITTING THE
3 TRIAD REGION.
4 The General Assembly of North Carolina enacts:
5 SECTION 1. There is appropriated from the General Fund to the Office of State
6 Budget and Management the sum of one million dollars ($1,000,000) in nonrecurring funds for
7 the 2026-2027 fiscal year to be allocated in the form of a directed grant to Forsyth County to be
8 used for the Cure Violence Program to address violence.
9 SECTION 2. This act becomes effective July 1, 2026.
