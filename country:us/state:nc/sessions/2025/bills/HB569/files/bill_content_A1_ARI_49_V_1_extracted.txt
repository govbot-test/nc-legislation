9

12
13 "Drinking Water Act in North Carolina, if the Secretary finds all of the following by sufficient

15 and necessary costs incurred by the public water system to remove, correct, or abate any adverse
16 effects upon the water supply resulting from contamination for which the person is responsible:";
17
18 and on page 2, lines 10 and 11,
19 by inserting between those lines:
20
21 "(4) The public water system has incurred costs in excess of fifty million dollars
22 ($50,000,000) to remove, correct, or abate adverse effects upon the water
23 supply resulting from contamination by the responsible party.";
24
25 and on page 2, lines 22 and 23,
26 by rewriting those lines to read:
27
28 "(e) A public water system shall reimburse ratepayers of the system through refunds or
29 credits to customers, or a reduction in future rates charged, in a manner that will not impair any

31 water system has previously expended funds to remove,".
