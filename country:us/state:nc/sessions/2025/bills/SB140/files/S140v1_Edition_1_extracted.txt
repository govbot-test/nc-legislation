GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF NORTH CAROLINA
SESSION 2025
S 1
SENATE BILL 140
Short Title: Funds for My Brother's Keeper. (Public)
Sponsors: Senator Waddell (Primary Sponsor).
Referred to: Rules and Operations of the Senate
February 25, 2025
1 A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
2 AN ACT TO APPROPRIATE FUNDS FOR MY BROTHER'S KEEPER OF CHARLOTTE.
3 The General Assembly of North Carolina enacts:
4 SECTION 1. There is appropriated from the General Fund to the Office of State
5 Budget and Management the sum of seventy-five thousand dollars ($75,000) in nonrecurring
6 funds for the 2025-2026 fiscal year to provide a directed grant to My Brother's Keeper of
7 Charlotte, a nonprofit organization, to support programs that improve the outcomes for boys and
8 young men of color.
9 SECTION 2. This act becomes effective July 1, 2025.
