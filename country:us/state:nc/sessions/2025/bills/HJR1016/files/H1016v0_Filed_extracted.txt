GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF NORTH CAROLINA

SESSION 2025
HOUSE PRINCIPAL CLERK

HOUSE JOINT RESOLUTION DRHJR40621-MC-220C
Sponsors: Committee on Rules, Calendar, and Operations of the House.
Referred
to:
1 A JOINT RESOLUTION FURTHER AMENDING RESOLUTION 2025-8, AS AMENDED
2 BY RESOLUTION 2025-11.
3 Be it resolved by the House of Representatives, the Senate concurring:
4 SECTION 1. Section 3(b) of Resolution 2025-8, as amended by Resolution 2025-11,
5 reads as rewritten:
6 "SECTION 3.(b) During the regular session that reconvenes on Monday, October 20, 2025,
7 only the following matters may be considered:
8 (1) Bills returned by the Governor with his objections under Section 22 of Article
9 II of the North Carolina Constitution, but solely for the purpose of considering
10 overriding of the veto upon reconsideration of the bill.
11 (2) Bills containing no matter other than one or more of the following:
12 a. The selection, appointment, or confirmation as required by law,
13 including the filling of vacancies of positions for which the appointees
14 were elected by the General Assembly upon recommendation of the
15 Speaker of the House of Representatives, President of the Senate,
16 President Pro Tempore of the Senate, or a minority leader of a chamber
17 of the General Assembly.
18 b. Actions on gubernatorial nominations or appointments.
19 c. Actions related to litigation challenging the legality of legislative
20 enactments.
21 d. Matters relating to election laws, including bills concerning the
22 districts for Congressional, State House, State Senate, judicial,
23 municipal, county, and other elected officials.
24 e. Impeachment pursuant to Article IV of the North Carolina
25 Constitution or Chapter 123 of the General Statutes.
26 f. Matters related to funding the Medicaid rebase.
27 …."
28 SECTION 2. This resolution is effective upon ratification.
