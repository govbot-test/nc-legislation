GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF NORTH CAROLINA
SESSION 2025
H 1
Short Title: CoA to Demolish Historic Districts/Davidson. (Local)
Sponsors: Representative Helfrich.
For a complete list of sponsors, refer to the North Carolina General Assembly web site.
Referred to: Rules, Calendar, and Operations of the House
May 7, 2026
1 A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
2 AN ACT AUTHORIZING THE TOWN OF DAVIDSON TO ADOPT ORDINANCES
3 REGULATING THE DEMOLITION OF HISTORIC STRUCTURES.
4 The General Assembly of North Carolina enacts:
5 SECTION 1. Section 2 of S.L. 2007-66, as amended by S.L. 2008-58 and S.L.
6 2008-75, reads as rewritten:
7 "SECTION 2. This act applies to the Towns of Cary, Chapel Hill, Davidson, and Wake
8 Forest and to the City of Wilson."
9 SECTION 2. This act is effective when it becomes law.
