1 A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
2 AN ACT TO APPROPRIATE FUNDS TO BRENNER CHILDREN'S HOSPITAL.
3 The General Assembly of North Carolina enacts:
4 SECTION 1. There is appropriated from the General Fund to the Office of State
5 Budget and Management the sum of two hundred fifty thousand dollars ($250,000) in
6 nonrecurring funds for the 2026-2027 fiscal year to provide a directed grant to the City of
7 Winston-Salem for the support of a rural maternal health program at the facility commonly
8 known as Atrium Health Levine Children's Brenner Children's Hospital.
9 SECTION 2. This act becomes effective July 1, 2026.
