GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF NORTH CAROLINA
SESSION 2025
H 1
HOUSE BILL 809
Short Title: Count NC Pre-K for School ADM. (Public)
Sponsors: Representatives Clark, Helfrich, Quick, and von Haefen (Primary Sponsors).
For a complete list of sponsors, refer to the North Carolina General Assembly web site.
Referred to: Rules, Calendar, and Operations of the House
April 8, 2025
1 A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
2 AN ACT TO INCLUDE NC PRE-K STUDENTS IN AVERAGE DAILY MEMBERSHIP IN
3 LOCAL SCHOOL ADMINISTRATIVE UNITS.
4 The General Assembly of North Carolina enacts:
5 SECTION 1. G.S. 115C-47 is amended by adding a new subdivision to read:
6 "(1b) A local board of education participating in the NC Prekindergarten (NC
7 Pre-K) program shall include NC Pre-K students receiving instruction from
8 an NC Pre-K program located in a building owned by a local school
9 administrative unit in the average daily membership for that local school
10 administrative unit."
11 SECTION 2. This act becomes effective July 1, 2025.
