Section 1:
FILED SENATE
Mar 24, 2025
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF NORTH CAROLINA
S.B. 404
SESSION 2025
PRINCIPAL CLERK
S D
Short Title: RIC FLAIR Act. (Public)
Sponsors: Senators Chaudhuri, Britt, and Hise (Primary Sponsors).
Referred to:
1 A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
2 AN ACT TO REMEMBER ICONIC COMBATANTS THROUGH FOSTERING LEARNING
3 AWARENESS AND INTEREST IN RASSLING.
4 The General Assembly of North Carolina enacts:
5 SECTION 1. There is appropriated from the General Fund to the Department of
6 Natural and Cultural Resources the sum of five hundred thousand dollars ($500,000) in
7 nonrecurring funds for the 2025-2026 fiscal year to study the feasibility of establishing a
8 professional wrestling museum in the State. By July 1, 2026, the Department shall report its
9 findings and recommendations to the Joint Legislative Oversight Committee on Agriculture and
10 Natural and Economic Resources.
11 SECTION 2. This act becomes effective July 1, 2025.
*DRS45215-LGa-20A*
