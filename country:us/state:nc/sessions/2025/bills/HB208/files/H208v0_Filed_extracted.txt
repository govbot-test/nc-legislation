H.B. 208
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF NORTH CAROLINA
Feb 25, 2025
SESSION 2025
HOUSE PRINCIPAL CLERK
H D
HOUSE BILL DRH30077-MC-63
Short Title: Partnership for Children Information Access. (Local)
Sponsors: Representative Alston.
Referred to:
1 A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
2 AN ACT TO EXPAND THE LOCALITIES TO WHICH THE PROVISIONS GOVERNING
3 LIMITED ACCESS TO IDENTIFYING INFORMATION OF MINORS PARTICIPATING
4 IN CERTAIN PROGRAMS APPLY.
5 The General Assembly of North Carolina enacts:
6 SECTION 1. G.S. 132-1.11A reads as rewritten:
7 "§ 132-1.11A. Limited access to identifying information of minors participating in local
8 government programs and programs funded by the North Carolina Partnership
9 for Children, Inc., or a local partnership in certain localities.
10 (a) A public record, as defined by G.S. 132-1, does not include, as to any minor
11 participating in a program sponsored by a local government or combination of local governments,
12 a program funded by the North Carolina Partnership for Children, Inc., under G.S. 143B-168.12,
13 or a program funded by a local partnership under G.S. 143B-168.14, any of the following
14 information as to that minor participant: (i) name, (ii) address, (iii) age, (iv) date of birth, (v)
15 telephone number, (vi) the name or address of that minor participant's parent or legal guardian,
16 (vii) e-mail address, or (viii) any other identifying information on an application to participate in
17 such program or other records related to that program. Notwithstanding this subsection, the name
18 of a minor who has received a scholarship or other local government-funded award of a financial
19 nature from a local government is a public record.
20 (b) The county, municipality, and zip code of residence of each participating minor
21 covered by subsection (a) of this section is a public record, with the information listed in
22 subsection (a) of this section redacted.
23 (c) Nothing in this section makes the information listed in subsection (a) of this section
24 confidential information.
25 (d) This section applies to the County of Chatham, Counties of Chatham and Durham,
26 the Towns of Apex, Cary, Fuquay-Varina, Garner, Holly Springs, Knightdale, Morrisville,
27 Rolesville, Wake Forest, Wendell, and Zebulon, and the City of Raleigh only."
28 SECTION 2. This act is effective when it becomes law.
