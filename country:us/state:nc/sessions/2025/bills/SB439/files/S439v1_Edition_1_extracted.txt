Section 1:
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF NORTH CAROLINA
SESSION 2025
S 1
Short Title: Moratorium on Opportunity Scholarships. (Public)
Sponsors: Senators Chitlik and Applewhite (Primary Sponsors).
Referred to: Rules and Operations of the Senate
March 25, 2025
1 A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
2 AN ACT TO IMPOSE A MORATORIUM ON THE AWARD OF NEW OPPORTUNITY
3 SCHOLARSHIPS, TO REDUCE FUNDS FOR OPPORTUNITY SCHOLARSHIPS, AND
4 TO REALLOCATE ADDITIONAL FUNDS MADE AVAILABLE FROM
5 OPPORTUNITY SCHOLARSHIPS TO SUPPORT PUBLIC SCHOOLS.
6 The General Assembly of North Carolina enacts:
7
8 PART I. MORATORIUM ON OPPORTUNITY SCHOLARSHIPS
9 SECTION 1. Beginning with the 2025-2026 fiscal year, the State Education
10 Assistance Authority shall not award any scholarship grant funds under Part 2A of Article 39 of
11 Chapter 115C of the General Statutes to new persons who did not receive scholarship grant funds
12 in the prior school year. It is the intent of the General Assembly to eliminate the program for
13 awarding scholarship grants under Part 2A of Article 39 of Chapter 115C of the General Statutes
14 beginning in the 2037-2038 school year or once all current recipients become ineligible for the
15 receipt of scholarship grants.
16
17 PART II. REDUCE FUNDS FOR OPPORTUNITY SCHOLARSHIPS
18 SECTION 2.(a) G.S. 115C-562.8 reads as rewritten:
19 "§ 115C-562.8. The Opportunity Scholarship Grant Fund Reserve.
20 …
21 (b) The General Assembly finds that, due to the critical need in this State to provide
22 opportunity for school choice for North Carolina students, it is imperative that the State provide
23 an increase of funds for 15 years to the Opportunity Scholarship Grant Fund Reserve. Therefore,
24 there is appropriated from the General Fund to the Reserve the following amounts for each fiscal
25 year to be used for the purposes set forth in this section:
26 Fiscal Year Appropriation
27 2017-2018 $44,840,000
28 2018-2019 $54,840,000
29 2019-2020 $64,840,000
30 2020-2021 $74,840,000
31 2021-2022 $84,840,000
32 2022-2023 $94,840,000
33 2023-2024 $176,540,000
34 2024-2025 $191,540,000
35 2025-2026 $625,000,000
36 2026-2027 $675,000,000
*S439-v-1*
General Assembly Of North Carolina Session 2025
1 2027-2028 $700,000,000
2 2028-2029 $725,000,000
3 2029-2030 $750,000,000
4 2030-2031 $775,000,000
5 2031-2032 $800,000,000
6 For the 2032-2033 2027-2028 fiscal year and each fiscal year thereafter, there is appropriated
7 from the General Fund to the Reserve the sum of eight hundred twenty-five million dollars
8 ($825,000,000) five hundred forty-one million five hundred forty thousand dollars
9 ($541,540,000) to be used for the purposes set forth in this section. When developing the base
10 budget, as defined by G.S. 143C-1-1, for each fiscal year specified in this subsection, the Director
11 of the Budget shall include the appropriated amount specified in this subsection for that fiscal
12 year.
13 …."
14 SECTION 2.(b) Notwithstanding any other provision of law to the contrary, funds
15 appropriated for the award of opportunity scholarship grants pursuant to Part 2A of Article 39 of
16 Chapter 115C of the General Statutes are reduced as follows:
17 (1) Of the recurring funds appropriated to the Board of Governors of The
18 University of North Carolina for the opportunity scholarship program
19 pursuant to S.L. 2021-180, by the sum of thirty million dollars ($30,000,000)
20 for the 2025-2026 fiscal year.
21 (2) Of the funds appropriated to the Opportunity Scholarship Grant Fund Reserve
22 for the 2024-2025 fiscal year and allocated from the Reserve for the award of
23 scholarship grants in the 2025-2026 fiscal year, by the sum of twenty-eight
24 million four hundred sixty thousand dollars ($28,460,000) in nonrecurring
25 funds for the 2025-2026 fiscal year.
26 (3) Of the funds appropriated to the Opportunity Scholarship Grant Fund Reserve,
27 as follows:
28 a. By the sum of eighty-three million four hundred sixty thousand dollars
29 ($83,460,000) in recurring funds for the 2025-2026 fiscal year.
30 b. By the sum of an additional fifty million dollars ($50,000,000) in
31 recurring funds for the 2026-2027 fiscal year.
32 SECTION 2.(c) It is the intent of the General Assembly that any funding increases
33 in the 2027-2029 fiscal biennium and future biennia for the award of scholarship grant funds
34 under Part 2A of Article 39 of Chapter 115C of the General Statutes will be limited to no more
35 than the annual percentage increase in funding for public schools as reflected in the State Public
36 School Fund.
37
38 PART III. FUNDS FOR PUBLIC SCHOOLS
39 SECTION 3. There is appropriated from the General Fund to the Department of
40 Public Instruction the following amounts to be allocated to local school administrative units,
41 based on average daily membership, to address the needs in our public schools:
42 (1) For the 2025-2026 fiscal year, the sum of one hundred thirteen million four
43 hundred sixty thousand dollars ($113,460,000) in recurring funds and the sum
44 of twenty-eight million four hundred sixty thousand dollars ($28,460,000) in
45 nonrecurring funds.
46 (2) For the 2026-2027 fiscal year, the additional sum of fifty million dollars
47 ($50,000,000) in recurring funds.
48
49 PART IV. EFFECTIVE DATE
50 SECTION 4. This act becomes effective July 1, 2025, and applies beginning with
51 the 2025-2026 school year.
