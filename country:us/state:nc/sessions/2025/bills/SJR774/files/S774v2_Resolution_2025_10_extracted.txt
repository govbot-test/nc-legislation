Section 1:
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF NORTH CAROLINA
SESSION 2025
RESOLUTION 2025-10
SENATE JOINT RESOLUTION 774
A JOINT RESOLUTION PROVIDING FOR THE CONFIRMATION OF STEPHANIE
LYNCH TO THE NORTH CAROLINA INVESTMENT AUTHORITY BOARD OF
DIRECTORS.
Whereas, under the provisions of G.S. 147-71.1, appointments by the State Treasurer
to the North Carolina Investment Authority Board of Directors are subject to confirmation by the
General Assembly by joint resolution; and
Whereas, the State Treasurer wishes to appoint Stephanie Lynch to the North Carolina
Investment Authority Board of Directors; Now, therefore,
Be it resolved by the Senate, the House of Representatives concurring:

Section 2:
SECTION 1. The appointment of Stephanie Lynch to the North Carolina Investment
Authority Board of Directors for a term that began on July 1, 2025, and expires June 30, 2028, is
confirmed.

Section 3:
SECTION 2. This resolution is effective upon ratification.
In the General Assembly read three times and ratified this the 24th day of September,

Section 4:
2025.
s/ Ralph Hise
Presiding Officer of the Senate
s/ Howard Penny, Jr.
Presiding Officer of the House of Representatives

Raw Text:
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF NORTH CAROLINA
SESSION 2025
RATIFIED BILL
RESOLUTION 2025-10
SENATE JOINT RESOLUTION 774
A JOINT RESOLUTION PROVIDING FOR THE CONFIRMATION OF STEPHANIE
LYNCH TO THE NORTH CAROLINA INVESTMENT AUTHORITY BOARD OF
DIRECTORS.
Whereas, under the provisions of G.S. 147-71.1, appointments by the State Treasurer
to the North Carolina Investment Authority Board of Directors are subject to confirmation by the
General Assembly by joint resolution; and
Whereas, the State Treasurer wishes to appoint Stephanie Lynch to the North Carolina
Investment Authority Board of Directors; Now, therefore,
Be it resolved by the Senate, the House of Representatives concurring:
SECTION 1. The appointment of Stephanie Lynch to the North Carolina Investment
Authority Board of Directors for a term that began on July 1, 2025, and expires June 30, 2028, is
confirmed.
SECTION 2. This resolution is effective upon ratification.
In the General Assembly read three times and ratified this the 24th day of September,
2025.
s/ Ralph Hise
Presiding Officer of the Senate
s/ Howard Penny, Jr.
Presiding Officer of the House of Representatives
