FILED SENATE
Sep 22, 2025
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF NORTH CAROLINA
S.J.R. 774
SESSION 2025
PRINCIPAL CLERK
S D
SENATE JOINT RESOLUTION DRSJR35322-LG-184
Sponsors: Senator Rabon (Primary Sponsor).
Referred
to:
2 LYNCH TO THE NORTH CAROLINA INVESTMENT AUTHORITY BOARD OF
3 DIRECTORS.
4 Whereas, under the provisions of G.S. 147-71.1, appointments by the State Treasurer
5 to the North Carolina Investment Authority Board of Directors are subject to confirmation by the
6 General Assembly by joint resolution; and
7 Whereas, the State Treasurer wishes to appoint Stephanie Lynch to the North Carolina
8 Investment Authority Board of Directors; Now, therefore,
9 Be it resolved by the Senate, the House of Representatives concurring:
10 SECTION 1. The appointment of Stephanie Lynch to the North Carolina Investment
11 Authority Board of Directors for a term that began on July 1, 2025, and expires June 30, 2028, is
12 confirmed.
13 SECTION 2. This resolution is effective upon ratification.
*DRSJR35322-LG-184*
