Section 1:
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF NORTH CAROLINA
SESSION 2025
H 1
Short Title: Funds for Haywood County Schools. (Public)
Sponsors: Representative Pless.
For a complete list of sponsors, refer to the North Carolina General Assembly web site.
Referred to: Appropriations, if favorable, Rules, Calendar, and Operations of the House
April 3, 2025
1 A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
2 AN ACT TO APPROPRIATE FUNDS TO THE HAYWOOD COUNTY SCHOOL SYSTEM
3 FOR VARIOUS PROJECTS.
4 The General Assembly of North Carolina enacts:
5 SECTION 1. There is appropriated from the General Fund to the Office of State
6 Budget and Management the sum of one million five hundred twenty-four thousand nine hundred
7 seventy-eight dollars ($1,524,978) in nonrecurring funds for the 2025-2026 fiscal year to provide
8 a directed grant to the Haywood County School system to be used as follows:
9 (1) Nine hundred ninety-eight thousand five hundred thirty-eight dollars
10 ($998,538) to install a turf field at Tuscola High School.
11 (2) Twenty-six thousand four hundred forty dollars ($26,440) to purchase a metal
12 building for use as a multipurpose locker room at the C.E. Weatherby
13 Stadium.
14 (3) Five hundred thousand dollars ($500,000) for stadium improvements at Bethel
15 Middle School.
16 SECTION 2. This act becomes effective July 1, 2025.
*H676-v-1*
