1 A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
2 AN ACT PROVIDING THAT CHOICE OF LAW AND FORUM SELECTION PROVISIONS
3 SHALL NOT APPLY TO GOVERNMENT CONTRACTS.
4 The General Assembly of North Carolina enacts:
5 SECTION 1. G.S. 1G-2 reads as rewritten:
6 "§ 1G-2. Definitions.
7 The following definitions apply in this Chapter:
8 (1) Business contract. – A contract or undertaking, contingent or otherwise,
9 entered into primarily for business or commercial purposes. The term does not
10 include a consumer contract or an contract, employment contract.contract, or
11 government contract.
12 …
13 (4) Government contract. – A contract or undertaking, contingent or otherwise, to
14 which the State of North Carolina or a county, municipality, or other public
15 body of the State of North Carolina is a party."
16 SECTION 2. This act is effective when it becomes law and applies to contracts
17 entered into, renewed, or amended on or after that date.
