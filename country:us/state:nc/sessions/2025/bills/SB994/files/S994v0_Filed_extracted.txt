Section 1:
FILED SENATE
Apr 30, 2026
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF NORTH CAROLINA
S.B. 994
SESSION 2025
PRINCIPAL CLERK
S D
Short Title: Early Voting Procedures at Fire Stations. (Public)
Sponsors: Senators Johnson, Settle, and Moffitt (Primary Sponsors).
Referred to:
1 A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
2 AN ACT TO ENSURE CERTAIN PROVISIONS ARE IN PLACE AT EARLY VOTING
3 SITES LOCATED AT FIRE STATIONS.
4 The General Assembly of North Carolina enacts:
5 SECTION 1. G.S. 163-166.35 is amended by adding a new subsection to read:
6 "(d1) For early voting occurring at fire stations, county boards of elections shall do each of
7 the following:
8 (1) Provide traffic control measures.
9 (2) Provide security as a physical safeguard for county board staff and officials,
10 voters, and fire station staff at the voting place.
11 (3) Provide phone support for voters needing assistance with matters pertaining
12 to the elections process.
13 (4) Provide sufficient signage on election day to voters if the same fire station will
14 not be a voting place on election day.
15 (5) Agree to indemnify and hold harmless a fire station for any claims for
16 damages to voters or property arising from use of the fire station as a voting
17 place while engaged in the voting process.
18 (6) Ensure service levels for those fire stations are not affected, including entrance
19 and exit accessibility."
20 SECTION 2. This act is effective when it becomes law and applies to elections
21 occurring on or after that date.
