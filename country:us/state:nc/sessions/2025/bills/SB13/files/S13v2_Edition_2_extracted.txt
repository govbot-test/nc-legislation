Section 1:
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF NORTH CAROLINA
SESSION 2025
S 2
Commerce and Insurance Committee Substitute Adopted 2/4/25
Short Title: Clarify Insurance Fee Referral Cap. (Public)
Sponsors:
Referred to:
January 30, 2025
1 A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
2 AN ACT CLARIFYING THE APPLICABILITY OF THE CAP ON INSURANCE REFERRAL
3 FEES PAID TO NONLICENSED PERSONS.
4 The General Assembly of North Carolina enacts:
5 SECTION 1. G.S. 58-33-82(f) reads as rewritten:
6 "(f) No commission, fee, or other valuable consideration authorized under subsection (e)
7 of this section for the referral of insurance business by an unlicensed individual to a licensed
8 insurance agent or broker producer licensed under G.S. 58-33-26(c1)(6) shall exceed fifty dollars
9 ($50.00) in value. A violation of this subsection may be punished by a fine not to exceed two
10 thousand dollars ($2,000) for each violation. This subsection shall not apply to title insurance."
11 SECTION 2. This act becomes effective October 1, 2025, and applies to any referral
12 of personal lines insurance business made on or after that date.
*S13-v-2*
