10 following apply:subject to the following conditions:

22 document showing an anticipated date of separation. Each local school
23 administrative unit may determine the document or documents
24 sufficient to establish an anticipated date of separation so long as the
25 document required is easily accessible by the parent or legal guardian.
26 (3)c. A parent or legal guardian completes and submits the local school
27 administrative unit's required enrollment forms and documentation,
