6 "(a9) A student who is not a domiciliary of a local school administrative unit shall be

13 duty within a 12-month period.
14 (2) Upon request by the local school administrative unit where the student seeks

18 document showing the anticipated date of separation or date of projected

21 administrative unit's required enrollment forms and documentation, except

27 related to the disciplinary actions pursuant to G.S. 115C-366(a4) upon the
28 child commencing attendance. If the proof of residency has not yet become
29 available because the parent or legal guardian and child are residing in
