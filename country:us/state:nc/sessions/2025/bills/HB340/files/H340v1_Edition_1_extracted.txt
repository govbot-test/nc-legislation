Section 1:
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF NORTH CAROLINA
SESSION 2025
H 1
Short Title: Universal AIG Screening in Middle Schools. (Public)
Sponsors: Representatives R. Pierce, Johnson-Hostler, Baker, and F. Jackson (Primary
Sponsors).
For a complete list of sponsors, refer to the North Carolina General Assembly web site.
Referred to: Appropriations, if favorable, Rules, Calendar, and Operations of the House
March 11, 2025
1 A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
2 AN ACT TO REQUIRE THE STATE BOARD OF EDUCATION TO DEVELOP OR
3 PURCHASE AND DISSEMINATE A SCREENING ASSESSMENT FOR
4 ACADEMICALLY OR INTELLECTUALLY GIFTED CHILDREN TO BE
5 ADMINISTERED IN SIXTH GRADE.
6 The General Assembly of North Carolina enacts:
7 SECTION 1. G.S. 115C-150.6 is amended by adding a new subdivision to read:
8 "(1a) (i) Develop or purchase and (ii) disseminate a screening assessment to be used
9 in all middle schools to identify academically or intellectually gifted
10 students."
11 SECTION 2. G.S. 115C-150.7(b) is amended by adding a new subdivision to read:
12 "(1a) The use of the screening assessment provided pursuant to
13 G.S. 115C-150.6(1a) for all students in grade six to assist in identifying
14 academically or intellectually gifted students."
15 SECTION 3. There is appropriated from the General Fund to the Department of
16 Public Instruction the sum of seven million dollars ($7,000,000) in recurring funds for the
17 2025-2026 fiscal year to cover the costs of the screening assessments provided pursuant to this
18 act.
19 SECTION 4. This act becomes effective July 1, 2025.
