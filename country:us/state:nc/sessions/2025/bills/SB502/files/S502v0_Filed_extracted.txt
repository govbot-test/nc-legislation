3 CAPITAL NEEDS.
4 The General Assembly of North Carolina enacts:
5 SECTION 1. There is appropriated from the General Fund to the Office of State
6 Budget and Management the sum of three million three hundred thousand dollars ($3,300,000)
7 in nonrecurring funds for the 2025-2026 fiscal year to provide a directed grant to the Town of
8 Cape Carteret for the construction of a new facility to serve as the town hall, police headquarters,
9 and emergency operations center.
10 SECTION 2. This act becomes effective July 1, 2025.
