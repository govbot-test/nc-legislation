Section 1:
FILED SENATE
Mar 24, 2025
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF NORTH CAROLINA
S.B. 411
SESSION 2025
PRINCIPAL CLERK
S D
Short Title: School Bus Cameras/Evidence & Contract Length. (Public)
Sponsors: Senator Hise (Primary Sponsor).
Referred to:
1 A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
2 AN ACT ESTABLISHING THAT CERTAIN SCHOOL BUS CAMERA RECORDED
3 IMAGES ARE PRIMA FACIE EVIDENCE DEMONSTRATING A FAILURE TO STOP
4 FOR A SCHOOL BUS AND EXTENDING THE MAXIMUM LENGTH FOR
5 AUTOMATED SCHOOL BUS SAFETY CAMERA SERVICE CONTRACTS WITH
6 PRIVATE VENDORS.
7 The General Assembly of North Carolina enacts:
8 SECTION 1.(a) G.S. 20-217 reads as rewritten:
9 "§ 20-217. Motor vehicles to stop for properly marked and designated school buses in
10 certain instances; evidence of identity of driver.
11 …
12 (h) Automated school bus safety cameras, as defined in G.S. 115C-242.1, may be used
13 to detect and prosecute violations of this section. Any photograph or video recorded by an
14 automated school bus safety camera shall, if consistent with the North Carolina Rules of
15 Evidence, be admissible as evidence in any proceeding alleging a violation of subsection (a) of
16 this section. Recorded images from a school bus safety camera showing that a school bus was
17 stopped and was displaying its mechanical stop signal or flashing red lights shall be prima facie
18 evidence that (i) the school bus was stopped for the purpose of receiving or discharging
19 passengers and (ii) the school bus was equipped and marked in compliance with this section.
20 Failure to produce a photograph or video recorded by an automated school bus safety camera
21 shall not preclude prosecution under this section."
22 SECTION 1.(b) This section becomes effective December 1, 2025, and applies to
23 offenses committed on or after that date.
24 SECTION 2.(a) G.S. 115C-242.1 reads as rewritten:
25 "§ 115C-242.1. Installation and operation of automated school bus safety camera.
26 …
27 (b) Installation and Operation. – Automated school bus safety cameras may be installed
28 and operated on any school bus operated by a local board of education within a county that has
29 adopted an ordinance under G.S. 153A 246 as follows:
30 …
31 (2) A local board of education may enter into a service contract to install and
32 operate automated school bus safety cameras with a private vendor. Contracts
33 shall be let in accordance with the provisions of G.S. 143 129 applicable to
34 purchases of apparatus, supplies, materials, or equipment. The maximum
35 length of any contract entered into under this subdivision shall be three five
36 years. A contract entered into under this subdivision may contain an option to
*DRS15189-NE-84*
General Assembly Of North Carolina Session 2025
1 renew or extend the contract for only one additional term not to exceed three
2 five years.
3 …."
4 SECTION 2.(b) This section becomes effective July 1, 2025, and applies to contracts
5 issued, renewed, or amended on or after that date.
6 SECTION 3. Except as otherwise provided, this act is effective when it becomes
7 law.
