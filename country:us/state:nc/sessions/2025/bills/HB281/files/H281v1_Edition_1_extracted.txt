Section 1:
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF NORTH CAROLINA
SESSION 2025
H 1
Short Title: Restore Down-Zoning/Multiple Municipalities. (Local)
Sponsors: Representative Cairns.
For a complete list of sponsors, refer to the North Carolina General Assembly web site.
Referred to: Judiciary 3, if favorable, Housing and Development, if favorable, Rules, Calendar,
and Operations of the House
March 5, 2025
1 A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
2 AN ACT TO RESTORE THE AUTHORITY TO INITIATE DOWN-ZONING IN
3 MOREHEAD CITY, THE TOWN OF BEAUFORT, THE TOWN OF BOGUE, THE
4 TOWN OF CAPE CARTERET, THE TOWN OF CEDAR POINT, AND THE TOWN OF
5 NEWPORT.
6 The General Assembly of North Carolina enacts:
7 SECTION 1. G.S. 160D-601(d), as amended by Section 3K.1(a) of S.L. 2024-57,
8 reads as rewritten:
9 "(d) Down-Zoning. – No amendment to zoning regulations or a zoning map that
10 down-zones property shall be initiated, enacted, or enforced initiated nor is it enforceable without
11 the written consent of all property owners whose property is the subject of the down-zoning
12 amendment. amendment, unless the down-zoning amendment is initiated by the local
13 government. For purposes of this section, "down-zoning" means a zoning ordinance that affects
14 an area of land in one of the following ways:
15 (1) By decreasing the development density of the land to be less dense than was
16 allowed under its previous usage.
17 (2) By reducing the permitted uses of the land that are specified in a zoning
18 ordinance or land development regulation to fewer uses than were allowed
19 under its previous usage.
20 (3) By creating any type of nonconformity on land not in a residential zoning
21 district, including a nonconforming use, nonconforming lot, nonconforming
22 structure, nonconforming improvement, or nonconforming site element."
23 SECTION 2.(a) This act applies only to the municipality of Morehead City and the
24 Towns of Beaufort, Bogue, Cape Carteret, Cedar Point, and Newport.
25 SECTION 2.(b) This act is effective when it becomes law and applies retroactively
26 to December 11, 2024. Any adopted ordinance affected by Section 3K.1 of S.L. 2024-57 shall
27 be in effect as it was on or before December 11, 2024.
