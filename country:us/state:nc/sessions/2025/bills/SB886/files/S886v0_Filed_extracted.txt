Section 1:
FILED SENATE
Apr 28, 2026
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF NORTH CAROLINA
S.B. 886
SESSION 2025
PRINCIPAL CLERK
S D
Short Title: Funds/Town of Grifton Resiliency. (Public)
Sponsors: Senator Smith (Primary Sponsor).
Referred to:
1 A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
2 AN ACT TO APPROPRIATE FUNDS TO THE TOWN OF GRIFTON FOR DESIGN OF
3 FLOOD RESILIENCY PROJECTS.
4 The General Assembly of North Carolina enacts:
5 SECTION 1. There is appropriated from the General Fund to the Office of State
6 Budget and Management the sum of three million dollars ($3,000,000) in nonrecurring funds for
7 the 2026-2027 fiscal year to provide a directed grant to the Town of Grifton for design and
8 construction of flood mitigation and resiliency projects.
9 SECTION 2. This act becomes effective July 1, 2026.
*DRS35382-MCa-293*
