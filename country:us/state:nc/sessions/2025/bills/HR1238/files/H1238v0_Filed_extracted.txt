H.R. 1238
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF NORTH CAROLINA
Jun 23, 2026
SESSION 2025
HOUSE PRINCIPAL CLERK
H D
HOUSE SIMPLE RESOLUTION DRHR50035-MC-347C
Sponsors: Representative Bell.
Referred
to:
2 WINNER OF THE STANLEY CUP, AS CHAMPIONS OF THE NATIONAL HOCKEY
3 LEAGUE.
4 Whereas, the Carolina Hurricanes, representing the great State of North Carolina,
5 were originally founded as the Whalers and relocated from Hartford, Connecticut, to Raleigh,
6 North Carolina, in 1997, becoming a cornerstone of professional sports in the State; and
7 Whereas, the Carolina Hurricanes completed a historic 2025-2026 National Hockey
8 League regular season with 53 wins, 22 losses, and 7 overtime losses, claiming the Metropolitan
9 Division championship for the fourth time in six seasons, claiming the No. 1 seed in the Eastern
10 Conference for the first time in franchise history, scoring a franchise-record 291 goals, with seven
11 players scoring 20 or more goals, establishing themselves as one of the most complete teams in
12 the entire league through elite defense, relentless forechecking, veteran leadership, and
13 exceptional goaltending; and
14 Whereas, Head Coach Rod Brind'Amour, has been involved as a player or coach in
15 102 of Carolina's 104 all-time playoff victories, and guided this team to eight consecutive playoff
16 berths, including back-to-back deep runs and now a Stanley Cup championship; and
17 Whereas, the Hurricanes dominated through the 2026 Stanley Cup Playoffs with 16
18 wins and three losses, sweeping the Ottawa Senators and the Philadelphia Flyers, eliminating the
19 Montreal Canadiens in the conference finals in five games and defeating the Vegas Golden
20 Knights in six games to secure the Stanley Cup for the second time in franchise history; and
21 Whereas, team captain Jordan Staal won the Conn Smythe Trophy as the Most
22 Valuable Player of the 2026 Stanley Cup Playoffs, finishing the postseason with 12 points,
23 including a franchise-record six goals in the Final; and
24 Whereas, the Hurricanes' remaining offensive corps mounted a deep, well-balanced
25 attack built on relentless forechecking, possession dominance, and versatile scoring; the
26 defensive corps operated as a highly mobile, puck-moving, and aggressive unit; and the
27 goaltenders delivered a legendary performance in the playoffs; and
28 Whereas, team owner Tom Dundon, and many, many others, have all had an
29 instrumental role in the Canes' quest for the Cup; and
30 Whereas, the Carolina Hurricanes have been a consistent beacon of pride for the
31 citizens of North Carolina, fostering community engagement, youth hockey programs, and civic
32 pride in Raleigh and across the entire State; Now, therefore,
33 Be it resolved by the House of Representatives:
34 SECTION 1. The North Carolina General Assembly honors the accomplishments
35 and dedication of the Carolina Hurricanes Hockey Club, its owner, and management in winning
36 the 2026 Stanley Cup, in being a true partner with the State in community and charitable service,
*DRHR50035-MC-347C*
General Assembly Of North Carolina Session 2025
1 and for its perseverance, dedication, and sportsmanlike character during an exemplary season,
2 thrilling playoff, and exhilarating championship.
3 SECTION 2. The Principal Clerk shall transmit a certified copy of this resolution to
4 each member of the Carolina Hurricanes Hockey Club and the team's owner, Tom Dundon, and
5 head coach, Rod Brind'Amour.
6 SECTION 3. This resolution is effective upon adoption.
