FILED SENATE
Jun 25, 2026
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF NORTH CAROLINA
S.J.R. 1090
SESSION 2025
PRINCIPAL CLERK
S D
SENATE JOINT RESOLUTION DRSJR55026-BX-1
Sponsors: Senator Rabon (Primary Sponsor).
Referred
to:
2 FILL THE VACANCY ON THE NORTH CAROLINA UTILITIES COMMISSION.
3 Whereas, pursuant to G.S. 62-10(f), the nomination made by the Governor to fill the
4 vacancy on the North Carolina Utilities Commission is subject to confirmation by the General
5 Assembly by joint resolution; and
6 Whereas, a vacancy occurred as a result of Karen M. Kemerait stepping down; and
7 Whereas, the Governor has submitted to the presiding officers of the House of
8 Representatives and the Senate the name of his appointee, John Gajda of Wake County, to serve
9 on the North Carolina Utilities Commission for a term expiring June 30, 2027; Now, therefore,
10 Be it resolved by the Senate, the House of Representatives concurring:
11 SECTION 1. The appointment of John Gajda of Wake County to the North Carolina
12 Utilities Commission for a term expiring June 30, 2027, is confirmed.
13 SECTION 2. This resolution is effective upon ratification.
*DRSJR55026-BX-1*
