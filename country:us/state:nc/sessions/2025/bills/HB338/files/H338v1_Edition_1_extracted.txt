Section 1:
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF NORTH CAROLINA
SESSION 2025
H 1
Short Title: City of Southport/Deannexations. (Local)
Sponsors: Representative Miller.
For a complete list of sponsors, refer to the North Carolina General Assembly web site.
Referred to: State and Local Government, if favorable, Finance, if favorable, Rules, Calendar,
and Operations of the House
March 10, 2025
1 A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
2 AN ACT TO REMOVE CERTAIN DESCRIBED PROPERTY FROM THE CORPORATE
3 LIMITS OF THE CITY OF SOUTHPORT.
4 The General Assembly of North Carolina enacts:
5 SECTION 1. The following described property, identified by Brunswick County
6 Tax Office Parcel Identification Number, is removed from the corporate limits of the City of
7 Southport:
8 220NA002; 220NA011; 220NA014; 220NA019; 220NA021; 220NA04401; 220NA053;
9 220NA082; 220NA101; 220NA104; and 220NA110.
10 SECTION 2. This act has no effect upon the validity of any liens of the City of
11 Southport for ad valorem taxes or special assessments outstanding before the effective date of
12 this act. Such liens may be collected or foreclosed upon after the effective date of this act as
13 though the property were still within the corporate limits of the City of Southport.
14 SECTION 3. This act becomes effective June 30, 2025. Property in the territory
15 described in Section 1 of this act as of January 1, 2025, is no longer subject to municipal taxes
16 for taxes imposed for taxable years beginning on or after July 1, 2025.
*H338-v-1*
