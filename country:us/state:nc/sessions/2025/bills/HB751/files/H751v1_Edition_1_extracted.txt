Section 1:
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF NORTH CAROLINA
SESSION 2025
H 1
Short Title: Uniform Energy Rates for Seasonal Service. (Public)
Sponsors: Representatives McNeely, Arp, and Huneycutt (Primary Sponsors).
For a complete list of sponsors, refer to the North Carolina General Assembly web site.
Referred to: Energy and Public Utilities, if favorable, Rules, Calendar, and Operations of the
House
April 3, 2025
1 A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
2 AN ACT TO REQUIRE CERTAIN ELECTRIC PUBLIC UTILITIES TO OFFER UNIFORM
3 ENERGY RATES FOR SEASONAL OR INTERMITTENT SERVICE THROUGHOUT
4 THE STATE.
5 The General Assembly of North Carolina enacts:
6 SECTION 1. Any electric public utility, as defined in G.S. 62-3(23), serving at least
7 150,000 North Carolina retail jurisdictional customers as of January 1, 2025, that has established
8 a rate schedule for seasonal or intermittent service in a portion of the utility's service area shall,
9 no later than September 1, 2025, file an application for a rate case to offer that rate schedule for
10 seasonal or intermittent service to all eligible customers throughout the State served by the
11 electric public utility.
12 SECTION 2. This act is effective when it becomes law.
*H751-v-1*
