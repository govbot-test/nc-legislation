Section 1:
FILED SENATE
Mar 13, 2025
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF NORTH CAROLINA
S.B. 293
SESSION 2025
PRINCIPAL CLERK
S D
Short Title: Town of Pinetops Fire Dept. Appropriation. (Public)
Sponsors: Senator Smith (Primary Sponsor).
Referred to:
1 A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
2 AN ACT TO APPROPRIATE FUNDS TO THE TOWN OF PINETOPS FOR EQUIPMENT
3 NEEDS OF THE FIRE DEPARTMENT.
4 The General Assembly of North Carolina enacts:
5 SECTION 1. There is appropriated from the General Fund to the Town of Pinetops
6 the nonrecurring sum of one hundred thousand dollars ($100,000) for the 2025-2026 fiscal year
7 to be allocated to the volunteer fire department operated by the town for equipment needs.
8 SECTION 2. This act becomes effective July 1, 2025.
*DRS45154-MCa-80*
