Section 1:
FILED SENATE
Mar 25, 2025
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF NORTH CAROLINA
S.B. 608
SESSION 2025
PRINCIPAL CLERK
S D
Short Title: Revise Various Laws/Work 1st Cash Assistance. (Public)
Sponsors: Senators Salvador, Waddell, and Murdock (Primary Sponsors).
Referred to:
1 A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
2 AN ACT TO EXPAND TEMPORARY ASSISTANCE FOR NEEDY FAMILIES
3 (TANF)/WORK FIRST PROGRAM ASSISTANCE BENEFITS TO INCLUDE A
4 WOMAN WHO IS PREGNANT, INCREASE THE CASH ASSISTANCE BENEFIT
5 AMOUNT, INCREASE RESOURCE AND INCOME LIMITS FOR CASH ASSISTANCE,
6 AND REMOVE TIME LIMITATIONS ON CASH ASSISTANCE OTHER THAN THOSE
7 REQUIRED BY FEDERAL LAW.
8 The General Assembly of North Carolina enacts:
9 SECTION 1. G.S. 108A-24 reads as rewritten:
10 "§ 108A-24. Definitions.
11 As used in Chapter 108A:
12 …
13 (3c) "Family" means a unit consisting of a minor child or children and one or more
14 of their biological parents, adoptive parents, stepparents, or grandparents
15 living together. For purposes of the Work First Program, family also includes
16 a pregnant woman, blood or half-blood relative or adoptive relative limited to
17 brother, sister, great-grandparent, great-great-grandparent, uncle, aunt,
18 great-uncle, great-aunt, great-great-uncle, great-great-aunt, nephew, niece,
19 first cousin, stepbrother, and stepsister.
20 …
21 (15) "Work First Family Assistance" is a program of time-limited periodic
22 payments to assist in maintaining the children of eligible families while the
23 adult family members engage in activities to prepare for entering and to enter
24 the workplace.
25 …."
26 SECTION 2. G.S. 108A-27.01 reads as rewritten:
27 "§ 108A-27.01. Income eligibility and payment level for Work First Family Assistance.
28 The maximum net family annual income eligibility standards for Work First Family
29 Assistance are as provided in the table below. shall be equal to seventy-five percent (75%) of the
30 federal poverty level for the applicable family size. The payment level for Work First Family
31 Assistance shall be fifty percent (50%) of the standard of need.forty percent (40%) of the federal
32 poverty level for the applicable family size.

34 Size Level

36 2 5,664

7 SECTION 3. Part 2 of Article 2 of Chapter 108A of the General Statutes is amended
8 by adding a new section to read:
9 "§ 108A-27.02. Resource limits.
10 The resources of a person or family for cash assistance shall not exceed five thousand dollars
11 ($5,000). However, the equity value of one vehicle per family shall not be counted as a resource
12 of the family if the vehicle is used for work, training, or education in preparation for employment
13 or a job search; provided, further that an exclusion in the amount of four thousand six hundred
14 fifty dollars ($4,650) of the equity value of one additional vehicle is allowed."
15 SECTION 4. G.S. 108A-27.1 reads as rewritten:
16 "§ 108A-27.1. Time limitations on assistance.
17 (a) Under the Standard Work First Program, unless an extension or an exemption is
18 provided pursuant to the provisions of the Part or the State Plan, any cash assistance provided to
19 a person or family in the employment program shall only be provided for a cumulative total of
20 24 months. After having received cash assistance for 24 months, the person or the family may
21 reapply for cash assistance, but not until after 36 months from the last month the person or the
22 family received cash assistance. This subsection shall not apply to child-only cases.
23 (b) Electing Counties may set any time limitations on assistance it finds appropriate, so
24 long as the time limitations do not conflict with or exceed any federal time limitations.
25 The State shall not impose any time limitations on eligible families receiving Work First
26 Family Assistance, except for those time limitations set by federal law or regulations."
27 SECTION 5. This act is effective when it becomes law.
