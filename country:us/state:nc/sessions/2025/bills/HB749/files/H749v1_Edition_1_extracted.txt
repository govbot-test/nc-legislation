Section 1:
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF NORTH CAROLINA
SESSION 2025
H 1
Short Title: Greensboro Public Safety Funds. (Public)
Sponsors: Representatives Clark, Blust, Quick, and Harrison (Primary Sponsors).
For a complete list of sponsors, refer to the North Carolina General Assembly web site.
Referred to: Appropriations, if favorable, Rules, Calendar, and Operations of the House
April 3, 2025
1 A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
2 AN ACT TO APPROPRIATE FUNDS TO THE CITY OF GREENSBORO FOR VARIOUS
3 PUBLIC SAFETY NEEDS.
4 The General Assembly of North Carolina enacts:
5 SECTION 1. There is appropriated from the General Fund to the Office of State
6 Budget and Management the sum of nine million eight hundred eighteen thousand dollars
7 ($9,818,000) in nonrecurring funds for the 2025-2026 fiscal year to provide a directed grant to
8 the City of Greensboro to use as follows:
9 (1) Nine million dollars ($9,000,000) to construct a new fire station to service the
10 north side of the Piedmont Triad International Airport.
11 (2) Eight hundred eighteen thousand dollars ($818,000) to relocate, enhance, and
12 operate the Police Department's Real Time Intelligence Center.
13 SECTION 2. This act becomes effective July 1, 2025.
*H749-v-1*
