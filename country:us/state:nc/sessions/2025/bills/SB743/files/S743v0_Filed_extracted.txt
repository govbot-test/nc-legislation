Section 1:
FILED SENATE
Mar 25, 2025
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF NORTH CAROLINA
S.B. 743
SESSION 2025
PRINCIPAL CLERK
S D
Short Title: Funds for Davidson Emergency Services. (Public)
Sponsors: Senator Mohammed (Primary Sponsor).
Referred to:
1 A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
2 AN ACT TO APPROPRIATE FUNDS TO THE TOWN OF DAVIDSON FOR EMERGENCY
3 SERVICES NEEDS.
4 The General Assembly of North Carolina enacts:
5 SECTION 1. There is appropriated from the General Fund to the Office of State
6 Budget and Management the sum of twenty million four hundred forty thousand dollars
7 ($20,440,000) in nonrecurring funds for the 2025-2026 fiscal year to provide a directed grant to
8 the Town of Davidson for emergency services. These funds shall be used as follows:
9 (1) Seventeen million dollars ($17,000,000) for the construction of a fire station.
10 (2) Three million dollars ($3,000,000) to purchase an emergency vehicle.
11 (3) One hundred thousand dollars ($100,000) to purchase police equipment to
12 enhance community safety.
13 (4) Three hundred forty thousand dollars ($340,000) to replace breathing
14 apparatus equipment.
15 SECTION 2. This act becomes effective July 1, 2025.
*DRS45366-LGa-60*
