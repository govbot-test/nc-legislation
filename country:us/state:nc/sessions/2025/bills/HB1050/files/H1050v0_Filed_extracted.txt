Section 1:
H.B. 1050
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF NORTH CAROLINA
Apr 27, 2026
SESSION 2025
HOUSE PRINCIPAL CLERK
H D
Short Title: Funding for Sex Ed Health Awareness. (Public)
Sponsors: Representative K. Brown.
Referred to:
1 A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
2 AN ACT TO PROVIDE FUNDING FOR IMPROVING SEXUAL HEALTH AND
3 AWARENESS.
4 The General Assembly of North Carolina enacts:
5 SECTION 1. There is appropriated from the General Fund to the Office of State
6 Budget and Management the sum of thirty-one thousand dollars ($31,000) in nonrecurring funds
7 for the 2026-2027 fiscal year to provide a directed grant to Joyful Soul Treasures, Inc., a nonprofit
8 organization, for a marketing campaign and an eight-week workshop event, including materials,
9 instructor fees, refreshments, completion certificates, and awards, designed to enhance sexual
10 health education and awareness among adolescents aged 13-18.
11 SECTION 2. This act becomes effective July 1, 2026.
*DRH40660-MCa-319*
