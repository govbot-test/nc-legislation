Section 1:
H.B. 324
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF NORTH CAROLINA
Mar 6, 2025
SESSION 2025
HOUSE PRINCIPAL CLERK
H D
Short Title: Funds for Greensboro Transit Agency. (Public)
Sponsors: Representative Branson.
Referred to:
1 A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
2 AN ACT TO APPROPRIATE FUNDS TO THE GREENSBORO TRANSIT AGENCY.
3 The General Assembly of North Carolina enacts:
4 SECTION 1. Of the funds appropriated from the Highway Fund to the Department
5 of Transportation for the 2025-2026 fiscal year, the sum of eight hundred thousand dollars
6 ($800,000) in nonrecurring funds shall be allocated to the Greensboro Transit Agency to be used
7 for the following purposes:
8 North/South Crossmax Orange Bus Route $500,000
9 Pilot Program to transport high school juniors and seniors
10 to Guilford Technical Community College campuses $300,000
11 SECTION 2. This act becomes effective July 1, 2025.
*DRH30157-MWa-36*
