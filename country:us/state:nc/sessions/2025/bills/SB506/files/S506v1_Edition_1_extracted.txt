Section 1:
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF NORTH CAROLINA
SESSION 2025
S 1
Short Title: ATR Salary Sups and Grant Increases. (Public)
Sponsors: Senators Lee, Overcash, and Corbin (Primary Sponsors).
Referred to: Rules and Operations of the Senate
March 26, 2025
1 A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
2 AN ACT TO INCREASE THE FUNDING FOR ADVANCED TEACHING ROLES SALARY
3 SUPPLEMENTS AND GRANTS.
4 The General Assembly of North Carolina enacts:
5 SECTION 1. There is appropriated from the General Fund to the Department of
6 Public Instruction an additional sixteen million two hundred thousand dollars ($16,200,000) in
7 recurring funds for the 2025-2026 fiscal year and an additional twenty million one hundred
8 thousand dollars ($20,100,000) in recurring funds for the 2026-2027 fiscal year to bring the total
9 revised net appropriation to the Department to twenty-seven million one hundred thousand
10 dollars ($27,100,000) in recurring funds for the 2025-2026 fiscal year and thirty-one million
11 dollars ($31,000,000) in recurring funds for the 2026-2027 fiscal year for the Department to
12 allocate to public school units participating in the Advanced Teaching Roles program to provide
13 salary supplements under G.S. 115C-310.13.
14 SECTION 2. There is appropriated from the General Fund to the Department of
15 Public Instruction an additional one million dollars ($1,000,000) in recurring funds for the
16 2025-2026 fiscal year to bring the total revised net appropriation to the Department to six million
17 five hundred thousand dollars ($6,500,000) in recurring funds for the 2025-2026 fiscal year for
18 the Department to expand the Advanced Teaching Roles program in accordance with Article 20A
19 of Chapter 115C of the General Statutes.
20 SECTION 3. This act becomes effective July 1, 2025.
*S506-v-1*
