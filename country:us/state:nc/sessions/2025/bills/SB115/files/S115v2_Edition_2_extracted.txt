GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF NORTH CAROLINA
SESSION 2025
S 2
Second Edition Engrossed 2/18/25
Short Title: General Assembly Appointments. (Public)
Sponsors: Senator Rabon (Primary Sponsor).
Referred to: Rules and Operations of the Senate
February 18, 2025
1 A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
2 AN ACT TO APPOINT PERSONS TO VARIOUS PUBLIC OFFICES UPON THE
3 RECOMMENDATION OF THE PRESIDENT PRO TEMPORE OF THE SENATE.
4 The General Assembly of North Carolina enacts:
5
6 PART I. PRESIDENT PRO TEMPORE'S RECOMMENDATIONS
7 SECTION 1.1. A. Graham Shirley of the Third Judicial Division is appointed as a
8 Special Superior Court Judge for a term expiring December 31, 2032.
9 SECTION 1.2. Montell W. Irvin of Wake County is appointed to the North Carolina
10 Turnpike Authority for a term expiring January 13, 2029.
11 SECTION 1.3. Effective March 1, 2025, Pamela T. Thompson of Alamance County
12 and Benjamin Curtis of Rockingham County are appointed to the Governor's Crime Commission
13 for terms expiring February 28, 2027.
14 SECTION 1.4. Effective July 1, 2025, Michael C. Brown of New Hanover County
15 is appointed to the Local Government Commission for a term expiring June 30, 2029.
16 SECTION 1.5.(a) Katherine Martin of Wake County is appointed to the North
17 Carolina Child Care Commission for a term expiring June 30, 2026.
18 SECTION 1.5.(b) Terra Flint of Rockingham County is appointed to the North
19 Carolina Child Care Commission for a term expiring June 30, 2025, to fill the unexpired term of
20 Ashley B. Snyder.
21
22 PART II. EFFECTIVE DATE
23 SECTION 2.1. This act is effective when it becomes law. Unless otherwise provided,
24 terms begin upon the effective date of this act.
*S115-v-2*
