GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF NORTH CAROLINA
SESSION 2025
H 2
HOUSE BILL 346
Committee Substitute Favorable 4/30/25
Short Title: Central Carolina CC MCC FTE Calculation. (Public)
Sponsors:
Referred to:
March 11, 2025
1 A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
2 AN ACT TO DIRECT THE STATE BOARD OF COMMUNITY COLLEGES TO INCLUDE
3 THE HARNETT AND CHATHAM HEALTH SCIENCES LOCATIONS IN THE
4 FULL-TIME EQUIVALENT CALCULATION OF THE RESPECTIVE MAIN
5 CAMPUSES OF CENTRAL CAROLINA COMMUNITY COLLEGE FOR THE
6 PURPOSES OF MAINTAINING MULTICAMPUS CENTER STATUS.
7 The General Assembly of North Carolina enacts:
8 SECTION 1. Notwithstanding G.S. 115D-5(o) and any State Board of Community
9 Colleges policy to the contrary, the State Board of Community Colleges shall calculate the budget
10 full-time equivalent (FTE) enrolled at the Harnett Main Campus for the purposes of multicampus
11 center status by combining the total number of FTE at the Harnett Main Campus with the total
12 number of FTE at the Harnett Health Sciences Center.
13 SECTION 2. Notwithstanding G.S. 115D-5(o) and any State Board of Community
14 Colleges policy to the contrary, the State Board of Community Colleges shall calculate the budget
15 full-time equivalent (FTE) enrolled at the Chatham Main Campus for the purposes of
16 multicampus center status by combining the total number of FTE at the Chatham Main Campus
17 with the total number of FTE at the Chatham Health Sciences Center.
18 SECTION 3. This act is effective when it becomes law.
