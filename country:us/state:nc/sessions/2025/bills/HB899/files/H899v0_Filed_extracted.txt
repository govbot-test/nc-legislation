H.B. 899
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF NORTH CAROLINA
Apr 10, 2025
SESSION 2025
HOUSE PRINCIPAL CLERK
H D
HOUSE BILL DRH30249-MR-112
Short Title: Return Oversight of OSFM to DOI Commissioner. (Public)
Sponsors: Representative Blust.
Referred to:
1 A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
2 AN ACT TO ELIMINATE THE INDEPENDENCE OF THE OFFICE OF STATE FIRE
3 MARSHAL AND RETURN AUTHORITY TO THE COMMISSIONER OF THE
4 DEPARTMENT OF INSURANCE.
5 The General Assembly of North Carolina enacts:
6 SECTION 1.(a) Section 30.8 of S.L. 2023-134 is repealed.
7 SECTION 1.(b) Parts X, XI, and XII of S.L. 2023-151 are repealed.
8 SECTION 1.(c) Section 6.4 of S.L. 2021-1 is repealed.
9 SECTION 2.(a) All employees of the Office of the State Fire Marshal on October 1,
10 2025, shall continue as employees of the Office at their option or until further action is taken by
11 the Commissioner in accordance with law. All positions in the Office of the State Fire Marshal
12 on October 1, 2025, shall remain in the Office of the State Fire Marshal unless changed by the
13 General Assembly.
14 SECTION 2.(b) Rules adopted by the State Fire Marshal, if any, pursuant to Chapter
15 58 or Chapter 143 of the General Statutes shall remain in effect until amended by the
16 Commissioner as authorized by Chapter 150B of the General Statutes.
17 SECTION 2.(c) A unique fire department identification number (FDID) issued by
18 the State Fire Marshal, if any, pursuant to G.S. 58-84-41 on or before the date this act becomes
19 law shall continue to be used for the purposes provided for in G.S. 58-84-41(b).
20 SECTION 2.(d) The representative trustee appointed by the State Fire Marshal, if
21 any, pursuant to G.S. 58-84-30 shall continue to serve at the pleasure of the Commissioner.
22 SECTION 2.(e) The designee appointed by the State Fire Marshal, if any, pursuant
23 to the Firefighters' and Rescue Squad Workers' Pension Fund Advisory Panel under G.S. 58-86-6
24 shall continue to serve at the discretion of the Commissioner.
25 SECTION 2.(f) The current members of the board or commission that were
26 appointed by the State Fire Marshal may continue to serve until the expiration of their terms.
27 Upon the expiration of the term of each current member appointed by the State Fire Marshal, the
28 Commissioner shall appoint a successor in accordance with the provisions of the respective
29 section of the General Statutes.
30 SECTION 3. This act is effective October 1, 2025.
