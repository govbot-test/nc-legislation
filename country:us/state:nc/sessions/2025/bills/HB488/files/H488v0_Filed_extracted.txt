34 achievement grade. The school achievement grade shall be based on the following scale and shall
35 not be modified to add any other designation related to other performance measures, such as a
36 "plus" or "minus":
37 (1) A score of at least 85 is equivalent to a school achievement grade of A.
38 (2) A score of at least 70 is equivalent to a school achievement grade of B.
39 (3) A score of at least 55 is equivalent to a school achievement grade of C.
40 (4) A score of at least 40 is equivalent to a school achievement grade of D.
41 (5) A score of less than 40 is equivalent to a school achievement grade of F.
42 (c) Calculation of the School Growth Score. – Using the Education Value-Added
43 Assessment System (EVAAS), the State Board shall calculate the overall growth score earned
44 by schools. In calculating the total growth score earned by schools, the State Board of Education
45 shall weight student growth on the achievement measures as provided in subsection (b) of this
46 section that have available growth values; provided that for schools serving students in grades
47 nine through 12, the growth score shall only include growth values for measures calculated under
48 sub-subdivisions a. and b. of subdivision (2) of subsection (b) of this section. The numerical
49 values used to determine whether a school has met, exceeded, or has not met expected growth
50 shall be translated to a 100-point scale and used for school reporting purposes as provided in
51 G.S. 115C-12(9)c1., 115C-218.65, 115C-238.66, and 116-239.8.

1 (c1) Calculation of School Growth Grades. – For all schools, the score for school growth,
2 as provided in subsection (c) of this section, shall be converted by the State Board of Education
3 to a 100-point scale and used to determine the school growth grade. The school growth grade
4 shall be based on the following scale and shall not be modified to add any other designation
5 related to other performance measures, such as a "plus" or "minus":
6 (1) A score of at least 90 is equivalent to a school growth grade of A.
7 (2) A score of at least 80 is equivalent to a school growth grade of B.

16 shall account for twenty percent (20%) of the total sum. For all schools, the total school

25 (3) A school performance score of at least 55 is equivalent to an overall school
26 performance grade of C.
27 (4) A school performance score of at least 40 is equivalent to an overall school
28 performance grade of D.
29 (5) A school performance score of less than 40 points is equivalent to an overall
30 school performance grade of F.

32 minimum number of students in a subgroup served by a school that is necessary to disaggregate

35 (1) Economically disadvantaged students.
36 (2) Students from major racial and ethnic groups.
37 (3) Children with disabilities.
38 (4) English learners.

40 Scores and Corresponding Grades for Certain Subgroups of Students Served by a School. – In

42 corresponding grades awarded under subsections (b) through (c1) of this section, for each school
43 that serves a minimum number of students in a subgroup of students listed in subsection (d1) of

48 students shall not be included in the calculation of the overall school performance achievement
49 and school growth scores and corresponding grades under subsection (d) subsections (b) through
50 (c1) of this section.
