14 a probation and parole agency known as the "Cherokee Marshals Service."

20 of Public Safety to the same extent as a probation or post-release supervision officer of the
21 Department for any person over which the Cherokee Tribal Courts have jurisdiction to proceed
22 in a criminal case and impose a sentence, including a fine, community service, or imprisonment.

24 transferring information to the Cherokee Tribal Courts.

26 (e) Marshals of the Catawba Indian Nation shall (i) be required to meet the standards set
27 forth in G.S. 1E-12 for law enforcement officers and (ii) have the same territorial jurisdiction,
28 powers, and immunities as a law enforcement officer under G.S. 1E-12.
29 (f) Notwithstanding any other provision of law, Marshals of the Catawba Indian Nation
30 shall have access to all probation and parole records of the North Carolina Department of Public
31 Safety to the same extent as a probation or post-release supervision officer of the Department for
