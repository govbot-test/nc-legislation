Section 1:
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF NORTH CAROLINA
SESSION 2025
H 1
Short Title: Funds for PTSD Treatment for Police Officers. (Public)
Sponsors: Representatives Paré, Reeder, Chesser, and Campbell (Primary Sponsors).
For a complete list of sponsors, refer to the North Carolina General Assembly web site.
Referred to: Appropriations, if favorable, Rules, Calendar, and Operations of the House
April 1, 2025
1 A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
2 AN ACT TO APPROPRIATE FUNDS TO THE DEPARTMENT OF PUBLIC SAFETY TO
3 ESTABLISH A GRANT PROGRAM TO TREAT POLICE OFFICERS WHO HAVE BEEN
4 DIAGNOSED WITH POSTTRAUMATIC STRESS DISORDER (PTSD).
5 The General Assembly of North Carolina enacts:
6 SECTION 1. There is appropriated from the General Fund to the Department of
7 Public Safety the sum of five hundred thousand dollars ($500,000) in nonrecurring funds for the
8 2025-2026 fiscal year to assist the Department in establishing a grant program to treat police
9 officers who have been diagnosed with Posttraumatic Stress Disorder (PTSD).
10 SECTION 2. This act becomes effective July 1, 2025.
*H571-v-1*
