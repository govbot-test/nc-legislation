12 When used in this Chapter, unless the context otherwise requires, the term:

30 third-party provider that has been approved by the Appraiser Qualifications
31 Board of the Appraisal Foundation and the Appraisal Board, which meets the
32 standards for Practical Applications of Real Estate Appraisal and satisfies the
33 experience requirements for real estate appraiser licensure or certification as
34 required by this Chapter.

36 Board evidencing that the person named therein has satisfied the requirements
