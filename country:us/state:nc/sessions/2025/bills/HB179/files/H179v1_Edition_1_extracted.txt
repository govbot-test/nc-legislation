GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF NORTH CAROLINA
SESSION 2025
H 1
HOUSE BILL 179
Short Title: Labor Org. Membership Dues Tax Deductible. (Public)
Sponsors: Representatives Crawford, G. Pierce, R. Pierce, and von Haefen (Primary
Sponsors).
For a complete list of sponsors, refer to the North Carolina General Assembly web site.
Referred to: Rules, Calendar, and Operations of the House
February 25, 2025
1 A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
2 AN ACT TO CREATE A TAX DEDUCTION FOR LABOR ORGANIZATION
3 MEMBERSHIP DUES.
4 The General Assembly of North Carolina enacts:
5 SECTION 1. G.S. 105-153.5(b) is amended by adding a new subdivision to read:
6 "(17) The amount paid by the taxpayer during the taxable year as labor organization
7 membership dues. For purposes of this subdivision, "labor organization
8 membership dues" are dues, fees, assessments, or other monies required as a
9 condition of membership or participation in a labor organization as defined in
10 G.S. 168A-3."
11 SECTION 2. This act is effective for taxable years beginning on or after January 1,
12 2026.
