Section 1:
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF NORTH CAROLINA
SESSION 2025
H 1
Short Title: NC Folk Fest and FIFA Funds. (Public)
Sponsors: Representatives Clark, Branson, Quick, and Harrison (Primary Sponsors).
For a complete list of sponsors, refer to the North Carolina General Assembly web site.
Referred to: Appropriations, if favorable, Rules, Calendar, and Operations of the House
April 2, 2025
1 A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
2 AN ACT TO APPROPRIATE FUNDS TO THE CITY OF GREENSBORO FOR THE NC
3 FOLK FEST AND FOR THE 2025 FIFA CLUB WORLD CUP.
4 The General Assembly of North Carolina enacts:
5 SECTION 1. The sum of five hundred thousand dollars ($500,000) in nonrecurring
6 funds for the 2025-2026 fiscal year is appropriated from the General Fund to the Office of State
7 Budget and Management to provide a grant to the City of Greensboro (City) to be allocated for
8 the following purposes:
9 (1) Two hundred fifty thousand dollars ($250,000) for the provision of law
10 enforcement, security, and transportation services associated with the City's
11 designation as a training camp host for the FIFA Club World Cup 2025.
12 (2) Two hundred fifty thousand dollars ($250,000) for expenses associated with
13 the 2025 North Carolina Folk Festival.
14 SECTION 2. This act becomes effective July 1, 2025.
*H623-v-1*
