Section 1:
FILED SENATE
Feb 26, 2025
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF NORTH CAROLINA
S.B. 182
SESSION 2025
PRINCIPAL CLERK
S D
Short Title: Funds/Conover Water and Wastewater. (Public)
Sponsors: Senator Hollo (Primary Sponsor).
Referred to:
1 A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
2 AN ACT TO APPROPRIATE FUNDS TO THE CITY OF CONOVER FOR A WATER AND
3 SEWER PROJECT.
4 The General Assembly of North Carolina enacts:
5 SECTION 1. The sum of twenty-two million dollars ($22,000,000) in nonrecurring
6 funds for the 2025-2026 fiscal year is appropriated from the General Fund to the City of Conover
7 for water or wastewater system improvements.
8 SECTION 2. This act becomes effective July 1, 2025.
*DRS15077-MHa-67*
