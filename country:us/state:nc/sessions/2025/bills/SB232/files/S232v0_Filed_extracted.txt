SENATE BILL DRS45111-NG-64
Short Title: Central Carolina CC/Chatham Campus MCC Status. (Public)
Sponsors: Senators Burgin and Murdock (Primary Sponsors).
Referred to:
1 A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
2 AN ACT TO DIRECT THE STATE BOARD OF COMMUNITY COLLEGES TO INCLUDE
3 THE HEALTH SCIENCES LOCATIONS IN THE FULL-TIME EQUIVALENT
4 STUDENT CALCULATION OF THE RESPECTIVE MAIN CAMPUSES OF CENTRAL
5 CAROLINA COMMUNITY COLLEGE FOR THE PURPOSES OF MAINTAINING
6 MULTICAMPUS CENTER STATUS.
7 The General Assembly of North Carolina enacts:
8 SECTION 1. Notwithstanding G.S. 115D-5(o), the State Board of Community
9 Colleges shall calculate the number of full-time equivalent students (FTE) required for the
10 Harnett Main Campus to maintain its multicampus center status by combining the total number
11 of FTE at the Harnett Main Campus with the total number of FTE at the Harnett Health Sciences
12 Center.
13 SECTION 2. Notwithstanding G.S. 115D-5(o), the State Board of Community
14 Colleges shall calculate the number of full-time equivalent students (FTE) required for the
15 Chatham Main Campus to maintain its multicampus center status by combining the total number
16 of FTE at the Chatham Main Campus with the total number of FTE at the Chatham Health
17 Sciences Center.
18 SECTION 3. Except as otherwise provided, this act is effective when it becomes
19 law.
