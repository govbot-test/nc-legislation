GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF NORTH CAROLINA
Mar 17, 2025
SESSION 2025
HOUSE PRINCIPAL CLERK
H D
HOUSE BILL DRH40255-MQa-90
Short Title: School of the Arts Building Purchase. (Public)
Sponsors: Representative K. Hall.
Referred to:
1 A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
2 AN ACT TO PROVIDE FUNDS FOR THE PURCHASE OF A BUILDING IN
3 WINSTON-SALEM FOR THE UNIVERSITY OF NORTH CAROLINA SCHOOL OF
4 THE ARTS.
5 The General Assembly of North Carolina enacts:
6 SECTION 1. Notwithstanding G.S. 143C-5-2 and Article 6 of Chapter 146 of the
7 General Statutes, there is appropriated from the General Fund to the Board of Trustees of The
8 University of North Carolina the sum of four million five hundred thousand dollars ($4,500,000)
9 in nonrecurring funds for the 2025-2026 fiscal year to be used to purchase certain real property,
10 and improvements thereon, adjacent to the Stevens Center, located at 411 W. 4th Street in
11 Winston-Salem.
12 SECTION 2. This act becomes effective July 1, 2025.
