Section 1:
FILED SENATE
Apr 29, 2026
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF NORTH CAROLINA
S.B. 930
SESSION 2025
PRINCIPAL CLERK
S D
Short Title: Traffic Signal Preemption Software. (Public)
Sponsors: Senator Lowe (Primary Sponsor).
Referred to:
1 A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
2 AN ACT TO FUND TRAFFIC SIGNAL PREEMPTION TECHNOLOGY.
3 The General Assembly of North Carolina enacts:
4 SECTION 1. There is appropriated from the Highway Fund to the Department of
5 Transportation the sum of two million seven hundred thousand dollars ($2,700,000) in
6 nonrecurring funds for the 2026-2027 fiscal year to be used for traffic signal preemption
7 technology to allow real-time signal changes prioritizing first responder vehicles, reducing
8 response times and improving intersection safety.
9 SECTION 2. This act becomes effective July 1, 2026.
*DRS35392-MWa-102*
