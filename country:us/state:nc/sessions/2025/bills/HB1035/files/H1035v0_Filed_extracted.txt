Section 1:
H.B. 1035
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF NORTH CAROLINA
Apr 21, 2026
SESSION 2025
HOUSE PRINCIPAL CLERK
H D
Short Title: Even-Yr Elections/Voting/Pink Hill. (Local)
Sponsors: Representative Humphrey.
Referred to:
1 A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
2 AN ACT RESTORING CERTAIN VOTING METHODS IN ELECTIONS IN THE TOWN OF
3 PINK HILL AND REQUIRING THAT MUNICIPAL ELECTIONS IN THE TOWN OF
4 PINK HILL BE HELD IN EVEN-NUMBERED YEARS.
5 The General Assembly of North Carolina enacts:
6 SECTION 1. Section 3 of the Charter of the Town of Pink Hill, being Chapter 31 of
7 the Private Laws of 1915, as amended by Chapter 221 of the Private Laws of 1933, Chapter 760
8 of the 1955 Session Laws, Town Resolution adopted on June 9, 2020, and Town Resolution
9 adopted on February 10, 2026, reads as rewritten:
10 "Sec. 3. In elections for the Mayor and Board of Commissioners of the Town of Pink Hill in
11 2021 and biennially thereafter, voting shall be voters casting ballots in person, on the day of the
12 municipal election, at the local precinct, pursuant to the provisions of North Carolina General
13 Statutes Chapter 163.Conduct of Elections. – Regular municipal elections for officers in the
14 Town of Pink Hill shall be held at the time of the general election in each even-numbered year.
15 The election shall be conducted on a nonpartisan plurality basis and the result determined in
16 accordance with G.S. 163-292. Except as otherwise provided, the election shall be conducted in
17 accordance with the uniform municipal election laws in Chapter 163 of the General Statutes."
18 SECTION 2. Section 3.1 of the Charter of the Town of Pink Hill, being Chapter 31
19 of the Private Laws of 1915, as amended by Chapter 221 of the Private Laws of 1933 and S.L.
20 2011-46, reads as rewritten:
21 "Sec. 3.1. In 2011 and quadrennially thereafter, the Mayor shall be elected for a four-year
22 term. In 2011, three commissioners shall be elected, with the top vote getter receiving a four-year
23 term and the two persons receiving the next highest numbers of votes receiving two-year terms.
24 In 2013 and quadrennially thereafter, two commissioners shall be elected for four-year terms. In
25 2015 and quadrennially thereafter, one commissioner shall be elected for a four-year
26 term.Composition and Terms of Office for Mayor and Commissioners. – The governing body of
27 the Town of Pink Hill shall consist of a mayor and three commissioners. The mayor shall be
28 elected for a term of four years. The terms of office of commissioners shall be four-year staggered
29 terms."
30 SECTION 3. No municipal elections shall be conducted in the Town of Pink Hill in
31 2027. The terms of office for the mayor and one commissioner in the Town of Pink Hill serving
32 on the effective date of this act whose terms are set to expire in 2027 shall be extended by one
33 year. The terms of office for the two commissioners in the Town of Pink Hill serving on the
34 effective date of this act whose terms are set to expire in 2029 shall be extended by one year.
35 Regular municipal elections in the Town of Pink Hill shall resume in even-numbered years
*DRH40648-NTe-1A*
General Assembly Of North Carolina Session 2025
1 beginning in 2028 with the same staggering of terms as when elections were conducted in the
2 odd-numbered years.
3 SECTION 4. This act is effective when it becomes law and applies to elections held
4 on or after that date.
