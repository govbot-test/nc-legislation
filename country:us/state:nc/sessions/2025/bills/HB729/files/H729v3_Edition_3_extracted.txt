1 A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
2 AN ACT TO PHASE OUT THE PROPERTY TAX EXCLUSION FOR SOLAR ENERGY
3 ELECTRIC SYSTEMS.
4 The General Assembly of North Carolina enacts:
5 SECTION 1. Effective for taxable years beginning on or after July 1, 2026,
6 G.S. 105-275(45) reads as rewritten:
7 "(45) Eighty percent (80%) Sixty percent (60%) of the appraised value of a solar
8 energy electric system. For purposes of this subdivision, the term "solar
9 energy electric system" means all equipment used directly and exclusively for
10 the conversion of solar energy to electricity."
11 SECTION 2. Effective for taxable years beginning on or after July 1, 2027,
12 G.S. 105-275(45), as amended by Section 1 of this act, reads as rewritten:
13 "(45) Sixty percent (60%) Forty percent (40%) of the appraised value of a solar
14 energy electric system. For purposes of this subdivision, the term "solar
15 energy electric system" means all equipment used directly and exclusively for
16 the conversion of solar energy to electricity."
17 SECTION 3. Effective for taxable years beginning on or after July 1, 2028,
18 G.S. 105-275(45), as amended by Section 2 of this act, reads as rewritten:
19 "(45) Forty percent (40%) Twenty percent (20%) of the appraised value of a solar
20 energy electric system. For purposes of this subdivision, the term "solar
21 energy electric system" means all equipment used directly and exclusively for
22 the conversion of solar energy to electricity."
23 SECTION 4. Effective for taxable years beginning on or after July 1, 2029,
24 G.S. 105-275(45) is repealed.
25 SECTION 5. Except as otherwise provided, this act is effective when it becomes
26 law.
