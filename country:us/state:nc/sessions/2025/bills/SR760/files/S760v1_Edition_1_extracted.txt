GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF NORTH CAROLINA
SESSION 2025
S 1
SENATE RESOLUTION 760
Sponsors: Senator Rabon (Primary Sponsor).
Referred to: Commerce and Insurance
March 31, 2025
1 A SENATE RESOLUTION RELATING TO THE APPOINTMENT, NOMINATION, AND
2 CONFIRMATION OF LEE LILLEY AS SECRETARY OF THE DEPARTMENT OF
3 COMMERCE.
4 Whereas, pursuant to G.S. 143B-9(a), the Governor shall notify the President of the
5 Senate of the name of each person to be nominated as head of a principal State department; and
6 Whereas, each nomination is subject to senatorial advice and consent in conformance
7 with Section 5(8) of Article III of the North Carolina Constitution; and
8 Whereas, the Governor has appointed Lee Lilley to serve as Secretary of the
9 Department of Commerce pending further action by the Senate; Now, therefore,
10 Be it resolved by the Senate:
11 SECTION 1. That the Senate shall hereby consider whether to confirm Lee Lilley
12 as Secretary of the Department of Commerce.
13 SECTION 2. This resolution is effective upon adoption.
