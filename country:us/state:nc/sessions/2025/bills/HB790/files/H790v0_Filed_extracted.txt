H.B. 790
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF NORTH CAROLINA
Apr 3, 2025
SESSION 2025
HOUSE PRINCIPAL CLERK
H D
HOUSE BILL DRH40409-MQ-95
Short Title: Prohibit Vape/Tobacco Shops Near Schools. (Public)
Sponsors: Representative Buansi.
Referred to:
1 A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
2 AN ACT TO PROHIBIT THE SALE OF TOBACCO PRODUCTS, ALTERNATIVE
3 NICOTINE PRODUCTS, AND VAPOR PRODUCTS WITHIN ONE THOUSAND FEET
4 OF A SCHOOL BUILDING.
5 The General Assembly of North Carolina enacts:
6 SECTION 1. Article 52 of Chapter 14 of the General Statutes is amended by adding
7 a new section to read:
8 "§ 14-401.28. Sale of tobacco products, alternative nicotine products, and vapor products
9 near schools unlawful.
10 (a) It is unlawful for any person, firm, or corporation in this State to sell or to purchase
11 any tobacco products, alternative nicotine products, or vapor products at any of the following:
12 (1) Any location which is within 1,000 feet of any property line of a property on
13 which any public or nonpublic school is located.
14 (2) Within any structure, any portion of which is within 1,000 feet of any property
15 line of a property on which any public or nonpublic school is located.
16 (b) As used in this section, the terms "tobacco products," "alternative nicotine products,"
17 and "vapor products" have the same meaning as defined in G.S. 14-313. As used in this section,
18 "school" does not include home schools as defined in G.S. 115C-563 or institutions of higher
19 education.
20 (c) It shall not be a violation of this section to sell or purchase tobacco products,
21 alternative nicotine products, or vapor products in a location prohibited by subsection (a) of this
22 section if the sale or purchase of tobacco products, alternative nicotine products, or vapor
23 products is incidental to the primary operations of an established business facility.
24 (d) A violation of this section is a Class 2 misdemeanor."
25 SECTION 2. This act becomes effective December 1, 2025, and applies to offenses
26 committed on or after that date.
