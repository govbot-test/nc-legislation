Section 1:
FILED SENATE
Feb 21, 2025
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF NORTH CAROLINA
S.B. 130
SESSION 2025
PRINCIPAL CLERK
S D
Short Title: Revise Filing Period/Elections/Hendersonville. (Local)
Sponsors: Senator Moffitt (Primary Sponsor).
Referred to:
1 A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
2 AN ACT TO REVISE THE FILING PERIOD FOR MUNICIPAL ELECTIONS IN THE CITY
3 OF HENDERSONVILLE.
4 The General Assembly of North Carolina enacts:
5 SECTION 1. Section 4.2 of the Charter of the City of Hendersonville, being Chapter
6 874 of the 1971 Session Laws, as amended by S.L. 2016-15, S.L. 2023-44, and S.L. 2023-143,
7 reads as rewritten:
8 "Sec. 4.2. Method of Municipal Elections. The Mayor and Council Members shall be elected
9 on a nonpartisan basis and results determined by the plurality method as provided in
10 G.S. 163-292. The filing period shall be the same as for county officers elected on a partisan
11 basis."
12 SECTION 2. This act is effective when it becomes law and applies to elections held
13 on or after that date.
*DRS45069-LUe-57A*
