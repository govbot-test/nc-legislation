Section 1:
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF NORTH CAROLINA
SESSION 2025
H 2
Committee Substitute Favorable 4/16/25
Short Title: Remote License Renewal/Active Duty Military. (Public)
Sponsors:
Referred to:
February 21, 2025
1 A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
2 AN ACT AUTHORIZING CONSECUTIVE REMOTE DRIVERS LICENSE RENEWALS
3 FOR ACTIVE DUTY MILITARY.
4 The General Assembly of North Carolina enacts:
5 SECTION 1. G.S. 20-7 reads as rewritten:
6 "§ 20-7. Issuance and renewal of drivers licenses.
7 …
8 (f) Duration and Renewal of Licenses. – Drivers licenses shall be issued and renewed
9 pursuant to the provisions of this subsection:
10 …
11 (6) Remote renewal or conversion. – Subject to the following requirements and
12 limitations, the Division may offer remote renewal of a drivers license or
13 remote conversion of a full provisional license issued by the Division:
14 a. Requirements. – To be eligible for remote renewal or conversion under
15 this subdivision, a person must meet all of the following requirements:
16 …
17 4. For a remote renewal, the most recent renewal was an
18 in-person renewal and not a remote renewal under this
19 subdivision.subdivision, except that a member of the Armed
20 Forces of the United States or a reserve component of the
21 Armed Forces of the United States, serving on active duty and
22 stationed outside this State, may remotely renew a license a
23 second consecutive time if either of the following applies:
24 I. The license being renewed is not REAL ID compliant
25 pursuant to the REAL ID Act of 2005, 49 U.S.C. §
26 30301.
27 II. The license being renewed is REAL ID compliant but
28 is being converted to a non-REAL ID compliant license
29 for purposes of the renewal.
30 …."
31 SECTION 2. This act becomes effective October 1, 2025, and applies to licenses
32 renewed on or after that date.
