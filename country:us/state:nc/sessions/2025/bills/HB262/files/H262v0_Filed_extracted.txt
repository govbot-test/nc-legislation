HOUSE BILL DRH30123-MW-46
Short Title: Designate Chief R. Malloy Overpass. (Public)
Sponsors: Representative G. Pierce.
Referred to:
1 A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
2 AN ACT TO DESIGNATE THE CHIEF ROBERT LEE MALLOY OVERPASS.
3 The General Assembly of North Carolina enacts:
4 SECTION 1. Notwithstanding any provision of law to the contrary, the Department
5 of Transportation shall designate the overpass on Interstate 74 that crosses South Main Street in
6 the City of Laurinburg in Scotland County in honor of Chief of Police Robert Lee Malloy.
7 SECTION 2. This act is effective when it becomes law.
