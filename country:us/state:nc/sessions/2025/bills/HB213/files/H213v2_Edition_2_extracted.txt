GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF NORTH CAROLINA

Short Title: Post NC Veterans' Benefits. (Public)
Sponsors:
Referred to:
February 27, 2025
1 A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
2 AN ACT AMENDING THE LABOR LAWS TO REQUIRE EMPLOYERS TO POST NOTICE
3 OF VETERANS' BENEFITS.
4 The General Assembly of North Carolina enacts:
5 SECTION 1. Article 1 of Chapter 95 of the General Statutes is amended by adding
6 a new section to read:
7 "§ 95-9.2. Employers to post notice of veterans' benefits.
8 (a) It shall be the duty of every employer to keep posted in a conspicuous place in every
9 room where five or more persons are employed a printed notice stating the provisions of the law
10 relative to veterans' benefits in this State. The Commissioner of Labor shall furnish a digital form
11 on its website or the printed form of the notice upon request. The poster will have a digital Quick
12 Response (QR) Code that will link to the North Carolina Department of Military and Veterans
13 Affairs current year resource webpage, which shall be promptly updated to current State and
14 federal benefit changes and will include, at a minimum, information about the following services
15 for veterans:
16 (1) The Department of Military and Veterans Affairs' contact and website
17 information for veterans' services.
18 (2) Benefits and claims.
19 (3) Employment and training.
20 (4) Housing assistance, including assistance for homeless veterans.
21 (5) Requests for military records.
22 (b) The Department of Labor shall consult with the Department of Military and Veterans
23 Affairs to create and distribute the veterans' benefits and services poster. The Department of
24 Labor shall make available on its website a digital poster for voluntary use only to read: "We
25 Support Our Veterans. To learn more about benefits please scan the QR code." This Quick
26 Response (QR) Code will also link to the North Carolina Department of Military and Veterans
27 Affairs current year resource webpage."
28 SECTION 2. This act is effective January 1, 2026.
