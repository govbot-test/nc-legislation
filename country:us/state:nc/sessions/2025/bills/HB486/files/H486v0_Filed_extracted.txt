H.B. 486
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF NORTH CAROLINA
Mar 24, 2025
SESSION 2025
HOUSE PRINCIPAL CLERK
H D
HOUSE BILL DRH10233-LGa-146
Short Title: Funds for Blue Star Families, Inc. (Public)
Sponsors: Representative Campbell.
Referred to:
1 A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
2 AN ACT TO APPROPRIATE FUNDS TO BLUE STAR FAMILIES, INC., TO SUPPORT ITS
3 PROGRAMS.
4 The General Assembly of North Carolina enacts:
5 SECTION 1. There is appropriated from the General Fund to the Office of State
6 Budget and Management the sum of one million dollars ($1,000,000) in nonrecurring funds for
7 the 2025-2026 fiscal year to provide a directed grant to Blue Star Families, Inc., a nonprofit
8 organization, to be used as follows:
9 (1) Four hundred thousand dollars ($400,000) to help launch the Greater
10 Fayetteville Regional Chapter for Military Spouses Career Development
11 Programs.
12 (2) Three hundred thousand dollars ($300,000) for Family Strengthening
13 Programs.
14 (3) Three hundred thousand dollars ($300,000) for Military and Veteran
15 Caregiver Services.
16 SECTION 2. This act becomes effective July 1, 2025.
