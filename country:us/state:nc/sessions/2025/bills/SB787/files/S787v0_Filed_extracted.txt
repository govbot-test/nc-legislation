Section 1:
FILED SENATE
Apr 21, 2026
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF NORTH CAROLINA
S.B. 787
SESSION 2025
PRINCIPAL CLERK
S D
Short Title: AI Ethics and Literacy Across Education. (Public)
Sponsors: Senator Bradley (Primary Sponsor).
Referred to:
1 A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
2 AN ACT TO REQUIRE INSTRUCTION ON ARTIFICIAL INTELLIGENCE IN SCHOOLS.
3 The General Assembly of North Carolina enacts:
4 SECTION 1. Part 1 of Article 8 of Chapter 115C of the General Statutes is amended
5 by adding a new section to read:
6 "§ 115C-81.66. Artificial intelligence instruction.
7 (a) The State Board of Education shall adopt standards for age-appropriate instruction on
8 artificial intelligence (AI) for kindergarten through grade 12. The standards shall include at least
9 the following:
10 (1) The basic concepts of artificial intelligence, including an age-appropriate
11 understanding of how AI systems source and create content.
12 (2) Real-world applications of AI.
13 (3) Economic, legal, and social issues surrounding the use of information
14 provided by AI.
15 (4) Responsible, ethical use of AI, including the following topics:
16 a. Privacy.
17 b. Oversight.
18 c. Academic dishonesty.
19 d. The effect of human bias on AI models.
20 (b) The standards adopted by the State Board may allow for AI instruction pursuant to
21 subsection (a) of this section to be included in the standards for computer science courses offered
22 in middle school and high school."
23 SECTION 2. There is appropriated from the General Fund to the Department of
24 Public Instruction the sum of one hundred thousand dollars ($100,000) in recurring funds for the
25 2026-2027 fiscal year to be used to provide support to schools providing instruction pursuant to
26 G.S. 115C-81.66, as enacted by this act. The Department shall distribute funds to each public
27 school unit on the basis of average daily membership.
28 SECTION 3. This act becomes effective July 1, 2026, and applies beginning with
29 the 2026-2027 school year.
