Section 1:
FILED SENATE
Oct 20, 2025
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF NORTH CAROLINA
S.B. 777
SESSION 2025
PRINCIPAL CLERK
S D
Short Title: General Assembly Appointments. (Public)
Sponsors: Senator Daniel (Primary Sponsor).
Referred to:
1 A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
2 AN ACT TO APPOINT PERSONS TO VARIOUS PUBLIC OFFICES BY THE GENERAL
3 ASSEMBLY UPON THE RECOMMENDATIONS OF THE PRESIDENT PRO TEMPORE
4 OF THE SENATE AND THE SPEAKER OF THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES AND
5 TO MAKE CORRECTIONS TO PREVIOUS APPOINTMENTS.
6 The General Assembly of North Carolina enacts:
7
8 PART I. PRESIDENT PRO TEMPORE'S RECOMMENDATIONS
9 SECTION 1.1. Effective immediately, Renee Dowdy of Currituck County is
10 appointed to the College of The Albemarle Board of Trustees for a term expiring June 30, 2026,
11 to fill the unexpired term of Dr. Andrea Williams.
12 SECTION 1.2. Effective immediately, Linda Tolentino of Chatham County is
13 appointed to the Acupuncture Licensing Board for a term expiring June 30, 2028.
14
15 PART II. SPEAKER'S RECOMMENDATIONS
16 SECTION 2.1. Effective immediately, Kathy G. Lauten of Perquimans County is
17 appointed to the College of The Albemarle Board of Trustees for a term expiring June 30, 2027,
18 to fill the unexpired term of Edward Thornton.
19 SECTION 2.2. Kevin W. Earp of Johnston County is appointed to the Eastern North
20 Carolina School for the Deaf Board of Trustees for a term expiring June 30, 2026, to fill the
21 unexpired term of Patrick Miller.
22
23 PART III. CORRECTIONS
24 SECTION 3.1. Section 2.2 of S.L. 2025-90 is repealed.
25
26 PART IV. EFFECTIVE DATE
27 SECTION 4.1. This act is effective when it becomes law. Unless otherwise provided,
28 terms begin upon the effective date of this act.
*DRS15365-LG-187A*
