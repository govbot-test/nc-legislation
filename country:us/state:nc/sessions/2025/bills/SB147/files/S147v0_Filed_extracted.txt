Section 1:
FILED SENATE
Feb 24, 2025
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF NORTH CAROLINA
S.B. 147
SESSION 2025
PRINCIPAL CLERK
S D
Short Title: Teacher License Reciprocity. (Public)
Sponsors: Senators Hanig and Moffitt (Primary Sponsors).
Referred to:
1 A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
2 AN ACT TO PROHIBIT THE STATE BOARD OF EDUCATION FROM REQUIRING AN
3 OUT-OF-STATE APPLICANT FOR A CONTINUING PROFESSIONAL TEACHING
4 LICENSE TO COMPLETE AN EXAMINATION AS A CONDITION OF LICENSURE.
5 The General Assembly of North Carolina enacts:
6 SECTION 1. G.S. 115C-270.25 reads as rewritten:
7 "§ 115C-270.25. Out-of-state license applicants.
8 (a) The State Board of Education shall grant a CPL to an applicant who is a teacher
9 licensed in another state with substantially similar licensure requirements who and has at least
10 three years of teaching experience and is in good standing with the other state.
11 (b) The State Board of Education shall not require an out-of-state license applicant to
12 complete or have completed any type of standardized examination as a condition of receiving a
13 CPL pursuant to subsection (a) of this section."
14 SECTION 2. This act is effective when it becomes law and applies to out-of-state
15 license applications submitted on or after that date.
