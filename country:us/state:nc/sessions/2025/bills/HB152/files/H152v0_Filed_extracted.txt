1 A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
2 AN ACT TO PROVIDE FAIR REIMBURSEMENT OF TRANSCRANIAL MAGNETIC
3 STIMULATION SERVICES UNDER HEALTH BENEFIT PLANS OFFERED IN THIS
4 STATE.
5 The General Assembly of North Carolina enacts:
6 SECTION 1.(a) Article 3 of Chapter 58 of the General Statutes is amended by adding
7 a new section to read:
8 "§ 58-3-254. Coverage of transcranial magnetic stimulation.
9 (a) The following definitions apply in this section:
10 (1) Reserved for future codification purposes.
11 (2) Insurer. – As defined in G.S. 58-3-167.
12 (3) Reserved for future codification purposes.
13 (4) Transcranial magnetic stimulation. – A noninvasive healthcare treatment that
14 uses pulsed magnetic fields to induce an electric current in a localized region
15 of a patient's brain.
16 (b) Insurers offering a health benefit plan in this State that provides coverage for
17 transcranial magnetic stimulation shall provide coverage for all transcranial magnetic stimulation
18 procedures performed by a healthcare facility or properly licensed healthcare provider for whom
19 ordering, performing, and supervising the treatment of depression and other mental disorders is
20 within their scope of practice in accordance with Chapter 90 of the General Statutes and may not
21 penalize any properly licensed healthcare provider or healthcare facility that properly submits a
22 claim for services related to transcranial magnetic stimulation based on the medical specialty of
23 the healthcare provider or healthcare facility submitting the claim.
24 (c) Notwithstanding subsection (b) of this section, it shall be within the discretion of each
25 insurer offering a health benefit plan in the State that provides coverage for transcranial magnetic
26 stimulation to determine all of the following aspects of the coverage:
27 (1) Whether to cover transcranial magnetic stimulation procedures.
28 (2) Indications for which transcranial magnetic stimulation procedures shall be
29 covered.
30 (3) Requirements for coverage for transcranial magnetic stimulation procedures.
31 (4) Reimbursement rates for transcranial magnetic stimulation procedures.
32 (d) Penalties prohibited under this section include the denial of, or the reduction of, or
33 the placement of limitations on the reimbursement of a properly submitted claim, or any other
34 negative treatment of a properly submitted claim.
35 (e) Reimbursement rates that differ based upon any of the following are allowable and
36 shall not be considered a penalty prohibited under this section:

10 discretion of the Plan to determine all aspects of the coverage of transcranial
11 magnetic stimulation identified under G.S. 58-3-254(b)."
12 SECTION 2. This act is effective October 1, 2025, and applies to insurance contracts
13 issued, renewed, or amended on or after that date.
