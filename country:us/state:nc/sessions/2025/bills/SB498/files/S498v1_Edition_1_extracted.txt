GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF NORTH CAROLINA
SESSION 2025
S 1
SENATE BILL 498
Short Title: Funds for Beaufort's Boat Docks. (Public)
Sponsors: Senator Sanderson (Primary Sponsor).
Referred to: Rules and Operations of the Senate
March 26, 2025
1 A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
2 AN ACT TO APPROPRIATE FUNDS TO THE TOWN OF BEAUFORT FOR UPGRADES
3 AND REPAIRS TO THE TOWN'S BOAT DOCKS.
4 The General Assembly of North Carolina enacts:
5 SECTION 1. There is appropriated from the General Fund to the Office of State
6 Budget and Management the sum of twelve million dollars ($12,000,000) in nonrecurring funds
7 for the 2025-2026 fiscal year to provide a directed grant to the Town of Beaufort for upgrades
8 and repairs to the Town's boat docks.
9 SECTION 2. This act becomes effective July 1, 2025.
