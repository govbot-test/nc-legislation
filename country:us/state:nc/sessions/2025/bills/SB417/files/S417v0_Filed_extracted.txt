4 The General Assembly of North Carolina enacts:
5 SECTION 1.(a) Article 1 of Chapter 143B of the General Statutes is amended by
6 adding a new section to read:
7 "§ 143B-28.2. Fiscal transparency and accessibility.
8 In order to allow the citizens of this State to see and better understand how revenue of the
9 State is being expended, The University of North Carolina and each principal department under
10 Chapters 143A and 143B of the General Statutes shall collate and report monthly to, in a form
11 prescribed by, the Department of Administration an itemized list of all expenditures of the entity
12 with a cost of at least one dollar ($1.00) for the purchase of tangible personal property, real
13 property, and services, along with the sum total expenditures for the month. Within 10 days of
14 receiving a report required by this section, the Department of Administration shall publish the
15 report on its website in a form accessible to the public for viewing and inspection. Nothing in
16 this section shall be interpreted to make public any records that are confidential or otherwise
17 protected from disclosure by State or federal law."
18 SECTION 1.(b) G.S. 7A-343 reads as rewritten:
19 "§ 7A-343. Duties of Director.
20 The Director is the Administrative Officer of the Courts, and the Director's duties include all
21 of the following:
22 …
23 (9h) In order to allow the citizens of this State to see and better understand how the
24 revenue of the State is being expended, the courts of the State shall collate and
25 report monthly to, in a form prescribed by, the Director an itemized list of all
26 expenditures of each court with a cost of at least one dollar ($1.00) for the
27 purchase of tangible personal property, real property, and services, along with
28 the sum total expenditures for the month. Within 10 days of receiving a report
29 required by this section, the Director shall publish the report on its website in
30 a form accessible to the public for viewing and inspection. Nothing in this
