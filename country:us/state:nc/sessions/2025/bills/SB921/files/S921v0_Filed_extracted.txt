Section 1:
FILED SENATE
Apr 29, 2026
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF NORTH CAROLINA
S.B. 921
SESSION 2025
PRINCIPAL CLERK
S D
Short Title: Forsyth Tech First Responder Funding. (Public)
Sponsors: Senator Lowe (Primary Sponsor).
Referred to:
1 A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
2 AN ACT TO PROVIDE FUNDING TO FORSYTH TECHNICAL COMMUNITY COLLEGE
3 FOR CONSTRUCTION AND EQUIPMENT NECESSARY FOR FIRST RESPONDER
4 TRAINING.
5 The General Assembly of North Carolina enacts:
6 SECTION 1. There is appropriated from the General Fund to Forsyth Technical
7 Community College the sum of ten million four hundred thousand dollars ($10,400,000) in
8 nonrecurring funds for the 2026-2027 fiscal year for construction related to, and equipment
9 necessary for, expansion of training capacity and improvement of readiness and safety through a
10 comprehensive first responder program, including a training track, building, bridge, classrooms,
11 and fire apparatus.
12 SECTION 2. This act becomes effective July 1, 2026.
*DRS45499-MCa-267*
