GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF NORTH CAROLINA
SESSION 2025
S 2
SENATE BILL 369
Health Care Committee Substitute Adopted 4/9/25
Short Title: Medicaid Telehealth Services. (Public)
Sponsors:
Referred to:
March 24, 2025
1 A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
2 AN ACT TO ENSURE THAT CERTAIN PROVIDERS OF TELEHEALTH SERVICES ARE
3 ELIGIBLE TO ENROLL AS MEDICAID PROVIDERS.
4 The General Assembly of North Carolina enacts:
5 SECTION 1. The Department of Health and Human Services, Division of Health
6 Benefits, shall ensure that a health care provider duly licensed by the State that provides health
7 care services exclusively through telehealth services shall not be required to maintain a physical
8 presence in the State to be considered an eligible provider for enrollment as a Medicaid provider.
9 SECTION 2. The Department of Health and Human Services, Division of Health
10 Benefits, shall ensure that a health care provider group with health care providers duly licensed
11 by the State which exclusively offers telehealth services shall not be required to have an in-State
12 service address to be eligible to enroll as a Medicaid provider group.
13 SECTION 3. This act is effective when it becomes law.
