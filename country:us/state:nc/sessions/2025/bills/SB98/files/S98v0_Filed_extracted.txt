Short Title: Funds for Dolly Parton's Imagination Library. (Public)

Referred to:
1 A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
2 AN ACT TO APPROPRIATE FUNDS FOR DOLLY PARTON'S IMAGINATION LIBRARY.
3 The General Assembly of North Carolina enacts:
4 SECTION 1. There is appropriated from the General Fund to the Department of
5 Health and Human Services, Division of Child Development and Early Education, the sum of
6 one million five hundred thousand dollars ($1,500,000) in recurring funds for each year of the
7 2025-2027 fiscal biennium to be allocated to the North Carolina Partnership for Children, Inc.,
8 for Dolly Parton's Imagination Library, an early literacy program that mails age-appropriate
9 books to registered children on a monthly basis.
10 SECTION 2. This act becomes effective July 1, 2025.
