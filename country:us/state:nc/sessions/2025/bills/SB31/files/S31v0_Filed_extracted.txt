SENATE BILL DRS35013-MC-32
Short Title: The Wells Act. (Public)
Sponsors: Senators Burgin, Brinson, and Jones (Primary Sponsors).
Referred to:
1 A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
2 AN ACT TO FACILITATE THE ELIMINATION OF NONRESPONSIVE BOARDS,
3 COMMITTEES, AND COMMISSIONS.
4 The General Assembly of North Carolina enacts:
5 SECTION 1. The Legislative Library is directed to send a request for documentation
6 and confirmation of activity to all boards, committees, and commissions that have not expired or
7 been repealed. The documentation required by this section includes the current membership, last
8 reported minutes, current bylaws, and a listing of the entities to which reports are to be submitted.
9 For any board, committee, or commission that either (i) fails to respond within 120 days to the
10 request required by this section or (ii) responds but has not met within the previous 12 months,
11 the Legislative Library will add the board, committee, or commission to a list and will submit
12 the final compiled list to the Joint Legislative Administrative Procedure Oversight Committee.
13 The Committee is directed to recommend legislation to repeal the boards and commissions on
14 the list required by and submitted pursuant to this section.
15 SECTION 2. The Joint Legislative Administrative Procedure Oversight Committee
16 is directed to recommend legislation to the 2026 Regular Session of the 2025 General Assembly
17 upon its convening to repeal the boards and commissions on the list required by, and submitted
18 to it pursuant to, Section 1 of this act.
19 SECTION 3. This act is effective when it becomes law.
