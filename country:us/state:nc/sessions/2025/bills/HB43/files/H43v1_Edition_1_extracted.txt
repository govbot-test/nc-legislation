Section 1:
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF NORTH CAROLINA
SESSION 2025
H 1
Short Title: Designate State Balloon Rally. (Public)
Sponsors: Representatives Carver, Setzer, McNeely, and Ross (Primary Sponsors).
For a complete list of sponsors, refer to the North Carolina General Assembly web site.
Referred to: State and Local Government, if favorable, Rules, Calendar, and Operations of the
House
February 5, 2025
1 A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
2 AN ACT ADOPTING THE CAROLINA BALLOONFEST HELD IN THE CITY OF
3 STATESVILLE AS THE OFFICIAL BALLOON RALLY OF THE STATE OF NORTH
4 CAROLINA.
5 Whereas, the City of Statesville has held a hot air balloon festival since 1974, making
6 this year its 50th anniversary; and
7 Whereas, Statesville's balloon festival holds the record as the second oldest hot air
8 balloon festival in the United States; and
9 Whereas, the current festival is known as Carolina BalloonFest and is a
10 family-friendly, three-day event held each year in October; and
11 Whereas, the Carolina BalloonFest features colorful hot air balloons, sanctioned
12 competitions, hot air balloon flights, tethered balloon rides, live entertainment, a balloon glow, a
13 kids zone, wine and craft beer tasting, crafts, and many more activities; and
14 Whereas, the Carolina BalloonFest attracts thousands of visitors each year,
15 contributing to the local economy; and
16 Whereas, the Carolina BalloonFest donates a percentage of the proceeds from the
17 festival to local nonprofits and community organizations; and
18 Whereas, the Carolina BalloonFest should be adopted as the State's official balloon
19 rally; Now, therefore,
20 The General Assembly of North Carolina enacts:
21 SECTION 1. Chapter 145 of the General Statutes is amended by adding a new
22 section to read:
23 "§ 145-52. State balloon rally.
24 The Carolina BalloonFest, held in October of every year in the City of Statesville, is adopted
25 as the official balloon rally of the State of North Carolina."
26 SECTION 2. This act is effective when it becomes law.
*H43-v-1*
