H.B. 393
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF NORTH CAROLINA
Mar 13, 2025
SESSION 2025
HOUSE PRINCIPAL CLERK
H D
HOUSE BILL DRH40200-LGa-81
Short Title: Funds for One Love Festival. (Public)
Sponsors: Representative Baker.
Referred to:
1 A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
2 AN ACT TO APPROPRIATE FUNDS TO THE ARTS COUNCIL, INC., TO SUPPORT THE
3 ANNUAL 1LOVE FESTIVAL IN THE CITY OF WINSTON-SALEM.
4 The General Assembly of North Carolina enacts:
5 SECTION 1. There is appropriated from the General Fund to the Office of State
6 Budget and Management the sum of two hundred fifty thousand dollars ($250,000) in
7 nonrecurring funds for the 2025-2026 fiscal year to provide a directed grant to The Arts Council,
8 Inc., to support the 1Love Festival, a three-day festival held in the City of Winston-Salem
9 designed to celebrate diversity, foster unity, and provide a platform for cultural expression
10 through music, arts, and entertainment.
11 SECTION 2. This act becomes effective July 1, 2025.
