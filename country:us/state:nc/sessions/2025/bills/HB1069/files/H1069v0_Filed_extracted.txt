GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF NORTH CAROLINA
Apr 28, 2026
SESSION 2025
HOUSE PRINCIPAL CLERK
H D
HOUSE BILL DRH30526-ST-49A
Short Title: New Bern Unfit Dwelling Enforcement Options. (Local)
Sponsors: Representative Tyson.
Referred to:
1 A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
2 AN ACT TO AUTHORIZE THE CITY OF NEW BERN TO IMPOSE CIVIL PENALTIES
3 WHEN ENFORCING ORDERS DECLARING A DWELLING UNFIT FOR HUMAN
4 HABITATION.
5 The General Assembly of North Carolina enacts:
6 SECTION 1. G.S. 160D-1203 is amended by adding a new subdivision to read:
7 "(10) Civil penalties. – In addition to any other lawful remedy, the ordinance
8 adopted under this section may provide for the imposition of civil penalties
9 against any person or entity that fails to comply with an order entered in
10 accordance with this section."
11 SECTION 2. This act applies only to the City of New Bern.
12 SECTION 3. This act is effective when it becomes law and applies to order enforcing
13 G.S. 160D-1203 entered on or after that date.
