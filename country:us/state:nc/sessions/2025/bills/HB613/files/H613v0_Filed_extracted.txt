7 PART I. AUTHORIZE VEHICLE OWNERS TO ELECT MONTH OF REGISTRATION
8 RENEWAL
9 SECTION 1.(a) This act shall be known as "The Vehicle Registration Convenience
10 Act."
11 SECTION 1.(b) G.S. 20-66 reads as rewritten:
12 "§ 20-66. Renewal of vehicle registration.
13 (a) Annual Renewal. – When a vehicle registration is initially issued, the owner shall
14 elect a month within 12 months of issuance upon which the vehicle's registration expires. The
15 initial registration expires at midnight on the last day of the month elected by the owner. The
16 registration of a vehicle must thereafter be renewed annually.
17 (a1) Issuance Fees. – The fee for the initial issuance of vehicle registration shall be
18 determined as follows:
19 (1) For a vehicle whose owner has elected for the month of issuance to be the
20 month of renewal, the issuance fee shall be the annual fee provided in Part 7
21 of this Article, subject to any proration required by G.S. 20-95.
22 (2) For a vehicle whose owner has elected a month of renewal that differs from
23 the month of issuance, the issuance fee shall be one-twelfth of the annual fee
24 provided in Part 7 of this Article multiplied by the number of full months in
25 the period beginning the date the registration was issued until the date the
26 registration expires, rounded to the nearest dollar.
27 (a2) Notice. – In accordance with G.S. 105-330.5(b), upon receiving written consent from
28 the owner of the vehicle, the Division may send to an email address provided by the owner of the
29 vehicle any required notice of renewal electronically to an e-mail address provided by the owner
30 of the vehicle.renewal.
31 (a3) Renewal Application. – To renew the registration of a vehicle, the owner of the
32 vehicle must file an application with the Division and pay the required registration fee. The owner
33 of a vehicle registered in North Carolina may renew that vehicle registration by mail. The
34 Division may receive and grant an application for renewal of registration at any time before the
35 registration expires.
36 …."
