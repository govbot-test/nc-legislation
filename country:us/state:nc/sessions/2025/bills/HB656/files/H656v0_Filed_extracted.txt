Section 1:
H.B. 656
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF NORTH CAROLINA
Apr 1, 2025
SESSION 2025
HOUSE PRINCIPAL CLERK
H D
Short Title: Split Distr Ct/Defender Distr 43 into 43A/43B. (Public)
Sponsors: Representative Clampitt.
Referred to:
1 A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
2 AN ACT TO SPLIT DISTRICT COURT DISTRICT 43 INTO DISTRICT COURT DISTRICTS
3 43A AND 43B, TO SPLIT DEFENDER DISTRICT 43 INTO DEFENDER DISTRICTS
4 43A AND 43B, AND TO APPROPRIATE FUNDS.
5 The General Assembly of North Carolina enacts:
6 SECTION 1.(a) G.S. 7A-133 reads as rewritten:
7 "§ 7A-133. Numbers of judges by districts; numbers of magistrates and additional seats of
8 court, by counties.
9 (a) Each district court district shall have the numbers of judges as set forth in the
10 following table:
11
12 District Judges County
13 …
14 4343A 74 Cherokee
15 Clay
16 Graham
17 Haywood
18 Jackson
19 Macon
20 Swain.Swain
21 43B 4 Haywood
22 Jackson."
23 SECTION 1.(b) There is appropriated from the General Fund to the Administrative
24 Office of the Courts the sum of two hundred fifty-three thousand one dollars ($253,001) in
25 recurring funds and ten thousand three hundred seventy-six dollars ($10,376) in nonrecurring
26 funds for the 2026-2027 fiscal year to be used to hire one full-time district court judge pursuant
27 to Section 1(a) of this act.
28 SECTION 1.(c) There is appropriated from the General Fund to the Administrative
29 Office of the Courts the sum of seven thousand four hundred twenty-one dollars ($7,421) in
30 recurring funds for the 2026-2027 fiscal year to be added to the salary of the district court judge
31 selected as chief district court judge of District 43B created under Section 1(a) of this act.
32 SECTION 1.(d) There is appropriated from the General Fund to the Administrative
33 Office of the Courts the sum of seventy-one thousand nine hundred thirty-two dollars ($71,932)
34 in recurring funds and three thousand four hundred thirty-six dollars ($3,436) in nonrecurring
35 funds for the 2026-2027 fiscal year to be used to hire one full-time judicial assistant to serve
36 District Court District 43B.
*DRH40377-NDa-36*
General Assembly Of North Carolina Session 2025
1 SECTION 1.(e) Subsection (a) of this section becomes effective January 1, 2027,
2 and elections held in 2026 shall be held accordingly. The remainder of this section becomes
3 effective July 1, 2026.
4 SECTION 2.(a) G.S. 7A-498.7(a) reads as rewritten:
5 "(a) The following counties of the State are organized into the defender districts listed
6 below, and in each of those defender districts an office of public defender is established:
7
8 Defender District Counties
9 ___________________________________________________________________
10 …
11 4343A Cherokee, Clay, Graham,
12 Haywood, Jackson, Macon,
13 Swain
14 43B Haywood, Jackson
15 After notice to, and consultation with, the affected district bar, senior resident superior court
16 judge, and chief district court judge, the Commission on Indigent Defense Services may
17 recommend to the General Assembly that a district or regional public defender office be
18 established. A legislative act is required in order to establish a new office or to abolish an existing
19 office."
20 SECTION 2.(b) There is appropriated from the General Fund to the Administrative
21 Office of the Courts, Office of Indigent Defense Services, the sum of two hundred twenty-eight
22 thousand four hundred sixty-one dollars ($228,461) in recurring funds and four thousand eight
23 hundred ninety-five dollars ($4,895) in nonrecurring funds for the 2026-2027 fiscal year to be
24 used to hire one full-time chief public defender to serve Defender District 43B.
25 SECTION 2.(c) There is appropriated from the General Fund to the Administrative
26 Office of the Courts, Office of Indigent Defense Services, the sum of four hundred twenty-four
27 thousand four hundred eighty-eight dollars ($424,488) in recurring funds and fourteen thousand
28 six hundred eighty-five dollars ($14,685) in nonrecurring funds for the 2026-2027 fiscal year to
29 be used to hire three full-time assistant public defenders to serve Defender District 43B.
30 SECTION 2.(d) This section becomes effective July 1, 2026.
31 SECTION 3. Except as otherwise provided, this act is effective when it becomes
32 law.
