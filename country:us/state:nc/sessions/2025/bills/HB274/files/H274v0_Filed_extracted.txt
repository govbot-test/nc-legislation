H.B. 274
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF NORTH CAROLINA
Mar 4, 2025
SESSION 2025
HOUSE PRINCIPAL CLERK
H D
HOUSE BILL DRH40167-NBa-93
Short Title: Foster Care Services/Funds. (Public)
Sponsors: Representative Lambeth.
Referred to:
1 A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
2 AN ACT TO APPROPRIATE FUNDS TO CROSSNORE COMMUNITIES FOR CHILDREN
3 FOR FOSTER CARE SERVICES.
4 The General Assembly of North Carolina enacts:
5 SECTION 1. There is appropriated from the General Fund to the Office of State
6 Budget and Management the nonrecurring sum of five million dollars ($5,000,000) for the
7 2025-2026 fiscal year to provide a directed grant to Crossnore Communities for Children, a
8 nonprofit organization, to provide services that are designed to address the foster care crisis in
9 this State, including the Promise for Hope Campaign, the Center for Trauma Resilient
10 Communities, the Bridging Families program, youth independent living, foster care, family
11 reunification and preservation services, and community engagement. All services supported
12 through these funds shall be open to children from across North Carolina and shall be offered in
13 and delivered from Winston-Salem, Crossnore, and Hendersonville through Crossnore
14 Communities for Children.
15 SECTION 2. This act becomes effective July 1, 2025.
