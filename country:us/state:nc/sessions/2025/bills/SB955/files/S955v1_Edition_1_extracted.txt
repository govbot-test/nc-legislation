GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF NORTH CAROLINA
SESSION 2025
S 1
SENATE BILL 955
Short Title: Permit Self-Defense Devices on College Campus. (Public)
Sponsors: Senator Hise (Primary Sponsor).
Referred to: Rules and Operations of the Senate
May 4, 2026
1 A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
2 AN ACT TO PERMIT SELF-DEFENSE DEVICES ON HIGHER EDUCATION PROPERTY
3 AND TO APPROPRIATE FUNDS TO ADVERTISE THIS CHANGE.
4 The General Assembly of North Carolina enacts:
5 SECTION 1.(a) G.S. 14-269.2 is amended by adding a new subsection to read:
6 "(i2) The provisions of this section shall not apply to a person 18 years of age or older who
7 carries or possesses, whether openly or concealed, a self-defense device on any property owned,
8 used, or operated by a school of higher education. Further, a school of higher education shall not
9 prohibit a person 18 years of age or older, including a staff member, student, employee, or other
10 person lawfully present on the property owned, used, or operated by the school of higher
11 education, from carrying a self-defense device for purposes of self-defense while on property
12 owned, used, or operated by the school of higher education. For purposes of this subsection, the
13 following definitions apply:
14 (1) School of higher education. – A community college, college, or university.
15 (2) Self-defense device. – Pepper spray, pepper gel, mace, a taser, an ordinary
16 pocket knife, or other similar nonlethal devices. This term does not include
17 any device enumerated in subsection (b), (b1), or (d) of this section that is not
18 otherwise specifically allowed under this subdivision. For purposes of this
19 subdivision, the term "ordinary pocket knife" is as defined in G.S. 14-269."
20 SECTION 1.(b) G.S. 14-269 reads as rewritten:
21 "§ 14-269. Carrying concealed weapons.
22 (a) It shall be unlawful for any person willfully and intentionally to carry concealed about
23 his or her person any bowie knife, dirk, dagger, slung shot, loaded cane, metallic knuckles, razor,
24 shuriken, stun gun, or other deadly weapon of like kind, except when the person is on the person's
25 own premises.
26 …
27 (e) Subsection (a) of this section does not apply to a person 18 years of age or older who,
28 in accordance with G.S. 14-269.2(i2), carries concealed a self-defense device on property owned,
29 used, or operated by a school of higher education. The definitions set forth in G.S. 14-269.2(i2)
30 apply to the terms in this subsection."
31 SECTION 2. There is appropriated from the General Fund to the Board of Governors
32 of The University of North Carolina the sum of ten thousand dollars ($10,000) in nonrecurring
33 funds for the 2026-2027 fiscal year to advertise the changes occurring in Section 1 of this act to
34 members of the public and students and staff at public and private institutions of higher education
35 located in North Carolina.
