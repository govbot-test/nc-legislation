7 "§ 6-21.6. Reciprocal attorneys' fees provisions in business contracts.
8 (a) As used in this section, the following definitions apply:
9 (1) Business contract. – A contract entered into primarily for business or
10 commercial purposes. The term does not include a consumer contract, an
11 employment contract, or a contract to which a government or a governmental
12 agency of this State is a party.
13 (2) Consumer contract. – A contract entered into by one or more individuals
14 primarily for personal, family, or household purposes.
15 (3) Employment contract. – A contract between an individual and another party
16 to provide personal services by that individual to the other party, whether the
17 relationship is in the nature of employee-employer or principal-independent
18 contractor.
