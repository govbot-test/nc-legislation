GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF NORTH CAROLINA
SESSION 2025
S 1
SENATE BILL 468
Short Title: Alternative Additional Registration Fee. (Public)
Sponsors: Senator Grafstein (Primary Sponsor).
Referred to: Rules and Operations of the Senate
March 25, 2025
1 A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
2 AN ACT TO AUTHORIZE AN ALTERNATIVE ADDITIONAL REGISTRATION FEE FOR
3 ELECTRIC VEHICLES AND PLUG-IN HYBRID VEHICLES.
4 The General Assembly of North Carolina enacts:
5 SECTION 1. G.S. 20-87 reads as rewritten:
6 "§ 20-87. Passenger vehicle registration fees.
7 These fees shall be paid to the Division annually for the registration and licensing of
8 passenger vehicles, according to the following classifications and schedules:
9 …
10 (13) Additional fee for certain electric vehicles. – At Except as provided in
11 subdivision (13b) of this section, at the time of an initial registration or
12 registration renewal, the owner of a plug-in electric vehicle that is not a
13 low-speed vehicle and that does not rely on a nonelectric source of power shall
14 pay a fee in the amount of one hundred eighty dollars ($180.00) in addition to
15 any other required registration fees.
16 (13a) Additional fee for plug-in hybrid vehicles. – At Except as provided in
17 subdivision (13b) of this section, at the time of an initial registration or
18 registration renewal, the owner of a plug-in hybrid vehicle shall pay a fee in
19 the amount of ninety dollars ($90.00) in addition to any other required
20 registration fees.
21 (13b) Alternative additional fee. – At the option of the owner, the additional fee for
22 an electric vehicle in subdivision (13) of this section and a plug-in hybrid
23 vehicle in subdivision (13a) of this section may be satisfied by payment of a
24 mileage fee that is based upon the number of miles driven since the previous
25 registration of the vehicle.
26 …."
27 SECTION 2. The Division of Motor Vehicles shall adopt rules to implement
28 G.S. 20-87(13b) as enacted by Section 1 of this act.
29 SECTION 3. This act is effective when it becomes law and applies to electric and
30 plug-in hybrid vehicles registered on or after October 1, 2025.
