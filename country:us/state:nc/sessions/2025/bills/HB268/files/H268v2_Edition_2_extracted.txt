20 PARTI II. MISCELLANEOUS AND EFFECTIVE DATE
21
22 STATE BUDGET ACT APPLICABILITY
23 SECTION 2.1. If any provision of this act and G.S. 143C-5-4 are in conflict, the
24 provisions of this act shall prevail. The appropriations and the authorizations to allocate and
25 spend funds which are set out in this act shall remain in effect until the Current Operations
26 Appropriations Act for the applicable fiscal year becomes law, at which time that act shall
27 become effective and shall govern appropriations and expenditures. When the Current
28 Operations Appropriations Act for that fiscal year becomes law, the Director of the Budget shall
29 adjust allotments to give effect to that act from July 1 of the fiscal year.
30
31 EFFECTIVE DATE
32 SECTION 2.2. Except as otherwise provided, this act is effective retroactively to
33 July 1, 2025.
