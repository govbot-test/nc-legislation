22 "(c) A charter school shall operate under the written charter signed by the State

26 SECTION 1.(g) G.S. 115C-218.123 is amended by adding a new subsection to read:
27 "(c) If a school is operating under a charter that allows for a remote academy as part of the
28 charter, and the school enrolls or intends to enroll 250 or more students in the remote academy,
29 the school may request that the Review Board grant the remote academy portion of the school a
30 separate charter by submitting the information listed under subsection (a) of this section with the
31 request. Requests submitted pursuant to this section shall be reviewed through an expedited
32 process to be established by the Review Board. The Review Board shall not require a planning
33 year for remote academies granted a charter pursuant to this section."
34 SECTION 1.(h) G.S. 115C-218.125 reads as rewritten:
35 "§ 115C-218.125. Evaluation.
36 (a) The State Board of Education shall evaluate the success of remote charter academies
37 approved under this Part. Success shall be measured by school performance scores and grades,
38 retention rates, attendance rates, and, for grades nine through 12, high school completion and
39 dropout rates. The Board shall report by November 15 of each year to the Joint Legislative
40 Education Oversight Committee on the evaluation of these academies and on any recommended
41 statutory changes.
42 (b) If a school is operating under a charter that includes in-person instruction and a remote
43 charter academy, the remote charter academy shall receive a separate school performance grade
44 and be treated as a separate school for the purposes of assessing the performance of the remote
45 charter academy pursuant to G.S. 115C-12(9)c1., 115C-83.15, 115C-218.94, and 115C-218.95."
