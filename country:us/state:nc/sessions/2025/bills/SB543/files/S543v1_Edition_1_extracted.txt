Section 1:
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF NORTH CAROLINA
SESSION 2025
S 1
Short Title: State Auditor to Audit Private Schools. (Public)
Sponsors: Senator Everitt (Primary Sponsor).
Referred to: Rules and Operations of the Senate
March 26, 2025
1 A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
2 AN ACT TO DIRECT THE STATE AUDITOR TO PERIODICALLY AUDIT PRIVATE
3 SCHOOLS THAT RECEIVE STATE FUNDS.
4 The General Assembly of North Carolina enacts:
5 SECTION 1. G.S. 147-64.6(c) reads as rewritten:
6 "(c) Responsibilities. – The Auditor is responsible for the following acts and activities:
7 …
8 (24) The Auditor shall periodically audit nonpublic schools that enroll students
9 who receive scholarship grants under Part 2A of Article 39 of Chapter 115C
10 of the General Statutes."
11 SECTION 2. This act is effective when it becomes law.
