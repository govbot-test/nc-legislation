GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF NORTH CAROLINA
SESSION 2025
S 1
SENATE BILL 534
Short Title: Capital Project Funding at NCA&T/WSSU. (Public)
Sponsors: Senators Robinson, Lowe, and Blue (Primary Sponsors).
Referred to: Rules and Operations of the Senate
March 26, 2025
1 A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
2 AN ACT TO PROVIDE FUNDING FOR CAPITAL IMPROVEMENT PROJECTS AT
3 NORTH CAROLINA AGRICULTURAL AND TECHNICAL UNIVERSITY AND
4 WINSTON-SALEM STATE UNIVERSITY.
5 The General Assembly of North Carolina enacts:
6 SECTION 1. There is appropriated from the State Capital and Infrastructure Fund,
7 established in G.S. 143C-3-4.1(a), to the Board of Governors of The University of North Carolina
8 the sum of two hundred thirty-seven million five hundred thousand dollars ($237,500,000) in
9 nonrecurring funds for the 2025-2026 fiscal year to be allocated to the constituent institutions
10 listed in this section for the amounts provided and the projects identified as follows:
11 (1) One hundred twenty-five million dollars ($125,000,000) to North Carolina
12 Agricultural & Technical State University to be used for the Agricultural
13 Research Classroom.
14 (2) One hundred twelve million five hundred thousand dollars ($112,500,000) to
15 Winston-Salem State University to be used for the Health, Physical Education,
16 and Recreation Complex.
17 SECTION 2. This act becomes effective July 1, 2025.
