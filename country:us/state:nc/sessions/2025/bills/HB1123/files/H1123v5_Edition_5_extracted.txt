7 PART VI. USE OF CAPITAL FUNDS FOR PUBLIC-PRIVATE PARTNERSHIPS
8 SECTION 6.1. Article 8 of Chapter 143C of the General Statutes is amended by
9 adding a new section to read:
10 "§ 143C-8-7.2. Use of capital funds for public-private partnership projects.
11 (a) For the purposes of this section, the term "public-private partnership project" means
12 a capital improvement project undertaken for the benefit of a governmental entity and a private
13 entity that may involve a contract, a financing arrangement, or other agreement and includes
14 construction of a public facility or other improvements, including paving, grading, utilities,
15 infrastructure, reconstruction, or repair, and may include both public and private facilities.
16 (b) A State agency, including a constituent institution of The University of North
17 Carolina, shall not use State funds appropriated to the State agency in conjunction with or for the
18 benefit of a public-private partnership project for either of the following without express
19 authorization by an act of the General Assembly:
20 (1) A capital improvement project that is not a public-private partnership project.
21 (2) A self-liquidating project that is not a public-private partnership project.
22 (c) This section does not apply to any of the following:
23 (1) The entity created pursuant to G.S. 116-350.5.
24 (2) The Department of Transportation."
