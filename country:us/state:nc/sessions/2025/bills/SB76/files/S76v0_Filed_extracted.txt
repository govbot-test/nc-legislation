Section 1:
FILED SENATE
Feb 11, 2025
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF NORTH CAROLINA
S.B. 76
SESSION 2025
PRINCIPAL CLERK
S D
Short Title: Funds for the Franklinton Center. (Public)
Sponsors: Senator Smith (Primary Sponsor).
Referred to:
1 A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
2 AN ACT TO APPROPRIATE FUNDS FOR THE FRANKLINTON CENTER AT BRICKS,
3 INC.
4 The General Assembly of North Carolina enacts:
5 SECTION 1. There is appropriated from the General Fund to the Office of State
6 Budget and Management the sum of three hundred thousand dollars ($300,000) in nonrecurring
7 funds for the 2025-2026 fiscal year to provide a directed grant to the Franklinton Center at Bricks,
8 Inc., for operating expenses and capital improvements.
9 SECTION 2. This act becomes effective July 1, 2025.
*DRS35044-LGa-38*
