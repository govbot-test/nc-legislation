Section 1:
H.B. 942
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF NORTH CAROLINA
Apr 10, 2025
SESSION 2025
HOUSE PRINCIPAL CLERK
H D
Short Title: Funds to Support Survivors. (Public)
Sponsors: Representative Buansi.
Referred to:
1 A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
2 AN ACT TO APPROPRIATE FUNDS TO BE ALLOCATED TO THE NORTH CAROLINA
3 COALITION AGAINST SEXUAL ASSAULT AND DISTRIBUTED TO VICTIM
4 ADVOCACY ORGANIZATIONS.
5 The General Assembly of North Carolina enacts:
6 SECTION 1. There is appropriated from the General Fund to the Department of
7 Public Safety the sum of ten million dollars ($10,000,000) in recurring funds for the 2025-2026
8 fiscal year to be allocated as a directed grant to the North Carolina Coalition Against Sexual
9 Assault, a nonprofit corporation, to be used to allocate funds to partner organizations, including
10 domestic violence organizations, rape crisis centers, and child advocacy centers, to use for victim
11 advocacy.
12 SECTION 2. This act becomes effective July 1, 2025.
*DRH10438-NDa-63A*
