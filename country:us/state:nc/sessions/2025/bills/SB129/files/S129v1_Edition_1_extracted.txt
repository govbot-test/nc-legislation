Section 1:
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF NORTH CAROLINA
SESSION 2025
S 1
Short Title: Booking Photograph Privacy Act. (Public)
Sponsors: Senator Moffitt (Primary Sponsor).
Referred to: Rules and Operations of the Senate
February 24, 2025
1 A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
2 AN ACT TO PROHIBIT THE RELEASE OF BOOKING PHOTOGRAPHS.
3 The General Assembly of North Carolina enacts:
4 SECTION 1. G.S. 132-1.4 reads as rewritten:
5 "§ 132-1.4. Criminal investigations; intelligence information records; Innocence Inquiry
6 Commission records.
7 …
8 (b) As used in this section:
9 (1) "Records Records of criminal investigations" means all investigations. – All
10 records or any information that pertains to a person or group of persons that is
11 compiled by public law enforcement agencies for the purpose of attempting
12 to prevent or solve violations of the law, including information derived from
13 witnesses, laboratory tests, surveillance, investigators, confidential
14 informants, photographs, and measurements. The term also includes any
15 records, worksheets, reports, or analyses prepared or conducted by the North
16 Carolina State Crime Laboratory at the request of any public law enforcement
17 agency in connection with a criminal investigation.
18 (2) "Records Records of criminal intelligence information" means records
19 information. – Records or information that pertain to a person or group of
20 persons that is compiled by a public law enforcement agency in an effort to
21 anticipate, prevent, or monitor possible violations of the law.
22 (3) "Public Public law enforcement agency" means a agency. – A municipal
23 police department, a county police department, a sheriff's office, a company
24 police agency commissioned by the Attorney General pursuant to G.S. 74E-1,
25 et seq., and any State or local agency, force, department, or unit responsible
26 for investigating, preventing, or solving violations of the law.
27 (4) "Violations Violations of the law" means crimes law. – Crimes and offenses
28 that are prosecutable in the criminal courts in this State or the United States
29 and infractions as defined in G.S. 14-3.1.
30 (5) "Complaining witness" means an Complaining witness. – An alleged victim
31 or other person who reports a violation or apparent violation of the law to a
32 public law enforcement agency.
33 (6) Booking photograph. – An image of an individual taken by an arresting public
34 law enforcement agency for the purpose of identification or taken when the
35 individual was processed into a jail.
36 …
*S129-v-1*
General Assembly Of North Carolina Session 2025
1 (n) Notwithstanding any other provision of law, and except as otherwise provided in this
2 subsection, booking photographs are confidential, are not public records as defined in G.S. 132-1,
3 and public law enforcement agencies are prohibited from publishing, distributing, or releasing
4 booking photographs. A public law enforcement agency may release a booking photograph only
5 when the booking photograph is a record of criminal investigation related to a missing person
6 report. A court of competent jurisdiction may order the release of a booking photograph upon a
7 showing by the person requesting disclosure that the disclosure is actually necessary for
8 immediate law enforcement needs."
9 SECTION 2. This act becomes effective October 1, 2025.
