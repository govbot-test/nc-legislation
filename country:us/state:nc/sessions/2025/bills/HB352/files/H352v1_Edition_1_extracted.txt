GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF NORTH CAROLINA
SESSION 2025
H 1
HOUSE BILL 352
Short Title: Holly Springs/Fuquay-Varina Exempt Contracts. (Local)
Sponsors: Representative Paré.
For a complete list of sponsors, refer to the North Carolina General Assembly web site.
Referred to: Housing and Development, if favorable, State and Local Government, if
favorable, Rules, Calendar, and Operations of the House
March 11, 2025
1 A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
2 AN ACT TO EXEMPT THE TOWNS OF HOLLY SPRINGS AND FUQUAY-VARINA
3 FROM THE COMPETITIVE BIDDING REQUIREMENTS FOR PUBLIC
4 INFRASTRUCTURE PROJECTS.
5 The General Assembly of North Carolina enacts:
6 SECTION 1. The Town of Holly Springs and the Town of Fuquay-Varina may
7 contract for the construction of public infrastructure projects without being subject to Article 8
8 of Chapter 143 of the General Statutes provided that:
9 (1) The Town Council adopts a resolution approving the exemption of a public
10 infrastructure project from the competitive bidding requirements of Article 8
11 of Chapter 143 of the General Statutes.
12 (2) The Town Council conducts an annual independent audit of all contracts for
13 construction work that would otherwise be subject to the competitive bidding
14 requirements of Article 9 of Chapter 143 of the General Statutes.
15 (3) The Town Council makes public any contracts awarded pursuant to this act.
16 (4) The Town Council, after awarding the contract pursuant to this act, prepares,
17 places in the public files, and makes available to the public a document setting
18 forth the reasons for using the authority granted by this act to award the
19 contract.
20 SECTION 2. This act is effective when it becomes law and applies to contracts
21 entered into on or before December 31, 2030.
