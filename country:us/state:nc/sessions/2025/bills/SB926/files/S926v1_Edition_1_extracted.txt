GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF NORTH CAROLINA
SESSION 2025
S 1
SENATE BILL 926
Short Title: Funds for Winston-Salem Urban League. (Public)
Sponsors: Senator Lowe (Primary Sponsor).
Referred to: Rules and Operations of the Senate
April 30, 2026
1 A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
2 AN ACT TO APPROPRIATE FUNDS TO THE WINSTON-SALEM URBAN LEAGUE.
3 The General Assembly of North Carolina enacts:
4 SECTION 1. There is appropriated from the General Fund to the Office of State
5 Budget and Management the sum of one hundred thousand dollars ($100,000) in nonrecurring
6 funds for the 2026-2027 fiscal year to provide a directed grant to the Winston-Salem Urban
7 League to support economic empowerment, education, and workforce initiatives.
8 SECTION 2. This act becomes effective July 1, 2026.
