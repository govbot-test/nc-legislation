Section 1:
FILED SENATE
Mar 25, 2025
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF NORTH CAROLINA
S.B. 478
SESSION 2025
PRINCIPAL CLERK
S D
Short Title: Enhancing Ag. Opportunities in Rural NC. (Public)
Sponsors: Senators Barnes, Sanderson, and B. Newton (Primary Sponsors).
Referred to:
1 A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
2 AN ACT TO PROVIDE BANKS AN INCOME TAX DEDUCTION FOR INTEREST, FEES,
3 AND PENALTIES RECEIVED FROM CERTAIN AGRICULTURAL LOANS.
4 The General Assembly of North Carolina enacts:
5 SECTION 1. G.S. 105-130.5(b) is amended by adding a new subdivision to read:
6 "(33) Interest, fees, and penalties received by banks from loans secured by
7 "agricultural land" as that term is defined in G.S. 105-277.2."
8 SECTION 2. This act is effective for taxable years beginning on or after January 1,
9 2025.
*DRS35194-NIf-88A*
