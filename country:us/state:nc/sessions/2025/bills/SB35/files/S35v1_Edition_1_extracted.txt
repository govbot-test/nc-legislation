Section 1:
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF NORTH CAROLINA
SESSION 2025
S 1
Short Title: Modify Local Workforce Development Board. (Local)
Sponsors: Senator Hanig (Primary Sponsor).
Referred to: Rules and Operations of the Senate
February 3, 2025
1 A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
2 AN ACT TO MODIFY THE DUTIES OF THE NORTHEASTERN WORKFORCE
3 DEVELOPMENT BOARD.
4 The General Assembly of North Carolina enacts:
5 SECTION 1. This act applies to the Northeastern Workforce Development Board
6 serving Dare, Currituck, Camden, Pasquotank, Perquimans, Chowan, Gates, Hyde, Tyrrell, and
7 Washington Counties only.
8 SECTION 2. G.S. 143B-438.11 reads as rewritten:
9 "§ 143B-438.11. Local Workforce Development Boards.
10 (a) Duties. – Local Workforce Development Boards shall have the following powers and
11 duties:
12 …
13 (7a) To designate through a competitive selection process, designate, by no later
14 than July 1, 2014, and annually thereafter, the providers of adult and
15 dislocated worker services authorized in the Workforce Innovation and
16 Opportunity Act.
17 …."
18 SECTION 3. This act is effective when it becomes law.
*S35-v-1*
