Section 1:
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF NORTH CAROLINA
SESSION 2025
H 1
Short Title: Harnett County Jetport. (Local)
Sponsors: Representative Penny.
For a complete list of sponsors, refer to the North Carolina General Assembly web site.
Referred to: State and Local Government, if favorable, Housing and Development, if
favorable, Rules, Calendar, and Operations of the House
April 22, 2026
1 A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
2 AN ACT TO PROVIDE FOR ADOPTION OF LAND-USE PLANNING OVERLAYS FOR
3 THE HARNETT COUNTY JETPORT.
4 The General Assembly of North Carolina enacts:
5 SECTION 1. G.S. 160D-904 reads as rewritten:
6 "§ 160D-904. Airport zoning.
7 (a) Any local government may enact and enforce airport zoning regulations pursuant to
8 this Chapter or as authorized by Article 4 of Chapter 63 of the General Statutes.
9 G.S. 160D-601(d) shall not apply to airport zoning regulations.
10 (b) Airport zoning regulations for real property within 6 miles of any cargo airport
11 complex site subject to regulation by the North Carolina Global TransPark Authority are
12 governed by G.S. 63A-18."
13 SECTION 2. This act applies only to Harnett County.
14 SECTION 3. This act is effective when it becomes law.
*H1028-v-1*
