GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF NORTH CAROLINA
SESSION 2025
H 1
HOUSE BILL 907
Short Title: NC Recovery and Resiliency Act. (Public)
Sponsors: Representative Bell.
For a complete list of sponsors, refer to the North Carolina General Assembly web site.
Referred to: Rules, Calendar, and Operations of the House
April 14, 2025
1 A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
2 AN ACT TO ESTABLISH A DISASTER READINESS AND RESPONSE FUND.
3 The General Assembly of North Carolina enacts:
4 SECTION 1. Part 2 of Article 13 of Chapter 143B of the General Statutes is amended
5 by adding a new section to read:
6 "§ 143B-1042.25. Disaster Readiness and Response Fund.
7 The Disaster Readiness and Response Fund is established as a special fund in the Office of
8 the State Treasurer. The Fund shall be separate and distinct from the General Fund and all other
9 funds and reserves."
10 SECTION 2. This act is effective when it becomes law.
