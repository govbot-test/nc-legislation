23 CLARIFY RULES ON MULTIUSE CHILD CARE CENTERS
24 SECTION 1.4. The Department of Health and Human Services, Division of Child
25 Development and Early Education (Division), in coordination with the Child Care Commission,
26 shall work to clarify the rules governing multiuse child care centers to ensure each of the
27 following:
28 (1) Allow the use of existing buildings to house multiunit child care centers and
29 include mixed-age centers if they meet the requirements outlined in the rules
30 regarding multiunit child care centers.
31 (2) Grant individual licenses within a multiunit child care center based on the
32 square footage used by each owner.
33 Applicants who meet the pre-licensing guidelines and are awarded a license by the
34 Division shall be considered eligible to qualify as a tenant in a multiuse facility.
