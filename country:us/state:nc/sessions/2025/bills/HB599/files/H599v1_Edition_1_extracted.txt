Section 1:
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF NORTH CAROLINA
SESSION 2025
H 1
Short Title: Award NC Forest Service Pay Increase. (Public)
Sponsors: Representatives R. Pierce, Ager, Prather, and Turner (Primary Sponsors).
For a complete list of sponsors, refer to the North Carolina General Assembly web site.
Referred to: Appropriations, if favorable, Rules, Calendar, and Operations of the House
April 1, 2025
1 A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
2 AN ACT AWARDING A LEGISLATIVE SALARY INCREASE TO EMPLOYEES OF THE
3 NORTH CAROLINA FOREST SERVICE AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS FOR THAT
4 PURPOSE.
5 The General Assembly of North Carolina enacts:
6 SECTION 1. Appropriation. – There is appropriated from the General Fund to the
7 North Carolina Forest Service (NCFS) the sum of:
8 (1) Two million six hundred thousand dollars ($2,600,000) in recurring funds for
9 the 2025-2026 fiscal year to provide an across-the-board salary increase of
10 seven and one-half percent (7.5%) to NCFS employees, effective July 1, 2025.
11 (2) Five million two hundred thousand dollars ($5,200,000) in recurring funds for
12 the 2026-2027 fiscal year to provide an additional across-the-board salary
13 increase of seven and one-half percent (7.5%) to NCFS employees, effective
14 July 1, 2026.
15 SECTION 2. Effective Date. – This act becomes effective July 1, 2025.
*H599-v-1*
