Section 1:
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF NORTH CAROLINA
SESSION 2025
S 1
Short Title: Definitions for Advanced Recycling. (Public)
Sponsors: Senators Sawrey, Craven, and Settle (Primary Sponsors).
Referred to: Rules and Operations of the Senate
March 26, 2025
1 A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
2 AN ACT TO PROVIDE VARIOUS ADVANCED RECYCLING DEFINITIONS AND
3 PROCEDURES.
4 The General Assembly of North Carolina enacts:
5 SECTION 1.(a) Subdivisions (17a) and (41b) of G.S. 130A-290(a) are recodified as
6 subdivisions (17b) and (41c), respectively, of that subsection.
7 SECTION 1.(b) G.S. 130A-290, as amended by subsection (a) of this section, reads
8 as rewritten:
9 "§ 130A-290. Definitions.
10 (a) Unless a different meaning is required by the context, the following definitions shall
11 apply throughout this Article:
12 …
13 (17a) "Mass balance attribution" means an auditable chain of custody accounting
14 methodology pursuant to a third-party certification system that enables the
15 manufacturer to attribute quantities of feedstocks to one or more recycled
16 products.
17 …
18 (24a) "Recycled products" or "recycled product" means products that are produced
19 from recyclable material and via mass balance attribution for chemical
20 recycling processes. The designation of a product as a "recycled product" does
21 not exempt the product from otherwise applicable air, water, or hazardous
22 waste statutory or regulatory requirements.
23 …
24 (41b) "Third-party certification system" means an international and multi-national
25 third-party certification system that consists of a set of rules for the
26 implementation of mass balance attribution methodologies for the recycling
27 of materials.
28 …."
29 SECTION 2. This act is effective when it becomes law.
