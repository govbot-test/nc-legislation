GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF NORTH CAROLINA
Apr 9, 2025
SESSION 2025
HOUSE PRINCIPAL CLERK
H D
HOUSE BILL DRH10001-MGa-14
Short Title: Funds for NC Community Health Worker Assn. (Public)
Sponsors: Representative Cervania.
Referred to:
1 A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
2 AN ACT APPROPRIATING FUNDS TO THE DEPARTMENT OF HEALTH AND HUMAN
3 SERVICES, DIVISION OF PUBLIC HEALTH, TO SUPPORT THE NORTH CAROLINA
4 COMMUNITY HEALTH WORKER ASSOCIATION.
5 The General Assembly of North Carolina enacts:
6 SECTION 1. There is appropriated from the General Fund to the Department of
7 Health and Human Services, Division of Public Health, the sum of one million dollars
8 ($1,000,000) in nonrecurring funds for the 2025-2026 fiscal year to be allocated as a directed
9 grant to the North Carolina Community Health Worker Association, a nonprofit corporation, to
10 fund the association's continued mobilization, training, and certification of community health
11 workers for the North Carolina workforce.
12 SECTION 2. This act becomes effective July 1, 2025.
