Section 1:
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF NORTH CAROLINA
SESSION 2025
H 1
Short Title: Competition Parity Act. (Public)
Sponsors: Representative Bell.
For a complete list of sponsors, refer to the North Carolina General Assembly web site.
Referred to: Rules, Calendar, and Operations of the House
March 13, 2025
1 A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
2 AN ACT TO REPEAL SECTION 2 OF S.L. 2024-31.
3 The General Assembly of North Carolina enacts:
4 SECTION 1. Section 2 of S.L. 2024-31 is repealed.
5 SECTION 2. Prosecutions for offenses charged prior to the enactment of this act
6 shall be abated. Any charges shall be dismissed without leave.
7 SECTION 3. This act is effective when it becomes law and applies retroactively to
8 December 1, 2024.
