Section 1:
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF NORTH CAROLINA
SESSION 2025
S 1
Short Title: Medicaid Innovations Waiver Slot Expansion. (Public)
Sponsors: Senator Lowe (Primary Sponsor).
Referred to: Rules and Operations of the Senate
April 29, 2026
1 A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
2 AN ACT TO INCREASE NORTH CAROLINA INNOVATIONS WAIVER SLOTS.
3 The General Assembly of North Carolina enacts:
4 SECTION 1.(a) The Department of Health and Human Services, Division of Health
5 Benefits (DHB), shall amend the North Carolina Innovations waiver to increase the number of
6 waiver slots available under the waiver by 1,000 slots. These 1,000 additional slots shall be made
7 available on July 1, 2026, or as soon as practicable after that date.
8 SECTION 1.(b) There is appropriated from the General Fund to the Department of
9 Health and Human Services, Division of Health Benefits (DHB), the sum of thirty-six million
10 two hundred thousand dollars ($36,200,000) in recurring funds and associated receipts beginning
11 with the 2026-2027 fiscal year to be used to fund these additional slots.
12 SECTION 2. This act is effective July 1, 2026.
*S868-v-1*
