Section 1:
H.B. 944
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF NORTH CAROLINA
Apr 10, 2025
SESSION 2025
HOUSE PRINCIPAL CLERK
H D
Short Title: Fund Community Health Svcs/Mecklenburg County. (Public)
Sponsors: Representative Carney.
Referred to:
1 A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
2 AN ACT APPROPRIATING FUNDS TO THE DEPARTMENT OF HEALTH AND HUMAN
3 SERVICES, DIVISION OF CENTRAL MANAGEMENT AND SUPPORT, OFFICE OF
4 RURAL HEALTH, TO PROVIDE COMMUNITY HEALTH GRANT SUPPORT TO
5 MECKLENBURG COUNTY.
6 The General Assembly of North Carolina enacts:
7 SECTION 1. There is appropriated from the General Fund to the Department of
8 Health and Human Services, Division of Central Management and Support, Office of Rural
9 Health, the sum of one million five hundred thousand dollars ($1,500,000) in nonrecurring funds
10 for the 2025-2026 fiscal year to be allocated as a directed grant to support the operations of Care
11 Ring, Inc., a nonprofit community health clinic located in Mecklenburg County that provides
12 primary care services to medically vulnerable residents who would otherwise be unable to receive
13 needed primary care services due to geographic, economic, or other barriers.
14 SECTION 2. This act becomes effective July 1, 2025.
*DRH40114-MGa-65*
