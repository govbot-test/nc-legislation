8 "§ 115C-83.17A. School performance scores.
9 (a) School Scores. – The State Board of Education shall award school performance scores
10 as required by G.S. 115C-12(9)c1. and calculated as provided in this section.
11 (b) Performance Scores for Elementary Schools. – For schools serving any students in
12 kindergarten through fifth grade, the State Board shall assign points on the following measures
13 available for that school:
14 (1) One point for each percent of students who score at or above proficient on
15 annual assessments for mathematics in grades three through five.
16 (2) One point for each percent of students who score at or above proficient on
17 annual assessments for reading in grades three through five.
18 (3) One point for each percent of students who score at or above proficient on the
19 annual assessment for reading in grade three.
20 (4) One point for each percent of students who score at or above proficient on the
21 annual assessment for science in grade five.
22 (5) One point for each percent of students who progress in achieving English
23 language proficiency on annual assessments in grades three through five.
24 (6) One point for each percent of students who met or exceeded growth on annual
25 assessments for mathematics in grades three through five.
26 (7) One point for each percent of students who met or exceeded growth on annual
27 assessments for reading in grades three through five.
28 (8) One point for each percent of students scoring in the twenty-fifth percentile
29 on the prior annual assessment for mathematics who met or exceeded growth
30 on the current year's annual assessment for mathematics in grades four and
31 five.
32 (9) One point for each percent of students scoring in the twenty-fifth percentile
33 on the prior annual assessment for reading who met or exceeded growth on
34 the current year's annual assessment for reading in grades four and five.
