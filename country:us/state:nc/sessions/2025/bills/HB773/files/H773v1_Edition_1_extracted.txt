12 "§ 115C-83.17. Definitions.
13 The following definitions apply in this Part:
14 (1) Achievement score. – A numerical score on a scale of zero to 100 that is based
15 on the sum of points earned by a school or by a subgroup of students pursuant
16 to G.S. 115C-83.15.
17 (1a) Advanced course exam. – An examination given at the conclusion of an
18 associated Advanced Placement, International Baccalaureate, or Advanced
19 International Certificate of Education course.
20 (2) Growth score. – A numerical score measuring student growth calculated for a
21 school or for a subgroup of students pursuant to G.S. 115C-83.15.
22 (3) Overall school performance grade. – The letter grade earned by a school for
23 all students served by a school pursuant to G.S. 115C-83.15(d).
24 (4) Overall school performance score. – The numerical score earned by a school
25 that is calculated by adding the school achievement score and the school
26 growth score earned by a school pursuant to G.S. 115C-83.15(d).
27 (5) Subgroup performance grade. – The letter grade earned by a school for a
28 subgroup of students served by the school pursuant to G.S. 115C-83.15(d2).
29 (6) Subgroup performance score. – The numerical score earned by a school that
30 is calculated by adding the subgroup achievement score and the subgroup
31 growth score earned by a school pursuant to G.S. 115C-83.15(d2)."
