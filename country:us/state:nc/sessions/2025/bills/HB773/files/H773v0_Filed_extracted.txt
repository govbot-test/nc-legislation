8 "§ 115C-83.17. Definitions.
9 The following definitions apply in this Part:
10 (1) Achievement score. – A numerical score on a scale of zero to 100 that is based
11 on the sum of points earned by a school or by a subgroup of students pursuant
12 to G.S. 115C-83.15.
13 (1a) Advanced course exam. – An examination given at the conclusion of an
14 associated Advanced Placement, International Baccalaureate, or Advanced
15 International Certificate of Education course.
16 (2) Growth score. – A numerical score measuring student growth calculated for a
17 school or for a subgroup of students pursuant to G.S. 115C-83.15.
18 (3) Overall school performance grade. – The letter grade earned by a school for
19 all students served by a school pursuant to G.S. 115C-83.15(d).
20 (4) Overall school performance score. – The numerical score earned by a school
21 that is calculated by adding the school achievement score and the school
22 growth score earned by a school pursuant to G.S. 115C-83.15(d).
23 (5) Subgroup performance grade. – The letter grade earned by a school for a
24 subgroup of students served by the school pursuant to G.S. 115C-83.15(d2).
25 (6) Subgroup performance score. – The numerical score earned by a school that
26 is calculated by adding the subgroup achievement score and the subgroup
27 growth score earned by a school pursuant to G.S. 115C-83.15(d2)."
