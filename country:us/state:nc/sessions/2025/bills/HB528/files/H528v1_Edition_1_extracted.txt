Section 1:
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF NORTH CAROLINA
SESSION 2025
H 1
Short Title: Funds for Coastal Carolina Community College. (Public)
Sponsors: Representative Gable.
For a complete list of sponsors, refer to the North Carolina General Assembly web site.
Referred to: Appropriations, if favorable, Rules, Calendar, and Operations of the House
March 27, 2025
1 A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
2 AN ACT TO APPROPRIATE FUNDS TO COASTAL CAROLINA COMMUNITY
3 COLLEGE FOR RENOVATION OF ITS TRADES BUILDING.
4 The General Assembly of North Carolina enacts:
5 SECTION 1. There is appropriated from the General Fund to the Office of State
6 Budget and Management the sum of twenty million dollars ($20,000,000) in nonrecurring funds
7 for the 2025-2026 fiscal year to provide a directed grant to Coastal Carolina Community College
8 for renovation of its Trades Building.
9 SECTION 2. This act becomes effective July 1, 2025.
