GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF NORTH CAROLINA
SESSION 2025
H 2
HOUSE BILL 3
Committee Substitute Favorable 3/4/25
Short Title: Simpson Even-Year Elections. (Local)
Sponsors:
Referred to:
January 30, 2025
1 A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
2 AN ACT TO PROVIDE THAT REGULAR MUNICIPAL ELECTIONS IN THE VILLAGE OF
3 SIMPSON SHALL BE HELD IN EVEN-NUMBERED YEARS.
4 The General Assembly of North Carolina enacts:
5 SECTION 1.(a) Sections 4.1 and 4.2 of the Charter of the Village of Simpson, being
6 that Charter approved by the Municipal Board of Control and filed with the Secretary of State on
7 April 7, 1975, as amended by Ordinance No. 09-O-01 of the Village of Simpson, adopted on July
8 20, 2009, read as rewritten:
9 "Section 4.1. Regular Municipal Elections. Regular municipal elections shall be held on
10 the Tuesday after the first Monday in November of each odd-numbered year, beginning in 1975.
11 In the regular election of 1975, there shall be elected three (3) members of the Council. Beginning
12 in the regular election in 1975, and in subsequent elections all terms shall be for two (2) years.at
13 the time of the general election in each even-numbered year.
14 "Section 4.2. Regulation of Elections. All Except as otherwise provided, all municipal
15 elections shall be conducted by the Pitt County Board of Elections in accordance with the general
16 laws of North Carolina related to municipal elections, except as otherwise herein provided. the
17 uniform municipal election laws of Chapter 163 of the General Statutes. The municipal elections
18 shall be non-partisan and decided by a simple plurality. No primary election shall be
19 held.conducted on a nonpartisan basis and results determined by a nonpartisan plurality method
20 in accordance with G.S. 163-292."
21 SECTION 1.(b) No municipal elections shall be conducted in the Village of
22 Simpson in 2025. The terms of the Mayor and the three members of the Village Council serving
23 on the effective date of this act whose terms are set to expire in 2025 shall be extended by one
24 year. Regular municipal elections shall be conducted in the Village of Simpson in even-numbered
25 years beginning in 2026.
26 SECTION 2. This act is effective when it becomes law and applies to elections held
27 on or after that date.
