Section 1:
H.B. 31
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF NORTH CAROLINA
Feb 3, 2025
SESSION 2025
HOUSE PRINCIPAL CLERK
H D
Short Title: Make Election Day A State Holiday. (Public)
Sponsors: Representative Biggs.
Referred to:
1 A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
2 AN ACT TO AMEND THE STATE HUMAN RESOURCES ACT TO MAKE THE
3 STATEWIDE GENERAL ELECTION DAY A PAID HOLIDAY FOR STATE
4 EMPLOYEES.
5 The General Assembly of North Carolina enacts:
6 SECTION 1. G.S. 126-4 reads as rewritten:
7 "§ 126-4. Powers and duties of State Human Resources Commission.
8 Subject to the approval of the Governor, the State Human Resources Commission shall
9 establish policies and rules governing each of the following:
10 …
11 (5) Hours and days of work, holidays, vacation, sick leave, and other matters
12 pertaining to the conditions of employment. The legal public holidays
13 established by the Commission as paid holidays for State employees shall
14 include Martin Luther King, Jr.'s Birthday and Veterans Day. Birthday,
15 Veterans Day, and each statewide general election day. The Commission shall
16 not provide for more than 12 13 paid holidays per year, with three paid
17 holidays being given for Christmas.
18 (5a) In years in which New Year's Day falls on Saturday, the Commission may
19 designate December 31 of the previous calendar year as the New Year's
20 holiday, provided that the number of holidays for the previous calendar year
21 does not exceed 12 13 and the number of holidays for the current year does
22 not exceed 10. 11. When New Year's Day falls on either Saturday or Sunday,
23 the constituent institutions of The University of North Carolina that adopt
24 alternative dates to recognize the legal public holidays set forth in subdivision
25 (5) of this section and established by the Commission may designate, in
26 accordance with the rules of the Commission and the requirements of this
27 subdivision, December 31 of the previous calendar year as the New Year's
28 holiday.
29 …
30 The policies and rules of the Commission shall not limit the power of any elected or appointed
31 department head, in the department head's discretion and upon the department head's
32 determination that it is in the best interest of the Department, to transfer, demote, or separate a
33 State employee who is not a career State employee as defined by this Chapter."
34 SECTION 2. This act is effective when it becomes law.
