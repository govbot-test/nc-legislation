GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF NORTH CAROLINA

SESSION 2025
HOUSE PRINCIPAL CLERK
H D
HOUSE BILL DRH40698-MCa-272A
Short Title: Standing Up For Wilson County Vol. Fire Depts. (Public)
Sponsors: Representative Pittman.
Referred to:
1 A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
2 AN ACT TO APPROPRIATE FUNDS TO WILSON COUNTY FOR UPGRADES TO
3 VOLUNTEER FIRE DEPARTMENT EQUIPMENT.
4 The General Assembly of North Carolina enacts:
5 SECTION 1. There is appropriated from the General Fund to the Office of State
6 Budget and Management the sum of fifteen million dollars ($15,000,000) in nonrecurring funds
7 for the 2026-2027 fiscal year to provide a directed grant to Wilson County to make allocations
8 to fire departments located within the county for (i) upgrading emergency lighting on fire trucks
9 with blue lights that are activated when the vehicle is not in motion for the purpose of increasing
10 visibility and motorist compliance and (ii) other costs for upkeep, updating, or replacing of fire
11 equipment or vehicle parts.
12 SECTION 2. This act becomes effective July 1, 2026.
