Section 1:
H.B. 858
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF NORTH CAROLINA
Apr 9, 2025
SESSION 2025
HOUSE PRINCIPAL CLERK
H D
Short Title: Funds/N. Mecklenburg Public Safety. (Public)
Sponsors: Representative Helfrich.
Referred to:
1 A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
2 AN ACT TO APPROPRIATE FUNDS TO ENHANCE PUBLIC SAFETY IN NORTH
3 MECKLENBURG COUNTY.
4 The General Assembly of North Carolina enacts:
5 SECTION 1. There is appropriated from the General Fund to the Office of State
6 Budget and Management the sum of twenty million four hundred forty thousand dollars
7 ($20,440,000) in nonrecurring funds for the 2025-2026 fiscal year to provide a directed grant to
8 the Town of Davidson to be used as follows:
9 (1) Seventeen million dollars ($17,000,000) for construction of a new fire station.
10 (2) Three million dollars ($3,000,000) for emergency vehicle purchases.
11 (3) One hundred thousand dollars ($100,000) for police equipment to enhance
12 community safety.
13 (4) Three hundred forty thousand dollars ($340,000) for breathing apparatus.
14 SECTION 2. There is appropriated from the General Fund to the Office of State
15 Budget and Management the sum of six million dollars ($6,000,000) in nonrecurring funds for
16 the 2025-2026 fiscal year to provide a directed grant to the Town of Cornelius to be used as
17 follows:
18 (1) Three million dollars ($3,000,000) for acquisition of property for the
19 construction of a public safety facility.
20 (2) One million dollars ($1,000,000) to replace the fire department radios.
21 (3) Two million dollars ($2,000,000) to improve pedestrian safety at the
22 intersection of NC 115/Catawba Ave.
23 SECTION 3. There is appropriated from the General Fund to the Office of State
24 Budget and Management the sum of forty-seven million six hundred eighty-three thousand seven
25 hundred eighty dollars ($47,683,780) in nonrecurring funds for the 2025-2026 fiscal year to
26 provide a directed grant to the Town of Huntersville to be used as follows:
27 (1) One hundred one thousand seven hundred eighty dollars ($101,780) for new
28 firetruck apparatus.
29 (2) Eight hundred fourteen thousand dollars ($814,000) for breathing apparatus.
30 (3) Nine hundred ninety-eight thousand dollars ($998,000) for a new firetruck.
31 (4) One hundred ninety thousand dollars ($190,000) for anti-vehicle barriers.
32 (5) Five hundred eighty thousand dollars ($580,000) for 10 additional police
33 vehicles.
34 (6) Fifteen million dollars ($15,000,000) for the construction of a new fire station.
35 (7) Thirty million dollars ($30,000,000) for the construction of a new police
36 facility.
*DRH40475-LGa-163*
General Assembly Of North Carolina Session 2025
1 SECTION 4. This act becomes effective July 1, 2025.
