H.B. 621
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF NORTH CAROLINA
Mar 31, 2025
SESSION 2025
HOUSE PRINCIPAL CLERK
H D
HOUSE BILL DRH40347-LGa-164
Short Title: Funds for the Umbrella Center. (Public)
Sponsors: Representative Greenfield.
Referred to:
1 A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
2 AN ACT TO APPROPRIATE FUNDS FOR THE UMBRELLA CENTER IN CHARLOTTE.
3 The General Assembly of North Carolina enacts:
4 SECTION 1. There is appropriated from the General Fund to the Office of State
5 Budget and Management the sum of one million five hundred thousand dollars ($1,500,000) in
6 nonrecurring funds for the 2025-2026 fiscal year to provide a directed grant to Safe Alliance,
7 Inc., a nonprofit organization, for its Family Justice Center, known as the Umbrella Center, which
8 will provide additional services to those impacted by domestic violence, sexual assault, child
9 abuse, elder abuse, and human trafficking. These funds shall be used for additional land
10 acquisition and an added deceleration lane.
11 SECTION 2. This act becomes effective July 1, 2025.
