SENATE BILL 133
Short Title: NCCCS Learning Management System. (Public)
Sponsors: Senators Lee, Overcash, and Corbin (Primary Sponsors).
Referred to: Rules and Operations of the Senate
February 24, 2025
1 A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
2 AN ACT TO PROVIDE FOR THE STATE BOARD OF COMMUNITY COLLEGES TO
3 SOLICIT A LEARNING MANAGEMENT SYSTEM FOR ALL COMMUNITY
4 COLLEGES AND TO ADD THE COMMUNITY COLLEGES SYSTEM OFFICE TO THE
5 LIST OF ENTITIES EXEMPTED IN G.S. 143B-1320.
6 The General Assembly of North Carolina enacts:
7 SECTION 1.(a) The State Board of Community Colleges shall conduct a
8 competitive solicitation, including a request for information or a request for proposals, to provide
9 a learning management system to all community colleges. The competitive solicitation shall be
10 completed by December 31, 2025. Answers to the competitive solicitation shall include
11 information on how the learning management system would align with the learning management
12 systems (i) offered by the Department of Public Instruction to local school administrative units
13 and (ii) used by the constituent institutions of The University of North Carolina.
14 SECTION 1.(b) By December 31, 2025, the State Board shall report to the Senate
15 Appropriations Committee on Education/Higher Education, the House Appropriations
16 Committee on Education, and the Fiscal Research Division on the information received.
17 SECTION 2. G.S. 143B-1320 reads as rewritten:
