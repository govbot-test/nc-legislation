10 "Carrying Concealed Handguns.
11 "§ 14-415.35. Carrying concealed handguns.
12 (a) Carrying Concealed Handgun. – Any person who is a citizen of the United States and
13 is at least 18 years old may carry a concealed handgun in this State unless provided otherwise by
14 law.
15 (b) Offense. – It is unlawful for a person who meets any of the following criteria to carry
16 a concealed handgun:
17 (1) Is ineligible to own, possess, or receive a firearm under the provisions of State
18 or federal law.
19 (2) Is under indictment for a felony.
20 (3) Has been adjudicated guilty in any court of a felony, unless (i) the felony is
21 an offense that pertains to antitrust violations, unfair trade practices, restraints
22 of trade, or other similar offenses related to the regulation of business practices
23 or (ii) the person's firearms rights have been restored pursuant to
24 G.S. 14-415.4 or have been restored in another state pursuant to the laws of
25 that state.
26 (4) Is a fugitive from justice.
27 (5) Is an unlawful user of, or addicted to, marijuana, alcohol, or any depressant,
28 stimulant, or narcotic drug, or any other controlled substance as defined in 21
29 U.S.C. § 802.
30 (6) Is currently or has been previously adjudicated by a court to be lacking mental
31 capacity or mentally ill. Receipt of previous consultative services or outpatient
32 treatment alone shall not disqualify any citizen under this subdivision.
33 (7) Is or has been discharged from the Armed Forces of the United States under
34 dishonorable conditions.
35 (8) Except as provided in subdivision (9), (10), or (11) of this subsection, is or
36 has been adjudicated guilty of or received a prayer for judgment continued or

34 misdemeanor for a first offense and is guilty of a Class H felony for a second or subsequent
35 offense. Any person who violates subsection (c) of this section commits an infraction and shall
36 be punished in accordance with G.S. 14-3.1.
37 "§ 14-415.36. Unlawful to carry a concealed weapon into certain areas.
38 (a) It is unlawful to carry a concealed weapon into the following areas unless provided
39 otherwise by law:
40 (1) In an area prohibited by rule adopted under G.S. 120-32.1.
41 (2) In any area prohibited by 18 U.S.C. § 922 or any other federal law.
42 (3) In a law enforcement or correctional facility.
43 (4) On any private premises where notice that carrying a concealed handgun is
44 prohibited by the posting of a conspicuous notice or statement by the person
45 in legal possession or control of the premises.
46 (b) Any person who violates this section shall be guilty of an infraction and may be
47 required to pay a fine up to five hundred dollars ($500.00)."
