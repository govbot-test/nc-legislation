Section 1:
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF NORTH CAROLINA
SESSION 2025
H 1
Short Title: Birthplace of Am. Independence Special Plate. (Public)
Sponsors: Representatives R. Pierce, Branson, Cotham, and Miller (Primary Sponsors).
For a complete list of sponsors, refer to the North Carolina General Assembly web site.
Referred to: Transportation, if favorable, Finance, if favorable, Rules, Calendar, and
Operations of the House
March 24, 2025
1 A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
2 AN ACT TO AUTHORIZE THE DIVISION OF MOTOR VEHICLES TO PRODUCE A
3 BIRTHPLACE OF AMERICAN INDEPENDENCE SPECIAL REGISTRATION PLATE.
4 The General Assembly of North Carolina enacts:
5 SECTION 1. G.S. 20-63(b1) is amended by adding a new subdivision to read:
6 "() Birthplace of American Independence."
7 SECTION 2. G.S. 20-79.4(b) reads as rewritten:
8 "(b) Types. – The Division shall issue the following types of special registration plates:
9 …
10 () Birthplace of American Independence. – Issuable to a registered owner of a
11 motor vehicle. The plate shall have a blue background and bear a logo with a
12 feather pen and the phrase "Birthplace of American Independence" with red
13 and white lettering and a design commemorating the 250th anniversary of the
14 signing of the Halifax Resolves in Halifax, North Carolina, on April 12, 1776.
15 …."
16 SECTION 3. G.S. 20-79.7(a1) reads as rewritten:
17 "(a1) Fees. – All other special registration plates are subject to the regular motor vehicle
18 registration fee in G.S. 20-87 or G.S. 20-88 plus an additional fee in the following amount:
19
20 Special Plate Additional Fee Amount
21 …
22 Big Rock Blue Marlin Tournament $30.00
23 Birthplace of American Independence $30.00
24 Boy Scouts of America Expired July 1, 2016
25 …."
26 SECTION 4. G.S. 20-79.7(b) reads as rewritten:
27 "(b) Distribution of Fees. – The Special Registration Plate Account and the Collegiate and
28 Cultural Attraction Plate Account are established within the Highway Fund. The Division must
29 credit the additional fee imposed for the special registration plates listed in subsection (a1) of this
30 section among the Special Registration Plate Account (SRPA), the Collegiate and Cultural
31 Attraction Plate Account (CCAPA), the North Carolina Land and Water Fund (NCLWF), which
32 is established under G.S. 143B-135.234, and the Parks and Recreation Trust Fund, which is
33 established under G.S. 143B-135.56, as follows:
34
*H466-v-1*
General Assembly Of North Carolina Session 2025
1 Special Plate SRPA CCAPA NCLWF PRTF
2 …
3 Big Rock Blue Marlin
4 Tournament $10 $20 0 0
5 Birthplace of American
6 Independence $10 $20 0 0
7 Boy Scouts of America
8 …."
9 SECTION 5. G.S. 20-81.12 is amended by adding a new subsection to read:
10 "() Birthplace of American Independence. – The Division shall transfer quarterly the
11 money in the Collegiate and Cultural Attraction Plate Account derived from the sale of
12 "Birthplace of American Independence" plates to Halifax County Business Horizons, Inc., to
13 support commemorative and educational activities in conjunction with the 250th anniversary of
14 the signing of the Halifax Resolves."
15 SECTION 6. The Revisor of Statutes is authorized to alphabetize, number, and
16 renumber the special registration plates listed in G.S. 20-63(b1), 20-79.4(b), 20-79.7(a1) and (b),
17 and 20-81.12 to ensure that all the special registration plates are listed in alphabetical order and
18 numbered accordingly.
19 SECTION 7. This act is effective when it becomes law.
