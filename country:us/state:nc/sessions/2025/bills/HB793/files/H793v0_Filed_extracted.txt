H.B. 793
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF NORTH CAROLINA
Apr 7, 2025
SESSION 2025
HOUSE PRINCIPAL CLERK
H D
HOUSE BILL DRH40391-LGa-84
Short Title: Fayetteville Area Projects. (Public)
Sponsors: Representative Colvin.
Referred to:
1 A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
2 AN ACT TO APPROPRIATE FUNDS FOR VARIOUS PROJECTS IN THE CITY OF
3 FAYETTEVILLE.
4 The General Assembly of North Carolina enacts:
5 SECTION 1. There is appropriated from the General Fund to the Office of State
6 Budget and Management the sum of five million dollars ($5,000,000) in nonrecurring funds for
7 the 2025-2026 fiscal year to provide a directed grant to the City of Fayetteville to establish an
8 Innovation District, which will be established in a geographic location within the City to foster
9 economic growth and collaboration by bringing together various entities such as educational
10 institutions, businesses, universities, and start-ups in a concentrated area. The City of Fayetteville
11 will collaborate with Fayetteville State University and other local entities to establish the joint
12 venture to stimulate economic activities for the area residents and institutions in Cumberland
13 County, which is a tier one county.
14 SECTION 2. There is appropriated from the General Fund to the Office of State
15 Budget and Management the sum of two million five hundred thousand dollars ($2,500,000) in
16 nonrecurring funds for the 2025-2026 fiscal year to provide a directed grant to the City of
17 Fayetteville to consolidate the City's and Cumberland County's 911 Emergency Dispatch Center
18 to support the region's effort to ensure public safety, emergency services, and medical care.
19 SECTION 3. There is appropriated from the General Fund to the Office of State
20 Budget and Management the sum of twenty-five thousand dollars ($25,000) in nonrecurring
21 funds for the 2025-2026 fiscal year to provide a directed grant to the Hollywood Heights
22 Community Club's Community Center, a nonprofit organization, for facility repairs and
23 maintenance.
24 SECTION 4. There is appropriated from the General Fund to the Office of State
25 Budget and Management the sum of four hundred thousand dollars ($400,000) in recurring funds
26 for each year of the 2025-2027 fiscal biennium to provide a directed grant to the City of
27 Fayetteville to provide equal funding to support the following fire stations:
28 (1) Fayetteville Lake Rim Fire Station.
29 (2) Fayetteville Fire Station 3.
30 (3) Fayetteville Fire Station 15.
31 (4) Fayetteville Fire Station 14.
32 SECTION 5. There is appropriated from the General Fund to the Office of State
33 Budget and Management the sum of one hundred thousand dollars ($100,000) in nonrecurring
34 funds for each year of the 2025-2027 fiscal biennium to provide a directed grant to the City of
35 Fayetteville for maintenance and trail development at Lake Rim Park.
36 SECTION 6. This act becomes effective July 1, 2025.
