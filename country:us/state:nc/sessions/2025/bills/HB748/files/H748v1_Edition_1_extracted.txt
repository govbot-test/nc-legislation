GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF NORTH CAROLINA
SESSION 2025
H 1
HOUSE BILL 748
Short Title: Funds/Greensboro Water and Wastewater. (Public)
Sponsors: Representatives Clark, Blust, Branson, and Quick (Primary Sponsors).
For a complete list of sponsors, refer to the North Carolina General Assembly web site.
Referred to: Appropriations, if favorable, Rules, Calendar, and Operations of the House
April 3, 2025
1 A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
2 AN ACT TO APPROPRIATE FUNDS TO THE CITY OF GREENSBORO FOR WATER AND
3 WASTEWATER IMPROVEMENTS.
4 The General Assembly of North Carolina enacts:
5 SECTION 1. The sum of five million dollars ($5,000,000) in nonrecurring funds for
6 the 2025-2026 fiscal year is appropriated from the General Fund to the Department of
7 Environmental Quality, Division of Water Infrastructure, to provide a grant to the City of
8 Greensboro (City) for planning and other preliminary work for water and wastewater extensions
9 to economic development sites at Piedmont Triad International Airport.
10 SECTION 2. This act becomes effective July 1, 2025.
