GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF NORTH CAROLINA
SESSION 2025
S 1
SENATE JOINT RESOLUTION 1089
Sponsors: Senator Rabon (Primary Sponsor).
Referred to: Select Committee on Nominations
June 29, 2026
1 A JOINT RESOLUTION PROVIDING FOR THE CONFIRMATION OF MELANIE BUSH
2 AS DIRECTOR OF THE DIVISION OF HEALTH BENEFITS, DEPARTMENT OF
3 HEALTH AND HUMAN SERVICES.
4 Whereas, pursuant to G.S. 143B-216.85, appointments by the Governor to serve as
5 the Director of the Division of Health Benefits, Department of Health and Human Services, are
6 subject to confirmation by the General Assembly by joint resolution; and
7 Whereas, the Governor wishes to appoint Melanie Bush to serve as the Director of
8 the Division of Health Benefits, Department of Health and Human Services, for a term expiring
9 June 30, 2029, to fill the unexpired term of Jay Ludlam; Now, therefore,
10 Be it resolved by the Senate, the House of Representatives concurring:
11 SECTION 1. The appointment of Melanie Bush to serve as the Director of the
12 Division of Health Benefits, Department of Health and Human Services, beginning upon
13 confirmation and expiring June 30, 2029, is confirmed.
14 SECTION 2. This resolution is effective upon ratification.
