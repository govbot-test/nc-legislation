1 A SENATE RESOLUTION RELATING TO THE APPOINTMENT, NOMINATION, AND

3 DEPARTMENT OF MILITARY AND VETERANS AFFAIRS.
4 Whereas, pursuant to G.S. 143B-9(a), the Governor shall notify the President of the
5 Senate of the name of each person to be nominated as head of a principal State department; and
6 Whereas, each nomination is subject to senatorial advice and consent in conformance
7 with Section 5(8) of Article III of the North Carolina Constitution; and
8 Whereas, the Governor has appointed Jocelyn Mitnaul Mallette as Secretary of the
9 Department of Military and Veterans Affairs pending further action by the Senate; Now,
10 therefore,
11 Be it resolved by the Senate:
12 SECTION 1. That the Senate shall hereby consider whether to confirm Jocelyn
13 Mitnaul Mallette as Secretary of the Department of Military and Veterans Affairs.
14 SECTION 2. This resolution is effective upon adoption.
