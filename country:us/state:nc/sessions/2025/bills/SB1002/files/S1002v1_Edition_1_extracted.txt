14 (h) Micro-Budget Productions Account. – There is created in the Film and Entertainment
15 Grant Fund a special, nonreverting account to be known as the Micro-Budget Productions

17 within the State. The Department of Commerce shall adopt guidelines providing for the
18 administration of the program. Those guidelines shall apply to each grant from the account and
19 shall adhere to the provisions in this section, with the following modifications:
20 (1) The funds are reserved for a production on which the production company has
21 qualifying expenses of at least fifty thousand dollars ($50,000) in the State but
22 not more than one million four hundred ninety-nine thousand dollars

28 a. A bonus amount equal to five percent (5%) of the base amount if the
29 production company provides qualified accounting showing at least
30 seventy-five percent (75%) of the compensation and wages paid for

32 this sub-subdivision, "qualified accounting" by the production

34 Department of Revenue under Article 4A of Chapter 105 of the
35 General Statutes and (ii) payments for compensation to independent
36 contractors reported using a Form 1099. In calculating whether the
37 required percentage has been met, the following shall be included in

39 health, pension, and welfare contributions; per diems, stipends, and
40 living allowances paid for work being performed in this State; and

47 this sub-subdivision, a "development tier area" is as defined in
48 G.S. 143B-437.08.

50 (75%) of the filming occurred in the State by or with a production company
