H.B. 1198
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF NORTH CAROLINA
Apr 30, 2026
SESSION 2025
HOUSE PRINCIPAL CLERK
H D
HOUSE BILL DRH30596-NBa-146A
Short Title: SANE Nurses Data/Registration Info Reqs. (Public)
Sponsors: Representative Johnson-Hostler.
Referred to:
1 A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
2 AN ACT TO REQUIRE PROGRAMS APPROVED BY THE BOARD THAT TRAIN
3 SEXUAL ASSAULT NURSE EXAMINERS TO ANNUALLY REPORT CERTAIN
4 INFORMATION AND TO IMPLEMENT A NURSING DATABASE FOR NURSE
5 LICENSURE, TRAINING, AND EMPLOYMENT INFORMATION.
6 The General Assembly of North Carolina enacts:
7 SECTION 1.(a) Article 9A of Chapter 90 of the General Statutes is amended by
8 adding a new section to read:
9 "§ 90-171.38A. Sexual assault nurse examiners data collection.
10 On October 1 of each year, programs approved by the Board under G.S. 90-171.38 to train,
11 educate, and credential sexual assault nurse examiners shall annually report to the Board
12 information on all graduates from each program. This report shall include the number of total
13 graduates, the county of employment of each graduate, and, if known, where each graduate plans
14 to work or is employed."
15 SECTION 1.(b) Article 9A of Chapter 90 of the General Statutes is amended by
16 adding a new section to read:
17 "§ 90-171.43B. Nurse information database.
18 (a) All active licensees under this Article shall register with the Board their respective
19 training and education credentials and the location and business entity with which they practice.
20 Each active licensee shall update training and educational credentials, if necessary, when the
21 active licensee renews their license with the Board. Any location and business entity changes
22 shall be submitted to the Board within 30 business days of the change. Inactive licensees are not
23 subject to this section.
24 (b) The Board shall develop and maintain a database accessible to the Board with all of
25 the information required by this section."
26 SECTION 2. All active licensees under the North Carolina Board of Nursing shall
27 submit the information required under G.S. 90-171.43B, as enacted by this act, within one
28 calendar year of this act becoming law.
29 SECTION 3. The North Carolina Board of Nursing may adopt rules to implement
30 the provisions of this act.
31 SECTION 4. There is appropriated from the General Fund to the Department of
32 Health and Human Services the nonrecurring sum of fifty thousand dollars ($50,000) for the
33 2026-2027 fiscal year to assist the North Carolina Board of Nursing in implementing the data
34 collection required under G.S. 90-171.38A, as enacted by this act.
35 SECTION 5. Section 4 of this act becomes effective July 1, 2026. The remainder of
36 this act becomes effective October 1, 2026.
