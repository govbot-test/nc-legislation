GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF NORTH CAROLINA
SESSION 2025
S 1
SENATE BILL 925
Short Title: Cure Violence Program Grant. (Public)
Sponsors: Senator Lowe (Primary Sponsor).
Referred to: Rules and Operations of the Senate
April 30, 2026
1 A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
2 AN ACT TO APPROPRIATE FUNDS FOR VIOLENCE PREVENTION BENEFITTING THE
3 TRIAD REGION.
4 The General Assembly of North Carolina enacts:
5 SECTION 1. Notwithstanding G.S. 143C-5-2, there is appropriated from the
6 General Fund to the Office of State Budget and Management the sum of one million dollars
7 ($1,000,000) in nonrecurring funds for the 2026-2027 fiscal year to be allocated in the form of a
8 grant to Forsyth County to be used for the Cure Violence program to address violence.
9 SECTION 2. This act becomes effective July 1, 2026.
