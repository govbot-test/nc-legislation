Section 1:
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF NORTH CAROLINA
SESSION 2025
S 1
Short Title: State Auditor/Crisis Pregnancy Centers. (Public)
Sponsors: Senator Everitt (Primary Sponsor).
Referred to: Rules and Operations of the Senate
March 26, 2025
1 A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
2 AN ACT TO DIRECT THE STATE AUDITOR TO PERIODICALLY AUDIT CRISIS
3 PREGNANCY CENTERS THAT RECEIVE STATE FUNDS.
4 The General Assembly of North Carolina enacts:
5 SECTION 1. G.S. 147-64.6(c) reads as rewritten:
6 "(c) Responsibilities. – The Auditor is responsible for the following acts and activities:
7 …
8 (24) The Auditor shall periodically audit crisis pregnancy centers that receive State
9 funds."
10 SECTION 2. This act is effective when it becomes law.
*S544-v-1*
