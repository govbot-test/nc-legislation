GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF NORTH CAROLINA
SESSION 2025
S 1
SENATE BILL 284
Short Title: Guilford County Schools Funding Requests. (Public)
Sponsors: Senators Robinson and Garrett (Primary Sponsors).
Referred to: Rules and Operations of the Senate
March 17, 2025
1 A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
2 AN ACT TO APPROPRIATE FUNDS TO CONTINUE PROGRAMS IN GUILFORD
3 COUNTY SCHOOLS FOR TUTORING AND LEARNING HUBS.
4 The General Assembly of North Carolina enacts:
5 SECTION 1. There is appropriated from the General Fund to the Department of
6 Public Instruction for the 2025-2026 fiscal year the sum of eight million three hundred thousand
7 dollars ($8,300,000) in nonrecurring funds to be allocated as a directed grant to Guilford County
8 Schools to support academic support programs and improve facilities as follows:
9 (1) Five million dollars ($5,000,000) to support the high dosage tutoring program
10 by hiring more tutors, expanding tutoring subject areas, and offering more
11 tutoring sessions.
12 (2) Three million three hundred thousand dollars ($3,300,000) to support the
13 learning hub program by providing students that are at risk of not graduating
14 a flexible, individualized program to complete graduation requirements and
15 increase graduation rates.
16 SECTION 2. This act becomes effective July 1, 2025.
