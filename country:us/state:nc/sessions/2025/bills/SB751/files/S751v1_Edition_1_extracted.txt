GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF NORTH CAROLINA
SESSION 2025
S 1
SENATE BILL 751
Short Title: Protect Children from Leaking Garbage Trucks. (Public)
Sponsors: Senators Garrett and Robinson (Primary Sponsors).
Referred to: Rules and Operations of the Senate
March 26, 2025
1 A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
2 AN ACT TO RESTORE PUBLIC HEALTH PROTECTIONS FROM LEAKING SOLID
3 WASTE TRANSPORT VEHICLES.
4 The General Assembly of North Carolina enacts:
5 SECTION 1. Section 59.2 of S.L. 2013-413 is repealed.
6 SECTION 2. The Commission for Public Health shall readopt a rule substantively
7 identical to 15A NCAC 13B .0105 as it existed on August 22, 2013. Rules adopted pursuant to
8 this section are not subject to Part 3 of Article 2A of Chapter 150B of the General Statutes. Rules
9 adopted pursuant to this section shall become effective as provided in G.S. 150B-21.3(b1) as
10 though 10 or more written objections had been received as provided in G.S. 150B-21.3(b2).
11 SECTION 3. Until the effective date of the readopted rule that the Commission for
12 Public Health is required to adopt pursuant to Section 2 of this act, the Commission and the
13 Department of Environmental Quality shall implement 15A NCAC 13B .0105 as it existed prior
14 to the effective date of S.L. 2013-413.
15 SECTION 4. This act is effective when it becomes law.
