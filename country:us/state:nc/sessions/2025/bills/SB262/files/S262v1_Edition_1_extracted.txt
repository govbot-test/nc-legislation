GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF NORTH CAROLINA
SESSION 2025
S 1
SENATE BILL 262
Short Title: Rescind Old Calls/Constitutional Convention. (Public)
Sponsors: Senator Hise (Primary Sponsor).
Referred to: Rules and Operations of the Senate
March 11, 2025
1 A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
2 AN ACT RESCINDING ALL EXTANT APPLICATIONS BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY
3 TO CONGRESS HERETOFORE RATIFIED PRIOR TO DECEMBER 1, 2024, CALLING
4 FOR A CONVENTION PURSUANT TO THE TERMS OF ARTICLE V OF THE
5 CONSTITUTION OF THE UNITED STATES FOR PROPOSING ONE OR MORE
6 AMENDMENTS TO THAT CONSTITUTION.
7 The General Assembly of North Carolina enacts:
8 SECTION 1. The General Assembly rescinds all extant applications by the General
9 Assembly to the Congress of the United States ratified prior to December 1, 2024, to call for a
10 convention to propose amendments to the Constitution of the United States, pursuant to the terms
11 of Article V of the Constitution of the United States thereof, regardless of whether the
12 applications were for a limited convention to propose one or more amendments regarding one or
13 more specific subjects and purposes or for a general convention to propose an unlimited number
14 of amendments upon an unlimited number of subjects.
15 SECTION 2. This act is effective when it becomes law.
