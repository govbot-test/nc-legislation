GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF NORTH CAROLINA
SESSION 2025
H 1
Short Title: Divine Nine Special Registration Plate Act. (Public)
Sponsors: Representatives Roberson, Hawkins, and Dahle (Primary Sponsors).
For a complete list of sponsors, refer to the North Carolina General Assembly web site.
Referred to: Transportation, if favorable, Finance, if favorable, Rules, Calendar, and
Operations of the House
March 17, 2025
1 A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
2 AN ACT ENACTING THE DIVINE NINE SPECIAL REGISTRATION PLATE ACT BY
3 ESTABLISHING A SIGMA GAMMA RHO SORORITY SPECIAL REGISTRATION
4 PLATE AND ADJUSTING THE FEES AND DISTRIBUTION OF FEES FOR VARIOUS
5 OTHER SPECIAL REGISTRATION PLATES RELATED TO DIVINE NINE
6 FRATERNITIES AND SORORITIES.
7 The General Assembly of North Carolina enacts:
8 SECTION 1. This act shall be known as the "Divine Nine Special Registration Plate
9 Act."
10 SECTION 2. G.S. 20-79.4(b) reads as rewritten:
11 "(b) Types. – The Division shall issue the following types of special registration plates:
12 …
13 () Sigma Gamma Rho Sorority. – Issuable to a registered owner of a motor
14 vehicle. The plate shall bear the sorority's symbol and name and the phrase
15 "Greater Service, Greater Progress."
16 …."

46 Collegiate and Cultural Attraction Plate Account derived from the sale of the Alpha Kappa Alpha
47 Sorority plates to the Alpha Kappa Alpha Educational Advancement Foundation, Inc.
48 () Delta Sigma Theta Sorority. – The Division shall transfer quarterly the money in the
49 Collegiate and Cultural Attraction Plate Account derived from the sale of the Delta Sigma Theta
50 Sorority plates to the Delta Research and Educational Foundation.

1 () Kappa Alpha Psi Fraternity. – The Division shall transfer quarterly the money in the
2 Collegiate and Cultural Attraction Plate Account derived from the sale of the Kappa Alpha Psi
3 Fraternity plates to the Kappa Alpha Psi Foundation.
4 () Phi Beta Sigma Fraternity. – The Division shall transfer quarterly the money in the
5 Collegiate and Cultural Attraction Plate Account derived from the sale of the Phi Beta Sigma
6 Fraternity plates to the PBS Impact Foundation.
7 () Sigma Gamma Rho Sorority. – The Division shall transfer quarterly the money in the
8 Collegiate and Cultural Attraction Plate Account derived from the sale of the Sigma Gamma Rho
9 Sorority plates to the Sigma Gamma Rho Sorority National Education Fund, Inc."
10 SECTION 5. The Revisor of Statutes is authorized to alphabetize, number, and
11 renumber the special registration plates listed in G.S. 20-79.4(b) and G.S. 20-81.12 to ensure that
12 all the special registration plates are listed in alphabetical order and numbered accordingly.
13 SECTION 6. This act is effective when it becomes law.
