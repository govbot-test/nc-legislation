1 SECTION 2. This act has no effect upon the validity of any liens of the City of
2 Washington for ad valorem taxes or special assessments outstanding before the effective date of
3 this act. Such liens may be collected or foreclosed upon after the effective date of this act as
4 though the property were still within the corporate limits of the City of Washington.
5 SECTION 3. This act becomes effective June 30, 2026. Property in the territory
6 described in this act as of January 1, 2026, is no longer subject to municipal taxes for taxes
7 imposed for taxable years beginning on or after July 1, 2026.
