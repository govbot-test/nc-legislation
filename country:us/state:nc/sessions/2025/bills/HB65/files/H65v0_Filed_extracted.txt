H.B. 65
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF NORTH CAROLINA
Feb 5, 2025
SESSION 2025
HOUSE PRINCIPAL CLERK
H D
HOUSE BILL DRH30030-SUf-2
Short Title: Sigma Gamma Rho Special Registration Plate. (Public)
Sponsors: Representative R. Pierce.
Referred to:
1 A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
2 AN ACT TO AUTHORIZE THE DIVISION OF MOTOR VEHICLES TO PRODUCE A
3 SIGMA GAMMA RHO SORORITY SPECIAL REGISTRATION PLATE.
4 The General Assembly of North Carolina enacts:
5 SECTION 1. G.S. 20-79.4(b) reads as rewritten:
6 "(b) Types. – The Division shall issue the following types of special registration plates:
7 …
8 () Sigma Gamma Rho Sorority. – Issuable to a registered owner of a motor
9 vehicle. The plate shall bear the sorority's symbol and name and the phrase
10 "Greater Service, Greater Progress".
11 …."
12 SECTION 2. The Revisor of Statutes is authorized to alphabetize, number, and
13 renumber the special registration plates listed in G.S. 20-79.4(b) to ensure that all the special
14 registration plates are listed in alphabetical order and numbered accordingly.
15 SECTION 3. This act is effective when it becomes law.
