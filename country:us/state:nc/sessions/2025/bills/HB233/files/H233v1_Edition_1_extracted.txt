GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF NORTH CAROLINA
SESSION 2025
H 1
HOUSE BILL 233
Short Title: Funds for Town of Milton. (Public)
Sponsors: Representative Price.
For a complete list of sponsors, refer to the North Carolina General Assembly web site.
Referred to: Appropriations, if favorable, Rules, Calendar, and Operations of the House
February 27, 2025
1 A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
2 AN ACT TO APPROPRIATE FUNDS TO THE TOWN OF MILTON FOR REPAIRS AT THE
3 TOWN'S WASTEWATER PUMP STATION.
4 The General Assembly of North Carolina enacts:
5 SECTION 1. There is appropriated from the General Fund to the Office of State
6 Budget and Management the sum of thirteen thousand two hundred fifteen dollars ($13,215) in
7 nonrecurring funds for the 2025-2026 fiscal year to provide a directed grant to the Town of
8 Milton for repairs at the Town's wastewater pump station.
9 SECTION 2. This act becomes effective July 1, 2025.
