1 A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
2 AN ACT TO ALLOW THE MAYOR OF CONETOE TO VOTE IN ALL MATTERS BEFORE
3 THE BOARD.
4 The General Assembly of North Carolina enacts:
5 SECTION 1. Section 3.3.6 of the Charter of the Town of Conetoe, being Chapter
6 154 of the Private Laws of 1887, as amended, reads as rewritten:
7 "3.3.6 Mayor Not to Vote: Mayor's Voting Authority: The Mayor shall not may vote on any
8 question before the Board except in the case of a tie deadlocking decision of the Board of
9 Commissioners.Board."
10 SECTION 2. This act is effective when it becomes law.
