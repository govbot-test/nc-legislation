GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF NORTH CAROLINA
SESSION 2025
H 1
HOUSE BILL 227
Short Title: U.S. & N.C. Flags/Made In USA. (Public)
Sponsors: Representatives Goodwin, Chesser, Riddell, and Charles Smith (Primary
Sponsors).
For a complete list of sponsors, refer to the North Carolina General Assembly web site.
Referred to: State and Local Government, if favorable, Commerce and Economic
Development, if favorable, Rules, Calendar, and Operations of the House
February 27, 2025
1 A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
2 AN ACT PROVIDING THAT STATE INSTITUTIONS AND POLITICAL SUBDIVISIONS
3 OF THE STATE SHALL NOT USE PUBLIC FUNDS TO PURCHASE FLAGS OF THE
4 UNITED STATES OF AMERICA OR THE STATE OF NORTH CAROLINA UNLESS
5 THE FLAGS ARE MADE IN THE UNITED STATES.
6 The General Assembly of North Carolina enacts:
7 SECTION 1. Chapter 144 of the General Statutes is amended by adding a new
8 section to read:
9 "§ 144-10. Flags purchased with public funds.
10 A State institution or political subdivision of the State shall not use public funds to purchase
11 a flag of the United States of America or the State of North Carolina unless the flag has been one
12 hundred percent (100%) manufactured in the United States from articles, materials, or supplies
13 that have been grown or one hundred percent (100%) produced or manufactured in the United
14 States."
15 SECTION 2. This act becomes effective October 1, 2025, and applies to purchases
16 made on or after that date.
