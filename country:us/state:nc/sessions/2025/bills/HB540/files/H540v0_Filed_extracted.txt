Section 1:
H.B. 540
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF NORTH CAROLINA
Mar 26, 2025
SESSION 2025
HOUSE PRINCIPAL CLERK
H D
Short Title: Additional Funds for Patriot Star Scholarship. (Public)
Sponsors: Representative Goodwin.
Referred to:
1 A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
2 AN ACT TO APPROPRIATE FUNDS FOR THE PATRIOT STAR FAMILY SCHOLARSHIP
3 PROGRAM.
4 The General Assembly of North Carolina enacts:
5 SECTION 1. There is appropriated from the General Fund to the Board of Governors
6 of The University of North Carolina for the 2025-2026 fiscal year the sum of two million dollars
7 ($2,000,000) in recurring funds to be allocated to the Patriot Foundation for the operation of the
8 North Carolina Patriot Star Family Scholarship Program.
9 SECTION 2. This act becomes effective July 1, 2025.
*DRH10252-MTa-103*
