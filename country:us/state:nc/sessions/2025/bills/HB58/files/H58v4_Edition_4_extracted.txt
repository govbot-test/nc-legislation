22 elections held on or after that date.

24 amended by Chapter 838 of the 1959 Session Laws, Chapter 172 of the 1969 Session Laws,
25 Chapter 310 of the 1973 Session Laws, and Chapter 81 of the 1985 Session Laws, two members
26 of the Asheboro City Board of Education shall be elected in 2025 on a nonpartisan plurality basis
27 with the results determined in accordance with G.S. 163-292 to serve a one-year term.
28 SECTION 2.1.(b) This section does not affect the term of office of any member
29 elected in 2025 to the Asheboro City Board of Education. Any vacancy on the Asheboro City
30 Board of Education for a member elected in 2025 shall be filled by appointment of the remaining
31 members of the Asheboro City Board of Education to serve the remainder of the unexpired term.
32 Each member of the Asheboro City Board of Education elected in 2025, or any member
33 appointed to fill a vacancy for a member elected in 2025, shall serve until a successor has been
34 elected and qualified.
