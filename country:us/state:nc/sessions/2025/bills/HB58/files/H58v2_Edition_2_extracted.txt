GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF NORTH CAROLINA
SESSION 2025
H 2
HOUSE BILL 58
Committee Substitute Favorable 3/4/25
Short Title: Extend Elected Officials' Terms/Kittrell. (Local)
Sponsors:
Referred to:
February 5, 2025
1 A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
2 AN ACT TO EXTEND THE TERMS OF THE MAYOR AND MEMBERS OF THE BOARD
3 OF COMMISSIONERS FOR THE TOWN OF KITTRELL FROM TWO TO FOUR
4 YEARS.
5 The General Assembly of North Carolina enacts:
6 SECTION 1. Section 3 of the Charter of the Town of Kittrell, being Chapter 136 of
7 the Private Laws of 1885, reads as rewritten:
8 "Sec. 3. That the officers of said town shall consist of a mayor, mayor and three
9 commissioners and a constable; commissioners, and the commissioners shall have power to
10 appoint a secretary and treasurer.clerk and finance officer. The mayor and three commissioners
11 shall be elected for four-year terms."
12 SECTION 2. This act is effective when it becomes law and applies to elections held
13 on or after that date.
