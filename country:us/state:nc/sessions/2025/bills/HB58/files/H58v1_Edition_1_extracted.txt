Section 1:
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF NORTH CAROLINA
SESSION 2025
H 1
Short Title: Extend Elected Officials' Terms/Kittrell. (Local)
Sponsors: Representative Winslow.
For a complete list of sponsors, refer to the North Carolina General Assembly web site.
Referred to: State and Local Government, if favorable, Election Law, if favorable, Rules,
Calendar, and Operations of the House
February 5, 2025
1 A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
2 AN ACT TO EXTEND THE TERMS OF THE MAYOR AND MEMBERS OF THE BOARD
3 OF COMMISSIONERS FOR THE TOWN OF KITTRELL FROM TWO TO FOUR
4 YEARS.
5 The General Assembly of North Carolina enacts:
6 SECTION 1. Section 3 of the Charter of the Town of Kittrell, being Chapter 136 of
7 the Private Laws of 1885, reads as rewritten:
8 "Sec. 3 That the officers of said town shall consist of a mayor, three commissioners and a
9 constable; and the commissioners shall have power to appoint a secretary and treasurer.
10 Beginning at the regular municipal election in 2025 and quadrennially thereafter, the mayor and
11 the commissioners shall each be elected for four-year terms."
12 SECTION 2. This act is effective when it becomes law and applies to elections held
13 on or after that date.
