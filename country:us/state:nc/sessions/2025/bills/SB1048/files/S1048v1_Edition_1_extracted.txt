GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF NORTH CAROLINA
SESSION 2025
S 1
SENATE BILL 1048
Short Title: Certificate of Title/Off-Road Motorcycle. (Public)
Sponsors: Senator Jarvis (Primary Sponsor).
Referred to: Rules and Operations of the Senate
May 4, 2026
1 A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
2 AN ACT TO AUTHORIZE CERTIFICATES OF TITLE FOR OFF-ROAD MOTORCYCLES.
3 The General Assembly of North Carolina enacts:
4 SECTION 1. G.S. 20-53.6 reads as rewritten:
5 "§ 20-53.6. Titling of all-terrain vehicles vehicles, off-road motorcycles, and utility vehicles.
6 (a) Definition. – For purposes of this section, the term "off-road motorcycle" means a
7 motorcycle, as defined in G.S. 20-4.01(27)h., designed exclusively for off-road use by an
8 individual rider with not more than two wheels in contact with the ground.
9 (b) Certificate of Title. – Any owner of an all-terrain vehicle, modified utility vehicle,
10 off-road motorcycle, or utility vehicle may apply to the Division for a certificate of title by
11 completing an application on a form provided by the Division and paying the fee established
12 under G.S. 20-85(a)(1). The application form shall require, at a minimum, the following:
13 (1) The owner's name.
14 (2) The owner's mailing address or residence address.
15 (3) A drivers license number, which shall be provided as authorized by
16 G.S. 20-52(a)(1a)b. or G.S. 20-52(a)(1b).
17 (4) A description of the vehicle, including the make and model.
18 (5) A statement of the owner's title and of all liens upon the vehicle, including the
19 names and addresses of all lienholders in the order of their priority, and the
20 date and nature of each lien. The provisions of this Chapter that relate to
21 perfection of security interests shall not apply to vehicles issued a certificate
22 of title under this section."
23 SECTION 2. G.S. 20-54 reads as rewritten:
24 "§ 20-54. Authority for refusing registration or certificate of title.
25 The Division shall refuse registration or issuance of a certificate of title or any transfer of
26 registration upon any of the following grounds:
27 …
28 (8) The vehicle is a golf cart. If the vehicle is an an: (i) all-terrain vehicle vehicle,
29 (ii) off-road motorcycle, a defined in G.S. 20-53.6, or (iii) a utility vehicle,
30 the Division shall refuse registration or any transfer of registration but shall
31 issue a certificate of title as provided by G.S. 20-53.6.
32 …."
33 SECTION 3. This act becomes effective October 1, 2026.
