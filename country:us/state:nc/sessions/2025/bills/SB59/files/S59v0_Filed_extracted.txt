1 A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
2 AN ACT TO AMEND THE LAWS GOVERNING VOLUNTARY AGRICULTURAL
3 DISTRICTS TO ENHANCE PUBLIC PARTICIPATION AND THE DEVELOPMENT OF
4 ALTERNATIVES FOR LAND USE DECISIONS IMPACTING LANDS IN SUCH
5 DISTRICTS.
6 The General Assembly of North Carolina enacts:
7 SECTION 1. G.S. 106-740 reads as rewritten:
8 "§ 106-740. Public hearings on condemnation of farmland.
9 An ordinance adopted under this Part or Part 3 of this Article may shall provide that no State
10 or local public agency or governmental unit may formally initiate any action to condemn any
11 interest in qualifying farmland within a voluntary agricultural district under this Part or an
12 enhanced voluntary agricultural district under Part 3 of this Article until such agency has
13 requested the local agricultural advisory board established under G.S. 106-739 to hold a public
14 hearing on the proposed condemnation.condemnation that conforms to the following
15 requirements:
16 (1) Following a public hearing held pursuant to this section, the board shall
17 prepare and submit written findings and a recommendation to the
18 decision-making body of the agency proposing acquisition.
19 (2) The board designated to hold the hearing shall have 30 45 days after receiving
20 a request under this section to hold the public hearing and submit its findings
21 and recommendations to the agency.
22 (3) The agency may shall not formally initiate a condemnation action while the
23 proposed condemnation is properly before the advisory board within these
24 time limitations.until 120 days after the date the local agricultural advisory
25 board submits its findings and recommendations to the agency."
26 SECTION 2. This act is effective October 1, 2025, and applies to condemnation
27 actions initiated by any State or local public agency or governmental unit on or after that date.
